Thomas Skinner
Dr. Thomas Skinner is a founding member of Redbrick LMD and is one
of the firm’s three Managing Partners. He has invested in and developed
close to $2 billion in real estate assets and established a pipeline of over
$4 billion in future development projects.
Prior to founding Redbrick, Dr. Skinner invested in or founded numerous
operating businesses primarily in the real estate, technology, and financial
services industries. He began his professional career at McKinsey & Co.
where he worked with financial institutions on strategy issues.
A Ward 6 resident, Dr. Skinner earned his Bachelor of Arts in Politics,
Philosophy and Economics from Oxford University and his Doctor of
Philosophy in Economics from Massachusetts Institute of Technology.
