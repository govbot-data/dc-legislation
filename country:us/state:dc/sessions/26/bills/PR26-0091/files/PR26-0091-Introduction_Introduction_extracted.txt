Pursuant to section 451(c) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December 24, 1973 (87 Stat.
803; Pub. L. 93-198; D.C. Official Code 1-204.51(c)) and section 202 of the Procurement Practices Reform Act
of 2010, effective April 8, 2011 (D.C. Law 18-371; D.C. Official Code 2-352.02), enclosed for consideration and
approval by the Council of the District of Columbia is proposed emergency act, the “University of the District of
Columbia Change Order 1 and Multiyear Contract No PO-GF-2024-P-0144-BB with CDW Government,
LLC/Coastal Cloud Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2025,” and the accompanying
emergency declaration resolution, the “University of the District of Columbia Multiyear Contract No PO-GF-
2024-P-0144-BB with CDW Government, LLC/Coastal Cloud Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency
Declaration Resolution of 2025.” Also included are (1) the UDC Board of Trustees Approval Resolution No.
2024-54; (2) a Fiscal Impact Statement; (3) and a Legal Sufficiency Statement.
The legislation will approve Change Order 1 and Multiyear Contract No PO-GF-2024-P-0144-BB with CDW
Government, LLC/Coastal Cloud to ensure development continuity and support for the University’s Customer
Relation Management (CRM) system, Salesforce. This capital project will enhance the University’s recruitment
and admission of students, marketing, and communications activities. UDC entered into a single year contract
with CDW for Phase I of the project to end on June 11, 2025, at a cost of $926,132.40. Due to increased costs
delineated in Change Order 1, an additional $274,409.60 is required to complete Phase I: bringing the total cost
of Phase I to $1,200,542 and requiring Council approval. Phase II of the project is a proposed Multiyear contract
with CDW for a firm fixed price of $3,000,000 and period of performance from March 2025 through September
30, 2026. Performance on the remainder of Phase I and on Phase II of the CRM await Council approval to be
initiated. The total CRM contract amount for Phase I (including Change Order) and Phase II is $4,200,542.
I respectfully request the Council’s favorable consideration of the enclosed legislation. Should you have any
questions, please contact Ms. Mary Ann Harris, Chief Contracting Officer, at (202) 274-5426.
