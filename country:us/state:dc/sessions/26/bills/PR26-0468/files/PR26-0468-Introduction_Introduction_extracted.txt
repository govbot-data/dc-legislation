GeNita Finley
Ms. GeNita Finley is the founder, director, and grant writer of Medical
Ascension, Compurecycling Center – a nationally-recognized healthcare
pipeline program that aims to empower the youngest community
members in Mississippi, Arkansas, District of Columbia, and Maryland
regions by promoting healthcare career readiness through clinical skills
development, leadership growth, and social maturation.
Before founding Medical Ascension, Ms. Finley worked in project
management and grant writing for a variety of non-profits and community
advancement programs. Her experience includes time at Step Up for
Mental Health, Sister Circles (community health program for Black
women), and Community & Business COVID-19 Technical Services and
Resources.
A Ward 3 resident, Ms. Finley earned her Bachelor of Science in Psychology from Millsaps
College and Master of Science in Biology-Medical Sciences from Mississippi College.
