Dr. Tremikae Owens
Dr. Tremikae Owens is a dedicated family medicine physician with over
20 years of experience. She has worked in various clinical settings,
including military medicine, occupational health, and integrative health
providing comprehensive care for a wide range of medical conditions.
She completed her residency training in Family Medicine and is board
certified in both Family Medicine and Lifestyle Medicine. Committed to
lifelong learning, Dr. Owens further trained in Medical Acupuncture at
the Helms Medical Institute and became a certified Acupuncture
Physician. Her expertise in both Western and Eastern medicine allows Dr.
Owens to offer a holistic approach to care, integrating acupuncture as a
complementary therapy to enhance overall wellness.
She is passionate about empowering others to achieve better health through a balanced and
comprehensive approach. Dr. Owens is board certified by the American Board of Family Medicine
and the American College of Lifestyle Medicine. She is also a member of the National Medical
Association, and the American Academy of Medical Acupuncture
A Ward 5 resident, Dr. Tremikae earned her Bachelor of Science in Biology at Spelman College
and her Doctor of Medicine at the University of Arkansas.
