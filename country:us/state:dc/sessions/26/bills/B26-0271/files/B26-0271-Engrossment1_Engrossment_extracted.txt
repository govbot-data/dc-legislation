12 To amend, on a temporary basis, the District of Columbia Housing Authority Act of 1999 to
13 establish a temporary Stabilization and Reform Board to govern the District of Columbia
14 Housing Authority (“DCHA”) and to require that the Board and the Executive Director of
15 DCHA take specific actions to reform and revitalize the operations of DCHA.
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
18 act may be cited as the “District of Columbia Housing Authority Stabilization and Reform
19 Temporary Amendment Act of 2025”.
