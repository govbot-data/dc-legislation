6 A PROPOSED RESOLUTION
7 __________
8
9 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
10 ____________________
11
12 To declare the existence of an emergency, due to congressional review, with respect to the need
13 to amend the District of Columbia Housing Authority Act of 1999 to revise the structure
14 of the board of the Authority, provide flexibility for board and executive director training,
15 and update the public housing resident bill of rights; to amend the District of Columbia
16 Government Comprehensive Merit Personnel Act of 1978 to adjust Housing Authority
17 board stipends; and to amend the Confirmation Act of 1978 to conform terminology
18 regarding the Housing Authority board.
19
20 RESOLVED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
21 resolution may be cited as the “Housing Authority Resident Empowerment Congressional
22 Review Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
23 Sec. 2. (a) The Rebalancing Expectations for Neighbors, Tenants, and Landlords
24 (RENTAL) Amendment Act of 2025, enacted on November 13, 2025 (D.C. Act 26-199; 72 DCR
25 12894), sets forth a new permanent structure for the independent governing board of the District
26 of Columbia Housing Authority, to take the place of the Stabilization and Reform Board that has
27 led the Authority’s work since the end of 2022.
28 (b) On July 28, 2025, in addition to approving the RENTAL Act on first reading, the
29 Council passed the Housing Authority Resident Empowerment Emergency Amendment Act of
30 2025, effective September 4, 2025 (D.C. Act 26-145; 72 DCR 9614), to allow the Mayor and the
31 Authority to begin preparing for the board transition during the Council’s summer recess.
32 (c) The current emergency law will expire before the permanent RENTAL Act completes
33 congressional review.

34 (d) Emergency legislation is necessary to ensure that the Mayor and the Authority have a
35 clear mandate to continue working on the transition to the new board structure.
36 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
37 enumerated in section 2 constitute an emergency making it necessary that the Housing Authority
38 Resident Empowerment Congressional Review Emergency Amendment Act of 2025 be adopted
39 after a single reading.
40 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
