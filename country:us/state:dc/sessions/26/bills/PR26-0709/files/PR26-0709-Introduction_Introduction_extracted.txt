Otto Condon
Mr. Otto Condon is an Urban Design Principal at ZGF Architects.
With over 28 years of experience, Mr. Condon has been extensively
involved in guiding master plans for the public and private sectors,
district and neighborhood groups, and transit-oriented development
across the nation. Mr. Condon’s work has provided the framework for
significant public and private investment and his urban design and
planning approach focuses on solutions that address revitalization,
placemaking, triple bottom line sustainability, strategic implementation,
and prioritization of mobility modes. Mr. Condon’s design experience
encompasses mixed-use and institutional development projects, transit
infrastructure, streetscape and public real improvements, and low impact
development projects.
Mr. Condon is professionally affiliated with the American Planning Association and the American
Institute of Certified Planners amongst several others. Mr. Condon has presented at numerous
conferences on topics ranging from architectural design to urbanization and sustainability. In
addition, he has led and participated in projects such as the Minnesota Avenue Northeast Great
Street Design and the Deanwood/Ward 7 Strategic Development Plan.
A Ward 1 resident, Mr. Condon earned his Bachelor of Environmental Design from the University
of Colorado at Boulder, his Diploma in Urban Architecture from the Universidad Autonoma
Metropolitana Xochimilco in Mexico City, and his Master of Architecture and certificate in Urban
Design from the University of Washington at Seattle.
