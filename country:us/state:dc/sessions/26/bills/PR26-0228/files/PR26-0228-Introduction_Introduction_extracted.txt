Ms. Whitney McGill is a licensed cosmetology instructorand esthetician
with over 20 years of experience in the beauty industry. Her goal is to
help everyone look and feel their best, and she is grateful to have found a
career that allows her to do just that. Throughout her career, she has had

and students.
