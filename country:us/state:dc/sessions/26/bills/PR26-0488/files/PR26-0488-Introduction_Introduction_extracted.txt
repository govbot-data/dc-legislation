Donella Brockington
Ms. Donella Brockington was most recently the Vice President of
Business Development at Conduent. Before that, she served as Vice
Pres ident for the Government and Transportation Sector (GTS) of Xerox
Services. She was responsible for growing GTS’s largest contracts in the
District of Columbia, Baltimore City, and Prince George’s County,
Maryland. Her prior positions include Vice President and Client
Executive for the District of Columbia and the State of Maryland, Vice
President of Company Operations and head of the Municipal Services
operation for the District of Columbia, a position she held from 1996
through 1999.
Ms. Brockington joined ACS (then Lockheed Martin) in 1988 as Vice
President of Municipal Marketing. In 1991, she was named Vice President of Company
Marketing, where she was responsible for directing and supporting priority marketing efforts and
providing direct marketing support company wide. She also assisted in identifying and developing
new product areas.
Ms. Brockington is a recipient of numerous awards and recognitions, including the 2001 Inaugural
Heroines in Technology Lifetime Achievement Award from the March of Dimes. Notably, she
was selected as a 2011 Professional Woman of the Year by the National Association of
Professional Women, as Leadership Greater Washington’s 2010 Volunteer of the Year, and as one
of the Washington Business Journal’s 2017 Women Who Mean Business.
A Ward 5 resident, Ms. Brockington earned her Bachelor of Arts in Math and Psychology from
Clark University and both her Master of Education in Guidance and Counseling and her Master of
Urban Systems Engineering from Howard University.
