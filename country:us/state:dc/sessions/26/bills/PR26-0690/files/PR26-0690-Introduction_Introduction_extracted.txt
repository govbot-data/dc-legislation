Dr. Stephanie Johnican
Dr. Stephanie Johnican is currently a Primary Care Clinical Pharmacist at
the Washington DC Veterans Affairs Medical Center. Previously, she
served as a Clinical Pharmacist at the Oakland VA Outpatient Clinic. Dr.
Johnican completed her residency at the Federal Bureau of Prisons and is
licensed to practice pharmacy in Kentucky, California, Maryland,
Virginia, and the District of Columbia.
A Ward 6 resident, Dr. Johnican earned her Bachelor of Science from
Kentucky State University and her Doctor of Pharmacy from Touro
University California.
