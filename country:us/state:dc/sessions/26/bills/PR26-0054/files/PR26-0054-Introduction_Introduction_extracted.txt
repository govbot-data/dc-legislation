Dear Chairman Mendelson:
Enclosed for consideration and approval by the Council of the District of Columbia is an
emergency bill, the “Contract No. CW118832 with Modaxo Traffic Management USA, Inc.,
Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2025,” and the accompanying emergency
declaration resolution.
The legislation will approve Contract No. CW118832 with Modaxo Traffic Management USA,
Inc., to support the provision of ticket processing and adjudication services to support the District’s
enforcement of its parking and traffic laws.
My administration is available to discuss any questions you may have regarding the proposed
contract modification. In order to facilitate a response to any questions you may have, please have
your staff contact Marc Scott, Chief Operating Officer, Office of Contracting and Procurement, at
(202)724-8759.
I urge the Council to take prompt and favorable action on the enclosed legislation.
Sincerely,
Muriel Bowser

26 (b) On October 1, 2024, the Office of Contracting and Procurement, on behalf of the

31 (c ) Under section 451 (b )( 1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
32 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 803; D.C. Official Code§ 1-204.51(b)(l)), Council approval is
33 necessary as the value of the contract is more than $1 million during a 12-month period.

34 (d) Emergency legislation is necessary to allow the continuation of these vital services.
35 Without Council approval, Modaxo Traffic Management USA, Inc., cannot be paid for goods and
36 services provided in excess of $1 million for the period from October 1, 2024 through January
37 28, 2025.
38 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2 constitute
39 emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Contract No. CW118832 with Modaxo
40 Traffic Management USA, Inc., Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2025 be
41 adopted after a single reading.
42 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
