8 A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
9
10 _________________________
11
12 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
13
14 _________________________
15
16
17 To recognize, honor, and celebrate the life of Donna Lorraine Wright-Miller for her lifelong
18 service, compassion, and dedication to the people and communities of the District of
19 Columbia.
20
21 WHEREAS, Ms. Donna Lorraine Wright-Miller was born on March 14, 1964, to Earl
22 Wright, Jr. and Sarah M. Wright in Washington, D.C.;
23 WHEREAS, Ms. Wright-Miller was a proud graduate of McKinley Technology High
24 School, attended the New Sewell Music Conservatory with a focus on piano, and received her
25 Bachelor of Science in Psychology and Master of Social Work from Howard University;
26 WHEREAS, Ms. Wright-Miller was deeply rooted in her faith, and worshipped at Asbury
27 United Methodist Church and, in recent years, the Takoma Metaphysical Chapel;
28 WHEREAS, Ms. Wright-Miller was deeply committed to the field of social work and
29 holistic healing, and spent her career working with children, families, and seniors at the District
30 of Columbia Child and Family Services Agency and MBI Health Services, LLC;
31 WHEREAS, Ms. Wright-Miller spent her life in community and service to the people of
32 the District of Columbia;
1

33 WHEREAS, Ms. Wright-Miller was dedicated to honoring the legacy of her late father
34 and instrumental in securing the naming of the 3800 block of 10th St NW as Earl Wright Jr. Way;
35 WHEREAS, Ms. Wright-Miller spent her life rooted in love for her family and steadfast
36 devotion to her friends and loved ones;
37 WHEREAS, Ms. Wright-Miller touched countless lives through her warmth and light,
38 found joy in hosting gatherings, sharing her vegan home cooking and holistic care rituals, and
39 bringing people together in fellowship and community, and;
40 WHEREAS, Ms. Wright-Miller's lasting legacy of service, compassion, and kindness
41 will live on in the many people who knew her, loved her, and were uplifted by her life.
42 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, that this
43 resolution may be cited as the “Donna Lorraine Wright-Miller Memorial Recognition Resolution
44 of 2026”.
45 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia recognizes, honors, and remembers the life
46 of Ms. Donna Wright-Miller.
47 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
