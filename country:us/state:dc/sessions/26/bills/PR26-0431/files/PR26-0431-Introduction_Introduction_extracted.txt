_______________________________
Councilmember Trayon White, Sr.
A PROPOSED RESOLUTION
_____
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
_____
1 To disapprove reprogramming request No. 26-0097 of $7,000,000 of FY 2026 Capital Funds
2 Budget Authority and Allotment within the Department of Parks and Recreation from the
3 Anacostia pool replacement and Congress Heights modernization to complete the
4 construction of the Southeast Tennis Learning Center project.
5
6 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
7 resolution may be cited as the “Department of Parks and Recreation Funding Reprogramming
8 Request No. 26-0097 Disapproval Resolution of 2025”.
9 Sec. 2. (a) Pursuant to section 47-363 of the District of Columbia Official Code, the
10 Mayor transmitted to the Council reprogramming request No. 26-0097 in the amount of
11 $7,000,000 of FY 2026 Capital Funds Budget Authority and Allotment within the Department of
12 Parks and Recreation (DPR) from the Anacostia pool replacement and Congress Heights
13 Recreation Center modernization to complete the construction of the Southeast Tennis Learning
14 Center.
15 (b) The Council disapproves the $7,000,000 reprogramming request.

16 Sec. 3. The Secretary of the Council of the District of Columbia shall transmit a copy of
17 this resolution, upon its adoption, to the Mayor.
18 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
