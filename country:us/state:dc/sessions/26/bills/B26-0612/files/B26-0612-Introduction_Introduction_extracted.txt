20 Code to provide real property, deed recordation, and deed transfer tax exemptions for

25 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
26 Act may be cited as the “Archdiocese of Washington Parish Real Property, Deed Recordation,
