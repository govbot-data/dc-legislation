10 To amend, on a temporary basis, the Open Meetings Act to clarify the definition of meeting; to
11 provide for a public body’s ability to be briefed about potential terrorist or public health
12 threats so long as no official action is taken; to exempt from the act meetings between the
13 Council and the Mayor provided that no official action is taken at such meetings; to
14 provide that a meeting shall be deemed open to the public if the public body takes steps
15 reasonably calculated to allow the public to view or hear the meeting while the meeting is
16 taking place, or, if doing so is not technologically feasible, as soon thereafter as
17 reasonably practicable; and to further clarify the applicability date for expanded financial
18 reporting requirements for certain boards and commissions.
19
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
21 act may be cited as the “Open Meetings Clarification Temporary Amendment Act of 2026”.
22 Sec. 2. The Open Meetings Act, effective March 31, 2011 (D.C. Law 18-350; D.C.
23 Official Code § 2-571 et seq.), is amended as follows:
24 (a) Section 404 (D.C. Official Code § 2-574) is amended as follows:
25 (1) Paragraph (1) is amended to read as follows:
26 “(1)(A) “Meeting” means any gathering of a quorum of the members of a public
27 body, including hearings and roundtables, whether formal or informal, regular, special, or
28 emergency, at which the members consider, conduct, or advise on public business, including
29 gathering information, taking testimony, discussing, deliberating, recommending, and voting,
