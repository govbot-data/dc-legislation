11 To amend the Advisory Neighborhood Commissions Act of 1975 to modify the notice
12 requirement for certain business licenses; and to amend Chapter 28 of Title 47 of the
13 District of Columbia Official Code to exempt motor clubs that perform private tow
14 services from towing service storage lot requirements, to authorize 4-year basic business
15 licenses, to extend license renewal permission beyond 9 months after expiration, to
16 authorize the Mayor to issue refunds for erroneously paid business license fees, to revise
17 the eligibility requirements for accountancy licensure by authorizing a holder of a
18 baccalaureate degree with 2 years of experience and who has passed an examination in
19 accounting and auditing to obtain a license, to allow the Mayor to add additional
20 licensure requirements by rulemaking, and to amend mobility requirements for those
21 who are licensed in another jurisdiction and want to practice in the District.
22
23 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
24 act may be cited as the “Business Licensing Reform and Accountancy Practice Amendment Act
25 of 2026”.
