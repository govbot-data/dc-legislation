15 To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to amend An Act To establish a
16 code of law for the District of Columbia to authorize the Mayor to issue marriage licenses
17 and authorize temporary marriage officiants during a period of time when the Clerk of the
18 Superior Court of the District of Columbia is not issuing marriage licenses due to a
19 federal government shutdown.
20
21 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
22 resolution may be cited as the “Let Our Vows Endure Emergency Declaration Resolution of
23 2025”.
24 Sec. 2. (a) Under permanent law, the issuance of marriage licenses is considered a non-
25 essential function of the Superior Court. Therefore, in the instance of a federal government
26 shutdown, the Clerk of the Superior Court of the District of Columbia cannot issue marriage
27 licenses.
28 (b) This feature of the District’s judicial system inconveniences District residents
29 and others who have planned or would have pursued a marriage during a federal government
30 shutdown.
31 (c) Given the existing federal government shutdown, it is necessary to amend
32 District law on an emergency and temporary basis to allow the Mayor to issue marriage licenses
33 during a federal government shutdown, so that District residents and others who intend to
1

34 celebrate their marriages in the District may proceed with their plans despite the federal
35 government’s status.
36 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
37 enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Let Our
38 Vows Endure (LOVE) Emergency Amendment Act of 2025 be adopted after a single reading.
39 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
