18 To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to provide relief to Family Re-
19 Housing Stabilization Program participants who may otherwise by exited from FRSP
20 without having been approved for permanent housing.
21
22 RESOLVED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this Act
23 may be cited as the “Family Re-Housing Stabilization Program Protection Emergency Declaration
24 Resolution of 2025”.
25 Sec. 2. (a) The Family Re-Housing Stabilization Program Protection Emergency
26 Amendment Act of 2024 was unanimously approved by the Council on July 9, 2024, to ensure due
27 process protections remained in place for families exiting the Family Re-Housing Stabilization
28 Program.
29 (b) The prior emergency legislation was accompanied by the Family Re-Housing
30 Stabilization Protection Temporary Amendment Act of 2024, which is slated to expire on July 10,
31 2025.
32 (c) No subsequent legislation or proposed legislation has repealed the existing emergency
33 and temporary legislation, which apply specifically if funding is available within the program to
34 support extensions beyond 12 months for participants in the program.
1

35 (d) Legislation continuing these existing protections is necessary to maintain the status quo
36 and to allow extensions for families facing termination of housing assistance where funding is
37 available and circumstances warrant doing so.
38 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
39 enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Family
40 Re-Housing Stabilization Program Protection Emergency Amendment Act of 2025 should be
41 adopted after a single reading.
42 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
