Dr. Allison Hill
Allison Hill, PharmD, RPh is a licensed pharmacist, a pharmacy manager
at CVS Health, and is a current member of the Board of Pharmacy.
Dr. Hill has been a DC resident for over twelve years. She is a
humanitarian, community pharmacist, and world traveler. Dr. Hill has
spent her professional years in pharmacy with CVS Health. Five of those
years, she served as pharmacy manager, and she also serves as a preceptor
for student pharmacists. Dr. Hill has also served as CVS Pharmacy
outreach volunteer at health fairs and senior centers. In the community
currently, Dr. Hill holds memberships with Junior League of Washington,
Kappa Psi Pharmaceutical Fraternity, Inc. DC Grad Chapter, and The
Links, Inc. Metropolitan DC Chapter.
A Ward 7 resident, Dr. Hill earned her Bachelor of Arts in Chemistry from North Carolina State
University and her Doctor of Pharmacy from Howard University.
