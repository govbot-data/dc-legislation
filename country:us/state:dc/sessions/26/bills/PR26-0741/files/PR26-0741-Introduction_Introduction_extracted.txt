Marc Battle
Mr. Marc Battle is a partner in Taft Stettinius & Hollister LLP’s
Washington office with experience as a general counsel and executive
leader in the utility and infrastructure sectors.
Previously, as Chief Legal Officer and Executive Vice President of
Government Affairs for DC Water, he oversaw all legal, regulatory, and
policy matters for one of the nation’s leading regional utilities. He served
as executive sponsor of water resilience, security, and reuse initiatives,
and represented DC Water in international engagements and partnerships.
He advised the Board and executive management on governance and
strategy, directed litigation and investigations, and advanced the
organization’s legislative and regulatory priorities at the local, regional,
and federal levels.
Earlier in his career, Mr. Battle held senior roles with Potomac Electric Power Company, where
he led regulatory advocacy efforts and helped secure regulatory approval for a major merger. He
also achieved significant cost recovery through regulatory litigation. His background includes
positions with a global law firm, the NAACP Legal Defense Fund, the Council of the District of
Columbia, and the U.S. Congress.
Mr. Battle has a long history of community service, previously serving as a trustee of the Federal
City Council and on the boards of the Greater Washington Urban League, Leadership Greater
Washington, and as chair of the board of Step Afrika!. He currently serves as a trustee of
Georgetown Day School and on the boards of the DC Chamber of Commerce and the DC Public
Library Foundation.
A Maryland resident, Mr. Battle earned his Bachelor of Arts in Print Journalism from the
University of the District of Columbia and his Juris Doctor from Howard University.
