PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY
Experienced IT and cybersecurity professional with over 20 years in federal IT governance,
strategic planning, and capital investment. Expertise in IT budget management, risk analysis, and
small business engagement in federal procurement. Adept at managing IT portfolios exceeding
$2.9 billion and aligning technology investments with agency goals. Proven leader in
modernizing IT infrastructure and implementing cutting-edge technology solutions.

Mr. Laurence Gill is a Senior Information Technology Specialist at the
