14
15 To honor and recognize Howard University Bison men’s and women’s basketball teams for their
16 historic 2025–2026 seasons, outstanding achievements, and contributions to the District of
17 Columbia.
18
19 WHEREAS, Howard University, located in the heart of the District of Columbia, stands as
20 one of the nation’s most storied Historically Black Colleges and Universities, with a proud legacy
21 of academic excellence, athletic achievement, and cultural leadership;
22 WHEREAS, the Howard University Bison men’s and women’s basketball programs have
23 long served as pillars of pride for the University community, the District, and HBCU athletics
24 nationwide;
25 WHEREAS, during the 2025–2026 season, both the men’s and women’s basketball teams
26 delivered historic, nationally recognized performances that elevated the profile of Howard
27 University and inspired residents across all eight wards;
28 WHEREAS, the Howard University Bison men’s basketball team, under the leadership of
29 Head Coach Kenny Blakeney in his seventh season, completed a 24–11 overall record and an 11–3
30 Mid-Eastern Athletic Conference record, earning the 2026 Mid-Eastern Athletic Conference
31 Regular Season Championship;

32 WHEREAS, the Bison went on to win the 2026 Mid-Eastern Athletic Conference
33 Tournament Championship, securing an automatic bid to the NCAA Tournament and representing
34 the District of Columbia on the national stage;
35 WHEREAS, the men’s coaching staff provided exceptional leadership and development
36 throughout the season, including Head Coach Kenny Blakeney, Associate Head Coaches Rod
37 Balanis and Nate James, and Assistant Coaches Tramel Raggs and Thomas Weaver;
38 WHEREAS, the Howard University Bison women’s basketball team completed a
39 trailblazing 2025–2026 campaign, demonstrating excellence, resilience, and national-caliber
40 competitiveness;
41 WHEREAS, the women’s coaching staff provided outstanding leadership and mentorship,
42 including Head Coach Ty Grace in her 11th season, Associate Head Coach Brian Davis, and
43 Assistant Coaches Dr. Montel Jones and Casey Monroe-Gaskin;
44 WHEREAS, the women’s basketball team featured a dynamic and diverse group of student-
45 athletes whose talent, teamwork, and academic commitment exemplify the mission and values of
46 Howard University;
47 WHEREAS, both teams exemplified the highest standards of sportsmanship, academic
48 excellence, and community engagement, uplifting the University and the District through service,
49 leadership, and representation;
50 WHEREAS, the players, coaches, staff, families, alumni, and supporters contributed
51 immeasurably to these historic seasons, demonstrating the power of collective effort and the
52 importance of investing in equitable athletic opportunities for District youth; and
2

53 WHEREAS, the achievements of the 2025–2026 Bison men’s and women’s basketball
54 teams continue Howard University’s legacy of excellence and inspire future generations of athletes,
55 scholars, and community leaders.
56 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, that this
57 resolution may be cited as the “Howard University Bison Basketball Historic 2025–2026 Seasons
58 Recognition Resolution of 2026”.
59 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia honors and recognizes the Howard
60 University Bison men’s and women’s basketball teams for their historic achievements and
61 contributions to the District.
62 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
