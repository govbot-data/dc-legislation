ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-238
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
November 4, 2025
To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to approve multiyear
Contract No. CW130186 with Kaiser Foundation Health Plan of the Mid-Atlantic
States, Inc. to provide and administer fully insured health benefits to District
employees, their dependents, and retirees in accordance with the rules of the D.C.
Employees Health Benefits Program.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That
this resolution may be cited as the “Kaiser Foundation Health Plan of the Mid-Atlantic
States, Inc. Health Benefits Contract Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
Sec. 2. (a) The Office of Contracting and Procurement, on behalf of the
Department of Human Resources, proposes to enter into the following multiyear contract
to provide and administer fully insured health benefits to District employees, their
dependents, and retirees in accordance with the rules of the D.C. Employees Health
Benefits Program: Contract No. CW130186 with Kaiser Foundation Health Plan of the
Mid-Atlantic States, Inc. (“Kaiser”).
(b) The estimated price for the 5-year term of the contract is $806,032,713.45.
(c) Council approval is necessary to allow the District to receive the benefit of
these vital goods and services in a timely manner.
(d) These critical goods can only be obtained through an award of the multiyear
contract with Kaiser.
Sec 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the
“Kaiser Foundation Health Plan of the Mid-Atlantic States, Inc Health Benefits Contract
Emergency Approval Resolution of 2025” be adopted on an emergency basis.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
