7 A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
8 __________
9
10
11 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
12
13 ____________________
14
15 To honor and recognize the Howard University Bison Softball Team for their historic 2025
16 championship season, capturing both the Mid-Eastern Athletic Conference (MEAC) Regular
17 Season and Tournament titles, and for their outstanding contributions to athletics, academics, and
18 the pride of the District of Columbia.
19 WHEREAS, the Howard University softball team achieved its first MEAC Regular
20 Season Championship in 15 years, posting a record breaking 19–1 conference record and setting
21 a new program benchmark for wins;
22 WHEREAS, the Bison carried their success into postseason play, capturing the MEAC
23 Tournament Championship with a decisive 6–1 victory over South Carolina State, securing their
24 third ever MEAC Tournament title;
25 WHEREAS, for the first time in program history, Howard claimed both the MEAC
26 regular season and tournament championships in the same season;
27 WHEREAS, graduate student pitcher Ameenah Ballenger delivered a season defining
28 performance in the championship game, limiting South Carolina State to just four hits, and was
29 named the MEAC Tournament Most Outstanding Player;
30 WHEREAS, Head Coach Victoria “Tori” Tyson, who reached her 149th career win
31 during the tournament, was honored as the MEAC Tournament Most Outstanding Coach for her
32 leadership in guiding the Bison to their historic season;
33 WHEREAS, Assistant Coach Camille Navarro, a former standout player, brought
34 experience and continuity to the program, strengthening the foundation of the team’s success;

35 WHEREAS, nine Bison earned All-MEAC First Team honors, including Rookie of the
36 Year Lauryn Jones and All-Tournament Team selection Mia Gonzales, reflecting the
37 team’s extraordinary depth and talent;
38 WHEREAS, the team led the MEAC in offensive production, tallying 392 hits, 100 more
39 than any other team, and scoring 245 runs, far surpassing their competition;
40 WHEREAS, 14 members of the team were named to the MEAC All-Academic Team for
41 maintaining GPAs of 3.0 or higher, demonstrating their excellence as scholar-athletes;
42 WHEREAS, by virtue of their MEAC Championship, the Bison earned an automatic
43 berth in the 2025 NCAA Softball Tournament, where they advanced to the Durham Regional to
44 face nationally ranked Duke University, further cementing their reputation as a rising program on
45 the national stage;
46 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, that this resolution
47 may be cited as the “Howard University Softball Championship Recognition Resolution of
48 2025.”
49 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia recognizes and honors the Howard University
50 Bison Softball Team for their historic 2025 season, their championship victories, and their
51 embodiment of athletic and academic excellence. The Council commends the players, coaches,
52 and staff for their achievements and congratulates them on bringing honor and pride to Howard
53 University and the District of Columbia.
54
55 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately upon the first date of publication in the
56 District of Columbia Register.
