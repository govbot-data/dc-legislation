Dear Chairman Mendelson:
Enclosed for consideration and enactment by the Council of the District of Columbia is an
emergency bill, the “Modification Nos. M0013 and M0014 to Contract No. CW100362 with DC
Doors, Inc. Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2025,” and the accompanying
emergency declaration resolution in the amount not to excees $1,043,954.56.
The legislation will approve Modification Nos. M0013 and M0014 to Contract CW100362 with
DC Doors, Inc. to provide Permanent Supportive Housing III program case management services,
and to fully exercise option year three of the contract. In addition, the legislation will approve
payment for the goods and services received and to be received under the modifications.
My administration is available to discuss any questions you may have regarding the proposed
contract modification. In order to facilitate a response to any questions you may have, please have
your staff contact Marc Scott, Chief Operating Officer, Office of Contracting and Procurement, at
(202) 724-8759.
I urge the Council to take prompt and favorable action on the enclosed legislation.
Sincerely,
Muriel Bowser

26 (b) On June 25, 2025, by Modification No. M0013, the Office of Contracting and
27 Procurement, on behalf of the Department of Human Services, exercised a partial option of
28 option year three of Contract No. CW100362, for the period of July 1, 2025 through December

30 management services in the not-to-exceed amount of $493,329.62.

31 (c) Proposed Modification No. M0014 will exercise the remainder of option year three
32 and increase the not-to-exceed amount by $550,624.94, to a total not-to-exceed amount of
33 $1,043,954.56.
34 (d) Under section 451(b)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
35 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 803; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.51(b)(1)), Council approval is
36 necessary since the modifications will increase the value of the contract to more than $1 million
37 during a 12-month period.
38 (e) Approval is necessary to allow the continuation of these vital services. Without this
39 approval, DC Doors, Inc. cannot be paid for goods and services provided in excess of $1 million
40 for the period from July 1, 2025, through June 30, 2026.
41 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2 constitute
42 emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Modification Nos. M0013 and M0014 to
43 Contract No. CW100362 with DC Doors, Inc. Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency
44 Act of 2025 be adopted after a single reading.
45 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
