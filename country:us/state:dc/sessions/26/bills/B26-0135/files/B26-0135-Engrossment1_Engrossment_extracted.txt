11 To amend the District of Columbia Revenue Act of 1937 to require the Mayor to design and make
12 available for issue a motor-vehicle identification tag that identifies the owner of a motor
13 vehicle as a recipient of the Purple Heart award or as the surviving spouse of a Purple Heart
14 award recipient; and to amend the Office of Veterans Affairs Establishment Act of 2001 to
15 make conforming changes.
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
18 act may be cited as the “Purple Heart License Plate Amendment Act of 2025”.
19 Sec. 2. Title IV of the District of Columbia Revenue Act of 1937, approved August 17,
20 1937 (50 Stat. 679; D.C. Official Code § 50-1501.01 et seq.), is amended as follows:
21 (a) Section 2a(b)(4) (D.C. Official Code § 50-1501.02a(b)(4)) is repealed.
22 (b) A new section 2o is added to read as follows:
23 “Sec. 2o. Issuance of Purple Heart award recipient motor vehicle identification tags.
24 “(a) The Mayor shall design and make available for issue motor vehicle identification
25 tags with a design, identifying words, or emblem that designates the owner of the motor vehicle
26 as a Purple Heart recipient or as the surviving spouse of a Purple Heart recipient; provided, that
27 the surviving spouse has not remarried.

28 “(b)(1) A resident ordering a Purple Heart award recipient motor vehicle identification
29 tag shall pay a one-time application fee of $10, or such other amount as may be established by
30 the Mayor by rule. There shall be no annual display fee assessed for a Purple Heart award
31 recipient motor vehicle identification tag.
32 “(2) The application fee shall be deposited in the Office of Veterans Affairs Fund,
33 established by section 705 of the Office of Veterans Affairs Establishment Act of 2001, effective
34 October 3, 2001 (D.C. Law 14-28, D.C. Official Code § 49-1004).
35 “(c) No identification tags may be issued to any individual under this section unless due
36 proof is submitted that the individual is a bona fide recipient of the Purple Heart medal as may be
37 demonstrated to the Mayor at the time of the application for the identification tags by submission
38 of:
39 (1) DD-214 Certificate of Release or Discharge from Active Duty, or DD-215,
40 issued by the United States Department of Defense, or its successor agency;
41 (2) WD-AGO 53-55 Enlisted Record and Report of Separation Honorable
42 Discharge;
43 (3) General or permanent orders listing the award issuance, which shall be
44 accompanied by a copy of the photo identification of the applicant;
45 (4) A certificate of the award issuance signed by the secretary of the service
46 branch, or a general officer or flag officer; or
