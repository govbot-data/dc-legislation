8 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA

10 ____________________
11
12 To declare the existence of an emergency, due to congressional review, with respect to the need
13 to amend the Open Meetings Act to clarify the definition of meeting, to provide for a
14 public body’s ability to be briefed about potential terrorist or public health threats so long
15 as no official action is taken, to exempt from the act meetings between the Council and
16 the Mayor provided that no official action is taken at such meetings, and to provide that a
17 meeting shall be deemed open to the public if the public body takes steps reasonably
18 calculated to allow the public to view or hear the meeting while the meeting is taking
19 place, or, if doing so is not technologically feasible, as soon thereafter as reasonably
20 practicable.
21
22 RESOLVED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
23 resolution may be cited as the “Open Meetings Clarification Congressional Review Emergency
24 Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
25 Sec. 2. (a) On April 1, 2025, the Council adopted the Open Meetings Clarification
26 Emergency Amendment Act of 2025, enacted April 7, 2025 (D.C. Act 26-41; 71 DCR 4070)
27 which expires on July 6, 2025.
28 (b) On June 25, 2025, the Open Meetings Clarification Temporary Amendment Act of
29 2025 (D.C. Act 25-XXX) was enacted, which is pending congressional review.
30 (c) This identical emergency legislation is necessary to prevent a gap in the law between
31 the expiration of the emergency act and the effective date of the temporary measure.
32 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia finds that the circumstances enumerated
33 in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the “Cherry Blossom
34 Festival Grant Congressional Review Emergency Amendment Act of 2025” be adopted after a
35 single reading.
36 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
