16 To name the basketball court at the Marvin Gaye Recreation Center, located at 15 61st Street,
17 NE, the Rodney Wright Basketball Court to commemorate the life of Rodney Wright, a
18 native Washingtonian and 1997 all-Metropolitan pick who led the Howard Bisons to two
19 MEAC titles before becoming head coach at Eastern High School, where he was twice
20 named DCPS Coach of the Year.
21
22 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
23 act may be cited as the “Rodney Wright Basketball Court Designation Act of 2025”.
24 Sec. 2. Pursuant to section 401 of the Street and Alley Closing and Acquisitions
25 Procedures Act of 1982, effective March 10, 1983 (D.C. Law 4-201; D.C. Official Code § 9-
26 204.01), the basketball court at the Marvin Gaye Recreation Center is hereby named the “Rodney
27 Wright Basketball Court”.
28 Sec. 3. The Secretary to the Council shall transmit a copy of this act, after it becomes
29 effective, to the Director of the Department of Parks and Recreation.
30 Sec. 4. Fiscal impact statement.
31 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement in the committee report as the fiscal
32 impact statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975,
33 approved October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).

34 Sec. 5. Effective date.
35 This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by the
36 Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), a 30-day period of Congressional review as
37 provided in section 602(c)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December
38 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 813; D.C. Code § 1-206(c)(1)), and publication in the District of Columbia
