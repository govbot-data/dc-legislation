12 To amend, on a temporary basis, section 47-813 of the District of Columbia Official Code to
13 provide for more timely classification changes for commercial properties that are to be
14 put to residential use, to provide for an application process to make classification
15 changes, to provide for appeal rights if such application is denied, and to provide for a
16 claw back in the event the real property is not timely put to residential use.
17
18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
19 act may be cited as the “Residential Building Permit Classification Temporary Amendment Act
20 of 2025”.
