12 To amend on a temporary basis, the Small and Certified Business Enterprise Development and
13 Assistance Act of 2005 to require a business applying for certification as a local business
14 enterprise to be independently owned and operated; to amend minimum certified business
15 enterprise performance requirements in a certified joint venture; and to establish uniform
16 hearing procedures for enforcement actions.
17
18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
19 act may be cited as the “Certified Business Enterprise Program Compliance and Enforcement
20 Support Temporary Amendment Act of 2025”.
