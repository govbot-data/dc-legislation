10 To amend, on a temporary basis, the Small and Certified Business Enterprise Development and
11 Assistance Act of 2005 to require a business applying for certification as a local business
12 enterprise to be independently owned and operated, to amend minimum certified business
13 enterprise performance requirements in a certified joint venture, and to establish uniform
14 hearing procedures for enforcement actions.
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this act
17 may be cited as the “Certified Business Enterprise Program Compliance and Enforcement Support
18 Temporary Amendment Act of 2026”.

25 least a 51% interest in a business enterprise and whose personal net worth does not exceed the
26 limits set by the federal Department of Transportation, pursuant to 49 CFR § 26.68.”
