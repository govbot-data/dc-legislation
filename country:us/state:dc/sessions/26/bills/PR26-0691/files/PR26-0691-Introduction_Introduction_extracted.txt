John Auerbach
Mr. John Auerbach is the Senior Vice President for Health at ICF
International, serving as the company’s lead federal health expert. With
over30years inpublichealth, hehas heldseniorroles atthefederal, state,
and local levels.
Previously at the Center for Disease Control, Mr. Auerbach served as
Director of Intergovernmental and Strategic Affairs and earlier as
Associate Director, guidingpolicyandpartnershipswithCMS andmajor
health systems. He was also President and CEO ofTrust for America’s
Health, where he led efforts to advance disease prevention and public
health policy.
In Massachusetts, Mr. Auerbach served as Commissioner of Public Health, launching initiatives
focused on health equity and chronic disease prevention. He also spent nine years as Boston’s
health commissioner, overseeing services for homelessness, substance use, and emergency
medical care. Mr. Auerbach is recognized nationally for his leadership and innovation in shaping
effective, equitable public health systems,
Mr. Auerbach eared his Bachelor of Arts in History from Clark University and his Master of
