Dear Chairman Mendelson:
Enclosed for consideration and enactment by the Council of the District of Columbia is an
emergency bill, the “Contract No. CW103730 with Bound Tree Medical, LLC, Approval and
Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2025,” and the accompanying emergency declaration
resolution.
The legislation will approve Modification Nos. M0004, M0005, and M0006 to Contract No.
CW103730 with Bound Tree Medical, LLC, for emergency medical supplies, to fully exercise
option year two of the contract. In addition, the legislation will approve payment for the goods and
services received and to be received under the modifications.
My administration is available to discuss any questions you may have regarding the proposed
contract modifications. In order to facilitate a response to any questions you may have, please have
your staff contact Marc Scott, Chief Operating Officer, Office of Contracting and Procurement, at
(202) 724-8759.
I urge the Council to take prompt and favorable action on the enclosed legislation.
Sincerely,
Muriel Bowser

22 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
23 resolution may be cited as the "Contract No. CW103730 with Bound Tree Medical, LLC,
24 Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025".
25 Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to approve Modification Nos. M0004, M0005,
26 M0006 to Contract No. CW103730 with Bound Tree Medical, LLC, and to authorize payment
27 for the goods and services received and to be received under the modifications.
28 (b) On September 30, 2024, via Modification No. M0004, the Office of Contracting and
29 Procurement ("OCP"), on behalf of the Fire and Emergency Medical Services Department,
30 partially exercised option year two of Contract No. CW103730 with Bound Tree Medical, LLC,
31 for emergency medical supplies, in the not-to-exceed amount of $995,000 for the period October
32 1, 2024, through December 26, 2024.
1

33 (c) On November 13, 2024, via Modification No. M0005, OCP exercised the remainder
34 of option year two at no additional cost for the period December 27, 2024, through September
35 30, 2025.
36 (d) Modification No. M0006 will increase the not-to-exceed amount of option year two
37 by $605,000, from $995,000 to $1,600,000 for the period October 1, 2024, through September
38 30, 2025.
39 (e) Under section 451(b)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
40 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 803; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.51(b)(1)), Council approval is
41 necessary since the modifications will increase the value of the contract to more than $1 million
42 during a 12-month period.
43 (f) Council approval is necessary to allow the continuation of these vital services.
44 Without this approval, Bound Tree Medical, LLC, cannot be paid for goods and services
45 provided in excess of $1 million for the period October 1, 2024, through September 30, 2025.
46 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2 constitute
47 emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Contract No. CW103730 with Bound
48 Tree Medical, LLC Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2025 be adopted
49 after a single reading.
50 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
