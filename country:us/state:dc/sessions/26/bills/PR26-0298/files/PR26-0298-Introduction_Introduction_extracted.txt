12 To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to authorize bond issuances, to
13 establish special funds, to authorize the Mayor to enter into lease agreements, a
14 development and financing agreement, and other related agreements, and to establish
15 conditions on the expenditure of funds to facilitate redevelopment and management of the
16 approximately 180 acres of waterfront property located at the former RFK Memorial
17 Stadium site.
18
19 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
20 resolution may be cited as the “Robert F. Kennedy Campus Redevelopment Emergency
21 Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
22 Sec. 2. (a) In December 2024, Congress adopted the D.C. Robert F. Kennedy Memorial
23 Stadium Campus Revitalization Act (Pub. L. 118-274), authorizing the Secretary of the Interior
24 to transfer administrative jurisdiction of the 180-acre waterfront campus to the District of
25 Columbia.
26 (b) On February 4, 2025, the Council ratified the transfer with adoption of the Transfer of
27 Jurisdiction Over the Robert F. Kennedy Memorial Stadium Campus Emergency Approval
28 Resolution of 2025 (R. 26-16). With control over the RFK campus, the District began
29 negotiations with the Washington Commanders football team on redevelopment of the campus to
30 include a new stadium, mixed use development, and recreation and open space.
31 (c) On August 1, 2025, the Council adopted the Robert F. Kennedy Campus
32 Redevelopment Act of 2025 (Bill 26-288) on 1st Reading which authorizes the Mayor to enter
1

33 into various agreements with the Commanders to effectuate the development plan and authorized
34 the issuance of bonds to finance the development project. The vote was the latest step toward the
35 District exercising the use of the authority provided by Congress in P.L. 118-274 and finalizing
36 the agreement with the Commanders for development of the campus, to include a new stadium.
37 Bill 26-288 is scheduled for final reading on September 17, 2025.
38 (c) Passage of this emergency legislation allows the Washington Commanders
39 organization to begin critical elements of the stadium design and campus revitalization processes
40 immediately, as the permanent legislation completes the standard congressional review period.
41 Emergency passage will promote adherence to the construction timeline contemplated in the
42 legislation and transaction documents executed with the Executive, for both the stadium
43 structure (to be completed by 2030) and surrounding commercial retail and housing development
44 (to be completed on a schedule determined by the legislation and supporting documents from the
45 Executive).
46 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2 constitute
47 emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Robert F. Kennedy Campus
48 Redevelopment Emergency Act of 2025 be adopted after a single reading.
49 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately
