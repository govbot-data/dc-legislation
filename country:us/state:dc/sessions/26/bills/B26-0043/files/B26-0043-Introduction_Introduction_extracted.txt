15 A BILL
16
17
18 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
19
20
21 To amend the Youth Employment Act of 1979 to provide that participation in a Marion S.
22 Barry Summer Youth Employment Program will contribute to creditable service for
23 purposes of retirement for a participant meeting the specified requirements who
24 becomes a District government employee; and to amend the District of Columbia
25 Government Comprehensive Merit Personnel Act of 1978 to make a conforming
26 amendment.
27
28 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this act
29 may be cited as the "Marion S. Barry Summer Youth Employment Program Creditable
30 Service Benefit Amendment Act of 2025".
31
32 Sec. 2. The Youth Employment Act of 1979, effective January 5, 1980 (D.C. Law 3 46; D.C.
33 Official Code§ 32-241 et seq.), is amended by adding new sections 2d and 2e 10 read as
34 follows:
35 "Sec 2d. Creditable service.
36 "Beginning with fiscal year 1980, participation in a SYEP will be credited toward
37 required vesting periods in the District retirement program for a participant who becomes a
38 District of Columbia government employee following the participant's successful completion
39 of the SYEP.

40 "Sec. 2e. Definitions.
41 "For the purposes of this act, the term:
42 “(1) "Date of enrollment" means the date on which a participant enrolls in a
43 SYEP.
44 "(2) "Out-of-school" shall have the same meaning as provided in section
45 2(46) of the Workforce Innovation and Opportunity Act, approved July 22,
46 2014 (128 Stat. 1429; 29 u.s.c.§3102(46).
47 "(3) "Summer youth jobs" and "SYEP" mean the Marion S. Summer Youth
48 Employment Program.".
49 Sec. 3. Section 2604( I) of the District of Columbia Government Comprehensive Merit
50 Personnel Act of 1978, effective October I, 1987 (D.C. Law 7-27; D.C. Official Code§ J.
51 626.04(I)), is amended by adding a new subparagraph (D) to read as follows:
52 "(D) For the purposes of the Marion S. Barry Summer Youth Employment
53 Program ("SYEP"), established pursuant to the Youth Employment Act of 1979, effective
54 January 5, 1980 (D.C. Law 3-46; D.C. Official Code§ 32-241 et seq.) ("SYEP act"), for a
55 participant of a SYEP who meets the requirements of section 2d of the SYEP act creditable
56 service shall be calculated to include service beginning on the date of enrollment, as defined in
57 section 2e of the SYEP act.".
58 Sec. 4. Fiscal impact statement.
59 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement in the committee report as the fiscal
60 impact statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975,
61 approved October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code§ l-301.47a).
62 Sec. 5. Effective date.
63 This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by
64 the Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), a 30-day period of Congressional

65 review as provided in section 602(c)(l) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
66 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 813; D.C. Official Code§ 1-206.02(c)(l), and publication in the
67 District of Columbia Register.
