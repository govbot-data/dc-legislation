10 To amend, on an temporary basis, the District of Columbia Health Occupations Revision
11 Act of 1985 and the Consent for Vaccination of Minors Amendment Act of 2022
12 to authorize healthcare providers in the District to order and administer
13 immunizations recommended by competent medical and public health
14 organizations designated by the Director of the Department of Health.
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, that this
17 Act may be cited as the “Community Health Temporary Amendment Act of 2025”.
18 Sec. 2. The District of Columbia Health Occupations Revision Act of 1985,
19 effective March 25, 1986 (D.C. Law 6-99; D.C. Official Code § 3-1201.01 et seq.), is
20 amended as follows:
21 (a) Section 102(11)(A) (D.C. Official Code § 3-1201.02(11)(A)) is amended by
22 striking the phrase “in accordance with the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention’s
23 published guidelines and recommended immunization schedules” and inserting the
24 phrase “in accordance with the published guidelines and recommended immunization
25 schedules of the Advisory Committee on Immunization Practices of the Centers for
26 Disease Control and Prevention or a competent medical or public health organization
27 designated by the Director of the Department of Health through a public notice” in its
28 place.
