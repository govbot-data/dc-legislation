14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
15 act may be cited as the “Gallery Court Designation Act of 2025”.

17 Street and Alley Closing and Acquisition Procedures Act of 1982, effective March 10, 1983
18 (D.C. Law 4-201; D.C. Official Code §§ 9-204.01, 9-204.03, 9-204.21, and 9-204.21(f)), the
19 Council officially designates the public alley system in Square 775, bounded by 3rd Street, N.E.,
20 K Street, N.E., 4th Street, N.E., and I Street, N.E., as depicted by the plat in the committee
21 report, as “Gallery Court”.
