26 A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
27
28 __________
29
30 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
31
32 ____________________
33
34 To celebrate the annual 17th Street High Heel Race, a unique and beloved LGBTQ+ tradition in
35 the District of Columbia.
36
37 WHEREAS, on Halloween night in 1986, a group of 25 drag performers and enthusiasts
38 held an informal contest that involved running in high heels from JR’s Bar, up 17th Street, N.W.
39 to Annie’s Paramount Steakhouse, upstairs to the bar for a shot of schnapps, and back;
40
41 WHEREAS, the 17th Street High Heel Race has since become an annual tradition that
42 brings together participants showcasing the diversity, creativity, and vibrancy of the District’s
43 lesbian, gay, bisexual, transgender, queer, and other sexual and gender minority (LGBTQ+)
44 communities, along with thousands of enthusiastic spectators;
45
1

46 WHEREAS, in 2018, the District of Columbia Office of Lesbian, Gay, Bisexual,
47 Transgender, and Questioning Affairs assumed responsibility for organizing the annual 17th
48 Street High Heel Race;
49
50 WHEREAS, in the face of nationwide attacks on transgender people and drag artists, the
51 District of Columbia government still proudly protects and supports gender and sexual diversity
52 and celebrates the power of drag as a versatile, delightful form of art and play; and
53
54 WHEREAS, on Tuesday, October 28, 2025, the beloved tradition will continue, as drag
55 artists assemble to show off their dazzling drag outfits, and sprint or saunter down a stretch of
56 historic 17th Street, N.W., which remains a hub of LGBTQ+ nightlife.
57
58 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
59 resolution may be cited as the “17th Street High Heel Race Recognition Resolution of 2025”.
60
61 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia celebrates the 17th Street High Heel Race
62 and wishes the participants a fabulous evening.
63
64 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately upon the first date of publication in
65 the District of Columbia Register.
