ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-299
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
January 6, 2026
To approve Proposed Contract No. GF-2025-C-0020 with TPM Group LLC to provide janitorial
and related services across approximately 1.2 million square feet of University of the
District of Columbia property.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “University of the District of Columbia Contract No. GF-2025-C-
0020 with TPM Group LLC Approval Resolution of 2026”.
Sec. 2. Pursuant to section 451(b) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 803; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.51(b)), the Council approves the
base year of Contract No. GF-2025-C-0020 with TPM Group LLC to provide janitorial and
related services to the University of the District of Columbia in Fiscal Year 2026 in an amount
not-to-exceed $2,912,833.55.
Sec. 3. Transmittal.
The Council shall transmit a copy of this resolution, upon its adoption, to the University
of the District of Columbia President and the Mayor.
Sec. 4. Fiscal impact statement.
The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Chief financial Officer as the fiscal
impact statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975,
approved October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code §1-301.47a).
Sec. 5. Effective date.
This resolution shall take effect immediately.
