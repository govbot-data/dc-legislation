Dr, Deirdre Orceyre
Dr. Deirdre Orceyre is a naturopathic physician and acupuncturist,
licensedtopractice in Washington, DC. She is currently the Naturopathic
Medical Director and Formulary Director at the George Washington
(GW) Center for Integrative Medicine, where she has practiced since
2007. She has a general medicine practice with special focus on
naturopathic oncology, endocrinology, women's health and digestive
health. Herapproach includes both Chinese and Naturopathic medicine,
encompassing nutrition, acupuncture, lifestyle coaching, botanical and
nutritional supplementation, homeopathy and more. She greatly
appreciates working in an integrative setting, combining wisdom from
conventional medicine with traditional approaches. She believes this
leads tothe bestoverall healthcare forher patients.
In addition to her clinical practice, Dr. Orceyre has taught since 2014 in the Georgetown
Integrative Medicine and Health Sciences Master's Program. She teaches a core nutrition and
botanicals course as well as mentoring students for their summer practicum. She is a dedicated
teacher and considers teaching a core mission of her professional life. She has given countless
lectureson numerous topics over hercareer.
Dr. Orceyre is involved in naturopathic andacupuncture professional organizations andhas served
on the boardof the DC Association of Naturopathic Physicians for3 years. She has participated in
conversations with the Board of Medicine on behalf of her professional association, as well as
working with the Health Sub-Committee during past iterations.
Dr. Orceyreconsultedat the GW Breast Centerfrom 2009 until the pandemic in acomplementary
care clinictwicemonthly, providingacupuncture and nutritional recommendations topost-surgical
breast cancer patients. She hasauthoredtwo chapters inthe Textbook ofIntegrativeGeriatricsand
iscurrently participating ina research studyon the relationshipbetween mercury andbreastcancer.
A Ward 4resident, Dr. Orceyre earned her BachelorofArts in Biology from Reed College, and
bothher Masterof Science in Organizational Management in Oriental Medicineand Doctorate in
‘Naturopathic Medicine from theNational College ofNatural Medicine.
