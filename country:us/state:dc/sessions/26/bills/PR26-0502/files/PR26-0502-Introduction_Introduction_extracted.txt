Nicole Roehm
Ms. Nicole Roehm is the Administrator for the Division of Forensic
Science atthe Wisconsin Department ofJustice (DOJ).
Ms. Rochm has been at the Wisconsin DOJ Wisconsin State Crime
Laboratory (WSCL) since 2007 and has been the Administrator of the
DivisionofForensicSciencessince 2019. Ms. Roehmstartedherforensic
sciencecareeras a DNA Analyst attheWSCL —Milwaukee inJune 2007.
She has also worked on various initiatives for the WSCL and currently
serves as a technical assessor for ANAB (ANSI National Accreditation
Board),amemberoftheAmerican SocietyofCrime Laboratory Directors
(ASCLD), and a member of the Midwestern Association of Forensic
Scientists (MAFS).
A Wisconsin resident, Ms. Roehm earned her Bachelor of Science in Biology with a biomedical
science concentration from the University of Wisconsin and her Master of Science in Forensic
DNA and Serology from the University ofFlorida.
