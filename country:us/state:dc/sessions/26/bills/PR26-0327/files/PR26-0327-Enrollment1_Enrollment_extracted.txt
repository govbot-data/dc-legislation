ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-205
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
October 7, 2025
To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to approve Contract No.
CW125486 with Modaxo Traffic Management USA, Inc., and to authorize payment for
the goods and services received and to be received under the contract.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Contract No. CW125486 with Modaxo Traffic Management
USA, Inc. Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to approve Contract No. CW125486 with
Modaxo Traffic Management USA, Inc., and to authorize payment for the goods and services
received and to be received under the contract.
(b) On June 18, 2025, the Office of Contracting and Procurement, on behalf of the
Department of Motor Vehicles, executed emergency Contract No. CW125486 with Modaxo
Traffic Management USA, Inc. for ticket processing services for traffic violations in the not-to-
exceed amount of $6,002,005.94.
(c) Under section 451(b)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 803; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.51(b)(1)), Council approval is
necessary since the value of the contract to more than $1 million during a 12-month period.
(d) Council approval is necessary to allow the continuation of these vital services.
Without this approval, Modaxo Traffic Management USA, Inc. cannot be paid for goods and
services provided in excess of $1 million for the period from June 18, 2025, through October 16,
2025.
Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the
Contract No. CW125486 with Modaxo Traffic Management USA, Inc. Approval and Payment
Authorization Emergency Act of 2025 be adopted after a single reading.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
