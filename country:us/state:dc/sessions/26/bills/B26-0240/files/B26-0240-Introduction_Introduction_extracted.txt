21 To amend, on a temporary basis, section 215 of the Uniform College Athlete, Name, Image, or
22 Likeness Act of 2022 to permit an institution, conference, or athletic association to assist
23 a college athlete in selecting, arranging for, or providing payment to a name, image, or
24 likeness agent and in selecting, arranging for, or collecting payment from a third party
25 engaged in specific name, image, or likeness agreements with college athletes, and to
26 remove the prohibition prohibiting institutions or conferences from providing
27 compensation to a college athlete for the athlete's name, image, or likeness and to pause
28 applicability of expanded financial disclosure requirements for certain boards and
29 commissions.
30
31 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
32 act may be cited as the "Uniform College Athlete Name, Image, or Likeness Temporary
33 Amendment Act of 2025."
34 Sec. 2. Section 215 of the Uniform College Athlete Name, Image, or Likeness Act of
35 2022 (D.C. Law 24-245; D.C. Official Code § 38-1631.05), is amended as follows:
36 (a) Subsection (a) is amended as follows:
37 (1) Paragraph (2) is amended by striking the phrase “; and’ and inserting a
38 semicolon in its place.

43 likeness agent; and

51 Sec. 4. Fiscal impact statement.
52 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Budget Director as the fiscal impact
53 statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975, approved
54 October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
55 Sec. 5. Effective date.
56 (a) This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of
57 veto by the Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), a 30-day period of
58 Congressional review as provided in section 602(c)(1) of the District of Columbia Home
59 Rule Act, approved December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 813; D.C. Official Code § 1-
60 206.02(c)(1)), and publication in the District of Columbia Register.
61 (b) This act shall expire after 225 days of its having effect.
