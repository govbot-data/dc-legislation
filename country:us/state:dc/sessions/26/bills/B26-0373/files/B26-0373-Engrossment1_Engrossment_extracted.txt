10 To amend, on a temporary basis, the Language Access Act of 2004 to authorize the Office of

13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
14 act may be cited as the Language Access Rulemaking Temporary Amendment Act of 2025”.
15 Sec. 2. Section 6 of the Language Access Act of 2004, effective June 19, 2004 (D.C. Law
16 15-167; D.C. Official Code § 2-1935) is amended by adding a new subsection (c) to read as
17 follows:
18 “(c) The Mayor, pursuant to Title I of the District of Columbia Administrative Procedure
19 Act, approved October 21, 1968 (82 Stat. 1204; D.C. Official Code § 2-501 et seq.), shall issue
20 rules to implement the provisions of this act.
21 Sec. 3 Applicability.
22 This act shall apply as of June 19, 2004.
