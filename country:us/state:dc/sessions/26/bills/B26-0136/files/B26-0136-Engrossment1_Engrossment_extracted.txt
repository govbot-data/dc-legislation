10 To amend the District of Columbia Election Code of 1955 to incorporate into District law the
11 process for filling a vacancy, in extraordinary circumstances, in the office of Delegate to
12 the House of Representatives from the District of Columbia within 49 days after the
13 Speaker of the House of Representatives announces that a vacancy exists.
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
16 act may be cited as the “Special Election in the Office of Delegate Amendment Act of 2025”.
17 Sec. 2. Section 10(d)(2) of the District of Columbia Election Code of 1955, approved
18 August 12, 1955 (69 Stat. 699, D.C. Official Code § 1-1001.10(d)(2)), is amended by adding a
19 new subparagraph (C) to read as follows:
20 “(C) Notwithstanding subparagraphs (A) and (B) of this paragraph and any other
21 provision of District law, in extraordinary circumstances, as defined in section 26(b)(4)(A) of the
22 Revised Statutes of the United States (2 U.S.C. § 8(b)(4)(A)), a vacancy in the office of Delegate
23 shall be filled in accordance with section 26(b) of the Revised Statutes of the United States (2
24 U.S.C. § 8(b)).”.
25 Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
26 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement in the committee report as the fiscal
27 impact statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975,
