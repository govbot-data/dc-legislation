Maria Peace
Ms. Maria Peace is the owner and project manager of a local family-
owned business, PMGL LLC. She and her husband have been serving
families, businesses and government agencies in the District and
neighboring areas for over 14 years.
Prior to shifting her focus solely to PMGL LLC in 2019, Ms. Peace dually
served as Program Manager at General Electric (GE) - Government
Relations Global Research for 14 years. There she served as a liaison
between GE and the Global Research Center (GRC) facilities, and
interfaced with congressional, federal, and executive branches to build
long term relationships. She also translated all strategic budget planning
in efforts to financially fund businesses pertaining to technology,
research, and development within the company for healthcare, power & energy, defense &
security, and transportation in Latin America.
In addition to her 20 years in the real estate/development industry, Ms. Peace has held a variety of
roles such as Case Manager for The Child Center and Adult Services, Inc., Litigation Analyst at
Consolidated Analysis Centers, Inc. CACI – Randstad, and Bilingual Closing Director at
Millennium Title and Settlement Co., Inc.
Ms. Peace and her young daughter, Lincoln, frequently visit District libraries, especially their
neighborhood Francis A. Gregory Library and the MLK, Jr. Memorial Library downtown. Their
family has attended special events, accessed digital and online resources on the library computers,
and read books from the Kids Collection and from DCPL’s Books from Birth program together in
the colorful reading areas.
A Ward 7 resident, Ms. Peace earned her Bachelor of Arts in Communications and her Master of
Business Administration in International Trade from Trinity Washington University.
