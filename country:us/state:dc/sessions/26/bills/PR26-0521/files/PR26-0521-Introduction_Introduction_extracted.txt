20 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this

23 Sec. 2. The Council authorizes the Council’s Office of the General Counsel to initiate,
24 defend, intervene, participate, or take any other action in a matter in any court or tribunal on
25 behalf of and in the name of the Council of the District of Columbia to assert the interest of the
26 Council in obtaining certain agency budget enhancement requests that must be furnished to the
27 Council pursuant to D.C. Official Code § 47-318.05a.
28 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
