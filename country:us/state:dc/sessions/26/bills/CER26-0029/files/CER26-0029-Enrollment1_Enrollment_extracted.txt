ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
26-29
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
April 1, 2025
To honor the Old People Are Cool project, supported by Forest Hills of DC, and celebrate our
District’s seniors by declaring May 2025 as “Old People Are Cool Month” in the District
of Columbia.
WHEREAS, every May the Administration for Community Living within the US
Department of Health and Human Services leads a national observance of Older Americans
Month to honor the independence, integration, and inclusion of older adults throughout all
aspects of life;
WHEREAS, the Administration for Community Living’s theme for Older Americans
Month in 2025 is Flip the Script on Aging, which offers an opportunity to explore diverse aging
experiences and discuss how communities can challenge stereotypes;
WHEREAS, it is acknowledged that ageism currently isolates and marginalizes older
adults, which hurts our communities and inhibits our collective achievement and optimal growth;
WHEREAS, the Old People Are Cool project was created to confront the harmful ageism
that prevents communities from reaching their maximum potential by combatting ageism and
raising the profile of the senior care industry;
WHEREAS, the Old People are Cool project has the core mission to celebrate human life
by acknowledging elders as people, individuals, and recipients of love, and to facilitate thoughts,
words, and actions that promote a world that is loving, aging, and united;
WHEREAS, we additionally recognize the nobility of those healthcare workers who
uplift and care for our elders and seek to acknowledge their dedication to this valued group
within our community; and
WHEREAS, the Old People Are Cool project has built a supportive, inclusive, and
celebratory environment in the District through its efforts of bolstering morale and generating
inclusivity of older adults through community-building events and social media-story sharing.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Old People Are Cool Recognition Resolution of 2025.”
1

ENROLLED ORIGINAL
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia declares May 2025 Old People Are Cool
Month in the District of Columbia, celebrates and recognizes the Old People are Cool project
during the national celebration of Older Americans Month, and honors its commitment to
advancing the welfare and perception of seniors in the whole of the District of Columbia.
Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
