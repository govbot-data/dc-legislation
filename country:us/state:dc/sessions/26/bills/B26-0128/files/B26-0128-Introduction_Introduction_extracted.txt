________________________
Councilmember Brooke Pinto
A BILL
_________________________
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
_________________________
1 To amend, on an emergency basis, the Clemency Board Establishment Act of 2018 to authorize
2 the Clemency Board to, for applicants seeking a pardon, grant a waiver of the 5-year
3 waiting period in certain circumstances, and to amend the Second Chance Amendment
4 Act of 2022 to update the applicability date to October 1, 2027.
5
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
7 act may be cited as the “Safety Cluster Resource Alignment and Clarification Emergency
8 Amendment Act of 2025”.
9 Sec. 2. Section 205 of the Clemency Board Establishment Act of 2018, effective
10 December 13, 2018 (D.C. Law 22-197; D.C. Official Code § 24-481.05), is amended by adding a
11 new subsection (f) to read as follows:
12 “(f) The Board may, upon written request of the applicant, grant a waiver of the
13 requirements of subsection (c)(1) of this section; provided, that:
14 “(1) The Board shall only grant a waiver under this subsection where the Board is
15 in receipt of notice from the Office of the Pardon Attorney that the Pardon Attorney or the
16 President of the United States has provided the applicant with a waiver of the 5-year requirement
17 pursuant to § 9-140.112 of the Department of Justice’s Justice Manual;
18 “(2) The Board may decline to grant a waiver under this subsection even when the
19 Board is in receipt of the notice described in paragraph (1) of this subsection;

20 “(3) The Board shall not grant a waiver under this subsection when the applicant
21 is on probation, parole, or supervised release;
22 “(4) The Board may request additional information from the applicant to support
23 their request for a waiver of subsection (c)(1) of this section, which may include a request to
24 meet with the applicant to discuss their waiver request;
25 “(5) The Board shall provide the applicant with written notice of the Board’s
26 decision to grant or deny a waiver request pursuant to this subsection; and
27 “(6) Prior to the Board making a determination whether to grant a request for a
28 waiver pursuant to this subsection, the applicant’s application for a clemency recommendation
29 shall be deferred until a determination is made.”.
30 Sec. 3. Section 301 of the Second Chance Amendment Act of 2022, effective March 10,
31 2023 (D.C. Law 24-284; 70 DCR 913), is amended by striking the phrase “March 1, 2025” and
32 inserting the phrase “October 1, 2027” in its place.
33 Sec. 4. Fiscal impact statement.
34 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Budget Director as the fiscal impact
35 statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975, approved
36 October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
37 Sec. 5. Effective date.
38 This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by the
39 Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), and shall remain in effect for no longer than
40 90 days, as provided for emergency acts of the Council of the District of Columbia in section
41 412(a) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 788;
42 D.C. Official Code § 1-204.12(a)).
