Appointments, should the Council require additional information.

Randall Henderson
Mr. Randall Henderson is a long-time District of Columbia resident and
has been a healthcare provider for 35 years. He is an MRI Technologist at
Medstar Southern Maryland Hospital. Mr. Henderson also virtually
teaches radiation physics to students at the University of Haiti, preparing
them to take the National Registry Exam.
A Ward 5 resident, Mr. Henderson earned his Associate of Science in
Radiologic Sciences from the University of the District of Columbia, his
Bachelor of Science in Organizational Management from Columbia
Union College, and his Master of Business Administration from National
Louis University.
