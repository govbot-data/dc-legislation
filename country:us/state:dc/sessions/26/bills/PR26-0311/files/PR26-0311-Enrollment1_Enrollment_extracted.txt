ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-233
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
November 4, 2025
To appoint Dr. Maria J. Donoghue Velleca to the District of Columbia Commission on Judicial
Disabilities and Tenure.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “District of Columbia Judicial Disabilities and Tenure
Commission Dr. Maria J. Donoghue Velleca Appointment Resolution of 2025”.
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia appoints:
Dr. Maria J. Donoghue Velleca
22nd Street NW
Washington, DC 20037
(Ward 2)
as a nonlawyer member of the District of Columbia Commission on Judicial Disabilities and
Tenure, established by section 431(d) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 792; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.31(d)), replacing Dr. Patrick
Jackson, to serve the remainder of an unexpired 6-year term to end May 5, 2029.
Sec. 3. The Council shall transmit a copy of this resolution, upon its adoption, to the
appointee, to the chairperson of the District of Columbia Commission on Judicial Disabilities
and Tenure, and to the Mayor.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
