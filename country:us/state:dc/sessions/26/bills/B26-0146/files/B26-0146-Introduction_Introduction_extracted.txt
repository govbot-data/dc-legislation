15 To, on an emergency basis, due to congressional review, approve the development and financing
16 agreement for the redevelopment of the downtown arena; approve an agreement for the
17 disposition by lease of certain real property owned by the District government for the
18 redevelopment and operation of the downtown arena; approve a purchase and sale
19 agreement for the downtown arena; authorize the granting of certain easements related to
20 the downtown arena property; authorize airspace leases associated with the downtown
21 arena; continue and provide for property tax abatements and deed transfer and
22 recordation tax exemptions for the downtown arena; and authorize the issuance of rules
23 to provide for additional signage on the downtown arena.
24
25 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
26 act may be cited as the “Downtown Arena Revitalization Congressional Review Emergency Act
27 of 2025”.
