14 To declare the existence of an emergency, due to congressional review, with respect to the need
15 to amend the Small and Certified Business Enterprise Development and Assistance Act of
16 2005 to require a business applying for certification as a local business enterprise to be
17 independently owned and operated, to amend minimum certified business enterprise
18 performance requirements in a certified joint venture, and to establish uniform hearing
19 procedures for enforcement actions.
20
21 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
22 resolution may be cited as the “Certified Business Enterprise Program Compliance and
23 Enforcement Support Congressional Review Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2026”.
24 Sec. 2. (a) On May 5, 2026, the Council unanimously approved the Certified Business
25 Enterprise Program Compliance and Enforcement Support Emergency Amendment Act of 2026,
26 effective May 29, 2026 (D.C. Act 26-334; 73 DCR 8193) (“Emergency Act”). The Emergency
27 Act is set to expire on August 27, 2026.
28 (b) On June 2, 2026, the Council unanimously approved the Certified Business Enterprise
29 Program Compliance and Enforcement Support Temporary Amendment Act of 2026, enacted
30 June 25, 2026 (D.C. Act 26-349; 73 DCR 9206) (“Temporary Act”). The Temporary Act has
31 been transmitted to Congress and has a projected law date of September 19, 2026.
32 (d) This congressional review emergency legislation is necessary to prevent a gap in the law
33 between the expiration of the Emergency Act and the completion of the Temporary Act’s
34 congressional review period.
1

35
36 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2 constitute
37 emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Certified Business Enterprise Program
38 Compliance and Enforcement Support Congressional Review Emergency Amendment Act of
39 2026 be adopted after a single reading.
40
41 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
