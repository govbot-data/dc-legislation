21 Sec. 2. (a) The Fiscal Year 2026 Budget Support Act of 2025 contains various measures
22 necessary to support the Fiscal Year 2026 Budget and Financial Plan.
23 (b) There are several time-sensitive provisions contained in the Fiscal Year 2026 Budget
24 Support Act of 2025 that need to be in place in advance of October 1, 2025.
25 (c) Other provisions in the emergency bill will retain the October 1, 2025, applicability
26 date as provided in the permanent legislation but should be enacted prior to October 1 to allow
27 agencies and stakeholders to prepare for implementation.
28 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
29 enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Fiscal
30 Year 2026 Budget Support Emergency Act of 2025 be adopted after a single reading.
31 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
