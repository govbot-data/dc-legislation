ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-258
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
December 2, 2025
To reappoint Councilmember Brooke Pinto as the Council representative to the Child Support
Guideline Commission.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Child Support Guideline Commission Councilmember Brooke
Pinto Reappointment Resolution of 2025”.
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia reappoints:
Councilmember Brooke Pinto
Q Street, NW
Washington, DC 20009
(Ward 2)
as the Council of the District of Columbia designee to the Child Support Guideline Commission,
established by D.C. Official Code § 16-916.02, to serve a 4-year term to end October 23, 2029.
Sec. 3. The Council shall transmit a copy of this resolution, upon its adoption, to the
appointee, to the chairperson of the Child Support Guideline Commission, and to the Mayor.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
