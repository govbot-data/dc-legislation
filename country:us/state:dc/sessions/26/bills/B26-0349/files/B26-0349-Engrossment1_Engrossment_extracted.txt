10 To amend, on a temporary basis, An Act To create a Department of Corrections in the District of
11 Columbia and the Corrections Oversight Improvement Omnibus Amendment Act of 2022
12 to designate the Department of Corrections as the District agency to provide a free
13 appropriate public education under the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act and
14 District law to certain individuals in its custody and detained in its secure facilities.
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this act may
17 be cited as the “Special Education for Young Adults in the Custody of the Department of
18 Corrections Second Temporary Amendment Act of 2025”.
19 Sec. 2. Section 2(b) of An Act To create a Department of Corrections in the District of
20 Columbia, approved June 27, 1946 (60 Stat. 320; D.C. Official Code § 24-211.02(b)), is
21 amended as follows:
22 (a) Paragraph (9) is amended by striking the phrase “; and” and inserting a semicolon in
23 its place.
24 (b) The first paragraph (10) is amended by striking the period and inserting a semicolon
25 in its place.
26 (c) The second paragraph is redesignated as paragraph (11).

27 (d) The newly redesignated paragraph (11) is amended by striking the period and
28 inserting the phrase “; and” in its place.
29 (e) A new paragraph (12) is added to read as follows:
30 “(12) During school years 2024-2025 and 2025-2026, provide a free appropriate
31 public education (“FAPE”) under the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act, approved
32 April 13, 1970 (84 Stat. 175; 20 U.S.C. § 1400 et seq.) (“IDEA”), and District law, to individuals

34 through the end of the individual’s eligibility for FAPE under the IDEA and District law.”.
