ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-374
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
March 31, 2026
To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to amend the Robert F.
Kennedy Memorial Stadium and the District of Columbia National Guard Armory Public
Safety Act to include the Downtown Arena and the Southeast Arena in the prohibition on
unauthorized entry onto a playing surface.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Arena Safety Conformity Emergency Declaration Resolution of
2026”.
Sec. 2. Emergency legislation is necessary to prevent unauthorized individuals from
entering a playing surface at both the Downtown Arena, commonly known as the Capital One
Arena, and the Southeast Arena, commonly known as the CareFirst Arena. On Saturday,
February 28, 2026, a fan ran onto the court of the Downtown Arena during a Wizards game and
disrupted play. Under District law, it is illegal to run onto the playing field at RFK Stadium,
Nationals Park, and Audi Field; however, there is no such law that applies to the Downtown
Arena or Southeast Arena. This legislation is necessary to make the law consistent across all
professional sports venues in the District.
Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Arena
Safety Conformity Emergency Amendment Act of 2026 be adopted after a single reading.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
