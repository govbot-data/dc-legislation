Dr. Abidemi Oyedele
Dr. Oyedele became Chief Executive Officer of Education Forward DC
in September 2022, after more than five years overseeing all of the
organization's grantmaking and grantee support work as Managing
Partner. As a first-generation immigrant and English language learner, Dr.
Oyedele fundamentally believes in the transformative power of a high-
quality public education.
This belief guided his decision to join Education Forward DC as it seeks
to support the development of a public school system responsive to the
needs of students and families. Prior to joining Education Forward DC in
2017, Dr. Oyedele was a leadership effectiveness director at the Lynch
Leadership Academy at Boston College. There he was responsible for the
coaching and professional development of district, charter, and Catholic school leaders in Boston
and the Boston metro area. Earlier in his career, Dr. Oyedele was the Executive Director of Match
Charter Public High School and Principal of Nativity Preparatory School in Boston.
Dr. Oyedele started his career as a history and English teacher as well as a substitute teacher in
Boston Public Schools. He currently lives in the Brightwood/Manor Park neighborhood with his
wife, Natasha and two children, Nolan and Gem, who both attend DC public schools.
A Ward 4 resident, Dr. Oyedele earned his Bachelor of Arts in Political Science, his Master of
Education in Curriculum and Instruction, and his Doctor of Education in Education Administration
from Boston College
