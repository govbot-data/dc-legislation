Anne Ford
Ms. Anne Ford is a native Washingtonian and licensed practical nurse
with 42 years of experience. She currently holds 3 positions as a
Labcorp flu vaccinator, Medstar Health visiting nurse, and Hospital for
Sick Children visiting nurse.
She was previously appointed by Mayor Muriel Bowser to the Black
Homeownership Strike Force Team and the Quality and Compliance
Advisory Council in 2022.
Inher spare time, Ms. Ford currently volunteers with Rental Assistance
Demonstration Done Rightworking groupwith the DistrictofColumbia
Housing Authority housing residents. Ms. Ford also serves as chair at
Holy Comforter Saint Cyprians Roman Catholic Church and serves as co-chair on the Executive
BoardofNeighborhood Legal Service Program (NLSP). Shehas volunteered with the NLSPteam
forover 20 years.
One of her top accomplishments with the Washington Interfaith Network has been sitting at the
roundtable with Laura Zeilinger, Brenda Donald, Polly Donaldson, and Mayor Bowser regarding
the closingof the DC General Shelter. She isa motherof3 beautiful daughters, onegranddaughter
and two great grandchildren.

Murray Washington Career Center.
