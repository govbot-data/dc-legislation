18 To amend, on an emergency basis due to congressional review, the Day Care Policy Act of 1979
19 to update the minimum salaries child development facilities must pay assistant and lead
20 teachers beginning in January 2025 to participate in the Early Childhood Educator Pay
21 Equity Program.
22
23 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
24 act may be cited as the “Early Childhood Educator Pay Scales Congressional Review Emergency
25 Amendment Act of 2025”.
26 Sec. 2. The Day Care Policy Act of 1979, effective September 19, 1979 (D.C. Law 3-16;
27 D.C. Official Code § 4-401 et seq.), is amended as follows:
28 (a) Section 2 (D.C. Official Code § 4-401) is amended as follows:
29 (1) Paragraph (1)(B) is amended by striking the phrase “an expanded child
30 development home” and inserting the phrase “a child development home or an expanded child
31 development home” in its place.
32 (2) Paragraph (1D) is amended to read as follows:
33 “(1D) The term “CDA” means a child development associate credential
34 recognized by the Council for Professional Recognition and accepted by the Department to
35 demonstrate competency as a caregiver for young children or a state-awarded certificate that

36 meets or exceeds the requirements for a child development associate credential, as defined by the
37 Department.”.
38 (3) Paragraph (4B) is amended by striking the phrase “education.” and inserting
39 the phrase “education or a related field, as identified by the Department’s regulations governing
40 the qualifications of child development facility lead teachers and assistant teachers.”.
