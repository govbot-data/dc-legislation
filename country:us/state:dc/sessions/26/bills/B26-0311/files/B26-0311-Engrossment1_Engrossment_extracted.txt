9 To order the closing of a portion of a public alley in Square 3524 in Ward 5.

11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
12 act may be cited as the “Closing of a Public Alley in Public Alley in Square 3524, S.O. 23-
13 06402, Act of 2025”.
14 Sec. 2. (a) Pursuant to section 404 of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
15 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 787; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.04), and consistent with the Street
16 and Alley Closing and Acquisition Procedures Act of 1982, effective March 10, 1983 (D.C. Law
17 4-201; D.C. Official Code § 9-201.01 et seq.), the Council finds that the portion of the public
18 alley in Square 3524 shown on the Surveyor's plat filed under S.O. 23-06402 is unnecessary for
19 alley purposes and orders it closed, with title to the land to vest as shown on the Surveyor's plat.
20 (b) The ordering of the alley closure in subsection (a) of this section is contingent upon
21 the satisfaction conditions set forth by the District Department of Transportation in the official
22 file for 23-06402.
