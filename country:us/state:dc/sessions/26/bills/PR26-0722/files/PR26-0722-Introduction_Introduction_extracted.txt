Clarence Johnson
Mr. Clarence Johnson is a Cashier Assistant at Costco and a current
member of the Commission on Re-Entry and Returning Citizen Affairs.
Mr. Johnson is a strong community advocate and returning citizen who
has leveraged additional education and workforce development services
to successfully reintegrate into and improve the quality of life of his
community. Mr. Johnson serves as a voice and role model for
residents “East of the River.” In addition, Mr. Johnson has extensive
work experience in quality assurance, computer applications, and
industrial maintenance. He has also earned certificates in
entrepreneurship, financial systems, real estate, and computer applications along with
certifications in chlorofluorocarbon and commercial driving.
A Ward 8 resident, Mr. Johnson attended Ferris State University.
