ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-4
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
January 2, 2025
To reappoint Ms. Jennifer Budoff as Budget Director to the Council of the District of Columbia.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Budget Director to the Council of the District of Columbia
Jennifer Budoff Reappointment Resolution of 2025”.
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia reappoints:
Ms. Jennifer Budoff
49th Street, N.W.
Washington, D.C. 20016
(Ward 3)
as the Budget Director to the Council of the District of Columbia, beginning January 2, 2025.
Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
