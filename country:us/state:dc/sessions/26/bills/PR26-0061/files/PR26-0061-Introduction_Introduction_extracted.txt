Marcus Turner
Mr. Marcus Turner, a native Washingtonian, is a Certified Federal
Contract Manager (CFCM) with more than 12 years of experience in
contract and subcontract management. He has successfully facilitated
projects across multiple functional areas that have resulted in increased
revenue and has provided solutions to mitigate potential contract and
financial risks.
Mr. Turner grew up in North Michigan Park, attended Wilson High
School in Tenleytown, and later attended Howard University.
As a former student of DCPS and a parent to children in both private
and public charter schools, Mr. Turner understands the challenges
students and parents face every day. He is invested in the District’s school system and how its
athletic system can safely benefit our children through education and fair play.
He previously served as an ANC Commissioner for ANC 7B06 and believes his experience has
provided him with an opportunity to learn what DC residents want and need. It also helped improve
his listening skills to address concerns, provide optimal solutions, consider opposing views, and
