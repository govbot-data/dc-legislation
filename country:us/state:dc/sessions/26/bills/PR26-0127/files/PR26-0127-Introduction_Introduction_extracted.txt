emergency bill, the “Modification Nos. M0008, M0009, M0010, M0011, M0012, and M0013 to

20 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this

24 Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to approve Modification Nos. M0008,
25 M0009, M00l0, M00l 1, M0012, and M0013 to Contract No. CW101233 with My Sister's
26 Place, Inc., and to authorize payment for the goods and services received and to be received
27 under the contract and modifications.

29 Procurement ("OCP"), on behalf of the Department of Human Services, partially exercised
30 option of option year two of Contract No. CW101233 with My Sister's Place, Inc., in the not-to
31 exceed ("NTE") amount of $439,902.63 for the period October 1, 2024, through
32 December 31, 2024.

34 (d) On December 27, 2024, via Modification No. M0010, OCP reduced the NTE amount
35 of option year two by $16,200, from $439,902.63 to $423,702.63.
36 (e) On December 31, 2024, via Modification No. M0011, OCP partially exercised option
37 year two through March 31, 2025, and increased the NTE amount of option year two by
38 $423,702.83 from $423,702.83 to $847,405.26.
39 (f) On January 21, 2025, via Modification No. M0012, OCP partially exercised option
40 period two through April 30, 2025.
41 (g) Modification No. M0013 will exercise the remainder of option year two through
42 September 30, 2025, and increase the NTE amount for option year two by $847,405.83.26 from
43 $847,405.26 to $1,694,810.52.
44 (h) Under section 451(b)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
45 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 803; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.51(b)(1)), Council approval is
46 necessary since the modifications will increase the value of the contract to more than $1 million
47 during a 12-month period.
48 (i) Approval is necessary to allow the continuation of these vital services. Without this
49 approval, My Sister’s Place, Inc. cannot be paid for goods and services provided in excess of $1
50 million for the period from October 1, 2024, through September 30, 2025.
51 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2 constitute
52 emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Modification Nos. M0008, M0009,
53 M0010, M0011, M0012, and M0013 to Contract No. CW101233 with My Sister’s Place, Inc.
54 Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2025 be adopted after a single reading.
55 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
