9 To amend the District of Columbia Real Estate Deed Recordation Tax Act to authorize
10 transferors or transferees of real property to petition for review of the fair market value
11 used in calculating taxes imposed on the transfer of the property and to amend Title 47 of
12 the District of Columbia Official Code to allow transferors or transferees of real property
13 to appeal final determinations of a real property's fair market value to the Real Property
14 Tax Appeals Commission for the District of Columbia or the Superior Court as
15 appropriate under the law.
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
18 Act may be cited as the “Transfer and Recordation Tax Appeals Amendment Act of 2026”.
19 Sec. 2. Section 304 of the District of Columbia Real Estate Deed Recordation Tax Act,
20 approved March 2, 1962 (76 Stat. 12; D.C. Official Code § 42-1104), is amended
21 by adding a new subsection (d) to read as follows:
22 “(d)(1) The transferor or transferee of the real property or economic interest in real
23 property may, pursuant to D.C. Official Code § 47-825.0la(d), file a petition for review of the
24 determination of fair market value made under subsection (c) of this section or under section
25 303(a). The notice of final determination issued by the Office of Tax and Revenue on the petition
26 for review may be appealed to the Real Property Tax Appeals Commission for the District of
27 Columbia as provided in D.C. Official Code § 47-825 .0la(e).
