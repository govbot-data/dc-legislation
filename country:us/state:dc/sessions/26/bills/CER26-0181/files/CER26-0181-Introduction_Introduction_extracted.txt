18 A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
19
20 _________________________
21
22 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
23
24 _________________________
25
26 To recognize and honor Theresa DuBois, a Ward 4 District resident, for her over forty years of
27 dedicated service to the District of Columbia.
28
29 WHEREAS, Ms. DuBois has devoted more than four decades of her professional life to
30 service in the District of Columbia, demonstrating an enduring commitment to engage, excite,
31 and entertain D.C. residents while creating economic and community benefits across the city;
32 WHEREAS, Theresa DuBois served the District government with distinction, including
33 as Director of Community Engagement for Events D.C. where she has made everyone who steps
34 into the Convention Center feel welcome and acted as an incredible ambassador for DC to
35 visitors from near and far;
1

36 WHEREAS, Theresa DuBois has had many admirable achievements during her time with
37 Events D.C. including the success of Washington DC's Auto Show, which brought in over
38 100,000 attendees annually, making it one of the top auto shows in the country.
39 WHEREAS, Theresa DuBois’s efforts to support the Community Grant’s Program
40 created opportunities for non-profits in the District to give opportunities and life-long learned
41 skills in artist and athletic endeavors; and
42 WHEREAS, Theresa DuBois has earned the respect of District leaders, civic
43 organizations, and community members for her accomplishments but also for her kindness and
44 care. Colleagues and residents value her for her commitment to the community and building
45 lasting connections with the neighborhood.
46 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
47 resolution may be cited as the "Theresa DuBois Service Recognition Resolution of 2026".
48 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia recognizes and celebrates Theresa
49 DuBois for her outstanding service to the District of Columbia and commends Ms. DuBois for
50 her decades of exemplary public service, civic leadership, and lasting contributions to the
51 District’s government, institutions, and communities.
52 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately upon the first date of publication in
53 the District of Columbia Register.
