10 To amend the District of Columbia Health Occupations Revision Act of 1985 and the Consent
11 for Vaccination of Minors Amendment Act of 2022 to authorize healthcare providers in
12 the District to order and administer immunizations recommended by competent medical
13 and public health organizations designated by the Director of the Department of Health.
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
16 act may be cited as the “Community Health Amendment Act of 2025”.
17 Sec. 2. The District of Columbia Health Occupations Revision Act of 1985, effective
18 March 25, 1986 (D.C. Law 6-99; D.C. Official Code § 3-1201.01 et seq.), is amended as follows:
19 (a) Section 102(11)(A) (D.C. Official Code § 3-1201.02(11)(A)) is amended by striking
20 the phrase “in accordance with the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention’s published
21 guidelines and recommended immunization schedules” and inserting the phrase “in accordance
22 with the published guidelines and recommended immunization schedules of the Advisory
23 Committee on Immunization Practices of the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention or a
24 competent medical or public health organization designated by the Director of the Department of
25 Health through a public notice” in its place.
26 (b) Section 755(a-1) (D.C. Official Code § 3-1207.55(a-1)) is amended by striking the
27 phrase “in accordance with the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention’s published
