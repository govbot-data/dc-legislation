Barbara Jones
Ms. Barbara Jones is a current member of the Board of Architecture,
Interior Design, and Landscape Architecture and the President and CEO
of Reservations7.
Previously, Ms. Jones served the Department of Human Services for more
than 30 years. Ms. Jones’ management experience spans both the public
and private sectors, where she has proactively recommended and
executed changes in policy, procedures, and methods of operations to
achieve organizational efficiency. Ms. Jones equipped her team with the
tools needed to deliver services to their communities.
Ms. Jones previously served as Ward 8 Commissioner for the D.C.
Commission on the Arts and Humanities and has led several community activism efforts. Ms.
Jones was the Campaign Program and Development Director for Walter Whitman Clinic and Co-
Chair for Walk in the Name of Love Foundation, amongst many others.
A Ward 8 resident, Ms. Jones earned her Bachelor of Science in Public Health Administration and
Biology and her Master of Science in Counseling from the University of the District of Columbia.
