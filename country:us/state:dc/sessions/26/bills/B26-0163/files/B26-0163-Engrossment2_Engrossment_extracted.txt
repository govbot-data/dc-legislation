13 To amend, on a temporary basis, the Legalization of Marijuana for Medical Treatment Initiative
14 of 1999 to amend the definition of an unlicensed establishment to include establishments
15 that sell Schedule I substances or products that contain Schedule I substances; to
16 authorize enforcement actions to be taken against all unlicensed establishments beginning
17 on April 1, 2025, regardless of whether the unlicensed establishments have an application
18 pending application before the Alcoholic Beverage and Control Board (“ABC Board”); to
19 authorize certain enforcement actions before April 1, 2025, against an unlicensed
20 establishment that has an application pending before the ABC Board if the unlicensed
21 establishment sells a Schedule I substance or a product that contains a Schedule I
22 substance; to authorize summary closures and related enforcement actions to continue for
23 as long as the imminent danger to the public persists and is likely to recur; and to
24 authorize the ABC Board to issue a notice of summary closure to and order the
25 padlocking of, and to allow the Alcoholic Beverage and Cannabis Administration and
26 Metropolitan Police Department to take related enforcement actions against a licensed
27 medical cannabis establishment that engages in certain unlawful activity.
28
29 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
30 act may be cited as the “Unlicensed Establishment Enforcement Clarification Temporary
31 Amendment Act of 2025”.
