11 To amend section 47-850(a-2) of the District of Columbia Official Code to provide that a
12 surviving spouse or domestic partner of a veteran may claim the disabled veterans
13 homestead deduction.
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
16 act may be cited as the “Veteran Spouse Homestead Deduction Amendment Act of 2025”.
17 Sec. 2. Section 47-850(a-2) of the District of Columbia Official Code is amended as
18 follows:
19 (a) Paragraph (1) is amended as follows:
20 (1) The lead-in language is amended by striking the word “veteran” and inserting
21 the phrase “veteran or eligible spouse” in its place.
22 (2) Subparagraph (A) is amended to read as follows:
23 “(A) Veteran has been classified (or, in the case of an eligible spouse, had been
24 classified at the time of the veteran’s death) by the United States Department of Veterans Affairs
25 as having a total and permanent disability as a result of a service-incurred condition or service-
26 aggravated condition or is paid (or, in the case of an eligible spouse, had been paid at the time of
27 the veteran’s death) at the 100% disability rating level as a result of unemployability; and”.
1

28 (3) Subparagraph (B) is amended by striking the word “Veteran’s” and inserting
29 the phrase “Veteran’s or eligible spouse’s” in its place.
30 (b) Paragraph (3) is amended as follows:
31 (1) Subparagraph (A) is amended as follows:
32 (A) Strike the phrase “the veteran” both times it appears and insert the
33 phrase “the veteran or eligible spouse” in its place.
34 (B) Strike the phrase “the veteran’s legal guardian” and insert the phrase
35 “the veteran’s or eligible spouse’s legal guardian” in its place.
36 (2) Subparagraph (B) is amended by striking the phrase “this subsection.” and
37 inserting the phrase “this subsection. Upon the filing of an application by an eligible spouse
38 pursuant to subparagraph (A) of this paragraph, the District of Columbia Office of Veterans
39 Affairs shall certify to the Office of Tax and Revenue that the deceased veteran met the disability
40 requirements for the deduction provided pursuant to this subsection at the time of the veteran’s
41 death.” in its place.
42 (c) A new paragraph (4) is added to read as follows:
43 “(4) For the purposes of this subsection, the term “eligible spouse” means
44 the surviving spouse or domestic partner of a deceased veteran whose real property was
45 validly receiving the deduction provided under this subsection at the time of the veteran’s
46 death or whose real property would have been eligible for such deduction at the time of the
47 veteran’s death had the veteran applied for such deduction.”.
