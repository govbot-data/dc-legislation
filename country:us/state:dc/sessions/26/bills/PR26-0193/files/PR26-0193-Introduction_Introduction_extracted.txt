Dr. Bernardine Evans
Dr. Bernardine Evans, PT, DPT, GCS is a radiation therapy clinical
assistant professorat Howard University.
Dr. Evans is involved in instructing graduate Physical Therapy students,
Occupational Therapy students and undergraduate Radiation Therapy
students in Pathology. Other courses she teaches include Intro to Neuro,
‘Advanced Neuro and Principles of Therapeutic Exercise. She was
previously the Director of Restorative and Rehabilitative Services at
‘Asbury Methodist Village in Gaithersburg, Maryland where she was
responsible fortheoperationof a 2,000-residentcontinuing care retirement
community with a 250-bed rehabilitative service. She currently serves on
the editorial review board fortheJournal ofOrthopedic Research and Physiotherapy. Dr. Evans is
a member of the American Physical Therapy Association as well as the Geriatric Society of
Washington DC.
‘A Ward4 resident, Dr. EvansearnedherAssociateofArts inGeneral Studies from PrinceGeorge's
Community College, her Bachelor of Science in Physical Therapy from the University of
Maryland Eastern Shore, and her Post-Professional DoctorofPhysicalTherapyfrom Utica College
of Syracuse University.
