17 Acquisition Procedures Act of 1982, effective March 10, 1983 (D.C. Law 4-201; D.C. Official
18 Code §§ 9-204.01, 9-204.03, and 9-204.21), the Council officially designates the public alley
19 system in Square 2857, bounded by Harvard Street, N.W., Sherman Avenue, N.W., Girard
20 Street, N.W., and 11th Street, N.W., as depicted by the plat in the committee report, as “Henry E.
21 Baker Alley”.

23 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement in the committee report as the fiscal
