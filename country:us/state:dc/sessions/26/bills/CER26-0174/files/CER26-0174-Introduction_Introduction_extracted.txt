8 A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
9
10 _________________________
11
12 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
13
14 _________________________
15
16
17 To recognize honoree Grimaldi-Francesca Sanchez during the 2026 celebration of DC Black
18 Pride.
19
20 WHEREAS, Grimaldi-Francesca Sanchez is a community health professional, prevention
21 advocate, and entrepreneur with a strong focus on HIV prevention, health equity, and community
22 empowerment;
23
24 WHEREAS, with extensive experience supporting HIV testing, PrEP navigation, and
25 community-based outreach, Grimaldi-Francesca Sanchez has played a key role in developing and
26 implementing prevention programming that centers dignity, access, and culturally responsive
27 care;
28
29 WHEREAS, Grimaldi-Francesca Sanchez’s work is grounded in a commitment to
30 uplifting marginalized communities, particularly LGBTQIA+ and TGNC populations, through
31 education, representation, and intentional community building;
32
33 WHEREAS, Grimaldi-Francesca Sanchez continues to lead with integrity, compassion,
34 and a belief that access to information and services saves lives;
35
36 WHEREAS, DC Black Pride is an annual event to increase awareness of and showcase
37 pride in the diversity of the LGBTQIA+ populations in the African American community, and to
38 support organizations that focus on health disparities, education, youth, and families;
39
40 WHEREAS, as the very first Black Pride festival in the world, DC Black Pride fostered
41 the beginning of the Center for Black Equity and the “Black Pride Movement”;
42
1

43 WHEREAS, since the birth of DC Black Pride, more than 50 other Black Pride
44 celebrations now take place throughout the world and on 4 continents, with many using DC
45 Black Pride as a model;
46
47 WHEREAS, May 22, 2026, through May 25, 2026, marks the 35th annual DC Black
48 Pride celebration;
49
50 WHEREAS, the theme for this year’s celebration is “New Black Renaissance,” which
51 centers Black LGBTQIA+ “artists, leaders, healers, and organizers while confronting what still
52 makes Black Pride necessary, including HIV, inequity, homophobia, and transphobia;”
53
54 WHEREAS, for more than 30 years, DC Black Pride has been an opportunity for the
55 Black LGBTQIA+ community to gather and celebrate being both African American and
56 members of LGBTQIA+ communities;
57
58 WHEREAS, DC Black Pride is the oldest and one of the largest Black Pride events in the
59 world, drawing thousands of residents and visitors from around the globe; and
60
61 WHEREAS, DC Black Pride brings more than 500,000 people to the nation’s capital
62 from across the United States, Canada, the Caribbean, South Africa, Great Britain, France,
63 Germany, and the Netherlands and will carry that tradition this year and into the future to
64 celebrate their shared community, raise awareness and funding for HIV/AIDS prevention, and
65 stand against homophobia inspired by violence and bigotry.
66
67 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That
68 this resolution be cited as the “Grimaldi-Francesca Sanchez Recognition Resolution of 2026”.
69
70 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia recognizes and celebrates Grimaldi-
71 Francesca Sanchez during the 2026 celebration of DC Black Pride.
72
73 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
