Carol Calomiris
Ms. Carol Calomiris is a Senior Managing Attorney for the RGS Title,
LLC (RGS) Jenifer Street office in Washington, DC. She manages RGS
Title with over 30 years of real estate and legal expertise.
Ms. Calomiris began her career in real estate law in 1993 when she opened
her law firm, Calomiris & Calomiris PA, which provides a wide range of
legal services to the real estate community. She is a member of
Montgomery County, Maryland State, District of Columbia, and Supreme
Court Bar Associations. She is also a member of the Maryland, District
of Columbia, and Virginia Land Title Associations. In addition to
conducting residential and commercial settlements in MD, DC, and
VA for ROS, she is a member on the Public Policy Committee of the
District of Columbia Association of REALTORS (DCAR), in which she helps to draft legislation
and lobby for the realtor community. She is also a member of the DC Public Policy Committee for
the Greater Capital Area Association of REAL TORS (GCAAR), where she assists in reviewing
state legislation and lobby for the realtor community. In addition to her professional work, Carol
enjoys teaching continuing education classes for real estate professionals in MD, DC, and VA.
A Ward 3 resident, Ms. Calomiris earned her Bachelor of Arts in Political Science and History
from Duke University and her Juris Doctor from Georgetown University.
