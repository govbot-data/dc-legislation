Shannarese Sims
Shannarese Sims is a Licensed Professional Counselor based in
Washington, DC. Ms. Sims most recently created and developed a
therapeutic services program at a crime victims’ rights organization,
Volare, for which she serves as Director.
Ms. Sims currently serves on the District’s Domestic Violence Fatality
Review Board; is certified as a Forensic Teaching Associate, which she
utilizes to she instruct novice nurses on trauma-informed practices for
completing a medical-forensic exam; and is a Cohort 10 member of the
Office on Violence Against Women’s LEAP fellowship (via ValorUS).
Ms. Sims has worked at various victim service organizations in the District and has accumulated
over a decade of experience working with survivors of power-based violence. This experience
includes survivor-centered program development, crisis intervention and response, psychosocial
case-management services, sexual assault support services, and group facilitation.
A Ward 6 resident, Ms. Sims earned her Bachelor of Science in Human Ecology/Community
Health at The Ohio State University and her Master of Arts in Counseling Psychology from
Bowie State University.
