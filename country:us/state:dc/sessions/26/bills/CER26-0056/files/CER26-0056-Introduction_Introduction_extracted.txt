17 To recognize honoree Sean Norman during the 2025 celebration of DC Black Pride.
18
19 WHEREAS, Sean Norman is a Deaf queer advocate, educator, and community organizer
20 based in Washington, D.C.;
21
22 WHEREAS, as a graduate of Gallaudet University, Sean Norman is the founder and
23 captain of the first ASL Kickball team under Stonewall Sports and host of the popular ASL
24 Queer as F*bulous Social Night;
25
26 WHEREAS, Sean Norman’s advocacy spans roles with the National Association of the
27 Deaf and the D.C. Mayor’s Office of the Deaf, DeafBlind, and Hard of Hearing;
28
29 WHEREAS, Sean Norman currently serves as President of the D.C. Association of the
30 Deaf and chairs a WorldPride 2025 subcommittee;
31
32 WHEREAS, through visibility, organizing, and joy, Sean Norman builds bridges within
33 and beyond the Deaf LGBTQ+ community;
34
35 WHEREAS, DC Black Pride is an annual event to increase awareness of and showcase
36 pride in the diversity of the LGBTQIA+ populations in the African American community, and to
37 support organizations that focus on health disparities, education, youth, and families;
38
39 WHEREAS, as the very first Black Pride festival in the world, DC Black Pride fostered
40 the beginning of the Center for Black Equity (formerly known as the International Federation of
41 Black Prides, Inc. (IFBP)) and the “Black Pride Movement”;
42
1

43 WHEREAS, since the birth of DC Black Pride, more than 50 other Black Pride
44 celebrations now take place throughout the world and on 4 continents, with many using DC
45 Black Pride as a model;
46
47 WHEREAS, May 23, 2025, through May 26, 2025, marks the 34th annual DC Black
48 Pride celebration;
49
50 WHEREAS, the theme for this year’s celebration is “BLACK PRIDE IS FREEDOM”;
51
52 WHEREAS, for more than 30 years, DC Black Pride has been an opportunity for the
53 Black LGBTQIA+ community to gather and celebrate being both African American and
54 members of LGBTQIA+ communities;
55
56 WHEREAS, DC Black Pride is the oldest and one of the largest Black Pride events in the
57 world, drawing thousands of residents and visitors from around the globe; and
58
59 WHEREAS, DC Black Pride brings more than 500,000 people to the nation’s capital
60 from across the United States, Canada, the Caribbean, South Africa, Great Britain, France,
61 Germany, and the Netherlands and will carry that tradition this year and into the future to
62 celebrate their shared community, raise awareness and funding for HIV/AIDS prevention, and
63 stand against homophobia inspired by violence and bigotry.
64
65 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That
66 this resolution be cited as the “Sean Norman Recognition Resolution of 2025”.
67
68 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia recognizes and celebrates Sean Norman
69 during the 2025 celebration of DC Black Pride.
70
71 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
