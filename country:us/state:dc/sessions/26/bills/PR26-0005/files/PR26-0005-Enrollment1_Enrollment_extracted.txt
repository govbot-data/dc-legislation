ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-5
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
January 2, 2025
To reappoint Ms. Nyasha Howard as the Secretary to the Council of the District of Columbia.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Secretary to the Council of the District of Columbia Nyasha
Howard Reappointment Resolution of 2025”.
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia reappoints:
Ms. Nyasha Howard
Fort Davis Street, SE
Washington, DC 20020
(Ward 7)
as the Secretary to the Council of the District of Columbia, beginning January 2, 2025.
Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
