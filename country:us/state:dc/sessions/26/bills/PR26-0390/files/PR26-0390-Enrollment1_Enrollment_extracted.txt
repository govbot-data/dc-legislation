ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-285
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
December 16, 2025
To confirm the appointment of Angela Hardiman to the Board of Social Work.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Board of Social Work Angela Hardiman Confirmation
Resolution of 2025”.
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia confirms the appointment of:
Angela Hardiman
55th Street, NE
Washington, DC 20019
(Ward 7)
as a licensed graduate social worker member of the Board of Social Work, established by section
212 of the District of Columbia Health Occupations Revision Act of 1985, effective March 25,
1986 (D.C. Law 6-99; D.C. Official Code § 3-1202.12), for a term to end March 3, 2028.
Sec. 3. The Council shall transmit a copy of this resolution, upon its adoption, to the
nominee and to the Mayor.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
