Charles Bond
Mr. Charles Bond is a recreational therapist, educator, advocate and
leaderwho hasspentmorethan forty yearsadvocating fortherecreational
therapy profession and advocating forseniors in long-term care facilities.
While attending Howard University, Charles Bond was afforded the
opportunity to serve as a lecturer, teaching associate and teaching
assistant in which he taught the undergraduate classes in therapeutic
recreation, recreation and leadership.
Charles Bond has been an active member of the American Therapeutic
Recreation Association (ATRA) since 1998, As an active member of
ATRA Charles Bond has served in various positions including serving on
the ATRA-Board ofDirectors 2006-2008. In 2009, the District ofColumbia City Council passed
legislation that the Board of Occupational Therapy would oversee the practice of Recreational
Therapy in the District ofColumbia. The legislation also requires a recreational therapist to serve
on the Board of Occupational Therapy. In 2010 Charles Bond was appointed to serve on the
District ofColumbia Board of Occupational Therapy. For many years Mr. Bond advocated that
recreational therapy should be a licensed profession.
‘A Ward | resident, Mr. Bond earned his Bachelor of Arts in Psychology from North Carolina A
& T State University and his Master of Science in Health, Human Performance, and Leisure
Studies from Howard University.
