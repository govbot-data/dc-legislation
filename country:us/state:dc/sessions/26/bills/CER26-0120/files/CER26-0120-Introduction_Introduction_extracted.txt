17 A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
18
19 _________________________
20
21 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
22 _________________________
23
24
25 To recognize and honor Linda Greenan for her over thirty years of dedicated service to the
26 District of Columbia.
27
28 WHEREAS, Ms. Greenan has devoted more than three decades of her professional life to
29 service in the District of Columbia, demonstrating an enduring commitment to strengthening the
30 city’s civic institutions, communities, and public-private partnerships;
31 WHEREAS, Linda Greenan served the District government with distinction, including as
32 Chief of Staff to Councilmember Jack Evans for four years, where she played a critical role in
33 legislative operations and constituent services;
34 WHEREAS, Linda Greenan served for nearly two decades as the Associate Vice
35 President for External Relations at Georgetown University, where she acted as a principal liaison
36 between the University, the District government, community organizations, and neighborhood
1

37 stakeholders, and was widely respected for her deep knowledge of District policy, governance,
38 and community concerns;
39 WHEREAS, beginning in August 2016, Linda Greenan served the District for nine years
40 at PEPCO, where she continued her commitment to service by strengthening relationships
41 between the utility, District government, and the local community;
42 WHEREAS, Linda Greenan has earned the respect of District leaders, civic
43 organizations, and community members for her professionalism, steady judgment, and consistent
44 focus on solutions that advance the public interest; and
45 WHEREAS, throughout her career, Linda Greenan has served on numerous boards and
46 commissions, including the D.C. Chamber of Commerce, the Humanities Council, the
47 Georgetown Business Association, the D.C. Sports and Entertainment Commission, and the
48 Washington Convention and Sports Authority, and has been repeatedly recognized for her
49 leadership, integrity, and unwavering dedication to the betterment of the District of Columbia.
50 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
51 resolution may be cited as the "Linda Greenan Service Recognition Resolution of 2026".
52 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia recognizes and celebrates Linda Greenan
53 for her outstanding service to the District of Columbia and commends Ms. Greenan for her
54 decades of exemplary public service, civic leadership, and lasting contributions to the District’s
55 government, institutions, and communities.
56 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately upon the first date of publication in
57 the District of Columbia Register.
