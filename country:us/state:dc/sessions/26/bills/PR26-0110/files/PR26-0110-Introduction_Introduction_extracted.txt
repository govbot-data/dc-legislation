Dr. Keita Vanterpool
Dr. Keita Vanterpool, a licensed chiropractor and esteemed health and
wellness expert, is dedicated to educating the public on a wide range of
health care topics, including nutrition, fitness, stress management, and
chronic disease control.
As the founder of CHIROKEI Consulting, LLC, Dr. Vanterpool and her
team provide comprehensive health and wellness programs and
demonstrations to both corporate and non-corporate entities.
Additionally, she established TRILOGY Fitness Group, a 501(c) 3
organization that aims to improve access to healthcare in underserved
communities.
Beyond her successful practice, Dr. Vanterpool serves as a regulator and policy maker, holding
significant roles such as Chair of the Washington, DC Board Chiropractic and being the first
African American woman and first elected official on the Federation of Chiropractic Licensing
Board (FCLB). She is the immediate past Vice President of the FCLB.
Her dedication to the chiropractic profession is evident through her active involvement with the
National Board of Chiropractic Examiners (NBCE), where she served as the First African
American Female Director. She participated in testing and standard setting committees and graded
licensees who faced sanctions for the Ethics and Boundaries Assessment Services (EBAS).
A Ward 8 resident, Dr. Vanterpool earned her Bachelor of Science in Medicine from Elizabeth
City University and her Doctor of Chiropractic from Life University.
