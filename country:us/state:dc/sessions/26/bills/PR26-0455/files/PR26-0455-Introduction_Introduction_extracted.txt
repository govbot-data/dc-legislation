Kayla Smith
Ms. Kayla Smith is the Strategic Manager for Fresenius Medical Care
overseeing nutrition services at more than 20 dialysis facilities in DC and
Maryland. She is experienced in project management, technical writing,
statistical analysis, and data verification. She has authored several
publications, including the "Reference Food Manual for Faith-Based
Organizations," and has presented her work at national conferences.
Ms. Smith’s previous roles have included serving as a dietitian with US
Renal Care and the lead dietitian of NMS Healthcare. Throughout her
career, she has emphasized the importance of nutrition and dedication to
improving patient outcomes.
A Ward 7 resident, Ms. Smith earned her Bachelor of Science from Howard University.
