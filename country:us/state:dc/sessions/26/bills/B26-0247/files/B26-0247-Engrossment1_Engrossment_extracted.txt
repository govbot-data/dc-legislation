9 To amend the Business Improvement District Acts of 1996 to permit the Mayor to approve a
10 petition by the Anacostia BID to expand its geographic area to include exempt real
11 property owners, including real property owned by the District of Columbia.
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
14 act may be cited as the “Anacostia Business Improvement Districts Amendment Act of 2025.”.
15 Sec. 2. Section 10 of the Business Improvement Districts Act of 1996, effective May 29,
16 1996 (D.C. Law 11-134; D.C. Official Code § 2-1215.09), is amended by adding a new
17 subsection (d) to read as follows:
18 “(d) Notwithstanding any other provision of this act, the Mayor may approve a petition
19 by the Anacostia BID to expand its geographic area to include real property owned by the
20 District of Columbia adjacent to or abutting the Anacostia BID’s geographical area as delineated
21 in section 209; provided, that the expanded BID’s geographic area shall not become effective
22 until the effective date of an act of the Council approving such BID geographic expansion; and
23 provided, further that initial BID taxes for such expanded area, to the extent it includes taxable
24 property, shall be collected at the next practicable regularly scheduled billing pursuant to section
25 16.”.
