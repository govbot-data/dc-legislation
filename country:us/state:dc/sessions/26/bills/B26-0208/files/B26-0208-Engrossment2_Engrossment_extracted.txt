10 To amend the Open Meetings Act to clarify the definition of meeting; to provide for a public
11 body’s ability to be briefed about potential terrorist or public health threats so long as no
12 official action is taken; to exempt from the act meetings between the Council and the
13 Mayor provided that no official action is taken at such meetings; and to provide that a
14 meeting shall be deemed open to the public if the public body takes steps reasonably
15 calculated to allow the public to view or hear the meeting while the meeting is taking
16 place, or, if doing so is not technologically feasible, as soon thereafter as reasonably
17 practicable.
18
19 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
20 act may be cited as the “Open Meetings Clarification Emergency Amendment Act of 2026”.
21 Sec. 2. The Open Meetings Act, effective March 31, 2011 (D.C. Law 18-350; D.C.
22 Official Code § 2-571 et seq.), is amended as follows:
23 (a) Section 404 (D.C. Official Code § 2-574) is amended as follows:
24 (1) Paragraph (1) is amended to read as follows:
25 “(1)(A) “Meeting” means any gathering of a quorum of the members of a public
26 body, including hearings and roundtables, whether formal or informal, regular, special, or
27 emergency, at which the members consider, conduct, or advise on public business, including
28 gathering information, taking testimony, discussing, deliberating, recommending, and voting,
