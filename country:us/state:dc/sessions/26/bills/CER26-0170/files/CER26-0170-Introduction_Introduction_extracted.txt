Councilmember Trayon White, Sr.
A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
______________
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
_____________________
1
2 To recognize and honor Philip E. Pannell for his decades of civic leadership, political
3 activism, and community service in the District of Columbia.
4
5
6 WHEREAS, Philip E. Pannell is a native of Newport News, Virginia;
7
8 WHEREAS, Mr. Pannell pursued higher education at Fordham University and completed
9 graduate work in political science at Howard University;
10
11 WHEREAS, Mr. Pannell for decades has been involved in political and community
12 activities at both professional and volunteer levels, demonstrating a sustained commitment to
13 civic engagement in the District of Columbia;
14
15 WHEREAS, Mr. Pannell’s activism included organizing and participating in major
16 campaigns, reflecting a lifelong dedication to social justice and democratic participation;
17
18 WHEREAS, Mr. Pannell was selected in 1975 by then-Ward 4 Councilmember
19 Arrington Dixon to work on the Council’s Government Operations Committee and to establish,
20 organize, and run the District’s first constituent services office, helping to build a model for
21 effective constituent engagement and responsive local government;
22
23 WHEREAS, Mr. Pannell has served the District in numerous civic capacities, including
24 leadership roles in community and neighborhood organizations and service on boards supporting
25 education and public institutions;
26
27 WHEREAS, Mr. Pannell has received extensive recognition for his service, including
28 major community service honors and awards acknowledging his contributions to Washington,
29 D.C.;
1

30
31 WHEREAS, Mr. Pannell became Executive Director of the Anacostia Coordinating
32 Council in 1995 and has been the only person to serve in that role since the organization’s
33 inception, dedicating his leadership to strengthening communities east of the Anacostia River;
34
35 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
36 resolution may be cited as the “Philip E. Pannell Day Ceremonial Recognition Resolution of
37 2026”.
