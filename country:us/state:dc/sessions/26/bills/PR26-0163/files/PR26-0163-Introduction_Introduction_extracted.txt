11 To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to amend the Department of
12 For-Hire Vehicles Establishment Act of 1985 to authorize the Department of For-Hire
13 Vehicles to enforce traffic laws against carrier-for-hire vehicles.
14
15 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
16 resolution may be cited as the “Department of For-Hire Vehicles Delivery Vehicle Traffic
17 Enforcement Expansion Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
18 Sec. 2. (a) On July 8, 2024, the Council passed the Department of For-Hire Vehicles
19 Delivery Vehicle Traffic Enforcement Expansion Temporary Act of 2022, effective September
20 4, 2024 (D.C. Law 25-205; 71 DCR 10776), which grants the Department of For-Hire Vehicles
21 the authority to enforce traffic laws and issue citations to delivery vehicles, or “carriers-for-hire”,
22 defined as “a class of transportation service by which a network of private operators, couriers, or
23 carriers provide delivery of parcels, food, or beverages in the District for compensation”.
24 (b) The authority granted to DFHV by D.C. Law 25-025 is a continuation of emergency
25 and temporary measures passed by the Council in 2022 and 2023. The Department has exercised
26 this enforcement authority to promote safety for carrier-for-hire operators and customers, and for
27 other road users. In Fiscal Year 2024, a total of 1,297 warnings or notices of infraction were
28 issued by DFHV to carriers-for-hire.
29 (c) D.C. Law 25-205 is set to expire on April 17, 2025.
1

30 (d) On December 17, 2024, the Council passed the “Carrier-for-Hire Oversight and
31 Enforcement Act of 2024” (D.C. Act 25-693; 72 DCR 742), which includes provisions
32 substantively similar to D.C. Law 25-205. That permanent act is subject to appropriations, which
33 indicates a likely applicability date no earlier than October 1, 2025.
34 (e) Additional emergency and temporary measures are necessary to prevent a gap in the
35 Department of For-Hire Vehicles’ authority to conduct street enforcement of carrier-for-hire
36 operators.
37 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
38 enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the
39 Department of For-Hire Vehicles Delivery Vehicle Traffic Enforcement Expansion Emergency
40 Amendment Act of 2025 be adopted after a single reading.
41 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
