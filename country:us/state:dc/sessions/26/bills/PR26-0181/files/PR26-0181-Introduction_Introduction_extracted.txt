14 To declare the existence of an emergency, with respect to the need to amend the Fiscal Year
15 2026 Budget Submission Requirements Resolution of 2025 to provide for a new date for
16 transmission of the Fiscal Year 2026 budget by the Mayor to the Council.
17
18 RESOLVED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
19 resolution may be cited as the “Fiscal Year 2026 Budget Submission Requirements Emergency
20 Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
21 Sec. 2. (a) The Fiscal Year 2026 Budget Submission Requirements Resolution of 2024,
22 adopted on December 2, 2024 pursuant to the Home Rule Act, set the date as April 2, 2025 for
23 the transmission of the Fiscal Year 2026 Budget to the Council. However, the Mayor did not
24 meet that date.
25 (b) The February 28, 2025 revenue estimate upon which the Mayor’s proposed budget is
26 to be based included significant slowing of revenue growth, requiring reductions in recurring
27 programs as part of the Fiscal Year 2026 budget. In addition, Congress has excluded a standard
28 appropriations provision when adopting the Federal Fiscal Year 2025 continuing appropriations
29 resolution that has forced the District to revise its Fiscal Year 2025 spending levels to levels
30 appropriated in Fiscal Year 2024, a cut of over $1 billion from the current year budget.
31 (c) The May 15, 2025 date of transmission is based on:
32 (1) More certainty from the Mayor as to when they will be “pencils down” on the
33 Fiscal Year 2025 supplemental they have chosen to pursue; and

34 (2) A better understanding from the Office of the Chief Financial Officer how
35 long they will need to process the supplemental budget and how flexible they will be as to when
36 they issue newly revised revenue estimates, which would ordinarily issue after the Council
37 completes its work on the budget, but because of the Mayor’s delay, this year the revenue
38 estimates will issue during Council consideration.
39 (d) Approval on an emergency basis of the change in the date of the budget transmission
40 is necessary to provide the Council and the public certainty as to schedule for the Council to
41 complete action on the revised Fiscal Year 2025 budget and the Fiscal Year 2026 budget, and
42 will allow the Council to move forward with setting a revised schedule of Committee meetings,
43 markup, and adoption of the Fiscal Year 2026 budget.
44 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia finds that the circumstances enumerated
45 in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the “Fiscal Year 2026
46 Budget Submission Requirements Emergency Approval Resolution of 2025” be adopted after a
47 single reading.
48 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
