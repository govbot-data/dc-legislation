16 To amend the Rules of Organization and Procedure for the Council of the District of Columbia,
17 Council Period 26, Resolution of 2025 to change the Council’s 2026 recess dates,
18 eliminate the Committee on Business and Economic Development and transfer
19 jurisdiction of its subject matter and agencies to other committees; to create a
20 subcommittee of the Committee of the Whole; and to amend the Council Period 26
21 Appointment of Chairperson Pro Tempore, Committee Chairpersons, and Committee
22 Membership Resolution of 2025 to appoint a President Pro Tempore, to no longer appoint
23 a chairperson and membership of the Committee on Business and Economic
24 Development, and to make membership changes to other Council committees.
25
26 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
27 resolution may be cited as the “Council Period 26 Rules of Organization and Procedure and
28 Appointment of Committee Chairpersons and Membership Amendment Resolution of 2026”.
