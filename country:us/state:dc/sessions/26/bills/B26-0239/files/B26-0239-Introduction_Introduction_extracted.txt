21 To amend, on an emergency basis, section 215 of the Uniform College Athlete, Name, Image, or
22 Likeness Act of 2022 to permit an institution, conference, or athletic association to assist
23 a college athlete in selecting, arranging for, or providing payment to a name, image, or
24 likeness agent and in selecting, arranging for, or collecting payment from a third party
25 engaged in specific name, image, or likeness agreements with college athletes, and to
26 remove the prohibition prohibiting institutions or conferences from providing
27 compensation to a college athlete for the athlete's name, image, or likeness and to pause
28 applicability of expanded financial disclosure requirements for certain boards and
29 commissions.
30
31 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
32 act may be cited as the "Uniform College Athlete Name, Image, or Likeness Emergency
33 Amendment Act of 2025."
34 Sec. 2. Section 215 of the Uniform College Athlete Name, Image, or Likeness Act of
35 2022 (D.C. Law 24-245; D.C. Official Code § 38-1631.05), is amended as follows:
36 (a) Subsection (a) is amended as follows:
37 (1) Paragraph (2) is amended by striking the phrase “; and’ and inserting a
38 semicolon in its place.
1

39 (2) Paragraph (3) is amended by striking the phrase “; and” and inserting a
40 semicolon in its place.
41 (3) New paragraphs (4) and (5) are added to read as follows:
42 “(4) With selecting, arranging for, or providing payment to a name, image, or
43 likeness agent; and
44 “(5) With selecting, arranging for, or collecting payment from a third party
45 engaged in specific name, image, or likeness agreements with a college athlete or athletes.”.
46 (b) Subsection (e) is repealed.
47 Sec. 3. Chapter 57 of Title 3 of the District of Columbia Municipal Regulations is
48 amended by adding a new subsection 5710.03 to read as follows:
49 “5710.3 This section shall not apply before January 1, 2026.”
50 Sec. 4. Fiscal impact statement.
51 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Budget Director as the fiscal impact
52 statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975, approved
53 October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
54 Sec. 5. Effective date.
55 This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by the
56 Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), and shall remain in effect for no longer than
57 90 days, as provided for emergency acts of the Council of the District of Columbia in section
58 412(a) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 788;
59 D.C. Official Code § 1-204.12(a)).
