Jennifer Wade
Ms. Jennifer Wade brings over two decades of progressive leadership
experience within the United States. federal government, specializing in
strategic oversight, regulatory governance, and program management.
She has led diverse teams, navigated intricate policy landscapes, and
delivered impactful outcomes in high-stakes environments.
Ms. Wade serves as the Director of the Division of Regulation at the U.S.
Department of Energy where she provides strategic leadership and
oversight, managing complex federal statutes for natural gas
import/export. In addition, she directs regulatory processes, ensuring
compliance with federal guidelines and policy objectives.
Prior to joining the Department of Energy, Ms. Wade held various roles at the U.S. Energy
Information Administration for over 10 years, including serving as a statistician and analyst.
A Ward 8 resident, Ms. Wade earned her Bachelor of Science in Chemical Engineering from the
University of Virginia and her Master of Arts in Teaching from Towson University’s College of
Education.
