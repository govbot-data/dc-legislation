11 To require, on a temporary basis, Metropolitan Police Department officers to document
12 identifying information for all law enforcement officers present at the scene of an arrest
13 and any use of force in arrest reports and probable cause affidavits; and to amend the
14 Body-Worn Camera Regulation and Reporting Requirements Act of 2015 to require body
15 camera footage transparency in use-of-force events.
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
18 act may be cited as the “Full Accountability in Arrest Reporting Temporary Amendment Act of
19 2026”.
20 Sec. 2. Arrest report and probable cause affidavit transparency requirements.
21 (a) Following an arrest, a member of the Metropolitan Police Department shall include the
22 following information, to the extent known at the time, in documentation supporting the arrest,
23 including a probable cause affidavit:
24 (1) The name, badge number or other identifying number, and employing agency
25 of each law enforcement officer present at the scene;
26 (2) Whether any law enforcement officer present at the scene used force during the
27 arrest, and if so, a description of the force used; and
28 (3) The number of unidentified law enforcement officers at the scene and whether
