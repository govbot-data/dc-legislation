_____________________________
Councilmember Brianne K. Nadeau
A BILL
_________________________
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
_________________________
1 To authorize, on a temporary basis, the Deputy Mayor for Planning and Economic Development
2 to issue a grant in Fiscal Year 2025 or 2026 to the African American Civil War Memorial
3 Freedom Foundation, Inc. for the purpose of redeveloping the African American Civil
4 War Museum.
5
6 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
7 act may be cited as the “African American Civil War Museum Limited Grant-Making Authority
8 Temporary Amendment Act of 2025”.
9 Sec. 2. Section 2032 of the Deputy Mayor for Planning and Economic Development
10 Limited Grant-Making Authority Act of 2012, effective September 20, 2012 (D.C. Law 19-168;
11 D.C. Code § 1-328.04), is amended by adding a new subsection (kk) to read as follows:
12 “(kk) Notwithstanding the Grant Administration Act of 2013, effective December 24,
13 2013 (D.C. Law 20-61; D.C. Official Code § 1-328.11 et seq.), the Deputy Mayor may make a
14 grant in Fiscal Year 2025 or 2026 to the African American Civil War Memorial Freedom
15 Foundation, Inc. for the purpose of redeveloping the African American Civil War Museum,
16 located at 1925 Vermont Avenue, N.W.”.
17 Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
1

18 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Budget Director as the fiscal impact
19 statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975, approved
20 October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
21 Sec. 4. Effective date.
22 (a) This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by
23 the Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), a 30-day period of congressional review
24 as provided in section 602(c)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December
25 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 813; D.C. Official Code § 1-206.02(c)(1)), and publication in the District of
26 Columbia Register.
27 (b) This act shall expire after 225 days of its having taken effect.
