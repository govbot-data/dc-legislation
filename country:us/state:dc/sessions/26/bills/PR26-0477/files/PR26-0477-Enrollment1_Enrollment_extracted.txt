ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-352
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
March 3, 2026
To confirm the appointment of Stacie Birenbach to the Housing Production Trust Fund Board.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Housing Production Trust Fund Board Stacie Birenbach
Confirmation Resolution of 2026”.
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia confirms the appointment of:
Stacie Birenbach
14th Street, NE
Washington, DC 20017
(Ward 5)
as a representative of the nonprofit housing production community member of the Housing
Production Trust Fund Board, established by section 3a of the Housing Production Trust Fund Act
of 1988, effective June 8, 1990 (D.C. Law 8-133; D.C. Official Code § 42-2802.01), for a term to
end January 14, 2027.
Sec. 3. The Council shall transmit a copy of this resolution, upon its adoption, to the
nominee and to the Mayor.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
