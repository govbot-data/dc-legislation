31 A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
32 ______________
33
34
35 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
36
37 ____________________________
38

39 To honor the Old People Are Cool project, supported by Forest Hills of DC, and celebrate our
40 District’s seniors by declaring May 2026 as “Old People Are Cool Month” in the District of
41 Columbia.
42
43 WHEREAS, every May the Administration for Community Living within the US
44 Department of Health and Human Services leads a national observance of Older Americans
45 Month to honor the independence, integration, and inclusion of older adults throughout all
46 aspects of life;
47
48 WHEREAS, the Administration for Community Living’s theme for Older Americans
49 Month in 2026 is Champion Your Health, which shines the spotlight on healthy aging by
50 encouraging conversations about taking an active role in managing health needs, self-advocacy,
51 preventative care, wellness, and informed decision-making for older adults.
52
53 WHEREAS, it is acknowledged that ageism currently isolates and marginalizes older
54 adults, which hurts our communities and inhibits our collective achievement and optimal growth;
55
56 WHEREAS, the Old People Are Cool project was created to confront the harmful ageism
57 that prevents communities from reaching their maximum potential by combatting ageism and
58 raising the profile of the senior care industry;
59
60 WHEREAS, the Old People are Cool project has the core mission to celebrate human life
61 by acknowledging elders as people, individuals, and recipients of love, and to facilitate thoughts,
62 words, and actions that promote a world that is loving, aging, and united;
63
64 WHEREAS, we additionally recognize the nobility of those healthcare workers who
65 uplift and care for our elders and seek to acknowledge their dedication to this valued group
66 within our community; and
67
68 WHEREAS, the Old People Are Cool project has built a supportive, inclusive, and
69 celebratory environment in the District through its efforts of bolstering morale and generating
70 inclusivity of older adults through community-building events and social media-story sharing.
71
72 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
73 resolution may be cited as the “Old People Are Cool Recognition Resolution of 2026.”
74
75 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia declares May 2026 Old People Are Cool
76 Month in the District of Columbia, celebrates and recognizes the Old People are Cool project
77 during the national celebration of Older Americans Month, and honors its commitment to
78 advancing the welfare and perception of seniors in the whole of the District of Columbia.

79
80 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately upon the first date of publication in
81 the District of Columbia Register.
