Dear Chairman Mendelson:
Enclosed for consideration and approval by the Council of the District of Columbia is a proposed
resolution entitled the "Medical Cannabis Retailer Craft Preparation Endorsement Act of 2025."
The proposed legislation would create a new medical cannabis retailer craft preparation
endorsement, and to establish specific requirements that must be satisfied for the Alcoholic
Beverage and Cannabis Board to approve and issue a craft preparation endorsement to the holder
of a medical cannabis retailer license. Specifically, the legislation revises Chapter 16B of Title 7
of the D.C. Official Code by creating a new section titled "Craft Preparation Endorsement."
If you have any questions on this matter, please contact Donovan Anderson, Chair, Alcoholic
Beverage and Cannabis Board, at (202) 442-4423.

20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
21 act may be cited as the "Medical Cannabis Retailer Craft Preparation Endorsement Amendment
22 Act of 2025".
23 Sec. 2. The Legalization of Marijuana for Medical Treatment Initiative of 1999, effective
24 February 25, 2010 (D.C. Law 13-315; D.C. Official Code§ 7-1671.01 et seq.), is amended as
25 follows:
26 (a) Section 7 (D.C. Official Code § 7-1671.01) is amended by adding a new paragraph
27 (15B) to read as follows:
28 "(15B) "Preroll" means a cigarette containing raw or dried cannabis flower or
29 other material from the cannabis plant wrapped in rolling paper or other material.
30 (b) A new section 7g is added to read as follows:
31 "Sec. 7g. Craft preparation endorsement.
32 "(a) A holder of a retailer license shall be eligible to apply to the ABC Board for a craft
33 preparation endorsement.

34 “(b) A holder of a craft preparation endorsement may prepare the following types of
35 medical cannabis products at their licensed premises for sale to qualifying patients and
36 caregivers:
37 “(1) Baked goods, beverages, chocolates, confections, and other edibles, provided
38 that the cannabis products are made using cannabis oil, butter, or from other products purchased
39 from a licensed manufacturer;
40 “(2) Lotions, soaps, ointments, and other products intended to be applied to the
41 skin; and
42 “(3) Prerolls.
43 “(c)(1) Medical cannabis products prepared under a craft preparation endorsement shall
44 be prepared, tested, packaged, and labeled in accordance with the requirements of this act and
45 rules issued by the Mayor.
46 “(2) All medical cannabis product prepared under a craft preparation endorsement
47 shall be shelf-stable and shall not require refrigeration to avoid spoilation.
48 “(3) A holder of a craft preparation endorsement shall not prepare or manufacture
49 transdermal patches.
50 “(d)(1) Medical cannabis products prepared under a craft preparation endorsement may
51 be sold only to qualifying patients and their caregivers.
52 “(2) A holder of a craft preparation endorsement that also holds a delivery
53 endorsement may deliver medical cannabis products prepared under the endorsement to
54 qualifying patients and their caregivers.
55 “(3) A holder of a craft preparation endorsement shall not distribute or sell
56 medical cannabis products to retailers, internet retailers, manufacturers, or unlicensed
2

57 establishments or other persons and entities engaged in the resale, direct sale, or wholesale of the
58 product.”.
59 “(e) A holder of a craft preparation endorsement may devote no more than 20% of the
60 gross floor area of its licensed premises to the preparation of medical cannabis products.
61 “(f)(1) A holder of a craft preparation endorsement may store no more than 2 kilograms
62 of medical cannabis flower and no more than 1 liter combined of cannabis oil and tincture for the
63 purpose of preparing medical cannabis products.
64 “(2)(A) Cannabis flower stored pursuant to this subsection shall be sourced only
65 from licensed cultivation centers.
66 “(B) Cannabis oil or tincture stored pursuant to this subsection shall be
67 sourced only from licensed manufacturers.
68 “(g) In the preparation of medical cannabis products, a holder of a craft preparation
69 endorsement shall not extract cannabinoids from cannabis plant material or use butane or
70 explosive gas.
71 “(h) A holder of a craft preparation endorsement shall not prepare, sell, or distribute
72 medical cannabis products that are not prepared, tested, packaged, and labeled in accordance
73 with the requirements of this act and rules issued by the Mayor.
74 “(i) A holder of a craft preparation endorsement shall not permit odor, smoke, or by-
75 products from its preparation of medical cannabis products to be detectable by sense of smell in a
76 separate residence or property, whether located on the same lot as the holder’s licensed premises
77 or a separate lot.

78 “(j) A holder of a craft preparation endorsement shall not permit the consumption or use
79 of medical cannabis products at its licensed premises unless it holds a safe-use treatment facility
80 endorsement for the licensed premises where the consumption or use will occur.”.
