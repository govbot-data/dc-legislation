Hana Gebremeskel
Ms. Hana Gebremeskel is a community advocate dedicated to financial
empowerment, social welfare, and public service. With over a decade of
experience serving underserved communities, she combines financial
expertise with multilingual communication to help individuals and
families access critical resources and build long-term stability.
As Founder and Director of the Homeless Animals Protection Society in
Ethiopia, Ms. Gebremeskel established a pioneering organization focused
on public health and animal welfare, leading spay/neuter initiatives and
international awareness campaigns. She has represented these efforts at
global conferences across Europe and Africa. Across all areas of her
work, Ms. Gebremeskel remains committed to strengthening
communities through leadership, advocacy, and cross-cultural collaboration.
A Ward 7 resident, Ms. Gebremeskel earned her Bachelor of Arts in Business Administration from
Addis Ababa University.
