6 A PROPOSED RESOLUTION
7 ________
8
9 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
10 ______________________
11
12 To declare the existence of an emergency, due to congressional review, with respect to the need
13 to amend the Rental Housing Act of 1985 to maintain a moratorium on applications for,
14 and the Mayor’s issuance of, certificates of assurance associated with District rent
15 stabilization laws.
16
17 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this resolution
18 may be cited as the “Certificate of Assurance Moratorium Congressional Review Emergency
19 Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
20 Sec. 2. (a) On November 12, 2024, the Council passed a corresponding Certificate of
21 Assurance Moratorium Emergency Amendment Act of 2024 (D.C. Act 25-0633), which was
22 enacted on November 22, 2024, and expires on February 20, 2025.
23 (b) On November 26, 2024, the Council passed a corresponding Certificate of Assurance
24 Moratorium Temporary Amendment Act of 2024 (D.C. Act 25-0663), which was transmitted to
25 Congress on January 23, 2025, and is projected to become law on March 6, 2025.
26 (c) This emergency legislation is necessary to prevent a gap in the law between the
27 expiration of the emergency act and the effective date of the temporary act.
28 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
29 enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the
30 Certificate of Assurance Moratorium Emergency Amendment Act of 2025 be adopted after a
31 single reading.

32 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
