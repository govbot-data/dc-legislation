17 To declare the existence of an emergency, due to congressional review, with respect to the need to
18 amend section 8f of the Homeless Services Reform Act of 2005 to require specific
19 documentation from tenants establishing eligibility for Emergency Rental Assistance
20 Funds, to clarify the definition of a qualifying emergency situation, and to require the
21 Mayor to issue regulations implementing the amended provisions of that section; and to
22 amend section 501 of the Rental Housing Act of 1985 to permit a court to enter a stay,
23 rather than require a court to enter a stay, when a tenant submits documentation to the court
24 demonstrating that the tenant has a pending Emergency Rental Assistance Program
25 application, to require housing providers to reschedule evictions in certain circumstances
26 involving approved Emergency Rental Assistance Program applications, and to create an
27 affirmative defense in eviction cases on the basis of housing providers’ noncompliance
28 with the Emergency Rental Assistance Program application process.
29
30 RESOLVED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
31 resolution may be cited as the “Emergency Rental Assistance Program Reform Congressional
32 Review Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
33 Sec. 2. (a) On October 15, 2024, the Council adopted the Emergency Rental Assistance
34 Program Reform Temporary Amendment Act of 2025, enacted on October 28, 2024 (D.C. Act 25-
35 611; 71 DCR 13497), which expires on July 30, 2025.
36 (b) On May 28, 2025, the Emergency Rental Assistance Program Reform Amendment Act
37 of 2025 (D.C. Act 26-74) was enacted, and that legislation will soon be pending congressional
38 review.
1

39 (c) This emergency legislation is necessary to prevent a gap in the law between the
40 expiration of the temporary act and the effective date of the permanent measure.
41 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia finds that the circumstances enumerated in
42 section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the “Emergency Rental
43 Assistance Program Reform Congressional Review Emergency Amendment Act of 2025” be
44 adopted after a single reading.
45 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
