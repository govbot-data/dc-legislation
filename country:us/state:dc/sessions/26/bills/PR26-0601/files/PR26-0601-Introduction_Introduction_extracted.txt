Korie Booker
Ms. Korie Booker is currently a permanent make-up artist at Elite Image
in Washington, DC. Before working in permanent make-up, Ms. Booker
was a dance instructor. While teaching dance and managing an after-
school program she studied cosmetology, ultimately leading to her
current career in permanent make-up.
A Ward 6 resident, Ms. Booker earned her Bachelor’s Degree in Dance
Education from Berea College and her Associate Degree in Cosmetology
from Bennett Career Institute.
