ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-183
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
July 28, 2025
To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to officially designate the
public alley system within Square 1084 as Elmore-Friendship Court.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Elmore-Friendship Court Designation Emergency Declaration
Resolution of 2025”.
Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to officially designate the public alley system
within Square 1084 as Elmore-Friendship Court.
(b) The Elmore-Friendship Court Designation Act of 2025, passed on 2nd reading on July
28, 2025 (Enrolled version of Bill 26-220), was adopted on final reading by the Council on July
28, 2025.
(c) Emergency legislation will allow the applicant who owns a lot in fronting the alley to
obtain District agency approvals for re-development of an existing carriage house into a
residential unit, including permits and utility location, before the permanent legislation
completes the 30-day congressional review period.
Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia finds that the circumstances enumerated
in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Elmore-Friendship
Court Designation Emergency Act of 2025 be adopted after a single reading.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
