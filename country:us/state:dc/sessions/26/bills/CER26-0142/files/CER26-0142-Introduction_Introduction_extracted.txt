5 A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
6
7 _________________________
8
9 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
10
11 _________________________
12
13
14 To declare the month of March 2026 as “Developmental Disabilities Awareness Month” in the
15 District of Columbia.
16
17 WHEREAS, over 8 million Americans have developmental disabilities and people with
18 developmental disabilities are our friends, family, and neighbors in every Ward of the District of
19 Columbia;
20 WHEREAS, throughout our nation’s history, many individuals with developmental
21 disabilities were hidden, segregated, or excluded from full participation in community life;
22 WHEREAS, decades of advocacy, civil rights protections, and community-based
23 supports have expanded opportunities for people with developmental disabilities to live, work,
24 learn, and participate fully in society;
25 WHEREAS, community living is about more than services or programs — it is about
26 belonging, choice, independence, and the opportunity to live a whole life;
27 WHEREAS, we believe that the inclusion of people with developmental disabilities in
28 our workplaces, schools, and communities improves all of our lives;
29 WHEREAS, we encourage all residents to recognize the contributions of people with
30 developmental disabilities and to promote communities where everyone belongs;
1

31 WHEREAS, government agencies, community organizations, and individual and family
32 leaders work together to create accessible and high-quality education, housing, employment,
33 healthcare, recreational activities, and other community resources for people with developmental
34 disabilities and their families;
35 WHEREAS, the District of Columbia is committed to creating more opportunities for
36 economic self-sufficiency, belonging, and creative innovation by ensuring that residents with
37 disabilities have meaningful employment opportunities;
38 WHEREAS, people with developmental disabilities strengthen our workplaces, enrich
39 our neighborhoods, contribute to our economy, and help shape the future of the District of
40 Columbia;
41 WHEREAS, Developmental Disabilities Awareness Month provides an opportunity to
42 reflect on our progress, recognize ongoing challenges, and reaffirm our commitment to policies
43 and practices that support full participation in community life; and
44 WHEREAS, it is the responsibility of the District of Columbia to continue fostering
45 communities that value inclusion, remove barriers, and ensure that all people have the
46 opportunity to pursue their goals and aspirations.
47 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
48 resolution may be cited as the “Developmental Disabilities Awareness Month Recognition
49 Resolution of 2026”.
50 Sec. 2. The Council declares the month of March 2026 as “Developmental Disabilities
51 Awareness Month”.
52 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
