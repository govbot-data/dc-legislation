Reginald Mathis
Mr. Reginald Mathis is currently the Executive Director of Audrey’s
House, a non-profit he founded focused on overseeing violence
intervention initiations and trauma-informed programs. Previously, Mr.
Mathis was a Lead Grant Monitor at the Office of Gun Violence
Prevention.
Mr. Mathis’s professional service also includes his work as a Community
Affairs Consultant at Man Power CSO, where he advised clients on
community engagement strategies.
Mr. Mathis is a Ward 8 resident.
