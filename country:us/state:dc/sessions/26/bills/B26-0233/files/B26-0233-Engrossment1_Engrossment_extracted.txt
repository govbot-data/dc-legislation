9 To amend the District of Columbia Election Code of 1955 to require candidate nomination
10 petition affidavits to state that the circulator has not altered signer information on a
11 petition after a signer has signed the petition; to prohibit anyone from altering signer
12 information provided by the petition signer, except in cases where the signer requests
13 assistance in signing the petition before signing the petition; to amend the Initiative,
14 Referendum and Recall Procedures Act of 1979 to require initiative, referendum and
15 recall petition affidavits to state that the circulator has not altered signer information on a
16 petition after a signer has signed the petition; and, to prohibit anyone from altering signer
17 information provided by the petition signer, except in cases where the signer requests
18 assistance in signing the petition.
19
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this act may be
21 cited as the “Petition Administration Clarification Amendment Act of 2025”.
22 Sec. 2. The District of Columbia Election Code of 1955, approved August 12, 1955 (69
23 Stat. 701; D.C. Official Code § 1-1001.01 et seq.) is amended as follows:
24 (a) Section 8 (D.C. Official Code 1-1001.08) is amended as follows:
25 (1) Subsection (b)(3) is amended as follows:
26 (A) Subparagraph (B) is amended by striking the phrase “; and” and
27 inserting a semicolon in its place.
28 (B) Subparagraph (C) is amended by striking the period and inserting the
29 phrase “; and” in its place.

30 (C) A new subparagraph (D) is added to read as follows:
31 “(D) Not written, altered, corrected, clarified, or obscured information on the
32 petition about the signer of the petition after the signer affixed their signature to the petition.”.
33 (2) A new subsection (p-1) is added to read as follows:
34 “(p-1) No person other than the petition signer may write, alter, correct, or clarify on a
35 nominating petition any information about the petition signer unless the signer requests assistance
36 in writing, altering, correcting, or clarifying on the petition any information about the signer before
37 the signer affixes their signature. The use of correction tape or fluid is prohibited.”.
38 (b) Section 16 (D.C. Official Code § 1-1001.16) is amended as follows:
39 (1) Subsection (h) is amended by adding a new paragraph (3A) is added to read as
40 follows:
41 “(3A) That the circulator of the petition sheet has not written, altered,
42 corrected, clarified, or obscured information on the petition about the signer of the petition after the
43 signer affixed their signature to the petition.”.
44 (2) A new subsection (h-1) is added to read as follows:
45 “(h-1) No person other than the petition signer may write, alter, correct, or clarify on a
46 petition sheet for an initiative or referendum unless the signer requests assistance in writing,
47 altering, correcting, or clarifying on the petition any information about the petition signer before
48 the signer affixes their signature. The use of correction fluid or tape is prohibited.”.
