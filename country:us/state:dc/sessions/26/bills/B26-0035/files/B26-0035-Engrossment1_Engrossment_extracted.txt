10 To amend the Uniform Commercial Code to provide that the Recorder of Deeds may not refuse
11 to accept a written initial financing statement in the form provided by the International
12 Association of Commercial Administrators or in a form adopted by the Chief Financial
13 Officer by rule and to permit the Chief Financial Officer to promulgate rules setting fees
14 for filing and indexing of initial financing statements.
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
17 act may be cited as the “Uniform Commercial Code Financing Statement Forms Amendment Act
18 of 2025”.
