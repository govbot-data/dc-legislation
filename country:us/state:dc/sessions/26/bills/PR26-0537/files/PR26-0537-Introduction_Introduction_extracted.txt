CHRISTOPHER K. MURPHY
Senior government relations executive with a 30-year track record of proven success leading diverse
organizations and teams in the philanthropic, higher education, non-profit, private, and government sectors.
Demonstrated expertise in conceptualizing innovative strategies to solve pressing problems, building and
empowering strong, inclusive teams, and driving change to deliver meaningful and sustainable results at the
federal, state, and local levels.
Extensive leadership experience in government relations, public policy, strategic partnerships, grantmaking,
communications, stakeholder engagement, and organizational and financial management.

Christopher Murphy
Mr. Christopher Murphy serves as the Deputy Director for U.S. state and
local government relations on the Gates Foundation's North America
Team.
His career has included senior positions in the nonprofit and private
sectors and local and federal government, including Vice President of
Government Relations at Georgetown University, Chief of Staff to Mayor
Vincent C. Gray, and Deputy Chief of Staff at the U.S. Department of
Housing & Urban Development.
Mr. Murphy was named a Marshall Memorial Fellow by the German
Marshall Fund and has served on the board of several national and local
non-profits.
Mr. Murphy earned his Bachelor of Arts from Harvard University and his Juris Doctor from
Georgetown University.
