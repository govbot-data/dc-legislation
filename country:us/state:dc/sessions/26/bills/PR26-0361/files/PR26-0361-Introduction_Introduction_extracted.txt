Dear Chairman Mendelson:
Enclosed for consideration and approval by the Council of the District of Columbia, is proposed emergency
legislation, “University of the District of Columbia Change Order No. 2 to Contract No. GF 2021-C-2021 with
Consys, Inc Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2025” and the corresponding emergency
declaration resolution. The initial contract was deemed approved on February 5, 2022, as CA 24-0382 to construct
the Academic Commons – Relocation of Learning Resources Division and Center for Advanced Learning (LRD
& CAL) Project; and Change Order No.1 was deemed approved on April 15, 2024, as CA25 -0675.
Subsequently, the University added to the scope of work modifications for information technology, for security
access, and to address unforeseen conditions necessitating modifications for flooring, boilers, and the steam
pressure-reducing station. The services and equipment for Change Order No. 2 increased the cost of the Contract
by two million seventy thousand two hundred twenty-four dollars and eighty-six cents ($2,070,224.86) for a total
amount of twelve million six hundred fifty-eight thousand one hundred twenty-four dollars and seventy-eight
cents ($12,658,124.78).
However, this Change Order No. 2 was not submitted before the period of performance began. Thus, the
University seeks retroactive authorization and approval of this contract amount.
As always, I am available to discuss any questions you may have regarding the proposed emergency legislation
and order change. In order to facilitate a response to any questions you may have regarding this proposed action,
please have your staff contact Mr. David Franklin, Chief Operating Officer at (202) 274-6912. I look forward to
favorable action on this request and thank you in advance for your consideration.
Sincerely,
