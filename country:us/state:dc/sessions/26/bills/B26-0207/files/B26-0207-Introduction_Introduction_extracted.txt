‘The resolution and accompanyingemergency and temporary billsclose gaps in the District'sCertified
Business Enterprise(CBE) Program by making critical amendments to sections 2302, 2331, 2346, and
2363 ofthe Small and Certified Business Enterprise Developmentand Assistance Act of2005 (“the
Act”).
More specifically, theseamendments will: (1) establish uniform hearing procedures for all departmental
enforcement actions beforeOAH; (2) clarify currentdefinitionsor add definitionsto critical terms used
throughoutthe Act; (3)reinstate provisions previously passed by Council related to the local business
enterprise testthat have since lapsed; and (4) right sizejoint venture performance requirements for
certified businessenterprises.
‘These necessary, time-sensitivechanges will allow the Districtto more effectively and efficiently
administer the District'sCBE laws as well as enforce the District'sCBE compliance requirements.
T urge the Council totake prompt and favorable action on the enclosed legislation.
