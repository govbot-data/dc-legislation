Shantelle Wright
Ms. Shantelle D. Wright is an award-winning leader, recognized for her
work in education reform and the urban education landscape. She
currently serves as the Senior Vice President, Regional Field Executive
at Teach for America. Teach For America finds, develops and supports
equity-oriented leaders to transform education and expand opportunity
for all children. Prior to her current role, she was the chief program
officer at EdNavigator and was also the founder and chief executive
officer of Achievement Prep, a college-preparatory public charter
school network located in Southeast Washington, D.C.
Ms. Wright was selected as one of 13 outstanding leaders to complete
the BES fellowship program. BES is a highly competitive, rigorous, yearlong, full-time training
program in urban charter school creation and leadership. Ms. Wright is also a licensed attorney
having practiced commercial real estate and corporate law for several years at a large, well-
respected Washington, DC law firm.
A Ward 8 resident, Ms. Wright earned her Bachelor of Arts in Sociology from Hampton University
and her Juris Doctor from The George Washington University.
