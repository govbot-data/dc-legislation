ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-80
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
April 1, 2025
To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to approve Modification Nos.
M0009, M0010, M0011, M0012, and M0013 to Contract No. CW101632 with KBEC
Group, Inc., and to authorize payment for the goods and services received and to be
received under the contract.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Modification Nos. M0009, M0010, M0011, M0012, and M0013
to Contract No. CW101632 with KBEC Group, Inc. Approval and Payment Authorization
Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to approve Modification Nos. M0009, M0010,
M0011, M0012, and M0013 to Contract No. CW101632 with KBEC Group, Inc., and to
authorize payment for the goods and services received and to be received under the contract.
(b) On October 1, 2024, via Modification No. M0009, the Office of Contracting and
Procurement (“OCP”), on behalf of the Department of Human Services, partially exercised
option year 2 of Contract No. CW101632 with KBEC Group, Inc. in the not-to-exceed (“NTE”)
amount of $350,180.52 for the period of October 1, 2024, through December 31, 2024.
(c) Modification No. M0010 was an administrative modification.
(d) On December 31, 2024, via Modification No. M0011, OCP partially exercised option
year 2 through March 31, 2025, and increased the NTE amount by $356,559.15, to $690,539.67.
(e) On January 21, 2025, via Modification No. M0012, OCP partially exercised option
year 2 through April 30, 2025.
(f) Modification No. M0013 will exercise the remainder of option year 2 through
September 30, 2025, and increase the NTE amount by $713,118.30, to $1,403,657.97.
(g) Council approval is necessary because the modifications will increase the value of the
contract to more than $1 million during a 12-month period.
(h) Council approval is necessary to allow the continuation of these vital services.
Without Council approval, KBEC Group, Inc. cannot be paid for goods and services provided in
excess of $1 million for the period from October 1, 2024, through September 30, 2025.

Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the
Modification Nos. M0009, M0010, M0011, M0012, and M0013 to Contract No. CW101632
with KBEC Group, Inc. Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2025 be
adopted after a single reading.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
