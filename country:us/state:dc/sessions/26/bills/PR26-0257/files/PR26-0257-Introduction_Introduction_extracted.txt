_____________________________
Councilmember Brianne K. Nadeau
A PROPOSED RESOLUTION
______________
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
__________________
1 To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to authorize the Deputy Mayor
2 for Planning and Economic Development to issue a grant to the African American Civil
3 War Memorial Freedom Foundation, Inc. to support the completion of the African
4 American Civil War Museum.
5
6 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
7 resolution may be cited as the “African American Civil War Museum Limited Grant-Making
8 Authority Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
9 Sec. 2. (a) The African American Civil War Museum (“AACWM”) opened to the public
10 more than 20 years ago, and helps visitors understand the largely unknown role of Black and
11 brown soldiers who fought for freedom from slavery during the Civil War. The AACWM
12 displays photographs, documents, and artifacts, and holds regular seminars and historic
13 presentations by community members. The museum serves as a unique resource for teachers,
14 scholars, students, and professionals of museum studies, descendants of Civil War soldiers, and
15 the general public. More than 200,000 visitors come to the Memorial and Museum each year.
1

16 (b) Emergency legislation is necessary to extend the Deputy Mayor for Planning and
17 Economic Development’s authority to issue grant funds to AACWM, which it currently has
18 under expiring emergency and temporary legislation. Grant funding is necessary close the
19 funding gap experienced by the AACWM to build out the museum into the remainder of its
20 facility. Expansion of the AACWM will complete a key recommendation from Office of
21 Planning’s DUKE small area plan on the historic Cardozo/Shaw neighborhood, utilizing the
22 former Grimke School for the AACWM and its nonprofit foundation.
23 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances in
24 Section 2 constitute emergency circumstances, making it necessary that the African American
25 Civil War Museum Limited Grant-Making Authority Emergency Amendment Act of 2024 be
26 adopted after a single reading.
27 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
2
