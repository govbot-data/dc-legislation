17 To recognize honoree Reggie Monroe during the 2025 celebration of DC Black Pride.
18
19 WHEREAS, Reginald “Reggie Riich” Monroe is the visionary force behind Reggie Riich
20 Events, a premier LGBTQ+ event production company based in Washington, D.C.;
21
22 WHEREAS, starting his journey with DC Black Pride in 2015, Reggie Monroe has built
23 a national following for his inclusive, high-energy celebrations that bring together professionals,
24 creatives, and community leaders;
25
26 WHEREAS, known for signature events like the Sunday Day Party series and his iconic
27 New Year’s Eve bash, Reggie Monroe uses his platform to foster joy and unity within the
28 LGBTQ+ community;
29
30 WHEREAS, Reggie Monroe’s events are more than parties - they are affirming spaces of
31 pride and connection for thousands;
32
33 WHEREAS, DC Black Pride is an annual event to increase awareness of and showcase
34 pride in the diversity of the LGBTQIA+ populations in the African American community, and to
35 support organizations that focus on health disparities, education, youth, and families;
36
37 WHEREAS, as the very first Black Pride festival in the world, DC Black Pride fostered
38 the beginning of the Center for Black Equity (formerly known as the International Federation of
39 Black Prides, Inc. (IFBP)) and the “Black Pride Movement”;
40
41 WHEREAS, since the birth of DC Black Pride, more than 50 other Black Pride
42 celebrations now take place throughout the world and on 4 continents, with many using DC
43 Black Pride as a model;
44
1

45 WHEREAS, May 23, 2025, through May 26, 2025, marks the 34th annual DC Black
46 Pride celebration;
47
48 WHEREAS, the theme for this year’s celebration is “BLACK PRIDE IS FREEDOM”;
49
50 WHEREAS, for more than 30 years, DC Black Pride has been an opportunity for the
51 Black LGBTQIA+ community to gather and celebrate being both African American and
52 members of LGBTQIA+ communities;
53
54 WHEREAS, DC Black Pride is the oldest and one of the largest Black Pride events in the
55 world, drawing thousands of residents and visitors from around the globe; and
56
57 WHEREAS, DC Black Pride brings more than 500,000 people to the nation’s capital
58 from across the United States, Canada, the Caribbean, South Africa, Great Britain, France,
59 Germany, and the Netherlands and will carry that tradition this year and into the future to
60 celebrate their shared community, raise awareness and funding for HIV/AIDS prevention, and
61 stand against homophobia inspired by violence and bigotry.
62
63 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That
64 this resolution be cited as the “Reggie Monroe Recognition Resolution of 2025”.
65
66 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia recognizes and celebrates Reggie Monroe
67 during the 2025 celebration of DC Black Pride.
68
69 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
