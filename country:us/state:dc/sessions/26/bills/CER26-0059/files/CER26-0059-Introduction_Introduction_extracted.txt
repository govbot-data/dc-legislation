12 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA

17 To recognize honoree SaVanna Wanzer during the 2025 celebration of DC Black Pride.
18
19 WHEREAS, SaVanna Wanzer has been a groundbreaking advocate, educator, and
20 community builder;
21
22 WHEREAS, as the founder of TransPride and the visionary behind May is: All About
23 Trans, SaVanna Wanzer has created platforms that center and celebrate transgender voices
24 nationwide;
25
26 WHEREAS, SaVanna Wanzer has been a trailblazer in HIV education and peer
27 mentoring;
28
29 WHEREAS, SaVanna Wanzer was the first trans woman to serve on the boards of both
30 Whitman-Walker Health and Capital Pride;
31
32 WHEREAS, SaVanna Wanzer has received numerous honors, including the National
33 Trans Visibility March Torch Award and Capital Pride’s Hero Award;
34
35 WHEREAS, SaVanna Wanzer is a retired federal employee and proud member of
36 Westminster Presbyterian Church;
37
38 WHERAS, SaVanna Wanzer aims to take her “May Is” campaign national;
39
40 WHEREAS, DC Black Pride is an annual event to increase awareness of and showcase
41 pride in the diversity of the LGBTQIA+ populations in the African American community, and to
42 support organizations that focus on health disparities, education, youth, and families;
43
1

44 WHEREAS, as the very first Black Pride festival in the world, DC Black Pride fostered
45 the beginning of the Center for Black Equity (formerly known as the International Federation of
46 Black Prides, Inc. (IFBP)) and the “Black Pride Movement”;
47
48 WHEREAS, since the birth of DC Black Pride, more than 50 other Black Pride
49 celebrations now take place throughout the world and on 4 continents, with many using DC
50 Black Pride as a model;
51
52 WHEREAS, May 23, 2025, through May 26, 2025, marks the 34th annual DC Black
53 Pride celebration;
54
55 WHEREAS, the theme for this year’s celebration is “BLACK PRIDE IS FREEDOM”;
56
57 WHEREAS, for more than 30 years, DC Black Pride has been an opportunity for the
58 Black LGBTQIA+ community to gather and celebrate being both African American and
59 members of LGBTQIA+ communities;
60
61 WHEREAS, DC Black Pride is the oldest and one of the largest Black Pride events in the
62 world, drawing thousands of residents and visitors from around the globe; and
63
64 WHEREAS, DC Black Pride brings more than 500,000 people to the nation’s capital
65 from across the United States, Canada, the Caribbean, South Africa, Great Britain, France,
66 Germany, and the Netherlands and will carry that tradition this year and into the future to
67 celebrate their shared community, raise awareness and funding for HIV/AIDS prevention, and
68 stand against homophobia inspired by violence and bigotry.
69
70 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That
71 this resolution be cited as the “SaVanna Wanzer Recognition Resolution of 2025”.
72
73 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia recognizes and celebrates SaVanna
74 Wanzer during the 2025 celebration of DC Black Pride.
75
76 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
