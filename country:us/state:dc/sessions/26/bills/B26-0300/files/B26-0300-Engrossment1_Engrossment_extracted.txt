12 To amend, on an temporary basis, section 25-113 of the District of Columbia Official Code to
13 continue the extension of the Streatery Program endorsements through December 31,
14 2025 and to clarify endorsement deadlines for certain classes of licensees; and to amend
15 the Legalization of Marijuana for Medical Treatment Initiative of 1999 to make a
16 clarifying amendment.
17
18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
19 act may be cited as the “Streatery Program Endorsement Extension and Medical Cannabis
20 Clarification Temporary Amendment Act of 2025”.
21
22 Sec. 2. Section 25-113(a)(6) of the District of Columbia Code is amended as follows:
23 (a) Strike the phrase “2022 and 2023” wherever it appears and insert the phrase “2022,
24 2023, 2024, and through December 31, 2025” in its place.
25 (b) Subparagraph (A)(i)(II) is amended by striking the semicolon and inserting the
26 phrase “annually with the fee for 2025 due by April 1, 2025; except, that an on-premises retailer
27 licensee, class C/T, D/T, C/N, or D/N, or a manufacturer licensee class B, shall pay the fee for
28 2025 by September 30, 2025;” in its place.
29 Sec. 3. Section 7(q)(1) the Legalization of Marijuana for Medical Treatment Initiative of
30 1999, effective July 27, 2010 (D.C. Law 18-210; D.C. Official Code § 7-1671.06(q)(1)), is
31 amended to read as follows:
