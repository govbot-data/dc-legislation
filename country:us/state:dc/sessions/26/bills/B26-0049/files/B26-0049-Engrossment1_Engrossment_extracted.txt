12 To amend Title 28 of the District of Columbia Official Code to amend definitions of existing
13 terms; to allow for alternative calculation of normal retail prices for rental vehicles and
14 hotel rooms based on a seasonal pricing model; to prohibit stockpiling of certain goods
15 following a natural disaster or declaration of a public emergency; to make the declaration
16 of a public emergency a triggering event for the prohibition against price gouging; and to
17 provide for penalties if the court finds a person has engaged in price gouging or
18 stockpiling in a civil action brought by the Office of Attorney General for the District of
19 Columbia.
20
21 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
22 act may be cited as the “Seasonal Pricing and Price Gouging Amendment Act of 2026”.
