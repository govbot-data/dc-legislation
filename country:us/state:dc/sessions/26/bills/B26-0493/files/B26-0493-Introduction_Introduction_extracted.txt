30 To amend the Tenant Receivership Act to clarify grounds, notice, and procedures for
31 receivership, expand tools available to the Court and receiver, establish cost recovery and
32 lien mechanisms, and authorize tenant protections; to amend the Construction Codes to
33 strengthen injunctive relief; and to amend the Business Organizations Code to permit
34 dissolution and related remedies in cases of fraud or abuse, and for other purposes.
35
36 BE IT ENACTED by the Council of the District of Columbia, That this act may be cited
37 as the “Strengthening Tenant Receivership and Oversight for Neighborhood Growth (STRONG)
38 Homes Amendment Act of 2025”.
