Tiffany Edmundson
Professional Summary
Highly reliable and driven operations professional with hands-on experience supporting
mechanical and electrical systems in industrial environments. Demonstrates a strong foundation
in troubleshooting, repair support, and equipment maintenance, with the ability to quickly
diagnose issues and assist in restoring systems to full operation. Brings a strong technical
aptitude, safety-first mindset, and transferable skills that add immediate value to cross-
functional teams. Adaptable, fast learner with a strong desire to expand technical expertise and
advance within skilled trades and industrial operations.
Core Skills and Achievements
Operations Support
● Diagnosed mechanical and electrical malfunctions across industrial systems by
supporting troubleshooting and repair efforts, helping maintain continuous operations in
high-demand environments.
● Responded to emergency equipment breakdowns by rapidly assessing risks, isolating
faults, and coordinating repair support, reducing downtime and protecting personnel and
equipment.
● Collaborated with mechanics and technicians to translate technical diagnostics into
clear, actionable repair steps, improving efficiency and accuracy of maintenance
outcomes.
● Conducted detailed inspections and audits of industrial equipment by evaluating
operational integrity, safety compliance, and performance standards, identifying
deficiencies and maintenance needs.
Service Support
● Developed working knowledge of local utility systems, infrastructure layouts, and
regulatory requirements through hands-on field operations, supporting safe and
compliant work execution.
● Located and marked underground utility infrastructure and documented site findings
using mapping software, ensuring accurate reporting and record-keeping.
● Operated electromagnetic and radio-frequency locating equipment in accordance with
safety protocols, supporting damage prevention and infrastructure protection.
● Applied industry-standard utility locating techniques to support safe excavation activities
and reduce risk to underground assets.
Logistics Support

● Operated mail-processing and industrial equipment, including conveyor systems, by
following strict safety and handling protocols to maintain workflow efficiency.
● Adapted to rotating assignments and flex staffing needs by performing multiple
operational roles, supporting continuity in fast-paced environments.

reliability, productivity, and workplace safety.
● Operated powered industrial trucks (PITs) in compliance with safety guidelines and
operational standards.

Tiffany (Tre) Edmundson
Mr. Tiffany (Tre) Edmundson brings hands-on experience in mechanical
and electrical systems from roles in industrial operations and utility
services. His background includes troubleshooting complex systems,
supporting repairs, and ensuring operational continuity in high-demand
environments. He previously served as a Plant Engineer Specialist at UPS
and a Utility Line Locator at Utiliquest LLC.
His career interests include advancing knowledge in electrical and
mechanical systems and applying these principles to improve production
and efficiency. He is completing a certification in business and
entrepreneurship at Georgetown and has completed significant
coursework in electrical engineering.
Beyond technical and entrepreneurial pursuits, Mr. Edmundson is passionate about justice reform.
After returning home from five years of incarceration, he is committed to using his lived
experience to drive systemic change. His journey reflects resilience, growth, and a vision for
creating impact through business innovation and social advocacy.
A Ward 6 resident, Mr. Edmundson is pursuing a certificate from the McDonough School of
Business through the Georgetown University Pivot Program.
