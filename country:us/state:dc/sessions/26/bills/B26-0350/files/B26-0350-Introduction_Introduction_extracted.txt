14 To amend, on an emergency basis, the District of Columbia Health Occupations Revision Act of
15 1985 and the Consent for Vaccination of Minors Amendment Act of 2022 to authorize
16 healthcare providers in the District to order and administer immunizations recommended
17 by competent medical and public health organizations designated by the Director of the
18 Department of Health.
19
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
21 act may be cited as the “Community Health Emergency Amendment Act of 2025”.
22 Sec. 2. The District of Columbia Health Occupations Revision Act of 1985, effective
23 March 25, 1986 (D.C. Law 6-99; D.C. Official Code § 3-1201.01 et seq.), is amended as follows:
24 (a) Section 102(11)(A) (D.C. Official Code § 3-1201.02(11)(A)) is amended by striking
25 the phrase “in accordance with the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention’s published
26 guidelines and recommended immunization schedules” and inserting the phrase “in accordance
27 with the published guidelines and recommended immunization schedules of the Advisory
28 Committee on Immunization Practices of the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention or a
29 competent medical or public health organization designated by the Director of the Department of
30 Health through a public notice” in its place.
31 (b) Section 755(a-1) (D.C. Official Code § 3-1207.55(a-1)) is amended by striking the
32 phrase “in accordance with the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention’s published
33 guidelines and recommended immunization schedules” and inserting the phrase “in accordance

1 with the published guidelines and recommended immunization schedules of the Advisory
2 Committee on Immunization Practices of the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention or a
3 competent medical or public health organization designated by the Director of the Department of
4 Health through a public notice” in its place.
5 Sec. 3. Section 2 of the Consent for Vaccinations of Minors Amendment Act of 2022,
6 effective March 10, 2023 (D.C. Law 24-312; D.C. Official Code § 7-1653.01) is amended as
7 follows:
8 (a) Subsection (a) is amended by striking the phrase “recommended by the United States
9 Advisory Committee on Immunization Practices (“ACIP”), and where receipt of the vaccine is in
10 accordance with ACIP's recommended immunization schedule.” and inserting the phrase
11 “recommended by the Advisory Committee on Immunization Practices of the Centers for
12 Disease Control and Prevention (“ACIP”) or a competent medical or public health organization
13 designated by the Director of the Department of Health (“Director”) through a public notice, and
14 where receipt of the vaccine is in accordance with the recommended immunization schedule of
15 ACIP or the competent medical or public health organization designated by the Director.” in its
16 place.
17 (b) Subsection (b)(1) is amended by striking the phrase “recommended by ACIP in
18 accordance with ACIP’s recommended immunization schedule” and inserting the phrase
19 “recommended by ACIP or the competent medical or public health organization designated by
20 the Director, in accordance with the recommended immunization schedule of ACIP or the
21 competent medical or public health organization designated by the Director” in its place.
