ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-346
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
March 3, 2026
To confirm the reappointment of Teaira Brewer to the Commission on Fashion Arts and Events.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Commission on Fashion Arts and Events Teaira Brewer
Confirmation Resolution of 2026”.
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia confirms the reappointment of:
Teaira Brewer
Chesapeake Street, SE
Washington, DC 20032
(Ward 8)
as a voting member of the Commission on Fashion Arts and Events, established by section 2 of
the Commission on Fashion Arts and Events Establishment Act of 2008, effective April 15, 2008
(D.C. Law 17-148; D.C. Official Code § 3-651), for a term to end April 15, 2029.
Sec. 3. The Council shall transmit a copy of this resolution, upon its adoption, to the
nominee and to the Mayor.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
