20 To recognize and honor A. Litteri Inc. on its 100th anniversary of providing quality food, drink
21 and flavors of Italy to District residents.
22
23 WHEREAS, A. Litteri Inc. was opened in 1926 by Antonio Litteri, a World War One
24 veteran, who immigrated to the United States from Sicily in the early 1900s and was joined in
25 1932 by his brother in-law Mariano DeFrancisci, a native of Valguarnera, Sicily, and later by his
26 nephew Tommy DeFrancisci;
27 WHEREAS, A. Litteri Inc. was originally opened in what is now Ward 2 at 6th and G
28 Streets NW and in 1931 moved to 5th and K Street NE before settling at the current location in
29 Ward 5, 517 Morse Street, NE in 1932;

30 WHEREAS, A. Litteri Inc. is the oldest remaining business of Union Market’s
31 wholesaler district which has provided authentic, high-quality, Italian products to residents
32 across the city since it first opened in 19311;
33 WHEREAS, the business has been repeatedly recognized as a local favorite by outlets
34 like the Washingtonian and has received awards including Winner of the 2017 Leccio d'Oro
35 Award for Best Wine Shop from the Consorzio del vino Brunello di Montalcino; and
36 WHEREAS, Antonio’s nephew, Christopher Litteri DeFrancisci, ran A. Litteri from the
37 1960s until the 1980s, when his sons, Chris and Mike DeFrancisci, took over the family
38 business;
39 WHEREAS, Mike DeFrancisci, grandson of Mariano DeFrancisci, passed the business to
40 its current owner Max Evans in 2020, continuing the 100 year legacy of providing exceptional
41 products and service as a neighborhood staple beloved by residents and visitors alike;
42 WHEREAS, A. Litteri gives Italian Americans a place to connect with their heritage and
43 one another;
44 WHEREAS, some customers have been coming to A. Litteri for 80 years and still
45 remember first coming to the store with their grandparents;
46 WHEREAS, A. Litteri is often packed with patrons and filled with a lively, communal
47 atmosphere;
48 WHEREAS, A. Litteri brings together people from all walks of life and unites them
49 through their love of Italian food and drink.

50 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, that this
51 resolution may be cited as the “A. Litteri Inc. 100th Anniversary Recognition Resolution of
52 2026”.
53 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia recognizes and celebrates A Litteri Inc.
54 for its outstanding service to the District of Columbia and commends its lasting contributions to
55 the neighborhood and community it calls home.
56 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately upon the first date of publication in
57 the District of Columbia Register.
