16 To amend, on an emergency basis, the Green Building Act of 2006 to exempt certain District
17 owned and funded residential and nonresidential projects from complying with net-zero-
18 energy standards and to modify net-zero-energy standard compliance requirements for
19 nonresidential and residential projects; to amend section 2 of the Clean Energy DC Building
20 Code Amendment Act of 2022 to modify the definition of the net-zero-energy standard and
21 the types of projects to which the net-zero-energy standard applies; and for other purposes.
22
23 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this act
24 may be cited as the “Net Zero Continuity Emergency Amendment Act of 2026”.

111 (a) Subsection (a) is amended as follows:

113 “(2A) “Level 3 alteration” shall have the same meaning ascribed to it in the
114 Building Codes.”.
115 (2) Paragraph (3) is amended as follows:
116 (A) Subparagraph (A) is amended by striking the phrase “; and” and inserting a
117 semicolon in its place.

119 “(B) A building obtains energy from renewable energy sources in the amount
120 that would be required by the most recent version of Appendix Z;”.

122 “(C) Renewable energy shall be generated at the building site wherever
123 feasible; and”.

125 energy to the building except for backup power generation.”.
