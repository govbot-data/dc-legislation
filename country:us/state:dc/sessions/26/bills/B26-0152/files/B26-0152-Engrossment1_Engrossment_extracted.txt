12 To amend, on a temporary basis, the Second Chance Amendment Act of 2022 to clarify the
13 timeline for the automatic sealing of nonconvictions.
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
16 act may be cited as the “Second Chance Clarification Temporary Amendment Act of 2025”.
17 Sec. 2. Amended section 16-805(c)(1) within section 101(b) of the Second Chance
18 Amendment Act of 2022, effective March 10, 2023 (D.C. Law 24-284; 70 DCR 913), is
19 amended as follows:
20 (a) Subparagraph (A) is amended by striking the phrase “prior to the effective date of the
21 Second Chance Amendment Act of 2022, passed on 2nd reading on December 20, 2022
22 (Enrolled version of Bill 24-63)” and inserting the phrase “on or before September 30, 2027” in
23 its place.
24 (b) Subparagraph (B) is amended by striking the phrase “the effective date of the Second
25 Chance Amendment Act of 2022, on 2nd reading on December 20, 2022 (Enrolled version of
