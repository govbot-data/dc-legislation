ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-365
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
March 31, 2026
To confirm the reappointment of Anthony Washington to the Green Finance Authority Board of
Directors.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Green Finance Authority Board Anthony Washington
Confirmation Resolution of 2026”.
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia confirms the reappointment of:
Anthony Washington
17th Street, NW
Washington, DC 20001
(Ward 4)
as a member with experience in affordable housing or community development member of the
Green Finance Authority Board, established by section 203 of the Green Finance Authority
Establishment Act of 2018, effective August 22, 2018 (D.C. Law 22-155; D.C. Official Code §
8-173.21), for a term to end July 9, 2029.
Sec. 3. The Council shall transmit a copy of this resolution, upon its adoption, to the
nominee and to the Mayor.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
