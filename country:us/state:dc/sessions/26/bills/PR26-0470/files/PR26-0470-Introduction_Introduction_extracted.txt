Dr. Daniela Grigioni
Dr. Daniela Grigioni currently serves as the Executive Director for After-
School All-Stars DC. A physician by training, she worked in education
for over 25 years, starting as project manager for a comprehensive grant
that helped Latino parents support their children’s education, later
transitioning to an adult education charter school as the project manager,
and finally, joining DCPS central administration as the Manager of
External Relationships in the Out of School Time Office. In this role, she
worked with afterschool and summer school enrichment providers to
match need and availability between DCPS schools and hundreds of
providers.
Dr. Grigioni is a very passionate about serving our youth in an equitable
and just way that advance youth outcomes for all. She has become familiar with the needs of
families and communities in DC and, in response, has actively participated in promoting service
providers as partners of the school system. Her current work with After-School All-Stars fills a
specific niche of need, and it is informed by her past work in schools.
A Ward 3 resident, Dr. Grigioni earned her Doctor of Medicine from Catholic University of the
Sacred Heart in Rome, Italy.
