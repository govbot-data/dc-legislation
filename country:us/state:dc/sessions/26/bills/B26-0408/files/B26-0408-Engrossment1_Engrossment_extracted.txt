11 To amend Chapter 23 of Title 16 of the Official Code of the District of Columbia to require the
12 Department of Youth Rehabilitative Services to, after a scheduled predisposition meeting,
13 make a recurring effort to contact the child’s parent, guardian, or custodian and inform
14 them of the child’s progress on their individualized rehabilitation plan, and to require the
15 Department of Youth Rehabilitative Services to maintain an electronic record of its
16 attempts to contact the child’s parent, guardian, or custodian , which shall be made
17 available to the Chair of the Council committee with oversight of the Department of
18 Youth Rehabilitative Services.
19
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
21 act may be cited as the “Promoting Parental Engagement at DYRS Amendment Act of 2026”.
22 Sec. 2. Section 2319 of Title 16 of the District of Columbia Official Code is amended by
23 adding a new subsection (e-1) to read as follows:
24 “(e-1) Following the date of the scheduled predisposition meeting, the Department shall
25 make a recurring effort at least every 6 months to contact the child’s parent, guardian, or
26 custodian and inform them of the child’s progress on their individualized rehabilitation plan. The
27 Department shall keep an electronic log of all reasonable efforts made to contact the child’s
28 parent, guardian, or custodian and shall provide this electronic log to the Chair of the Council
