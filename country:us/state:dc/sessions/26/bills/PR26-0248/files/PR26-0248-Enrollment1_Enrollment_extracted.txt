ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-152
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
July 1, 2025
To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to approve Contract No.
CW123725 with Modaxo Traffic Management USA, Inc., and to authorize payment for
the goods and services received and to be received under the contract.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Contract No. CW123725 with Modaxo Traffic Management
USA, Inc. Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to approve Contract No. CW123725 with
Modaxo Traffic Management USA, Inc., and to authorize payment for the goods and services
received and to be received under the contract.
(b) On January 28, 2025, the Office of Contracting and Procurement, on behalf of the
Department of Motor Vehicles, executed emergency Contract No. CW123725 with Modaxo
Traffic Management USA, Inc., to provide ticket processing services for traffic violations for the
period of January 29, 2025, through May 29, 2025, in the not-to-exceed amount of
$6,002,005.94.
(c) Council approval is necessary because the value of the contract is more than $1
million during a 12-month period.
(d) Emergency legislation is necessary to allow the continuation of these vital services.
Without Council approval, Modaxo Traffic Management USA, Inc. cannot be paid for goods and
services provided in excess of $1 million for the period of January 29, 2025, through May 29,
2025.
Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the
Contract No. CW123725 with Modaxo Traffic Management USA, Inc. Approval and Payment
Authorization Emergency Act of 2025 be adopted after a single reading.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
