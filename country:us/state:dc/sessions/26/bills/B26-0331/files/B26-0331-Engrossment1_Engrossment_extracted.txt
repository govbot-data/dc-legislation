12 To officially designate the triangle park in Reservation 160 as Harmony Park.
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
15 act may be cited as the “Harmony Park Designation Act of 2026”.
16 Sec. 2. Pursuant to sections 401 and 422 of the Street and Alley Closing and Acquisition
17 Procedures Act of 1982, effective March 10, 1983 (D.C. Law 4-201; D.C. Official Code §§ 9-
18 204.01, and 9-204.22), the Council designates the triangle park in Reservation 160, as depicted
19 in the plat in the committee report, as “Harmony Park”.
20 Sec. 3. Applicability.
21 (a) This act shall apply upon the date of inclusion of its fiscal effect in an approved
22 budget and financial plan.
23 (b) The Chief Financial Officer shall certify the date of the inclusion of the fiscal effect in
24 an approved budget and financial plan, and provide notice to the Budget Director of the Council
25 of the certification.
26 (c)(1) The Budget Director shall cause the notice of the certification to be published in
27 the District of Columbia Register.
