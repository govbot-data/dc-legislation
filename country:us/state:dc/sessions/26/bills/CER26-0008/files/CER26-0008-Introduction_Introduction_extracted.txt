9 A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
10 __________
11
12
13 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
14
15 ____________________
16
17 To honor and recognize Coach David "Tee" Johnson for achieving his 100th win as head
18 coach of Jackson-Reed High School's boys' varsity basketball team—a milestone reached faster
19 than any coach in the school's history— and celebrate his exceptional leadership and unwavering
20 commitment to excellence.
21
22 WHEREAS, Coach David "Tee" Johnson has demonstrated exceptional leadership and
23 dedication as the head coach of the Jackson-Reed High School boys' varsity basketball team
24 since his appointment in August 2021;
25
26 WHEREAS, on January 15, 2025, Coach Johnson secured his 100th victory with a
27 commanding 90-11 win over Bell Multicultural High School, achieving this milestone in just
28 four seasons and setting a record as the fastest coach in Jackson-Reed athletics history to reach
29 100 wins;
30
31 WHEREAS, under Coach Johnson's guidance, the team has amassed an impressive
32 overall record of 101-22, clinched two District of Columbia Interscholastic Athletic Association
33 (DCIAA) championships, and maintained an outstanding home game performance with only two
34 losses;
35 WHEREAS, Coach Johnson's commitment to player development is evident, having
36 mentored 12 players who advanced to collegiate basketball programs, including institutions such
37 as Georgetown University, George Washington University, Texas A&M University, and Old
38 Dominion University;
39
40 WHEREAS, Coach Johnson's achievements have garnered regional recognition,
41 exemplified by his selection as the head coach for the Capital All-Stars in the All-American
42 Capital Classic District All-Stars game;
43

44 WHEREAS, under Coach Johnson’s leadership the Jackson-Reed basketball team
45 achieved national rankings, reaching as high as No. 22 in the Score Book Live/Sports Illustrated
46 Power 25 in 2024;
47
48 WHEREAS, beyond high school basketball, Johnson is also a coach for Team Durant, an
49 AAU powerhouse, contributing to youth basketball development;
50
51 WHEREAS, Coach Johnson’s is an alumnus of Gonzaga College High School and
52 Lyndon State College, reflecting his deep roots in the D.C. basketball scene;
53
54 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, this resolution
55 may be cited as the “Coach David "Tee" Johnson Recognition Resolution of 2025”.
56
57 The Council of the District of Columbia recognizes and honors the accomplishments of
58 the Coach David "Tee" Johnson and congratulates him on a great season.
59
60 This resolution shall take effect immediately upon the first date of publication in the
61 District of Columbia Register.
