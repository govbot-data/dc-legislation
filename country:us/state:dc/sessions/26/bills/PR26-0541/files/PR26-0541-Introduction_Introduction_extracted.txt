Ronnie Harris
Ms. Ronnie Harris is an account executive working with healthcare
systems and providers, managing a base of clients that exceeds millions
of dollars in revenue yearly.
Ms. Harris was invited to serve on the I Have a Dream Neighborhood
Civic Association during a time when lots of developments were
occurring in her neighborhood. Over a three-year period, she helped over
58 families transition from income-based housing to Section 8, and in
some successful cases, including herself, into a homeownership program.
A Ward 8 resident, Ms. Harris earned her Associate of Science Business
from the University of District of Columbia.
