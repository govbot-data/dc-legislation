Shawn Townsend
Mr. Shawn Townsend is the President and CEO of the Restaurant
Association of Metropolitan Washington (RAMW), leading the premier
advocacy and business resource organization for the region’s restaurant
industry. With a deep commitment to supporting operators, chefs, and
hospitality professionals, Shawn works to advance policies and initiatives
that foster growth, sustainability, and innovation within the industry.
Before joining RAMW, Mr. Townsend served as the Director of the
Mayor’s Office of Nightlife and Culture for Washington, D.C., where he
played a pivotal role in bridging the gap between local businesses,
government agencies, and community stakeholders. His leadership during
the COVID-19 pandemic was instrumental in developing relief programs
and reopening strategies for the city’s hospitality sector.
As RAMW’s leader, Mr. Townsend is focused on ensuring the restaurant industry remains a
vibrant and essential part of the D.C. metropolitan area, advocating for policies that promote
workforce development, operational sustainability, and equitable business practices.
A Ward 6 resident, Mr. Townsend earned his Bachelor of Arts in Journalism and Mass
Communications from New Mexico State University.
