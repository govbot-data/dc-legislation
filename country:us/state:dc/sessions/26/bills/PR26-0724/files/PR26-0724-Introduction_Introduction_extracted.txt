JOE “MR. WEFIT” HOUSTON JR.
PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY
Community wellness leader, entrepreneur, and advocate with over a decade of experience
developing health, reintegration, and community engagement initiatives across
Washington, DC. Founder and CEO of WeFitDC and Founder of We Fit University Inc.
(501c3), organizations dedicated to advancing health equity and expanding access to
wellness resources in underserved communities. Known for designing programs that
integrate physical health, behavioral change, and mentorship to support returning citizens,
youth development, and long-term community stability. Experienced in building
partnerships that connect wellness, workforce readiness, and prevention-focused public
health strategies.

Joe Houston, Jr.
Mr. Joe Houston, Jr. is a Washington, DC–based wellness entrepreneur,
community health advocate, and Founder & CEO of WeFitDC. With over
a decade of experience in fitness, behavioral change, and community
programming, he develops initiatives focused on improving health
outcomes, supporting returning citizens, and expanding access to
wellness resources in underserved communities. Through his nonprofit
organization, We Fit University Inc., he leads programs that integrate
physical activity, mentorship, and workforce development to promote
long-term stability and successful reintegration. His work centers on
using wellness as a tool for empowerment, prevention, and community
transformation across the District.
Mr. Houston is a Ward 8 resident.
