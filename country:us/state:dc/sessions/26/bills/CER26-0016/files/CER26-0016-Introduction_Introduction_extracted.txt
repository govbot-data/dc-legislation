5 A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
6 _______________
7
8 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
9 _______________
10
11 To declare the month of March 2025 as “Developmental Disabilities Awareness Month” in
12 the District of Columbia, and to recognize the important role that people with disabilities play in
13 shaping public policy, advocating for positive change, and enriching all our lives.
14
15 WHEREAS, people with developmental disabilities are our friends, family, and neighbors
16 in every Ward of the District of Columbia;
17
18 WHEREAS, we believe that inclusion of people with developmental disabilities in our
19 communities, workplaces, and schools makes us all better off;
20
21 WHEREAS, government agencies, community organizations, and individual and family
22 leaders work together to create accessible and high-quality education, housing, employment,
23 healthcare, recreational activities, and other community resources for people with developmental
24 disabilities;
25
26 WHEREAS, the District of Columbia is committed to creating more opportunities for
27 economic self-sufficiency, belonging, and creative innovation by ensuring that residents with
28 disabilities have meaningful employment opportunities;
29
30 WHEREAS, the 2025 observance of Developmental Disabilities Awareness Month
31 celebrates 39 years of advocacy in education, employment, housing, and community support; and
32
33 WHEREAS, this year’s theme, “We're Here All Year: Impacting Lives, Influencing
34 Change”, recognizes the important role that people with disabilities play in shaping public
35 policy, advocating for positive change, and enriching all our lives.
36
37 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
38 resolution may be cited as the “Developmental Disabilities Awareness Month Recognition
39 Resolution of 2025”.
40
41 Sec. 2. The Council declares the month of March 2025 as “Developmental Disabilities
42 Awareness Month” and recognizes the important role that people with disabilities play in
43 shaping public policy, advocating for positive change, and enriching all our lives.
44
45 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
