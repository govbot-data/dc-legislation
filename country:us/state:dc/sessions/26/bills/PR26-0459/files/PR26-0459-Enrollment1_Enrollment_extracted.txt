ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-351
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
March 3, 2026
To confirm the appointment of Patrick McAnaney to the Housing Production Trust Fund Board.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Housing Production Trust Fund Board Patrick McAnaney
Confirmation Resolution of 2026”.
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia confirms the appointment of:
Patrick McAnaney
Wisconsin Avenue, NW
Washington, DC 20016
(Ward 3)
as an individual with significant knowledge of an area related to the production, preservation,
and rehabilitation of affordable housing for lower-income households member of the Housing
Production Trust Fund Board, established by section 3a of the Housing Production Trust Fund
Act of 1988, effective June 8, 1990 (D.C. Law 8-133; D.C. Official Code § 42-2802.01), for a
term to end January 14, 2029.
Sec. 3. The Council shall transmit a copy of this resolution, upon its adoption, to the
nominee and to the Mayor.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
