16 To declare an emergency with respect to the need to close a cul-de-sac in Square 4350, abutting
17 Lot 6, and bounded by Douglas Street, N.E., in Ward 5.
18
19 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
20 resolution may be cited as the “Closing of a Cul-de-Sac in Square 4350, S.O. 22-05506,
21 Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
22 Sec. (a) There exists an immediate need to approve emergency legislation ordering the
23 closure of the Douglas Street, N.E., cul-de-sac in Square 4350, abutting Lot 6.
24 (b) The purpose of the closure and dedication is to support the Washington Metropolitan
25 Area Transit Authority’s (WMATA) Bladensburg Bus Garage Reconstruction Project, which
26 will expand the number of parking spots for Metro buses from 260 to 290, create infrastructure
27 to support WMATA’s Zero Emission Bus Transition Plan, and increase on-site employee
28 parking from 100 to 400.
29 (c) Permanent legislation (Bill 26-160) was approved by the Council at the October 21,
30 2025 Additional Legislative Meeting. Enacting emergency legislation prior to the completion of
31 the congressional review period is necessary to enable the project to proceed without risk of
32 further delay.

33 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
34 enumerated in Section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the
35 “Closing of a Cul-de-Sac in Square 4350, S.O. 22-05506, Emergency Act of 2025” be adopted
36 after a single reading.
37 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
