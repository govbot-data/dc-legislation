7 A PROPOSED RESOLUTION
8 _________________
9
10 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
11 _________________________
12
13 To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to amend section 25-
14 723 of the District of Columbia Code to allow the ABC Board to extend the hours
15 of operation, sales, service, and consumption of alcoholic beverages for cultural
16 events, sporting events, or tourism-related events for licensees registered with the
17 ABC Board.
18
19 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That
20 this resolution may be cited as the “On-Premises and On-Site Extended Hours Program
21 Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
22 Sec. 2. (a) Bill 26-0023, the On-Premises and On-Site Extended Hours Program
23 Amendment Act of 2025 would allow the Alcoholic Beverage and Cannabis Board
24 (“Board”) to extend the hours of operation, sales, service, and consumption of alcoholic
25 beverages for cultural events, sporting events, or tourism-related events for licensees
26 registered with the Board.
27 (b) On March 4, 2025, the Council approved B26-0023 on first reading. The
28 Council is scheduled to take its second and final vote on the legislation on April 1, 2025,
29 but before the measure takes effect it must complete Mayoral and congressional review,
30 which could take several months.
31 (c) Local businesses are preparing for increased patronage related to a spring
32 tourism event. To assist our local businesses in taking advantage of these economic
1

33 opportunities, it is imperative that legislation authorizing the Board to extend hours of
34 operation, sales, service, and consumption of alcoholic beverages for licensees take effect
35 immediately so that licensees have ample time to register with the Board to participate in
36 extended hours, the Board can provide notice to the Metropolitan Police Department of
37 participating establishments, and the District and businesses can plan in advance for
38 increased staffing and resources.
39 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2
40 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the On-Premises and On-
41 Site Extended Hours Program Emergency Amendment Act of 2025 be adopted after a
42 single reading.
43 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
