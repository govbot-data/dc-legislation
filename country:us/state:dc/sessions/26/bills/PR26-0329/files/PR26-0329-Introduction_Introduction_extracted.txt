The Honorable Phil Mendelson
Chairman
Council of the District of Columbia
1350 Pennsylvania Avenue NW, Suite 504

Dear Chairman Mendelson:
Pursuant to D.C. Official Code § 2-352.02, I have enclosed, for consideration by the Council of the District of
Columbia, the (a) Council Contract Summary for the Agreement to Enter into a Long-term Subsidy Contract
("ALTSC") between the District of Columbia Housing Authority (“DCHA”) and PBS Property Holdings, LLC, and (b)
proposed Local Rent Supplement Program Contract Number 2025-LRSP-04A with PBS Property Holdings, LLC
Approval Resolution of 2025. DCHA proposes to provide a housing subsidy to the Owner in the initial amount of
$255,522.00 annually for a multi-year term of twenty (20) years, in support of the District's Local Rent
Supplement Program ("LRSP") to provide eight (8) affordable housing units at the Kennedy Gardens Apartments
located at 145 Kennedy Avenue, NW .
As always, I am available to discuss any questions you may have regarding the proposed contract. In order to
facilitate a response to any questions concerning this ALTSC, please have your staff contact Hammere Gebreyes,
Director of External Affairs & Senior Advisor, at (202) 207-4694.
I look forward to your favorable consideration of this proposed ALTSC contract.
Sincerely,
Keith Pettigrew
Executive Director, District of Columbia Housing Authority

29 Sec. 2. (a) In 2007, the District passed Title II of the Fiscal Year 2007 Budget Support
30 Act of 2006 ("BSA") to provide funding for affordable housing for extremely low-income
31 households in the District. The passage of the BSA created the Local Rent Supplement Program
32 ("LRSP"), a program designed to provide affordable housing and supportive services to
33 extremely low-income District residents, including those who are homeless or in need of
34 supportive services, such as elderly individuals or those with disabilities, through project-based,
35 tenant-based, and sponsored-based LRSP affordable housing units. The BSA provided for the

3 (b) In 2019, the DCHA participated in a request for proposals issued by the District of
4 Columbia Department of Housing and Community Development. Of the multiple proposals
5 received, 11 developers were chosen to work with DCHA and other District agencies to develop
6 affordable housing and permanent supportive housing units for extremely low-income families
7 making zero to 30% of the area’s median income, as well as the chronically homeless and
8 individuals with mental or physical disabilities. Upon approval of the contract by the Council,
9 DCHA will enter into an agreement to enter into a long-term subsidy contract (“ALTSC”) with
10 the selected housing providers under the LRSP for housing services.
11 (c) There exists an immediate need to approve the long term subsidy contract with
12 Kennedy Gardens Apartments under the LRSP in order to provide long-term affordable housing
13 units for extremely low-income households for units located at 145 Kennedy Street, NW.
14 (d) The legislation to approve the contract will authorize an ALTSC between the District
15 of Columbia Housing Authority and PBS Property Holdings, LLC with respect to the payment of
16 a rental subsidy and allows the owner to lease the rehabilitated units at Kennedy Gardens
17 Apartments and house extremely low-income households with incomes at 30% or less of the area
18 median income.
