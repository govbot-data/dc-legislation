4 A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
5 ______________
6
7 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
8 _________________
9
10
11 To acknowledge and celebrate the 175th anniversary of the University of the District of
12 Columbia.
13
14 WHEREAS, The University of the District of Columbia (UDC), is District’s only public
15 university and the nation’s second-oldest historical black college and university (HBCU);
16
17 WHEREAS, UDC traces its origins to 1851, with the founding of the Miner Normal
18 School, established by Myrtilla Miner to train young Black women as teachers and expand
19 equitable access to education;
20
21 WHEREAS, From that foundation, the institution evolved significantly, and on August 1,
22 1977, through the consolidation of multiple predecessor schools including Federal City College,
23 Washington Technical Institute, and District of Columbia Teachers College, UDC took on the
24 form of the comprehensive public university we are familiar with today;
25
26 WHEREAS, UDC now offers more than 80 academic programs across six schools and
27 colleges, including a nationally recognized law school; a vibrant community college in the
28 Lamond-Riggs neighborhood, and workforce development programs in Congress Heights;
29
30 WHEREAS, UDC rose five places to #18 in the 2026 U.S. News & World Report HBCU
31 rankings, reflecting gains in academic quality, student success and institutional reputation. The
32 university was designated a Research College & University (RCU) in the Carnegie Classification,
33 a recognition that underscores the university’s growing research enterprise and expanding
34 investment in scholarship that addresses real-world challenges facing the District and beyond; and
35
36 WHEREAS, in 2026, UDC celebrates its 175th anniversary – with festivities throughout
37 the year designed to connect past, present and future Firebirds and engage the UDC community
38 and beyond in reflection, storytelling and forward-looking conversations about UDC’s impact and
39 its role in shaping the District’s future.
40
41 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
42 resolution may be cited as the “University of the District of Columbia 175th Anniversary
43 Ceremonial Recognition Resolution of 2026”.
44 Sec. 2. The Council acknowledges and celebrates the rich and vibrant history of the
45 University of the District of Columbia. The Council also commends the scores of the

46 administrators, students, faculty members and staff that have worked so diligently to create a
47 culture of academic excellence and community pride in and around the District’s only public
48 university.
49 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
