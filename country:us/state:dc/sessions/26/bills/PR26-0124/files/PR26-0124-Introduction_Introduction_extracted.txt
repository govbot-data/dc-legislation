emergency bill, the “Modification Nos. M0009, M0010, M0011, M0012, M0013, and M0014 to

21 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this

25 Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to approve Modification Nos. M0009,
26 M00l0, M00l 1, M0012, M0013, and M0014 to Contract No. CW101558 with Everyone Home
27 DC, and to authorize payment for the goods and services received and to be received under the
28 modifications.
29 (b) On October 1, 2024, via Modifications M0009, the Office of Contracting and
30 Procurement ("OCP"), on behalf of the Department of Human Services, partially exercised
31 option year two of Contract No. CW101558 with Everyone Home DC in the not-to-exceed
32 ("NTE") amount of $395,998.86 for the period of October 1, 2024, through December 31, 2024.
33 ( c) Modification No. MOO 10 was an administrative modification.

34 (d) On December 10, 2025, via Modification No. M0011, OCP reduced the NTE of
35 option year two by $16,200, from $395,998.86 to $379,798.86.
36 (e) One December 31, 2024, via Modification No. M0012, OCP partially exercised
37 option year two through March 31, 2025, and increased the NTE amount of option year two by
38 $379,798.86 from $379,798.86 to $759,597.72.
39 (f) On January 21, 2025, via Modification No. M0013, OCP partially exercised option
40 year two through April 30, 2025.
41 (g) Modification No. M0014 will exercise the remainder of option year two through
42 September 30, 2025 and increase the NTE amount by $759,597.72, from $759,597.72 to
43 $1,519,195.44.
44 (h) Under section 451(b)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
45 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 803; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.51(b)(1)), Council approval is
46 necessary since the modifications will increase the value of the contract to more than $1 million
47 during a 12-month period.
48 (i) Approval is necessary to allow the continuation of these vital services. Without this
49 approval, Everyone Home DC cannot be paid for goods and services provided in excess of $1
50 million for the period from October 1, 2024, through September 30, 2025.
51 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2 constitute
52 emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Modification Nos. M0009, M0010,
53 M0011, M0012, M0013, and M0014 to Contract No. CW101558 with Everyone Home DC
54 Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2025 be adopted after a single reading.
55 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
