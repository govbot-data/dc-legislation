16 To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to symbolically designating Alabama
17 Avenue, S.E. between Hartford Street, S.E. and Gainesville Street, S.E., as Rev. Dr. Judy
18 Talbert Way.
19
20 RESOLVED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
21 resolution may be cited as the “Rev. Dr. Judy Talbert Way Designation Emergency Declaration
22 Resolution of 2025”.
23 Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to symbolically designate Alabama Avenue,
24 S.E. between Hartford Street, S.E. and Gainesville Street, S.E., as Rev. Dr. Judy Talbert Way.
25 (b) Bill 26-280, the “Rev. Dr. Judy Talbert Way Designation Act of 2025” was adopted
26 on final reading by the Council on July 28, 2025.
27 (c) The underlying legislation will allow the District Department of Transportation to
28 work with the community to install signage prior to a community celebration of the life of Rev.
29 Dr. Judy Talbert, before the permanent legislation completes the 30-day congressional review
30 period.
31 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia finds that the circumstances enumerated
32 in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the “Rev. Dr. Judy
33 Talbert Way Designation Emergency Act of 2025” be adopted after a single reading.
34 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
