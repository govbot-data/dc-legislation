16 To declare the existence of an emergency, due to congressional review, with respect to the need
17 to amend section 215 of the Uniform College Athlete, Name, Image, or Likeness Act of 2022, to
18 permit an institution, conference, or athletic association to assist a college athlete in selecting,
19 arranging for, or providing payment to a name, image, or likeness agent and in selecting,
20 arranging for, or collecting payment from a third party engaged in specific name, image, or
21 likeness agreements with college athletes, and to remove the prohibition prohibiting institutions
22 or conferences from providing compensation to a college athlete for the athlete's name, image, or
23 likeness, and to amend section 5710 of Title 3 of the District of Columbia Municipal
24 Regulations to delay applicability of expanded financial disclosure requirements for certain
25 boards and commissions.
26
27 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
28 resolution may be cited as the “Uniform College Athlete Name, Image, or Likeness
29 Congressional Review Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
30 Sec. 2. (a) On May 6, 2025, the Council adopted the Uniform College Athlete Name,
31 Image, or Likeness Emergency Amendment Act of 2025, enacted May 19, 2025 (D.C. Act 26-
32 69; 72 DCR 6112), which expires on August 17, 2025.
33 (b) On June 3, 2025, the Council adopted the Uniform College Athlete Name, Image, or
34 Likeness Temporary Amendment Act of 2025, passed on 2nd reading on June 3, 2025 (enrolled
35 version of Bill 26-240) (“temporary act”), which has not completed congressional review.
36 (c) Identical emergency legislation is necessary to prevent a gap in the law between the
37 expiration of the emergency act and the effective date of the temporary act.

38 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2 constitute
39 emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Uniform College Athlete Name, Image, or
40 Likeness Congressional Review Emergency Amendment Act of 2025 be adopted after a single
41 reading.
42 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
