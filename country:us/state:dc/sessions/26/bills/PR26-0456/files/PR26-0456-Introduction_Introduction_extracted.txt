Rachel Gabauer
Ms. Rachel Gabauer is a clinical dietitian and nutrition expert with
extensive experience in both inpatient and outpatient settings. With a
focus on individualized nutrition care, Ms. Gabauer has worked with
diverse patient populations, including those with eating disorders,
gastrointestinal conditions, and chronic diseases. Currently serving as the
Clinical Director at Monte Nido, she leads a multidisciplinary team,
overseeing patient care, treatment planning, and staff development.
In addition to clinical work, Ms. Gabauer is a private practice dietitian at
Tepper Nutrition, where she provides personalized counseling on topics
like intuitive eating, chronic disease management, and healthy living. She
is also a passionate advocate for nutrition education and policy, with a history of presenting at
conferences and working collaboratively with healthcare professionals to improve patient
outcomes. Outside of work, she enjoys giving back to the community by providing nutrition advice
on a Fox 5 DC News segment as well as volunteering at a community food drive in Anacostia,
DC.
A Ward 6 resident, Ms. Gabauer earned her dual Bachelor of Science in Biology and Nutritional
Sciences from The Pennsylvania State University.
