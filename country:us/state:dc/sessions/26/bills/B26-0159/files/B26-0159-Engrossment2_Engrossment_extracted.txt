10 To amend the Prohibition of Discrimination in the Provision of Insurance Act of 1986 to prohibit
11 health benefit plans and health insurers from imposing cost sharing, prior authorization,
12 or medically unnecessary restrictions on at least one regimen of HIV prevention
13 medication treatment and services, from using prescription information for HIV
14 prevention treatment and services for medical underwriting for individual patients, and to
15 prohibit insurance companies from factoring the use of HIV prevention treatment in
16 decisions related to the issuance of disability, life, or long-term care insurance policies;
17 and to amend the Department of Health Functions Clarification Act of 2001 to establish a
18 non-lapsing DC Health and Wellness Center Fund to collect revenue from insurance and
19 out-of-pocket costs paid for services provided by the DC Health and Wellness Center and
20 affiliated clinics, and to require the funds be spent solely on the operations of the DC
21 Health and Wellness Center and affiliated clinics.
22
23 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
24 act may be cited as the “PrEP DC Amendment Act of 2025”.
