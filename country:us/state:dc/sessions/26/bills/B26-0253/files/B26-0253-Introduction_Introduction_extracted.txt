14 To amend the Small and Certified Business Enterprise Development and Assistance Act of 2005
15 to require a business applying for certification as a local business enterprise to be inde-
16 pendently owned and operated, to amend minimum certified business enterprise perfor-
17 mance requirements in a certified joint venture, to amend the requirements for a benefi-
18 ciary submitting a quarterly subcontracting report, to require a certified business enterprise
19 to report a material change to the Department of Small and Local Business Development, and
20 to establish uniform hearing procedures for enforcement actions.
21
22 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
23 act may be cited as “Certified Business Enterprise Program Protection Amendment Act of 2025”.
