ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
26-181
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
June 30, 2026
To recognize and honor Theresa DuBois, a Ward 4 District resident, for her over 40 years of
dedicated service to the District of Columbia.
WHEREAS, Ms. DuBois has devoted more than 4 decades of her professional life to
service in the District of Columbia, demonstrating an enduring commitment to engage, excite,
and entertain D.C. residents while creating economic and community benefits across the city;
WHEREAS, Theresa DuBois served the District government with distinction, including
as Director of Community Engagement for Events D.C. where she has made everyone who steps
into the Convention Center feel welcome and acted as an incredible ambassador for DC to
visitors from near and far;
WHEREAS, Theresa DuBois has had many admirable achievements during her time with
Events D.C. including the success of Washington DC's Auto Show, which brought in over
100,000 attendees annually, making it one of the top auto shows in the country;
WHEREAS, Theresa DuBois’s efforts to support the Community Grant’s Program
created opportunities for non-profits in the District to give opportunities and life-long learned
skills in artist and athletic endeavors; and
WHEREAS, Theresa DuBois has earned the respect of District leaders, civic
organizations, and community members for her accomplishments but also for her kindness and
care. Colleagues and residents value her for her commitment to the community and building
lasting connections with the neighborhood;
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the "Theresa DuBois Service Recognition Resolution of 2026".
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia recognizes and celebrates Theresa
DuBois for her outstanding service to the District of Columbia and commends Ms. DuBois for
