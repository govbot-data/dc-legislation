ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-428
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
June 30, 2026
To confirm the appointment of Dr. Jewell Reddick to the Board of Pharmacy.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Board of Pharmacy Dr. Jewell Reddick Confirmation Resolution
of 2026”.
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia confirms the appointment of:
Hon. Dr. Jewell Reddick
Hayes Street, NE
Washington, DC 20019
(Ward 7)
as a pharmacist licensed in the District member of the Board of Pharmacy, established by section
208 of the District of Columbia Health Occupations Revision Act of 1985, effective March 25,
1986 (D.C. Law 6-99; D.C. Official Code § 3-1202.08), for a term to end March 12, 2028.
Sec. 3. The Council shall transmit a copy of this resolution, upon its adoption, to the
nominee and to the Mayor.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
