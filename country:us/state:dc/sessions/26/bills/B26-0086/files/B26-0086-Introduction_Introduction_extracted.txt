15 To amend, on an emergency basis, due to congressional review, the Retail Incentive Act of 2004
16 to designate certain neighborhoods as Retail Priority Areas, including neighborhoods
17 within the Mount Vernon Triangle, Eastern Market, Barracks Row, and Georgia Avenue
18 areas.
19
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
21 act may be cited as the “Great Streets Program Expansion Neighborhood Retail Priority
22 Congressional Review Emergency Amendment Act of 2025”.
