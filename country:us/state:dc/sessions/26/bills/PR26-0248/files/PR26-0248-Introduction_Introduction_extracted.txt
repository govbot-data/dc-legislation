Dear Chairman Mendelson:
Enclosed for consideration and enactment by the Council of the District of Columbia is an
emergency bill, the “Contract No. CW123725 with Modaxo Traffic Management USA, Inc.
Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2025,” and the accompanying emergency
declaration resolution.
The legislation will approve Contract No. CW123725 with Modaxo Traffic Management USA,
Inc., to support the provision of ticket processing and adjudication services to support the District’s
enforcement of its parking and traffic laws.
My administration is available to discuss any questions you may have regarding the proposed
contract. In order to facilitate a response to any questions you may have, please have your staff
contact Marc Scott, Chief Operating Officer, Office of Contracting and Procurement, at (202) 724-
8759.
I urge the Council to take prompt and favorable action on the enclosed legislation.
Sincerely,
Muriel Bowser

17 To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to approve Contract No.
18 CW123725 with Modaxo Traffic Management USA, Inc., and to authorize payment for
19 the goods and services received and to be received under the contract.
20
21 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
22 resolution may be cited as the "Contract No. CW123725 with Modaxo Traffic Management
23 USA, Inc. Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025".
24 Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to approve Contract No. CW123725 with
25 Modaxo Traffic Management USA, Inc., and to authorize payment for the goods and services
26 received and to be received under the contract.
27 (b) On January 28, 2025, the Office of Contracting and Procurement, on behalf of the
28 Department of Motor Vehicles, executed emergency Contract No. CW123725 with Modaxo
29 Traffic Management USA, Inc., to provide ticket processing services for traffic violations for the
30 period January 29, 2025, through May 29, 2025, in the not-to-exceed amount of $6,002,005.94.
31 ( c) Under section 451 (b )( 1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
32 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 803; D.C. Official Code§ 1-204.51(b)(l)), Council approval is
33 necessary since the value of the contract is more than $1 million during a 12-month period.

34 (d) Emergency legislation is necessary to allow the continuation of these vital services.
35 Without Council approval, Modaxo Traffic Management USA, Inc. cannot be paid for goods and
36 services provided in excess of $1 million for the period January 29, 2025, through May 29, 2025.
37 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2 constitute
38 emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Contract No. CW123725 with Modaxo
39 Traffic Management USA, Inc. Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2025 be
40 adopted after a single reading.
41 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
