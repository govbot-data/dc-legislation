15 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA

19 To disapprove reprogramming request No. 26-0021 of $1,018,211.64 of FY 2025 Local School
20 funds within the District of Columbia Public Schools to cover administrative add-ons.
21
22 RESOLVED, BY COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this resolution
23 may be cited as the “No. 26-0021 District of Columbia Public Schools Local Funds
24 Reprogramming Request Disapproval Resolution”.
25 Sec. 2. (a) Pursuant to section 47-363 of the District of Columbia Official Code, the
26 Mayor transmitted to the Council reprogramming request No. 26-0021 in the amount of
27 $1,018,211.64 in FY 2025 Local funds within the District of Columbia Public Schools from
28 Schools to Schoolwide to cover costs associated with additional “administrative add-ons”.
29 (b) The Council disapproves the $1,018,211.64 reprogramming request.
30 Sec. 3. The Secretary of the Council of the District of Columbia shall transmit a copy of
31 this resolution, upon its adoption, to the Mayor.
32 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
