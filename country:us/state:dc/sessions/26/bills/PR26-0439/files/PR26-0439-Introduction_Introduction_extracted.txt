Dear Chairman Mendelson:
Enclosed for consideration and enactment by the Council of the District of Columbia is an
emergency bill, the “Modification No. 6 and Proposed Modification No. 7 to Contract No. DCAM-
22-CS-RFP-0019 with Smoot Construction Company of Washington, DC. Approval and Payment
Authorization Emergency Act of 2025,” and the accompanying emergency declaration resolution
in the amount not to exceed $61,462,436.00.
The legislation will approve Modification No. 6 and Proposed Modification No. 7 to a contract to
build out the District of Columbia Infrastructure Academy at the Spingarn High School building
and stabilize and make safe the unoccupied portion of the building, and will authorize payment to
Smoot Construction Company of Washington, DC. for services received and to be received under
the contract.
My administration is available to discuss any questions you may have regarding the proposed
contract modification. In order to facilitate a response to any questions you may have, please
contact Delano Hunter, Director, DGS, or have your staff contact Xavier Beltran, Interim Chief
Procurement Officer, DGS, at (202) 340-6358.
I urge the Council to take prompt and favorable action on the enclosed legislation.
Sincerely,
Muriel Bowser
