10 To amend The Grandparent Caregivers Pilot Programs Establishment Act of 2005 to increase the
11 rate of subsidy provided to grandparent and closes relative caregivers, and to annually
12 adjust the subsidy for inflation.
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
15 act may be cited as the “Increasing Support for Grandparent and Close Relative Caregivers
16 Amendment Act of 2025”.
17 Sec. 2. The Grandparent Caregivers Pilot Programs Establishment Act of 2005, effective
18 March 8, 2006 (D.C. Law 16-69; D.C. Official Code § 4-251.01 et seq.) is amended as follows:
19 (a) Section 103(d) (D.C. Official Code § 4-251.03(i)) is amended by striking the phrase
20 “for a period of not more than 60 months,”.
21 (b) Section 104(b) (D.C. Official Code § 4-251.04(b)) is amended by striking the
22 phrase “the Mayor shall establish by rule the amount of a subsidy a grandparent or
23 relative caregiver is eligible to receive under this subchapter, provided, that the subsidy
24 shall be at least 66%, but no more than 105%,” and inserting the phrase “the Mayor shall
25 establish by rule the amount of a subsidy a grandparent or relative caregiver is eligible to
