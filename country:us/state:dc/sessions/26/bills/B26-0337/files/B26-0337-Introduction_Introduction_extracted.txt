14 To officially designate, on an emergency basis, the public alley system within Square 6153, Lots
15 84 and 85, located at 3929-3931 4th Street, SE, close to the intersection 4th Street SE and
16 Atlantic Street, SE, as Congress Cove Court.
17
18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
19 act may be cited as the “Congress Cove Court Designation Emergency Act of 2025”.
20 Sec. 2. Pursuant to sections 401, 403, and 421 of the Street and Alley Closing and
21 Acquisition Procedures Act of 1982, effective March 10, 1983 (D.C. Law 4-201; D.C. Official
22 Code §§ 9-204.01, 9-204.03, and 9-204.21), the Council officially designates the public alley
23 system within Square 6153, bounded by 4th Street S.E. and Atlantic Street S.E., as depicted by
24 the plat in the committee report, as “Congress Cove Court”.
25 Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
26 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Budget Director as the fiscal impact
27 statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975, approved
28 October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
29 Sec. 4. Effective date.
30 This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by the
31 Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), and shall remain in effect for no longer than
32 90 days, as provided for emergency acts of the Council of the District of Columbia in section
33 412(a) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 788;
34 D.C. Official Code § 1-204.12(a)).
