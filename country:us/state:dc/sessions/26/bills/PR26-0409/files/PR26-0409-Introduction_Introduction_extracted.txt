14 To declare the existence of an emergency, due to congressional review, with respect to
15 the need to amend the District of Columbia Health Occupations Revision Act of
16 1985 and the Consent for Vaccination of Minors Amendment Act of 2022 to
17 authorize healthcare providers in the District to order and administer
18 immunizations recommended by competent medical and public health
19 organizations designated by the Director of the Department of Health.
20
21 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, that
22 this resolution may be cited as the “Community Health Congressional Review
23 Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
24 Sec. 2. (a) On September 17, 2025, the Council adopted the Community Health
25 Emergency Amendment Act of 2025, effective September 22, 2025 (D.C. Act 26-0149;
26 72 DCR 010314) (“emergency act”), which expires on December 21, 2025.
27 (b) The Community Health Temporary Amendment Act of 2025 was approved by
28 the Council on final reading at the October 7, 2025 legislative meeting. Because the
29 temporary measure must undergo congressional review, it will not be in effect before the
30 emergency measure expires.
31 (c) The congressional review emergency legislation is necessary to prevent a gap
32 in the law between the expiration of the emergency act and the effective date of the
33 temporary act.

1 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
2 enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the
3 Community Health Congressional Review Emergency Amendment Act of 2025 be
4 adopted after a single reading.
5 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
