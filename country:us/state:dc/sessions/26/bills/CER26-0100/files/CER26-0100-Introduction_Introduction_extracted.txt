20 A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
21
22 _________________________
23
24 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
25
26 _________________________
27
28
29 To celebrate the 50th anniversary of the Marine Corps Marathon, “The People’s Marathon,” and
30 the 40,000 active-duty service members, veterans, and civilians participating in the race.
31
32 WHEREAS, the Marine Corps Marathon was established in 1975 by Colonel James L.
33 Fowler in a memo to his supervisor, Major General Michael P. Ryan, outlining his idea of
34 creating a Marine Corps Reserve Marathon to promote goodwill between the military and the
35 post-Vietnam community;
36 WHEREAS, the inaugural running of the Marine Corps Marathon saw 1,175 participants
37 race a course through northern Virginia and finish at the Marine Corps War Memorial, becoming
38 the first of thousands of runners over a span of 50 years to take the final hill and finish at the Iwo
39 Jima memorial;
1

40 WHEREAS, the Marine Corps Marathon is one of the largest marathons in the U.S. and
41 the world and stands as the largest marathon in the world that doesn’t offer prize money, earning
42 its nickname, “The People’s Marathon”;
43 WHEREAS, the 50th anniversary race is an event of unprecedented size and scale with
44 over 40,000 participants registered to run the race, surpassing the size of the Boston marathon
45 and becoming the third largest marathon in North America this year;
46 WHEREAS, marathons are a test of character and determination and a journey of self-
47 discovery that is made all the more special through sharing the experience and struggle alongside
48 other participants;
49 WHEREAS, marathon running symbolizes the animating principals of what it means to
50 be a Marine, with no qualifying standards, celebrating the commitment, community, and the
51 perseverance of the human spirit; and
52 WHEREAS, the People's Marathon honors the U.S. Marine Corps’ values of honor,
53 courage, and commitment, bringing together active-duty service members, veterans, and civilians
54 in a shared pursuit of physical and mental endurance, while witnessing the beauty of the National
55 Mall and the Marine Corps War Memorial.
56 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
57 resolution may be cited as the "Marine Corps Marathon 50th Anniversary Recognition
58 Resolution of 2025".
59 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia recognizes and celebrates the Marine
60 Corps Marathon event organizers, active-duty service members, veterans, and civilians that are
61 participating in the 50th anniversary race.
62 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately upon the first date of publication in
63 the District of Columbia Register.
