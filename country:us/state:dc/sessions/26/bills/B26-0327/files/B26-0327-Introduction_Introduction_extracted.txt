12 To amend the method for calculating individual school budgets within the District of Columbia
13 Public Schools System by adding average position cost as a factor.
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
15 act may be cited as the “Schools First Amendment Act of 2025”.
16 Sec. 2. The Schools First in Budgeting Amendment Act of 2022, effective March 10,
17 2023 (D.C Law 24-300; D.C. Official Code § 38-2851.01 et seq.), is amended as follows:
18 (a) Section 102 (D.C. Official Code § 38-2851.01) is amended as follows:
19 (1) Paragraph (1A) is redesignated as paragraph (1B)
20 (2) A new paragraph (1A) is added to read as follows:
21 “(1A) “Average position cost for educators” means the cost, including salary, of a
22 school-based educator that DCPS uses to calculate each school’s annual budget for the next
23 fiscal year.”.
24 (b) Section 104(a) (D.C. Official Code § 38-2851.03(a)), is amended as follows:
25 (1) A new paragraph (5A) is added to read as follows:
26 “(5A) Increase the amount derived pursuant to paragraph (5) of this subsection by
27 the projected increase over the previous fiscal year in the average position cost for Washington
28 Teacher Union members.”.
29 (2) Paragraph (6) is amended by striking the phrase “paragraph (5)” and inserting
30 the phrase “paragraph (5A)” in its place.

31 Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
32 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement in the committee report as the fiscal
33 impact statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975,
34 approved October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code§ 1-301.47a).
35 Sec. 4. Effective date.
36 This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by the
37 Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto) and a 30-day period of congressional review
38 as provided in section 602(c)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December
39 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 813; D.C. Official Code § 1-206.02(c)(1)).
