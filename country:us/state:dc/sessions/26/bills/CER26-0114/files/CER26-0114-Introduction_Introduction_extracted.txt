12 A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
13 ____________
14 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
15 _____________________________
16
17 To celebrate the official grand opening of the Washington School for Girls' campus expansion at
18 the Town Hall Education Arts Recreation Campus as part of the school's "Building
19 Dreams Campaign".
20 WHEREAS, the Washington School for Girls (WSG) is an independent, tuition-free
21 Catholic day school serving girls in grades 3 through 8, primarily from historically underserved
22 communities in southeast D.C.;
23 WHEREAS, the WSG was founded in 1998 through a collaboration between the Society
24 of the Holy Child Jesus, the Religious of Jesus and Mary, and the National Council of Negro
25 Women;
26 WHEREAS, the WSG's mission is to close educational gaps by providing a rigorous,
27 culturally responsive academic program alongside holistic support for social, emotional, and
28 spiritual growth;
29 WHEREAS, the WSG emphasizes leadership, service, and faith-filled lives, with a
30 student-teacher ratio of about 9:1 and a focus on literacy, STEM, arts, and character
31 development;
32 WHEREAS, the WSG has served over 300 students to date, boasting at 99% high school
33 graduation rate for alumnae through its Graduate Support Program;
34 WHEREAS, the WSG operates as an anchor institution at the Town Hall Education Arts
35 Recreation Campus (THEARC), a 16-acre nonprofit hub in Anacostia that integrates education,
36 arts, health, and recreation services to revitalize the community;
37 WHEREAS, the WSG is holding a Ribbon Cutting Ceremony on December 9, 2025,
38 marking the opening of a $25 million campus expansion at THEARC that consists of a 33,000-
39 square-foot, three-story facility designed to dramatically enhance educational opportunities for
40 its students;

41 WHEREAS, ground was broken on this project on May 1, 2024, as part of the school's
42 "Building Dreams Campaign", a joint fundraising effort with partner organization Building
43 Bridges Across the River;
44 WHEREAS, key features of the new building include: 14 spacious classrooms for
45 expanded enrollment, a multi-purpose cafeteria/auditorium for assemblies and meals, an
46 expansive library/media lab and a dedicated science lab, a makerspace/STEM lab to foster
47 innovation, a flexible art/music/dance studio, and outdoor access to green space and THEARC
48 Farm for experiential learning;
49 WHEREAS, this project highlights the school's growth from its original modest space at
50 THEARC to a state-of-the-art anchor for girls' education; and
51 WHEREAS, this ribbon cutting is a milestone not just for the WSG, but for broader
52 efforts in educational equity, community development, and empowerment of Black and low-
53 income girls in the District of Columbia;
54 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
55 resolution may be cited as the "Washington School for Girls Expansion Recognition Resolution
56 of 2025".
57 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia celebrates the campus expansion at the
58 Town Hall Education Arts Recreation Campus and the educational opportunities it represents for
59 the students at the Washington School for Girls.
60 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately upon the first date of publication in
61 the District of Columbia Register.
