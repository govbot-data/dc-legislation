10 To amend, on a temporary basis, the District of Columbia Rental Housing Act of 1985 to clarify
11 that any rental unit participating in the Low Income Housing Tax Credit program are
12 exempt from the Rent Stabilization Program.
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
15 act may be cited as the “LIHTC Rent Stabilization Exemption Clarification Temporary
16 Amendment Act of 2025”.
17 Sec. 2. Section 205(a)(1) of the District of Columbia Rental Housing Act of 1985, effective
18 July 17, 1985, (D.C. Law 6-10; D.C. Official Code §42-3502.05(a)(1)), is amended by striking the
19 phrase “as defined in section 206(f)(2)(A);” and inserting the phrase “as defined in section
20 206(f)(2)(A), or any rental unit for which the housing provider receives tax credits pursuant to the
21 Low-Income Housing Tax Credit Program as defined by section 2(5) of the Low-Income Housing
22 Tax Credit Fund Act of 2004, effective April 8, 2005, (D.C. Law 15-299; D.C. Official Code § 42-
23 2853.01(5));”.
24 Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.

26 statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975, approved
