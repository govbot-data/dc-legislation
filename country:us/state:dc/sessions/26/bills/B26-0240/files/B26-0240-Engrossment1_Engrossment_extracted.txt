12 To amend, on a temporary basis, section 215 of the Uniform College Athlete, Name, Image, or
13 Likeness Act of 2022 to permit an institution, conference, or athletic association to assist
14 a college athlete in selecting, arranging for, or providing payment to a name, image, or
15 likeness agent and in selecting, arranging for, or collecting payment from a third party
16 engaged in specific name, image, or likeness agreements with college athletes, and to
17 remove the prohibition prohibiting institutions or conferences from providing
18 compensation to a college athlete for the athlete's name, image, or likeness and to pause
19 applicability of expanded financial disclosure requirements for certain boards and
20 commissions.
21
22 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
23 act may be cited as the "Uniform College Athlete Name, Image, or Likeness Temporary
24 Amendment Act of 2025."
