11 To amend the District of Columbia Rental Housing Act of 1985 to clarify that any rental unit
12 participating in the Low Income Housing Tax Credit program are exempt from the Rent
13 Stabilization Program.
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
16 act may be cited as the “LIHTC Rent Stabilization Exemption Clarification Amendment Act of
17 2025”.
18 Sec. 2. Section 205(a)(1) of the District of Columbia Rental Housing Act of 1985, effective
19 July 17, 1985, (D.C. Law 6-10; D.C. Official Code §42-3502.05(a)(1)), is amended by striking the
20 phrase “as defined in section 206(f)(2)(A);” and inserting the phrase “as defined in section
21 206(f)(2)(A), or any rental unit for which the housing provider receives tax credits pursuant to the
22 Low Income Housing Tax Credit Program as defined by section 2(5) of the Low-Income Housing
23 Tax Credit Fund Act of 2004, effective April 8, 2005, (D.C. Law 15-299; D.C. Official Code § 42-
24 2853.01(5));” in its place.
25 Sec 3. Applicability
26 Section 2 shall apply as of July 17th, 1985.
