In accordance with section 2 of the Confirmation Act of 1978, effective March 3, 1979 (D.C. Law 2-
142; D.C. Official Code § 1-523.01), and pursuant to section 4 of the Maternal Mortality Review
Committee Establishment Act of 2018, effective June 5, 2018 (D.C. Law 22-111; D.C. Official Code
§ 7-671.03), I am pleased to nominate the following individual:
Dr. Monique Powell-Davis
Madison Street, NW
Washington, D.C 20011
(Ward 4)
for reappointment as a person with experience in obstetrics and gynecology from a District of Columbia
hospital or birthing center member of the Maternal Mortality Review Committee, for a term to end
January 10, 2028.
Enclosed, you will find biographical information detailing the experience of the above-mentioned
nominee, together with a proposed resolution to assist the Council during the confirmation process.
I would appreciate the Council’s earliest consideration of this nomination for confirmation. Please
do not hesitate to contact me, or Steven Walker, Director, Mayor’s Office of Talent and
Appointments, should the Council require additional information.
Sincerely,
Muriel Bowser

Dr. Monique Powell-Davis
Dr. Monique Powell-Davis currently serves as the Executive Vice
President of Medical Services at Mary’s Center. At this federally
qualified health center, she provides full scope outpatient care,
Dr. Powell-Davis is an active member of the American Medical
Association and possesses certification and licensure in the District of
Columbia. She has been cited in medical publications, including
“Avoiding Common Obstetrics and Gynecology Errors” in 2011.
A Ward 4 resident, Dr. Powell-Davis earned her Bachelor of Science in
Biology from the University of the Virgin Islands and her Doctor of
Medicine from Howard University.
