Gloria Dunbar
Ms. Gloria Dunbar is a senior transformation and governance leader with
deep experience overseeing complex, highly regulated initiatives across
healthcare, cloud computing, and global enterprise environments. She
brings a strong governance mindset shaped by leading large-scale
programs involving regulatory compliance, risk management, data
integrity, and cross functional accountability at organizations including
Amazon, F5 Networks, and major healthcare systems.
With a career spanning public sector, healthcare, and technology, Ms.
Dunbar has partnered closely with executive leadership and boards to
align strategy with execution, monitor performance against key metrics,
and ensure transparency in decision making. Her experience includes
managing multimillion dollar portfolios, establishing program management and reporting
mechanisms, and guiding organizations through digital transformation, mergers and integrations,
and evolving regulatory landscapes.
A Ward 6 resident, Ms. Dunbar earned her Bachelor of Arts in English from the University of
South Carolina.
