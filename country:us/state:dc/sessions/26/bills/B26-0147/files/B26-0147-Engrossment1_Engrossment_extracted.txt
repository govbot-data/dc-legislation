11 To amend Title 25 of the District of Columbia Official Code to allow a retailer with commercial
12 street frontage at a location under the jurisdiction of the Washington Convention and
13 Sports Authority to be licensed for a streatery; to require that alcohol businesses obtain a
14 streatery endorsement from the Alcoholic Beverage and Cannabis Board to operate a
15 streatery; to require streatery, summer garden, and sidewalk café endorsement and
16 streatery license applications to undergo a 45-day public comment period; to expand
17 existing carry out and delivery license requirements to retailers with commercial street
18 frontage at a location under the jurisdiction of the Washington Convention and Sports
19 Authority; and to expressly require notice and an opportunity to protest applications for
20 streatery, summer garden, and sidewalk café endorsements and licenses.
21
22 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
23 act may be cited as the “Streatery Program Endorsements and Protest Process Amendment Act of
24 2025”.
