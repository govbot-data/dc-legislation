Ms. Robyn Wells
Branch Avenue, SE

(Ward 7)

nominee, together with a proposed resolution to assist the Council during the confirmation process.

Appointments, should the Council require additional information.

Summary Statement
Emerging leader in the transportation industry with over ten years of experience in planning, design, and
construction of high-profile, complex infrastructure projects. Demonstrated ability to manage large-scale capital
improvement projects by leading multi-disciplinary teams, engaging stakeholders, and ensuring project delivery
within scope, schedule, and budget. Expertise in project management, federal procurement processes, and
effective communication with a wide cross section of stakeholders.
Summary of Experience
● Licensed Professional Engineer (PE) and Project Management Professional (PMP).
● 10+ years of progressive program management experience in construction, design, and planning.
● Leads high performing teams of civil engineers, consultants, and contractors.
● Experienced in communication to various stakeholders including, executive staff, and elected
officials
