Dr. Tyris Ford
Dr. Tyris Ford is a board-certified Doctor ofNursing Practice (DNP) and
Nurse Practitioner specializing in holistic primary care, sexual health,
HIV care and prevention, mental health integration, and wellness
coaching. He currently services as the Founder, Medical Director and a
PrimaryCare Nurse Practitioner with TrustN theJourney Wellness.
With over eight years of nursing experience, Dr. Ford has worked across
various settings, including critical care, HIV primary care, ambulatory
care, community health, long-term care, and nursing leadership. His
expertise extends to roles as a nurse educator, advanced practice nurse
manager, and directorofnursing, As a nurseentrepreneur and consultant,
he collaborates with mental health organizations, public health agencies,
andcommunity-based initiatives to promote health equity and integrative wellness.
A Ward 7 resident, Dr. Ford eared both his Bachelor of Science in Nursing and his Doctor of
Nursing Practice from Marian University.
