Dr. Cheryl Williams
Dr. Cheryl Williams is a semi-retired pediatrician. She has aided the
citizens of the District of Columbia for more than 23 years as a physician
in the public health care system. Dr. Williams also served as a Medical
Officer and Supervisory Medical Officer for the District of Columbia
Community and School Health Program and worked as Medical
Director of the Child and Family Services Agency (CFSA). In this
capacity, she provided executive leadership, strategic planning, and
organizational development in administrative and clinical oversight in
all matters related to the well-being of children, including physical and
mental health and ancillary services.
Dr. Williams played a role in creating CFSA’s on-site Medicaid-reimbursable medical clinic,
which is staffed with nurse practitioners, for children entering, re-entering, and who experience a
re-placement while in foster care.
A Ward 7 resident, Dr. Williams earned her Bachelor of Science in Zoology from Howard
University and her Doctor of Medicine from Case Western Reserve University.
