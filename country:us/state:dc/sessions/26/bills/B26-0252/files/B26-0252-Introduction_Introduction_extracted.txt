14 To amend Title 47 of the DC Official Code to provide extended assessment notice and first level
15 appeal dates for large-valued real properties so that their final valuations may take into
16 account the prior year’s income and expense information, to revise which real properties
17 are required to file income and expense information with the Office of Tax and Revenue,
18 to require that large-valued real property owners file income and expense information
19 electronically when directed by the Office of Tax and Revenue, to allow the Office of
20 Tax and Revenue to send electronic appeal responses to the property owner, and to
21 provide technical and conforming clarifications.
22 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
23 act may be cited as the “Real Property Assessment and Appeals Schedule Revision Act of 2025”.
