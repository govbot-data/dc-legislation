16 To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to officially designating the public alley
17 system within Square 6153, bounded by 4th Street S.E. and Atlantic Street S.E. as
18 Congress Cove Court.
19
20 RESOLVED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
21 resolution may be cited as the “Congress Cove Court Designation Emergency Declaration
22 Resolution of 2025”.
23 Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to officially designate the public alley system
24 within Square 6153, bounded by 4th Street S.E. and Atlantic Street S.E. as Congress Cove Court.
25 (b) Bill 26-120, the “Congress Cove Court Designation Act of 2025” was adopted on
26 final reading by the Council on July 28, 2025.
27 (c) The underlying legislation will allow the applicant who owns two lots fronting the
28 alley to obtain District agency approvals for development of several residential units, including
29 permits and utility location, before the permanent legislation completes the 30-day congressional
30 review period.
31 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia finds that the circumstances enumerated
32 in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the “Congress Cove
33 Court Designation Emergency Act of 2025” be adopted after a single reading.
34 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
