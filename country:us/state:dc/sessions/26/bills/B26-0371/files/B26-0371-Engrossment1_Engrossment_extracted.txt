11 To order the closing of a portion of the public alley system in Square 2319, and to extend the

15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
16 act may be cited as the “Closing of a Portion of a Public Alley and Establishment of a Building
17 Restriction Line in Square 2319, S.O. 22-02364, Act of 2026”.
18 Sec. 2. (a) Pursuant to section 404 of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
19 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 787; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.04), and consistent with the Street
20 and Alley Closing Acquisition Procedures Act of 1982, effective March 10, 1983 (D.C. Law
21 4-201; D.C. Official Code § 9-201.01 et seq.), the Council finds that the portion of the public
22 alley system in Square 2319, as shown on the Surveyor’s plat filed under S.O. 22-02364, is
23 unnecessary for alley purposes and orders it closed with title to the land to vest as shown on the
24 Surveyor’s plat.
25 (b) The existing building restriction line in Lot 77 of Square 2319 shall be extended over
26 the portion of the alley closed pursuant to subsection (a) of this section, as shown on the
27 Surveyor’s plat in the official file for S.O. 22-02364.
