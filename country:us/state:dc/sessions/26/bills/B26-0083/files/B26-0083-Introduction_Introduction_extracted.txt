15 To amend, on an emergency basis, due to congressional review, the Retail Electric Competition
16 and Consumer Protection Act to clarify the definition of the term subscriber organization;
17 to amend the Lead-Hazard Prevention and Elimination Act of 2008 to clarify the definition
18 of the term lead-based paint; and to amend the Sustainable Solid Waste Management
19 Amendment Act of 2014 to clarify the definition of the term producer.
20
21 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
22 act may be cited as the “Department of Energy and Environment Definitions Clarification
23 Congressional Review Amendment Act of 2025”.
24 Sec. 2. Section 101(27A) of the Retail Electric Competition and Consumer Protection Act
25 of 1999, effective May 9, 2000 (D.C. Law 13-107; D.C. Official Code § 34-1501(27A)), is
26 amended by striking the phrase “Columbia law that owns” and inserting the phrase “Columbia
27 law, or government entity, that owns” in its place.
28 Sec. 3. Section 2(20) of the Lead-Hazard Prevention and Elimination Act of 2008, effective
29 March 31, 2009 (D.C. Law 17-381; D.C. Official Code § 8-231.01(20)), is amended by striking
30 the phrase “(0.7 µg/cm²)” and inserting the phrase “(0.7 mg/cm²)” in its place.
31 Sec. 4. Section 128(11)(A) of the Sustainable Solid Waste Management Amendment Act
32 of 2014, effective March 16, 2021 (D.C. Law 23-211; D.C. Official Code § 8-771.01(11)(A)), is

33 amended by striking the phrase “who manufacturers a” and inserting the phrase “who
34 manufactures a” in its place.
35 Sec. 5. Applicability.
36 (a) Section 2 shall apply as of December 13, 2013.
37 (b) Section 3 shall apply as of March 16, 2021.
38 (c) Section 4 shall apply as of March 16, 2021.
39 Sec. 6. Fiscal impact statement. The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the
40 Budget Director as the fiscal impact statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative
41 Procedures Act of 1975, approved October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § l-
42 301.47a).

47 of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 788; D.C.
48 Official Code § 1-204.12(a)).
