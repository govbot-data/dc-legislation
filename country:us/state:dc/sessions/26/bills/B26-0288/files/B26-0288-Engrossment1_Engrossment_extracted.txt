113 Sec. 3. Findings.
114 (a) The RFK Campus consists of approximately 180 acres of waterfront property located
115 in the District within Reservation 343F, as shown on the transfer of jurisdiction plat recorded in
116 the Surveyor’s Office on February 5, 2025, on page 13 of subdivision book 223. The District has
117 been unable to redevelop the site over the last decade due to federal restrictions on development
118 at the site, and the site has remained vacant, blighted, and fallow. Over the course of the last
119 decade, the District has worked to gain broader control of the site and to remove the federal
120 development restrictions so that a new, multi-use future for the area could be realized.
121 (b) With bipartisan support from Congress, the President signed the D.C. Robert F.
122 Kennedy Memorial Stadium Campus Revitalization Act, approved on January 6, 2025 (Pub. L.
123 No. 118-274; 138 Stat. 3234), into law. Pursuant to this law, the National Park Service
124 transferred administrative jurisdiction over the RFK Campus to the District. The Council
125 approved the transfer of jurisdiction pursuant to the Transfer of Jurisdiction Over the Robert F.
126 Kennedy Memorial Stadium Campus Emergency Approval Resolution of 2025, effective
127 February 4, 2025 (Res. 26-16; 72 DCR 1436).
7

ENGROSSED ORIGINAL
128 (c) The District now has a once-in-a-generation opportunity to continue to revitalize the
129 Anacostia Waterfront, catalyze growth, and develop a vibrant, mixed-use development featuring
130 community sports, professional sports, public recreation, public park space, and cultural
131 amenities. The development will provide the District with significant economic, cultural, social,
132 and financial benefits.
133 (d) Since gaining administrative jurisdiction over the RFK Campus, the District
134 government has undertaken a comprehensive public engagement process, considering the
135 specific interests of communities near the RFK Campus; economic development opportunities;
136 the capacity of the campus to generate jobs for District residents and opportunities for District
137 businesses; park, open space, and recreational options; river access options; potential community
138 amenities; opportunities to create new housing units; and environmental and historical factors,
139 among other considerations, to determine the best uses for the site.
140 (e) Considering the public input received, the existing infrastructure of the campus, and
141 the possibility of a catalytic investment in the District, it was determined that the construction
142 and operation of a new first-class, state-of-the-art, sports stadium with supporting facilities,
143 including parking, and infrastructure, for use by the professional football franchise owned by
144 Pro-Football LLC, and an adjacent Sportsplex would be of considerable public benefit to the
145 District as the first stage of the redevelopment and revitalization of the entire campus.
8

ENGROSSED ORIGINAL
146 (f) To ensure delivery of the Stadium, supporting facilities, and Sportsplex by 2030, a
147 limited zoning exemption is necessary for portions of the RFK Campus.
148 (g) Planning for other residential and commercial development at the RFK Campus, in
149 addition to other uses permitted by the transfer of jurisdiction and the Declaration of Covenants,
150 will continue as the Stadium and Sportsplex projects move forward, and will inform the
151 Comprehensive Plan rewrite (DC 2050).
152 (h) The District intends for the comprehensive redevelopment of the RFK Campus to
153 create a vibrant, multi-use community and destination that will create new jobs, business
154 opportunities, housing, community amenities, and neighborhood-serving retail and achieve the 5
155 Anacostia Waterfront Framework Plan themes: (1) a clean and active river, (2) gaining access to,
156 along, and across the river, (3) a great riverfront park system, (4) a riverfront of distinct places
157 and cultural destinations, and (5) building and sustaining strong waterfront neighborhoods.
158 (i) The District and Developer have entered into the Term Sheet for the development of
159 the Stadium and supporting facilities and the redevelopment of a portion of the RFK Campus
160 that sets forth the terms and conditions upon which the parties are willing to undertake the
161 projects.
162 (j) The Commanders have made additional commitments regarding the redevelopment of
163 the RFK Campus in a July 29, 2025, letter to the Council.
