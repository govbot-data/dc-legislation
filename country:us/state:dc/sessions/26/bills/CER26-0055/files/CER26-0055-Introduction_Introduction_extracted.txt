9
10 _________________________

12 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA

14 _________________________
15
16
17 To recognize honoree Reggie Van Lee during the 2025 celebration of DC Black Pride.
18
19 WHEREAS, Reggie Van Lee is an acclaimed business strategist, philanthropist, and arts
20 patron who currently serves as Executive Partner at AlixPartners;
21
22 WHEREAS, with a long career spanning leadership roles at The Carlyle Group and Booz
23 Allen Hamilton, Reggie Van Lee is known for guiding major institutions through transformative
24 growth;
25
26 WHEREAS, Reggie Van Lee chairs numerous cultural and civic organizations, including
27 the D.C. Commission on the Arts and Humanities and the National CARES Mentoring
28 Movement;
29
30 WHEREAS, Reggie Van Lee was named Black Engineer of the Year and one of the top
31 25 consultants globally, and holds degrees from MIT and Harvard;
32
33 WHEREAS, Reggie Van Lee’s work unites corporate innovation with deep community
34 impact;
35
36 WHEREAS, DC Black Pride is an annual event to increase awareness of and showcase
37 pride in the diversity of the LGBTQIA+ populations in the African American community, and to
38 support organizations that focus on health disparities, education, youth, and families;
39
40 WHEREAS, as the very first Black Pride festival in the world, DC Black Pride fostered
41 the beginning of the Center for Black Equity (formerly known as the International Federation of
42 Black Prides, Inc. (IFBP)) and the “Black Pride Movement”;
43
1

44 WHEREAS, since the birth of DC Black Pride, more than 50 other Black Pride
45 celebrations now take place throughout the world and on 4 continents, with many using DC
46 Black Pride as a model;
47
48 WHEREAS, May 23, 2025, through May 26, 2025, marks the 34th annual DC Black
49 Pride celebration;
50
51 WHEREAS, the theme for this year’s celebration is “BLACK PRIDE IS FREEDOM”;
52
53 WHEREAS, for more than 30 years, DC Black Pride has been an opportunity for the
54 Black LGBTQIA+ community to gather and celebrate being both African American and
55 members of LGBTQIA+ communities;
56
57 WHEREAS, DC Black Pride is the oldest and one of the largest Black Pride events in the
58 world, drawing thousands of residents and visitors from around the globe; and
59
60 WHEREAS, DC Black Pride brings more than 500,000 people to the nation’s capital
61 from across the United States, Canada, the Caribbean, South Africa, Great Britain, France,
62 Germany, and the Netherlands and will carry that tradition this year and into the future to
63 celebrate their shared community, raise awareness and funding for HIV/AIDS prevention, and
64 stand against homophobia inspired by violence and bigotry.
65
66 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That
67 this resolution be cited as the “Reggie Van Lee Recognition Resolution of 2025”.
68
69 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia recognizes and celebrates Reggie Van
70 Lee during the 2025 celebration of DC Black Pride.
71
72 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
