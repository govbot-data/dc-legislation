12 To amend, on an emergency basis, Title 25 of the District of Columbia Official Code to
13 allow a retailer with commercial street frontage at a location under the jurisdiction
14 of the Washington Convention and Sports Authority to be licensed for a streatery;
15 to require that alcohol businesses obtain a streatery endorsement from the
16 Alcoholic Beverage and Cannabis Board to operate a streatery; to require
17 streatery, summer garden, and sidewalk café endorsement and streatery license
18 applications to undergo a 45-day public comment period; to expand existing carry
19 out and delivery license requirements to retailers with commercial street frontage
20 at a location under the jurisdiction of the Washington Convention and Sports
21 Authority; and to expressly require notice and an opportunity to protest
22 applications for streatery, summer garden, and sidewalk café endorsements and
23 licenses.
24
25 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA,
26 That this act may be cited as the “Streatery Program Endorsements and Protest Process
27 Emergency Amendment Act of 2025”.

29

31
