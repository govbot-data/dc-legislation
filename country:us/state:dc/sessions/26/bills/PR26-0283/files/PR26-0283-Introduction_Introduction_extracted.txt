Cheryle Doggett
Ms, Cheryle Doggett, a current memberof the Washington Convention
and Sports Authority Board of Directors, is the President and Treasurer
ofDoggett Enterprises, Inc.
Ms. Doggett works to oversee the parent corporation for Doggett’s
parking company, Doggett Enterprises, which was established in 1926 as
the first parking company in Washington, DC. She is Chairman Emeritus
ofthe Board for HEROES, Inc.,a non-profitorganization dedicatedto the
welfare of families offirefighters and law enforcement officers whogave
their lives in the lineofduty in metropolitan Washington, DC.
Ms. Doggett is a former member of the Executive Committee and the
Board ofDirectors ofthe Federal City Council and current memberof the District ofColumbia
Police Foundation. Ms. Doggett is a member ofthe Board ofDirectorsofthe National Symphony
Orchestra at the John F, Kennedy Center for the Performing Arts and is Vice President for
Development and Special Events for the Orchestra Board. She is also a memberofthe Board of
Directors of the Greater Washington Board of Trade. In addition, she is a member of the
International Women’s Forum ofWashington, DC.
Ms. Doggett has in the past served as a Commissioner on the District ofColumbia Commission
for Women and as a member of the National Association ofCommissions for Women. She also
served asthe Vice Chairmanof theBoardofTrusteesoftheSibley Memorial Hospital Foundation
andas a Trusteeofthe Corcoran Gallery and the Corcoran College ofArtand Design, holdingthe
offices ofTreasurer, Chairmanofthe Finance Committee, andChairman ofthe Audit Committee.
She also served on the Board of Directors ofWETA Public Television, including as a member of
the Executive and Finance Committees and Chairmanofthe Investment Committee.
A Ward 3 resident, Ms, Doggett studied at schools in Texas and Colorado.
