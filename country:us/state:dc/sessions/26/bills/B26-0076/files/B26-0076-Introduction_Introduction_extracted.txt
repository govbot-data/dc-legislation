9 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA

12 To amend on an emergency basis, due to congressional review, the Rental Housing Act of 1985
13 to maintain a moratorium on applications for, and the Mayor’s issuance of, certificates
14 of assurance associated with District rent stabilization laws.
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this act may
17 be cited as the “Certificate of Assurance Moratorium Congressional Review Emergency
18 Amendment Act of 2025”.
19 Sec. 2. The Rental Housing Act of 1985, effective July 17, 1985 (D.C. Law 6-10; D.C.
20 Official Code § 42-3501.01 et seq.), is amended by adding a new section 221a to read as follows:
21 “Sec. 221a. Certificate of assurance moratorium.
22 “Notwithstanding the requirements of section 221, beginning on November 2, 2020, no owner of
23 a housing accommodation shall request a certificate of assurance, and the Mayor shall not issue a
24 certificate of assurance for any request received.”.
25 Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
26 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Budget Director as the fiscal impact
27 statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975, approved
28 October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
29 Sec. 4. Effective date.

32 90 days, as provided for emergency acts of the Council of the District of Columbia in section
33 412(a) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 788;
