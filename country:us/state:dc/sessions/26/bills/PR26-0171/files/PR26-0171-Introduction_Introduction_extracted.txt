Mr. Rob Hawkins
30th Street, NW
Washington, DC 20008
(Ward 3)
for appointment as an alternate District resident member of the Board of Directors of the District of
Columbia Water and Sewer Authority , filling a vacant seat formerly held by Ivan Frishberg, for the
remainder of an unexpired term to end September 12, 2028.

nominee, together with a proposed resolution to assist the Council during the confirmation process.

Appointments, should the Council require additional information.
