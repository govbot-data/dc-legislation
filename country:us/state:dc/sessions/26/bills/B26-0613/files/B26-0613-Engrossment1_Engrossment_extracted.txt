10 To amend, on a temporary basis, Chapter 10 of Title 47 of the District of Columba Official Code
11 to provide real property, deed recordation, and deed transfer tax exemptions for properties
12 held under the ownership of The Roman Catholic Archbishop of Washington & his
13 successors in office, a corporation sole, or the respective parishes of the Archdiocese of
14 Washington
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
17 Act may be cited as the “Archdiocese of Washington Parish Real Property, Deed Recordation,
18 and Transfer Tax Exemption Temporary Amendment Act of 2026”.
19 Sec. 2. Chapter 10 of Title 47 of the District of Columbia Official Code is amended as
20 follows:
21 (a) The table of contents is amended by adding a new section designation to read as
22 follows:
23 “Sec. 47-1099.16. “Archdiocese of Washington Parish Real Property, Deed Recordation,
24 and Deed Transfer Tax Exemptions”
25 (b) A new section 47-1099.16 is added to read as follows:
26 “§ 47-1099.16. Archdiocese of Washington Parish Real Property, Deed Recordation, and
27 Deed Transfer Tax Exemptions.
