7 A PROPOSED RESOLUTION
8 _________________
9
10 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
11 _________________________
12
13 To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to amend Title 25 of
14 the District of Columbia Official Code to allow a retailer with commercial street
15 frontage at a location under the jurisdiction of the Washington Convention and
16 Sports Authority to be licensed for a streatery; to require that alcohol businesses
17 obtain a streatery endorsement from the Alcoholic Beverage and Cannabis Board
18 to operate a streatery; to require streatery, summer garden, and sidewalk café
19 endorsement and streatery license applications to undergo a 45-day public
20 comment period; to expand existing carry out and delivery license requirements to
21 retailers with commercial street frontage at a location under the jurisdiction of the
22 Washington Convention and Sports Authority; and to expressly require notice and
23 an opportunity to protest applications for streatery, summer garden, and sidewalk
24 café endorsements and licenses.
25
26 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That
27 this resolution may be cited as the “Streatery Program Endorsements and Protest Process
28 Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
29 Sec. 2. (a) Bill 26-147, the Streatery Program Endorsements and Protest Process
30 Amendment Act of 2025, would permanently codify existing emergency legislation that
31 allows streatery endorsements through December 31, 2025. As of January 1, 2026, the
32 bill would prohibit establishments from selling, serving, or permitting patrons to consume
33 beer, wine, or spirits on a streatery unless the establishment obtains the appropriate
34 endorsement or license from the Alcoholic Beverage and Cannabis Board (“Board”).
35 (b) On November 18, 2025, the Council approved Bill 26-147 at its Committee of
36 the Whole meeting and placed the measure on the consent agenda for the Council’s
1

37 December 2, 2025, Legislative Meeting. Before the measure takes effect, it must
38 complete Council, Mayoral, and congressional review, which could take several months.
39 (c) To assist our local establishments in transitioning to the permanent streatery
40 endorsements process after December 31, 2025, it is imperative that legislation
41 authorizing businesses to obtain a streatery endorsement or license from the Board to
42 operate a streatery take effect immediately. This will allow licensees to have ample time
43 to register with the Board before the deadline.
44 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2
45 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Streatery Program
46 Endorsements and Protest Process Emergency Amendment Act of 2025 be adopted after
47 a single reading.
48 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
