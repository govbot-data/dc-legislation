12 To amend, on a temporary basis, An Act To create a Department of Corrections in the District of
13 Columbia and the Corrections Oversight Improvement Omnibus Amendment Act of 2022
14 to designate the Department of Corrections as the District agency to provide a free
15 appropriate public education under the Individuals with Disabilities Education Act and
16 District law to certain individuals in its custody and detained in its secure facilities.
17
18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
19 act may be cited as the “Special Education for Young Adults in the Custody of the Department of
20 Corrections Temporary Amendment Act of 2025”.
21 Sec. 2. Section 2(b) of An Act To create a Department of Corrections in the District of
22 Columbia, approved June 27, 1946 (60 Stat. 320; D.C. Official Code § 24-211.02(b)), is
23 amended as follows:
24 (a) Paragraph (9) is amended by striking the phrase “; and” and inserting a semicolon in
25 its place.
26 (b) The first paragraph (10) is amended by striking the period and inserting a semicolon
27 in its place.
28 (c) The second paragraph is redesignated as paragraph (11).
