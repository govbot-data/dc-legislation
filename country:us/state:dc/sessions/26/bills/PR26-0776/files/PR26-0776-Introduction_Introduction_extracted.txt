Dear Chairman Mendelson:
Enclosed for consideration and enactment by the Council of the District of Columbia is an
emergency bill, the “Contract No. CW128161 with OceanPro Industries, Ltd. and Modification
Nos. M001, M002, M003, M004 and M004a Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency
Act of 2026” and the accompanying emergency declaration resolution, not-to-exceed amount of
$1,737,357.81.
The legislation will approve Contract No. CW128161 with OceanPro Industries, Ltd., to provide
groceries for the preparation of meals for youth, and Modification Nos. M001, M002, M003,
M004, and M004a thereto. In addition, the legislation will approve payment for the goods
and services received and to be received under the contract and the modifications.
My administration is available to discuss any questions you may have regarding the proposed
contract modification. In order to facilitate a response to any questions you may have, please
have your staff contact Marc Scott, Chief Operating Officer, Office of Contracting and
Procurement, at (202) 724-8759.
I urge the Council to take prompt and favorable action on the enclosed legislation.
Sincerely,
Muriel Bowser
