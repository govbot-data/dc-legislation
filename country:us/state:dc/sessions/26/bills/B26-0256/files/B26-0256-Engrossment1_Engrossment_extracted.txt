12 To amend, on a temporary basis, the Small and Certified Business Enterprise Development and
13 Assistance Act of 2005 to extend the time in which compliant subcontracting plans for
14 contracts for health benefits for District employees and their families shall be provided.
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
17 act may be cited as the “Subcontracting Requirements Exemption for Contracts for Health
18 Benefits to District Employees Temporary Amendment Act of 2025”.
19 Sec. 2. Section 2346(d) of the Small and Certified Business Enterprise Development and
20 Assistance Act of 2005, effective October 20, 2005 (D.C. Law 16-33; D.C. Official Code § 2-
21 218.46(d)), is amended by adding a new paragraph (4) to read as follows:
22 “(4) Notwithstanding the foregoing paragraphs (1) and (3) of this subsection, the
23 subcontracting plan for contracts to provide health benefits to District government employees
24 and their families shall be provided prior to commencement of performance of the plan year, and
25 a bid or proposal that does not include a subcontracting plan may be deemed responsive if it is
26 submitted in response to a solicitation to provide health benefits to District government
27 employees and their families.”
