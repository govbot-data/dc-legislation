Councilmember Brianne K. Nadeau
A BILL
_________________________
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
_________________________
1 To amend the Language Access Act of 2004 to include enforcement and relief provisions for
2 violations of the act; to authorize the Office of Human Rights to adopt rules and
3 regulations to implement the provisions of this act.
4
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
6 act may be cited as the “Office of Human Rights Rulemaking Authority Amendment Act of
7 2026”.
8 Sec. 2. The Language Access Act of 2004, effective June 19, 2004 (D.C. Law 15-167;
9 D.C. Official Code § 2-1931 et seq.) is amended as follows:
10 (a) Section 6 (D.C. Official Code § 2-1935) is amended by adding new subsections (d)
11 through (i), to read as follows:
12 “(d) A covered entity shall be strictly liable for failure to comply with the requirements of
13 this act.
14 “(e) To establish a violation, the complainant need only demonstrate that:
15 "(1) They were entitled to language access services under this act; and
16 "(2) Such services were not provided in the manner or timeframe required by this
1

17 act.
18 "(f) Proof of intent, discrimination, or animus shall not be required to establish non-
19 compliance.
20 “(g) Lack of funding, staffing limitations, or administrative burden shall not constitute a
21 defense to liability.”.
22 “(h) Upon a finding of non-compliance, the Director of Language Access shall order
23 remedies sufficient to place the complainant in the position they would have been in but for the
24 denial of required language access. Such remedies shall include:
25 "(1) Corrective action; and
26 "(2) Statutory damages awarded to the complainant.
27 “(i) Statutory damages awarded under this section shall be paid directly to the
28 complainant and shall not be contingent upon proof of actual damages.".
29 (b) A new section 9a is added to read as follows:
30 “Sec. 9a. Rulemaking.
31 The Mayor, pursuant to Title I of the District of Columbia Administrative Procedure Act,
32 approved October 21, 1968 (82 Stat. 1204; D.C. Official Code § 2-501 et seq.), shall issue rules
33 to implement the provisions of this act.”.
34 Sec. 3. Applicability.
35 (a) Section 2(a) shall apply upon the date of inclusion of its fiscal effect in an approved
36 budget and financial plan.
37 (b) Section 2(b) shall apply as of June 19, 2004.
38 Sec. 4. Fiscal impact statement.
39 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement in the committee report as the fiscal
2

40 impact statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975,
41 approved October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
42 Sec. 5. Effective date.
43 This act shall take effect after approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by the
44 Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), a 30-day period of congressional review as
45 provided in section 602(c)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December
46 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 813; D.C. Official Code § 1-206.02(c)(1)), and publication in the District of
47 Columbia Register.
