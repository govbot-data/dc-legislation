11 To amend, on a temporary basis, the Department of Transportation Establishment Act of 2002 to
12 reduce the fees for participating in the Streatery Program, to establish a start date for
13 enforcement of the Streatery Guidelines or applicable rules, to declare the District’s policy
14 of promoting streateries, to authorize the Public Space Committee to grant waivers of
15 certain program requirements, if such waiver would not create an undue risk, and to require
16 the Public Space Committee to give great weight to recommendations issued by the Old
17 Georgetown Board.
18
19 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
20 act may be cited as the “Streatery Program Temporary Amendment Act of 2025”.
21 Sec. 2. The Department of Transportation Establishment Act of 2002, effective May 21,
22 2002 (D.C. Law 14-137; D.C. Official Code § 50-921.01 et seq.), is amended by adding a new
23 section 9v to read as follows:
24 “Sec. 9v. Streatery program.
25 “(a)(1) The annual public space rental fee for a streatery permit shall be assessed at no more
26 than $15 per square foot.
27 “(2) District agencies shall begin enforcement activities relating to noncompliance
28 with the Streatery Guidelines or applicable rules no sooner than January 15, 2026.

29 “(b)(1) It is the policy of the District to promote streateries, as streateries are a beneficial
30 use of public space that create a more vibrant dining scene and support local businesses.
31 “(2) In furtherance of the policy described in paragraph (1) of this subsection, the
32 Public Space Committee may waive the following program requirements; provided, that the waiver
33 would not create an undue risk to pedestrians or other roadway users:
34 “(A) The minimum required distance between streateries and curb cuts,
35 driveways, alleys, and trees;
36 “(B) The prohibition on streatery enclosures or vertical support structures
37 that exceed 42 inches in height; and
38 “(C) The maximum seating capacity of a streatery.
39 “(3) Recommendations by the Old Georgetown Board on the design of streateries
40 in Georgetown shall be given great weight by the Public Space Committee.”.
41 “(3) A streatery permit holder that received an exception under paragraph (2) of this
42 subsection shall apply for a streatery permit renewal through the Public Space Committee every 2
43 years.”.

47 “(4) One of the roadways selected pursuant to this subsection shall include the
48 2000-2400 blocks of 18th Street, N.W., in Ward 1”.
