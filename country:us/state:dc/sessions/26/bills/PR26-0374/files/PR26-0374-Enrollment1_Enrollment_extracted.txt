ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-230
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
November 4, 2025
To declare the existence of an emergency, due to congressional review, with respect to the need
to enact and amend provisions of law necessary to support the Fiscal Year 2026 budget.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Fiscal Year 2026 Budget Support Congressional Review
Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
Sec. 2. (a) On July 28, 2025, the Council passed the Fiscal Year 2026 Budget Support
Emergency Act of 2025, effective September 3, 2025 (D.C. Act 26-146; 72 DCR 9623)
(“Emergency Act”), which enacted and amended provisions of law necessary to support the Fiscal
Year 2026 budget and will expire on December 2, 2025.
(b) On July 28, 2025, the Council also passed the Fiscal Year 2026 Budget Support Act of
2025, enacted on September 4, 2025 (D.C. Act 26-148; 72 DCR 9825) (“Permanent Act”), which is
substantially similar to the Emergency Act. The Permanent Act was transmitted to Congress on
September 10, 2025.
(c) Due to the congressional calendar, the Permanent Act is not expected to become law
until after the Emergency Act expires, thereby creating a gap in authority between expiration of
the Emergency Act and the effective date of the Permanent Act. Congressional review emergency
legislation is necessary to avoid that gap in the implementation of the Fiscal Year 2026 Budget and
Financial Plan.
Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Fiscal
Year 2026 Budget Support Congressional Review Emergency Act of 2025 be adopted after a
single reading.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
