Dear Chairman Mendelson:
Enclosed for consideration and approval by the Council of the District of Columbia is an
emergency bill, the “Modification Nos. 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, and 6 to Contract No. DCAM-22-CS-RFP-
0026 with Chiaramonte Construction Company Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency
Act of 2025”, along with the accompanying emergency declaration resolution.
The legislation will approve Modifications No. 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, and 6 to a Contract No. DCAM-22-
CS-RFP-0026 with Chiarmonte Construction Company to provide construction services for the
Major Renovations of Public Safety Communications Center (“PSCC”) Building project. The
legislation will also authorize payment under that contract for goods and services received and to
be received in the not-to-exceed amount of $13,693,879.06.
My administration is available to discuss any questions you may have regarding the proposed
contract modification. In order to facilitate a response to any questions you may have, please
contact Delano Hunter, Director, Department of General Services (“DGS”), or have your staff
contact George G. Lewis, Chief of Contracts and Procurement, DGS, at (202) 727-2800.
I look forward to the Council’s favorable consideration of this contract modification.
Sincerely,
Muriel Bowser

38 (b) From April 17, 2024, to September 05, 2024, via Modification Nos. 1, 2, 3, 4, and 5,
39 the Department of General Services incorporated the added costs for additional construction
40 services into Contract No. DCAM-22-CS-RFP-0026, increasing the total contract value amount
41 by $998,611.36 to $12,470,486.36.
42 (c) Proposed Modification No. 6 would increase the total contract value amount by
43 $1,223,392.70 to $13,693,879.06. The aggregate value of Modification Nos. 1, 2, 3, 4, and 5 and
44 Proposed Modification No. 6 is $2,222,004.06, an amount over $1 million; therefore, pursuant to
45 section 451 of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December 24, 1973 (87 Stat.
46 803; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.51), Council approval of the contract modifications is required.
47 (d) Approval is necessary to allow the continuation of the building renovation services
48 provided pursuant to the contract. Without this approval, Chiarmonte Construction Company
49 cannot be paid for goods and services provided in excess of $1 million.

53 Chiaramonte Construction Company Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of
54 2025 be adopted after a single reading.
