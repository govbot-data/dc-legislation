Dear Chairman Mendelson:
Enclosed for consideration and adoption by the Council of the District of Columbia is legislation entitled
“Proposed Closing of a Public Alley in Square 3524, S.O. 23-06402, Act of 2025”,
“Proposed Closing of a Public Alley in Square 3524, S.O. 23-06402, Temporary Act of 2025”,
“Proposed Closing of a Public Alley in Square 3524, S.O. 23-06402, Emergency Act of 2025”, and
“Proposed Closing of a Public Alley in Square 3524, S.O. 23-06402, Emergency Declaration Resolution
of 2025”.
The purpose of this legislation is to allow for the consolidation of several lots around an excess portion
of the alley system in Square 3524 to be closed to create allow for the development of 27 residential
units that will be sold as a combination of two-family townhouses and single-family homes in Ward 5.
Pursuant to D.C. Official Code § 9-202.02, the Surveyor drew a draft plat showing the portion of the
alley proposed to be closed and the lot to which title of the proposed alley closing would revert and
provided notice to relevant parties. The affected District agencies and public utilities responded either in
support of, or not objecting to, the proposed alley closing, although the District Department of
Transportation included two conditions in its letter of no objection. Advisory Neighborhood Commission
5F submitted a resolution in support of this application.
I urge the Council to take prompt and favorable action on the enclosed legislation.
