Elizabeth Blakeslee
Ms. Elizabeth Blakeslee is a licensed real estate broker affiliated with
Coldwell Banker Residential Brokerage and is a current member of the
Real Estate Commission.
Ms. Blakeslee has been in residential real estate sales for over 30 years.
Currently and during her time with Prudential Preferred Properties, Ms.
Blakeslee trained and mentored new sales agents and served as an
advocate for buyers’ interests in the real estate purchasing process.
Ms. Blakeslee has been actively involved in the real estate community,
servingin numerous leadership capacities for the District of Columbia
Association of Realtors, Capital Area Association of Realtors, and National Association of
Realtors.
A Ward 2 resident, Ms. Blakeslee received a Bachelor of Arts in Speech and Communication from
Kent State University.
