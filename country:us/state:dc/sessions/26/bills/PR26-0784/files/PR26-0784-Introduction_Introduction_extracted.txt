18 To amend the Rules of Organization and Procedures for the Council of the District of Columbia,
19 Council Period 26, Resolution of 2025, to authorize the submission of a grant budget
20 modification for Fiscal Year 2026 grant funds submitted by the University of the District
21 of Columbia, to clarify requirements related to Racial Equity Impact Assessments, and to
22 authorize the Committee on Executive Administration and Labor to hold up to three
23 roundtables during the recess.
24
25 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
26 resolution may be cited as the “Council Period 26 Recess Rules Amendment Resolution of 2026”.
27 Sec. 2. The Rules of Organization and Procedure for the Council of the District of
28 Columbia, Council Period 26, Resolution of 2025 effective January 2, 2025 (Res. 26-1; 72 DCR
29 1289), is amended as follows:
30 (a) Section 306(b) is amended by adding a new paragraph (5) to read as follows:
31 “(5) A request for a budget modification for Fiscal Year 2026 grant funds that is
32 submitted by the University of the District of Columbia may be transmitted to the Secretary from
33 July 16 through September 15, 2026.”.
34 (b) Section 311 is amended as follows:
35 (1) Subsection (a)(1) is amended by striking the phrase “shall” and inserting the
36 phrase “may” in its place.
1

37 (2) Subsection (b) is amended as follows:
38 (A) Paragraph (9) is amended by striking the phrase “; or” and inserting a
39 semicolon in its place;
40 (B) Paragraph (10) is redesignated as Paragraph (11); and
41 (C) A new paragraph (10) is added to read as follows:
42 “(10) Legislation introduced a the request of the Uniform Law Commission; or”.
43 (c) Subsection (a) of this section shall expire on September 15, 2025.
44 Sec. 3. The Committee on Executive Administration and Labor is authorized to hold up
45 to three roundtables during the period of July 15, 2026, through September 15, 2026, on the
46 matters of elections or campaign finance.
47 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
