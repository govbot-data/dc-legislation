Julio Jay Haddock Ortiz
Mr. Julio Jay Haddock the senior advisor to the Carlos Rosario
International Public Charter School’s Chief Executive Officer, a current
member of the Washington Convention and Sports Authority Board of
Directors (Events DC), a principal at Absolute Hospitality, and a more
than 50-year veteran of the hotel industry.
In 2016, Mr. Haddock Ortiz formed Absolute Hospitality. He is also the
former President of Capital Hotels, served as Executive Vice of Potomac
Hotel, and held roles with the Grand Metropolitan Hotels of London and
the Sheraton Corporation.
Mr. Haddock Ortiz currently serves on the board of directors of the Hotel
Association of Washington, DC and the advisory boards of the GALA Hispanic Theater and the
Carlos Rosario International Charter School’s culinary program. In addition, he raised over $1
million during his Whitman Walker Clinic “Art for Life” chairmanship to raised funds for Latino
programs for HIV/AIDS during the pandemic. Mr. Haddock Ortiz also received the Washington
Business Journal’s Minority Business Award and the Greater Hispanic Chamber of Commerce
Chairman’s Award, and recognition from Vice President Al Gore on his work tackling HIV/AIDS
in the Latino community.
A Ward 6 resident, Mr. Haddock Ortiz earned his Bachelor of Arts from St. John’s University of
New York.
