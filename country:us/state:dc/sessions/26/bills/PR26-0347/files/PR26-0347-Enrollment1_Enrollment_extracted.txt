ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-223
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
October 21, 2025
To approve an Assignment, Assumption, and Amendment of the Long-Term Subsidy Contract
by and between the District of Columbia Housing Authority and Jubilee Housing Limited
Partnership II that will extend the contract for 20 years and will assign the contract from
Jubilee Housing Limited Partnership II to Jubilee ADMO Apartments LP in support of
the District of Columbia’s Local Rent Supplement Program to continue funding housing
costs associated with the affordable housing units for the Euclid Apartments, located at
1740 Euclid Street, NW.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Assignment, Assumption, and Amendment of Long-Term
Subsidy Contract for the Euclid Apartments by and among the District of Columbia Housing
Authority, Jubilee Housing Limited Partnership II, and Jubilee ADMO Apartments LP Approval
Resolution of 2025”.
Sec. 2. Pursuant to section 451 of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 803; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.51), and section 202 of the
Procurement Practices Reform Act of 2010, effective April 8, 2011 (D.C. Law 18-371; D.C.
Official Code § 2-352.02), the Council approves the Assignment, Assumption, and
Amendment of Long-Term Subsidy Contract by and among Jubilee Housing Limited
Partnership II (“Jubilee Housing”), Jubilee ADMO Apartments LP (“Jubilee ADMO”), and
the District of Columbia Housing Authority to assign the Euclid Apartments long-term
subsidy contract (“LTSC”) to Jubilee ADMO, to have Jubilee ADMO assume the Euclid
Apartments LTSC, and to extend the Euclid Apartments LTSC for 20 years to continue to
provide an operating subsidy in support of 32 affordable housing units at Euclid Apartments,
located at 1740 Euclid Street, NW.
Sec. 3. Transmittal.
The Council shall transmit a copy of this resolution, upon its adoption, to the District of
Columbia Housing Authority and the Mayor.
