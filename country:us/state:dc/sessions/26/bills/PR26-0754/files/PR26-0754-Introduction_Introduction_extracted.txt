Dear Chairman Mendelson:
Enclosed for consideration and enactment by the Council of the District of Columbia is an
emergency bill, the “Modification Nos. M0026 and M0027 to Contract CW90941 with Aramark
Correctional Services, LLC Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2026,” and
the accompanying emergency declaration resolution, not-to-exceed $7,812,415.
The legislation will approve Modification Nos. M0026 and M0027 to Contract No. CW90941 with
Aramark Correctional Services, LLC to provide inmate food management services and to increase
the not-to-exceed amount for option year four of the contract. In addition, the legislation will
approve payment for the goods and services received and to be received under the modifications.
My administration is available to discuss any questions you may have regarding the proposed
contract modification. In order to facilitate a response to any questions you may have, please have
your staff contact Marc Scott, Chief Operating Officer, Office of Contracting and Procurement, at
(202) 724-8759.
I urge the Council to take prompt and favorable action on the enclosed legislation.
Sincerely,
Muriel Bowser

20 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
21 resolution may be cited as the "Modification Nos. M0026 and M0027 to Contract No. CW90941
22 with Aramark Correctional Services, LLC Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency
23 Declaration Resolution of 2026".
24 Sec. 2.(a) There exists an immediate need to approve Modification Nos. M0026 and
25 M0027 to Contract No. CW90941 with Aramark Correctional Services, LLC, and to authorize
26 payment for the goods and services received and to be received under the contract and
27 modifications.
28 (b) On July 11, 2025, by Modification No. M0022, the Office of Contracting and
29 Procurement ("OCP"), on behalf of the Department of Corrections, exercised a full option of
30 option year four of Contract No. CW90941 with Aramark Correction Services, LLC to provide
31 inmate food management services in the not-to-exceed amount of $6,107,415 for the period
32 September 15, 2025 through September 14, 2026 (CA26-0303).
1

33 (c) By Modification No. M0026, OCP increased the not-to-exceed amount for option year
34 four by $955,000, to a total not-to-exceed amount of $7,062,415.
35 (d) Proposed Modification No. M0027 will increase the not-to-exceed amount for option
36 year four by $750,000, to a total not-to-exceed amount of $7,812,415.
37 (e) Under section 451(b)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
38 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 803; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.51(b)(1)), Council approval is
39 necessary since the modifications will increase the value of the contract to more than $1 million
40 during a 12-month period.
41 (f) Approval is necessary to allow the continuation of these vital services. Without this
42 approval, Aramark Correctional Services, LLC cannot be paid for goods and services provided in
43 excess of $1 million for the period September 15, 2025, through September 14, 2026.
44 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2 constitute
45 emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Modification Nos. M0026 and M0027 to
46 Contract No. CW90941 with Aramark Correctional Services, LLC Approval and Payment
47 Authorization Emergency Act of 2026 be adopted after a single reading.
48 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
