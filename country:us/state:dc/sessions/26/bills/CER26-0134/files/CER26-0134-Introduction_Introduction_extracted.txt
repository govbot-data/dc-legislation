_____________________________
Councilmember Brianne K. Nadeau
A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
_________________________
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
_________________________
1 To recognize, honor, and celebrate the 15th anniversary of the Bernice Fonteneau Senior Wellness
2 Center.
3
4 WHEREAS, Bernice Fonteneau Senior Wellness Center, opened in 2011, is a cornerstone
5 of the Ward 1 community, known for its vibrant energy and “bold living” philosophy, serving as
6 a community hub for our seniors; and
7 WHEREAS, the heart of the Center’s daily activity is supporting seniors’ health and fitness,
8 providing exercise classes, specialized fitness programs, health screenings, and support groups;
9 and
10 WHEREAS, the Center’s mission includes ensuring our seniors are well-nourished through
11 cooking classes, food and nutrition programs, and daily lunch; and
12 WHEREAS, the Center emphasizes lifelong learning and full social lives through
13 educational programming, day trips, book clubs, special events, and other daily activities and
14 programming; and
15 WHEREAS, the Center uplifts the legacy of its namesake, Bernice Fonteneau, a prominent
16 community activist in the District recognized for her advocacy on behalf of seniors and children.
17 Fonteneau, a 1993 D.C. Women’s Hall of Fame inductee, co-founded Tender Loving Care, a group
1

18 that worked with infants abandoned at birth and was active with the Capital Children's Museum
19 and Capital Area Girl Scouts.
20 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, that this
21 resolution may be cited as the “Bernice Fonteneau Senior Wellness Center 15th Anniversary
22 Recognition Resolution of 2026”.
23 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia recognizes and celebrates the 15th
24 anniversary of Bernice Fonteneau Senior Wellness Center and its dedication to helping seniors
25 take charge of their health, wellness, and social life.
26 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately upon the first date of publication in
27 the District of Columbia Register.
