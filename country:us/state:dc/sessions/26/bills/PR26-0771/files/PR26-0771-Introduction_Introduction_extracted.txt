20 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this

24 Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to approve Modification Nos. M0l, M02 and
25 M03 to Contract No. CW131628 with ID3 Trucking, LLC and to authorize payment for the
26 goods and services received and to be received under the contract.
27 (b) On January 21, 2026, the Office of Contracting and Procurement ("OCP"), on behalf
28 of the Department of Public Works, executed Contract No. CW131628 with ID3 Trucking, LLC
29 to provide equipment and qualified operators for snow plowing and removal in the not-to-exceed
30 amount of $1 million for the period from January 21, 2026, through January 20, 2027.
31 (c) On January 22, 2026, by Emergency Modification No. M0l, OCP increased the not
32 to-exceed amount by $4 million, to a total not-to-exceed amount of $5 million.
1

33 (d) On April 27, 2026. by Emergency Modification No. M02, OCP decreased the not-to-
34 exceed amount by $2.5 million, to a total not-to-exceed amount of $2.5 million.
35 (e) On June 2, 2026, OCP executed Emergency Modification No. M03: an administrative
36 modification to clarify the price schedule but did not change the total not-to-exceed amount of
37 $2.5 million.
38 (f) Under section 451(b)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
39 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 803; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.51(b)(1)), Council approval is
40 necessary since the modifications increased the value of the contract by more than $1 million
41 during a 12-month period.
42 (g) Approval is necessary to pay for these vital goods and services. Without this
43 approval, ID3 Trucking, LLC cannot be paid for goods and services provided in excess of $1
44 million for the period from January 21, 2026, through January 20, 2027.
45 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2 constitute
46 emergency circumstances making it necessary that Modification Nos. M01, M02 and M03 to
47 Contract No. CW131628 with ID3 Trucking, LLC Approval and Payment Authorization
48 Emergency Act of 2026 be adopted after a single reading.
49 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
