ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-418
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
June 23, 2026
To confirm the reappointment of Lauren Pair as Rent Administrator.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Rent Administrator Lauren Pair Confirmation Resolution of
2026”.
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia confirms the appointment of:
Lauren Pair
Farragut Street, NW
Washington, DC 20010
(Ward 4)
as Rent Administrator, established by section 203a of the Rental Housing Act of 1985, effective
March 26, 2009 (D.C. Law 17-366; D.C. Official Code § 42-3502.03a), for a term to end June
27, 2029.
Sec. 3. The Council shall transmit a copy of this resolution, upon its adoption, to the
nominee and to the Mayor.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
