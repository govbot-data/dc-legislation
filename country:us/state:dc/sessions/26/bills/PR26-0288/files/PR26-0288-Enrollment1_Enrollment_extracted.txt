ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-184
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
July 28, 2025
To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to symbolically designate
Alabama Avenue, SE, between Hartford Street, SE and Gainesville Street, SE, as Rev.
Dr. Judy Talbert Way.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Rev. Dr. Judy Talbert Way Designation Emergency Declaration
Resolution of 2025”.
Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to symbolically designate Alabama Avenue,
SE, between Hartford Street, SE and Gainesville Street, SE, as Rev. Dr. Judy Talbert Way.
(b) The Rev. Dr. Judy Talbert Way Designation Act of 2025, passed on 2nd reading on
July 28, 2025 (Enrolled version of Bill 26-280), was adopted on final reading by the Council on
July 28, 2025.
(c) Emergency legislation will allow the District Department of Transportation to work
with the community to install signage prior to a community celebration of the life of Rev. Dr.
Judy Talbert before the permanent legislation completes the 30-day congressional review period.
Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia finds that the circumstances enumerated
in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Rev. Dr. Judy
Talbert Way Designation Emergency Act of 2025 be adopted after a single reading.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
