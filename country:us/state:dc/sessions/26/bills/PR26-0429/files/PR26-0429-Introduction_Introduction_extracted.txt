5 A RESOLUTION
6 ___________________
7 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
8 ___________________
9
10 To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to amend the Uniform College
11 Athlete Name, Image, or Likeness Temporary Amendment Act of 2025 to further clarify
12 the applicability date for expanded financial reporting requirements for certain boards
13 and commissions under Section 5710 of Title 3 of the District of Columbia Municipal
14 Regulations.
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
17 act may be cited as the “District of Columbia Boards and Commissions Financial Reporting
18 Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
19 Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to further clarify the applicability date for
20 expanded financial reporting requirements for certain boards and commissions under Section
21 5710 of Title 3 of the District of Columbia Municipal Regulations.
22 (b) The Board of Ethics and Government Accountability (BEGA) identified
23 approximately 45 boards and commissions as containing members that BEGA considered
24 appropriate to file annual financial disclosures. BEGA took this action pursuant to D.C. Official
25 Code 1-1161.01(47)(I), adopting a rulemaking at 3 DCMR § 5710.1.
26 (c) In May 2025, the Council took action to delay the implementation of these expanded
27 financial reporting requirements until the Council could take an opportunity to consider the issue

28 more fully. In July 2025, Chairman Mendelson introduced permanent legislation on the issue that
29 is currently under Council review.
30 (e) The temporary act that contains the provisions delaying implementation of these
31 expanding reporting requirements will expire on April 3, 2026.
32 (f) This emergency is necessary to align the applicability date contained in the temporary
33 act (January 1, 2026) with the expiration of the temporary act itself and provide sufficient time
34 for the Council to consider Bill 26-343, the Board of Ethics and Government Accountability
35 Authority Clarification Amendment Act of 2025 -- the permanent legislation on the issue that is
36 already under Council review.
37 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia finds that the circumstances in section 2
38 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the District of Columbia Boards
39 and Commissions Financial Reporting Emergency Amendment Act of 2025 be adopted after a
40 single reading.
41 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
