22 A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
23
24 _____
25
26
27 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
28
29 __________
30
31
32 To declare the month of March 2025 as “Autoimmune Awareness Month” in the District of
33 Columbia, and to support efforts to increase awareness of autoimmune diseases and
34 increase funding for autoimmune disease research.
35
36 WHEREAS, researchers have identified the category of autoimmune diseases includes
37 more than 100 diseases that are chronic and in many cases debilitating, with no known cures;
38
39 WHEREAS, autoimmune diseases can affect any part of the body, including the blood,
40 blood vessels, muscles, nervous system, gastrointestinal tract, endocrine glands, and multiple-
41 organ system, and can be life-threatening;
42
43 WHEREAS, researchers have identified a close genetic relationship and a common
44 pathway of disease that exists among autoimmune diseases, explaining the clustering of
45 autoimmune diseases in individuals and families;
1

46
47 WHEREAS, the family of autoimmune diseases is underrecognized and poses a major
48 healthcare challenge to the United States;
49
50 WHEREAS, the National Institutes of Health estimates that autoimmune diseases afflict
51 up to 50,000 people in the United States, 80 percent of whom are women, and the prevalence of
52 autoimmune diseases is rising;
53
54 WHEREAS, autoimmune diseases are among the top 10 leading causes of death in women
55 under age 65;
56
57 WHEREAS, autoimmune diseases frequently affect children and young adults, leading to
58 a lifetime of disability;
59
60 WHEREAS, diagnostic tests for most autoimmune diseases are not standardized, making
61 autoimmune diseases very difficult to diagnose;
62
63 WHEREAS, because autoimmune diseases are difficult to diagnose, treatment is often
64 delayed, sometimes resulting in irreparable damage and unnecessary suffering, which underscores
65 the importance of early diagnoses and treatment;
66
67 WHEREAS, the Institute of Medicine of the National Academies reported that the United
68 States is behind other countries in research into immune system self-recognition, which is the cause
69 of autoimmune diseases;
70
71 WHEREAS, there is a significant need for research focusing on the etiology of all
72 autoimmune-related diseases, to increase understanding of the root causes of these diseases rather
73 than treating the symptoms after the disease has had its destructive effect;
74
75 WHEREAS, the National Coalition of Autoimmune Patient Groups is a coalition of
76 national organizations focused on autoimmune diseases working to consolidate the voices of
77 patients with autoimmune diseases and to promote increased education, awareness, and research
78 into all aspects of autoimmune diseases through a collaborative approach; and
79
80 WHEREAS, designating the month of March 2025 as “Autoimmune Awareness Month”
81 would help educate the public about autoimmune diseases and the need for research funding,
82 accurate diagnosis, and effective treatments.
83
84 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
85 resolution may be cited as the “Autoimmune Awareness Month Recognition Resolution of 2025”.
86
87 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia declares the month of March 2025 as
88 “Autoimmune Awareness Month” in the District of Columbia and supports efforts to increase
89 awareness of autoimmune diseases and increase funding for autoimmune disease research.
2

90
91 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
