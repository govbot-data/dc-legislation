16 To declare an emergency with respect to the need to disapprove reprogramming request No. 26-
17 91 of $5,500,000 Local Funds within the Public Safety and Justice Cluster.
18
19 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
20 resolution may be cited as the “Reprogramming Request No. 26-91 Disapproval Emergency
21 Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
22 Sec. 2. (a) On October 24, 2025, the Mayor transmitted a reprogramming request to the
23 Council to reprogram $5,500,000 of Local Funds from the Office of Unified Communications
24 and the Department of Corrections to the Metropolitan Police Department to “address year-end
25 spending pressures.”
26 (b) This reprogramming proposes to supplement the Metropolitan Police Department’s
27 overtime budget for Fiscal Year 2025. This year-end reprogramming does not affect whether
28 officers are actually paid, because they already have been. This year-end reprogramming is
29 solely to balance accounts and avoid MPD spending as being deficient – i.e., overspending its
30 budget.

31 (c) This reprogramming request would be in addition to $13.7 million that was added to
32 the MPD’s budget for overtime in the Fiscal Year 2025 Supplemental Budget adopted by the
33 Council on July 28, 2025. It is also additional to the $16.3 million that was added to the MPD’s
34 budget for overtime by a reprogramming deemed approved by the Council last month.
35 (d) For Fiscal Year 2025 the Mayor and MPD requested, and the Council approved, an
36 overtime budget of $23.9 million. For Fiscal Year 2025 the MPD has spent approximately $150
37 million on overtime.
38 (e) The Executive has failed to control costs, including overtime costs at the Metropolitan
39 Police Department, resulting in an overtime budget that far exceeds what was appropriated by
40 the Council.
41 (f) There exists an immediate need to act on this reprogramming because it will be
42 deemed approved on November 10, 2025.
43 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
44 enumerated in Section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the
45 “Reprogramming Request No. 26-91 Emergency Disapproval Resolution of 2025” be adopted
46 after a single reading.
47 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
