ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-73
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
April 1, 2025
To confirm the appointment of Dr. Keita Vanterpool to the Board of Integrative Healthcare.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Board of Integrative Healthcare Dr. Keita Vanterpool
Confirmation Resolution of 2025”.
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia confirms the appointment of:
Dr. Keita Vanterpool
Marion Barry Ave, SE
Washington, DC 20020
(Ward 8)
as a doctor of chiropractic licensed in the District member of the Board of Integrative Healthcare,
established by section 224 of the District of Columbia Health Occupations Revision Act of 1985,
effective July 19, 2024 (D.C. Law 25-191; D.C. Official Code § 3-1202.24), for a term of 3
years.
Sec. 3. The Council shall transmit a copy of this resolution, upon its adoption, to the
nominee and to the Mayor.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
