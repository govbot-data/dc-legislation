16 To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to approve Contract No.
17 CW116019 with Immediate Mailing Services, Inc., and Modification Nos. 1, 2, and 3 to
18 Contract No. CW116019, and to authorize payment for the goods and services received
19 and to be received under the contract and modifications.
20
21 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
22 resolution may be cited as the “Modification Nos. 1, 2, and 3 to Contract No. CW116019 with
23 Immediate Mailing Services, Inc. Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Declaration
24 Resolution of 2025”.
25 Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to approve Contract No. CW116019 with
26 Immediate Mailing Services, Inc., and Modifications Nos. 1, 2, and 3 thereto, and to authorize
27 payment for the goods and services received and to be received under the contract and
28 modifications.
29 (b) On May 28, 2024, the Office of Contracting and Procurement (“OCP”), on behalf of
30 the Department of Health Care Finance (“DHCF”), executed Contract No. CW116019 with
31 Immediate Mailing Services, Inc., for printing and mailing services for the District of Columbia
32 Access System, in the not-to-exceed (“NTE”) amount of $379,731.38.
1

33 (c) By Modification No. 1, dated September 27, 2024, OCP partially exercised option
34 year one of Contract No. CW116019 for the period October 1, 2024, through January 31, 2025,
35 in the NTE amount of $389,565.92.
36 (d) By Modification No. 2, dated January 14, 2025, OCP exercised an additional partial
37 option year one of Contract No. CW116019 through March 31, 2025, and increased the NTE
38 amount of option year one by $194,873.94, from $389,565.92 to $584,439.86.
39 (e) Modification No. 3 will fully exercise option year one of Contract No. CW116019
40 and increase the NTE amount of option year one by $584,046.19, from $584,439.86 to
41 $1,168,486.05.
42 (f) Under section 451(b)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
43 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 803; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.51(b)(1)), Council approval is
44 necessary since the modifications will increase the value of the contract to more than $1 million
45 during a 12-month period.
46 (g) Approval is necessary to allow the continuation of these vital services. Without this
47 approval, Immediate Mailing Services, Inc. cannot be paid for goods and services provided in
48 excess of $1 million for the period from October 1, 2024, through September 30, 2025.
49 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2 constitute
50 emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Modification Nos. 1, 2, and 3 to Contract
51 No. CW116019 with Immediate Mailing Services, Inc. Approval and Payment Authorization
52 Emergency Act of 2025 be adopted after a single reading.
53 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
