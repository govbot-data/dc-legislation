Mark Mogle
Mr. Mark Mogle joined the U.S. Department of Justice’s International
Criminal Investigative Assistance Program (ICITAP) in 2005 and
curr ently serves as Assistant Director for Forensic Operations. In this
position, Mr. Mogle oversees foreign assistance programs that strengthen
criminal investigations, facilitate the international accreditation of
forensic laboratories, and support the effective implementation of new
technologies.
Prior to joining ICITAP, Mr. Mogle served 14 years with the
Pennsylvania State Police Bureau of Forensic Services and assisted the
organization achieve accreditation in 2001. He is a member of the
American Society of Crime Laboratory Directors, American Academy of
Forensic Sciences, and he is a past president of the Mid-Atlantic Association of Forensic Scientists.
Mr. Mogle has been recognized numerous times by the Justice Department, Department of State,
and Pennsylvania State Police for his exemplary service.
A Ward 1 resident, Mr. Mogle earned his Bachelor of Science from Pennsylvania State University.
