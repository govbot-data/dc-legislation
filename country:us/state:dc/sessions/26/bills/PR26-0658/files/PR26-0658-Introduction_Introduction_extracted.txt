Dr. Sannido De Poukn
Dr. Sannido De Poukn is an accomplished professional in the field of
computer science and engineering. He currently holds the position of
Visiting Assistant Professor at the University of the District of Columbia,
where he combines teaching with his extensive background in IT
management and support, totaling over 13 years of experience in the
industry. In addition to his academic role, Dr. De Poukn is a Cisco
Certified Instructor, highlighting his expertise in network engineering and
cybersecurity.
Aside from his academic and professional pursuits, he actively
participates in community and cultural events. Notably, Dr. De Poukn has
been involved in celebrating Black History Month and promoting Ivorian
culture, which reflects his commitment to cultural representation and awareness. He is also fluent
in English, French, and Ewe.
A Ward 5 resident, Dr. De Poukn earned his Bachelor of Science in Computer and Information
Sciences, his Master of Science in Computer Science, and his Doctor of Philosophy in Computer
Science and Engineering from the University of the District of Columbia.
