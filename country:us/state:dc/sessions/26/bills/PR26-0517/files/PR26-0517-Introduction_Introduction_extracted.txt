17 To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to create sustainable economic
18 revitalization initiatives in the Rhode Island Avenue Corridor.
19
20 RESOLVED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
21 resolution may be cited as the “Rhode Island Avenue Corridor Long-Term Lease Adjustment
22 Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2026”.
23 Sec. 2. (a) The Economic Revitalization Initiatives Amendment Act of 2025 was passed
24 to create economic revitalization initiatives in several areas of the District, including the Rhode
25 Island Avenue Corridor. The Rhode Island Avenue Support Grant Program was to be established
26 in Fiscal Year 2026, with its purpose to award grants through a competitive process to eligible
27 businesses and eligible commercial property owners in the Rhode Island Avenue Corridor.
28 (b) In order to be eligible for this Program, in addition to other criteria, a business would
29 be required to sign or intend to sign a long-term lease of a commercial property in the Rhode
30 Island Avenue Corridor, while a commercial property owner must sign or intend to sign a long-
31 term lease with an eligible business for the commercial property in the Rhode Island Avenue
32 Corridor. The stated timeframe for a long-term lease was a lease period of no fewer than 10
33 years, exclusive of options. This timeframe differed from the original proposal of 5 years, which
1

34 was in line with industry standards. The impact of this longer timeframe may be a substantially
35 decreased pool of eligible businesses to revitalize the Rhode Island Avenue Corridor, and runs
36 counter to the purpose of the Economic Revitalization Initiatives Amendment Act of 2025.
37 (c) The underlying emergency would amend the Deputy Mayor for Planning and
38 Economic Development Limited Grant-Making Authority Act of 2012 to ensure that the
39 intended business population, which are those who sign or intend to sign a long-term lease, with
40 a period of no fewer than 5 years, of or for a commercial property in the Rhode Island Avenue
41 Corridor, are eligible for grants under the Rhode Island Avenue Support Grant Program.
42 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances in
43 section 2 constitute emergency circumstances, making it necessary that the Rhode Island Avenue
44 Corridor Long-Term Lease Adjustment Emergency Amendment Act of 2026 be adopted after a
45 single reading.
46 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
