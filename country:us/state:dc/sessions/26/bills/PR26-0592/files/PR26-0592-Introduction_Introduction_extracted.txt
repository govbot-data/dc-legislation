David Bediz
Mr. David Bediz is a licensed property manager and salesperson and the
founder of the Bediz Group. David was elected for two terms to the Board
of Directors of GCAAR, the area’s REALTOR association, with 8,800
agents and ownership in MRIS, the area’s only MLS provider. In addition,
he was elected for two terms to the Board of Directors to DCAR, the state-
level association of REALTORS.
His real estate experience began in 2000 as an entrepreneurial real
estate investor and then continued in real estate sales and consulting for
clients beginning in 2004. With five years of professional web design and
photography experience to boot, he brings a unique, personal marketing
perspective to sellers and the first-hand knowledge necessary for buyers.
He’s also extremely proud of his team, which he takes pride in directing with great care to ensure
the highest standards of customer service and market knowledge.
Owning as many as 20 rental properties at one time, Mr. Bediz understands the needs
of commercial and landlord clients and has direct knowledge of local laws and processes necessary
to operate a successful rental housing endeavor. In DC, rental housing laws are the toughest in
the nation for landlords, and he knows the pitfalls and shortcuts to obey the law and operate a
business efficiently and effectively.
In addition to Mr. Bediz’s real estate accomplishments, he also finds time to volunteer for
the Whitman-Walker Clinic, Food & Friends, Cherry and other local charities. He loves to cook,
is still in training for his private pilot’s license and has recently taken on oil painting as a hobby,
completing several works that have been displayed in local art shows.
A Ward 2 resident, Mr. Bediz earned his Bachelor of Arts in Fine Arts and History from
Duke University.
