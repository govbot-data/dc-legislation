ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-361
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
March 31, 2026
To confirm the reappointment of Donella Brockington to the Board of Library Trustees.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Board of Library Trustees Donella Brockington Confirmation
Resolution of 2026”.
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia confirms the reappointment of:
Donella Brockington
13th Place, NE
Washington, DC 20017
(Ward 5)
as a member of the Board of Library Trustees, established by section 4 of An Act To establish
and provide for the maintenance of a free public library and reading room in the District of
Columbia, approved June 3, 1896 (29 Stat. 244; D.C. Official Code § 39-104), for a term to end
January 5, 2031.
Sec. 3. The Council shall transmit a copy of this resolution, upon its adoption, to the
nominee and to the Mayor.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
