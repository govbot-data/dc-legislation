18 To amend the Rules of Organization and Procedures for the Council of the District of Columbia,
19 Council Period 26, Resolution of 2025, to authorize the Committee of the Whole to take
20 official action during the Council Recess.
21
22 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
23 resolution may be cited as the “Council Period 26 Second Recess Rules Amendment Resolution
24 of 2025”.
25 Sec. 2. The Rules of Organization and Procedure for the Council of the District of
26 Columbia, Council Period 26, Resolution of 2025 effective January 2, 2025 (Res. 26-1; 72 DCR
27 1289), is amended as follows:
28 (a) Section 306(b) is amended by adding a new paragraph (6) to read as follows:
29 “(5)(A) The Committee of the Whole may hold a committee meeting to take
30 official action on a measure during a recess of the Council, provided it files and circulates notice,
31 which shall be made publicly available, at least 48 hours before the meeting, of the date, hour,
32 place, of the meeting, and a list of measures to be considered. A draft agenda and a draft of each
33 measure to be considered at the meeting shall be circulated at least 24 hours before the meeting.
34 “(B) Upon circulation of a notice described in subparagraph (A), any
35 committee that is earlier in the sequence of a sequentially referred measure included in list of
1

36 measures to be considered by the Committee of the Whole may also hold a committee meeting to
37 take official action on the same measure prior to the meeting of the Committee of the Whole,
38 provided the committee files and circulates notice at least 24 hours before a meeting, of the date,
39 hour, and place of a committee meeting. Such notice shall include a copy of the agenda and a
40 draft of any measures to be considered at the meeting.
41 “(C) Notwithstanding any other rule, the adoption of the committee report
42 and the committee print on a measure by a committee described in subparagraph (B) shall render
43 the measure ripe for consideration by the Committee of the Whole, and such measure shall be
44 presented at the meeting described in subparagraph (A) in conjunction with the Committee of the
45 Whole markup of the same measure.”.
46 (c) This section shall expire on September 15, 2025.
47 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
