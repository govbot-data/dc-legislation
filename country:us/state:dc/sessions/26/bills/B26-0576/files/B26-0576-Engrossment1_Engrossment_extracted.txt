10 To amend, on a temporary basis, the Department of Transportation Establishment Act of 2022 to
11 authorize the Director to make a grant to Friends of Wangari Gardens for the assessment,
12 design, and installation of a water meter and potable water source directly on the Wangari
13 Gardens property with a spigot for gardeners’ free use.
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
16 act may be cited as the “Friends of Wangari Gardens Grant Temporary Amendment Act of
17 2026”.
18 Sec. 2. Section 3(c) of the Department of Transportation Establishment Act of 2022,
19 effective May 21, 2002 (D.C. Law 14-137; D.C. Official Code § 50-921.02(c)) is amended by
20 inserting a new paragraph (5) to read as follows:
21 “(5) Notwithstanding paragraph (1) of this subsection, the Director may issue a grant to
22 the Friends of Wangari Gardens for the purpose of assessment, design, and installation of a water
23 meter and potable water source directly on the Wangari Gardens property with a spigot for
24 gardeners’ free use.”
