10 To amend, on an temporary basis, the Green Building Act of 2006 to exempt certain residential and
11 nonresidential projects from maintaining net zero energy compliance and to modify net zero
12 energy compliance requirements for nonresidential and residential projects; to amend
13 section 2 of the Clean Energy DC Building Code Amendment Act of 2022 to modify the
14 definition of net-zero-energy standards and the types of projects for which the net-zero-
15 energy standards apply; and to repeal section 6 of the Housing Production Trust Fund Act of
16 1988 pertaining to additional net zero energy requirements for subsidized projects.
17
18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this act
19 may be cited as the “Net Zero Modification and Preservation Temporary Amendment Act of 2026”.
