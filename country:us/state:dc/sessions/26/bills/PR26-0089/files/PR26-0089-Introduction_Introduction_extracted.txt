Dear Chairman Mendelson:
Enclosed for consideration and enactment by the Council of the District of Columbia is an
emergency bill, the “Modification Nos. M02, M03, M04, and M05 to Contract No. DCAM-24-
NC-RFP-0003A with Jerome L. Taylor Trucking, Inc. Approval and Payment Authorization
Emergency Act of 2025”, and the accompanying emergency declaration resolution.
The legislation will approve Modification Nos. M02, M03, M04, and M05 to Contract No. DCAM-
24-NC-RFP-0003A with Jerome L. Taylor Trucking, Inc. to provide waste collection and disposal
services, and will authorize payment to Jerome L. Taylor Trucking, Inc. for services received and
to be received under option year one of the contract.
My administration is available to discuss any questions you may have regarding the proposed
legislation. In order to facilitate a response to any questions you may have, please contact Delano
Hunter, Director, DGS, or have your staff contact Eric Njonjo, Interim Chief Procurement Officer,
DGS, at (202) 727-2800.
I urge the Council to take prompt and favorable action on the enclosed legislation.
Sincerely,
Muriel Bowser

21 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
22 resolution may be cited as the "Modification Nos. M02, M03, M04, and MOS to Contract No.
23 DCAM-24-NC-RFP-0003A with Jerome L. Taylor Trucking, Inc. Approval and Payment
24 Authorization Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025".
25 Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to approve Modification Nos. M02, M03,
26 M04, and MOS to Contract No. DCAM-24-NC-RFP-0003A with Jerome L. Taylor Trucking,
27 Inc. to provide waste collection and disposal services, and to authorize payment for the goods
28 and services received and to be received under the modifications.
29 (b) On September 11, 2024, via Modification No. M02, the Department of General
30 Services ("DGS") partially exercised option year one of Contract No. DCAM-24-NC-RFP-
31 0003A in the not-to-exceed ("NTE") amount of $937,087.38 for the period October 1, 2024,
32 through November 18, 2024.

33 (c) On November 18, 2024, via Modification No. M03, DGS exercised an additional
34 partial option year one through February 24, 2025, at no additional cost.
35 (d) On January 13, 2025, via Modification No. M04, DGS exercised an additional partial
36 option year one through April 14, 2025, at no additional cost.
37 (e) Modification No. M05 will exercise the remainder of option year one through
38 September 30, 2025, and increase the NTE amount by $3,175,966.98, from $937,087.38 to
39 $4,113,054.36.
40 (f) Under section 451(b)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
41 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 803; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.51(b)(1)), Council approval is
42 necessary since the modifications will increase the value of the contract to more than $1 million
43 during a 12-month period.
44 (g) Approval is necessary to allow the continuation of these vital services. Without
45 approval, Jerome L. Taylor Trucking, Inc. cannot be paid for goods and services provided in
46 excess of $1 million for the period October 1, 2024, through September 20, 2025.
47 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
48 enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the
49 Modification Nos. M02, M03, M04, and M05 to Contract No. DCAM-24-NC-RFP-0003A with
50 Jerome L. Taylor Trucking, Inc. Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2025
51 be adopted after a single reading.
52 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
