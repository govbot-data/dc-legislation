ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-489
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
July 14, 2026
To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to amend the Omnibus Barry
Farm Redevelopment Act of 2022 to authorize the Surveyor to record the street and alley
closing and dedication plats in multiple phases.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Omnibus Barry Farm Redevelopment Clarification Emergency
Declaration Resolution of 2026”.
Sec. 2. (a) On September 20, 2022, the Council approved the Omnibus Barry Farm
Redevelopment Act, effective December 13, 2022 (D.C. Law 24-192; 69 DCR 12698)(“Barry
Farm Redevelopment Act”). The Barry Farm Redevelopment Act included provisions in sections
2 and 3 requiring the Surveyor to record street and alley closing plats and street and alley
dedication and designation plats in multiple phases to facilitate the phased nature of the
development project and to mitigate the adverse impacts of street and alley closures on vehicular
and pedestrian traffic flows.
(b) While the development project has made significant progress, including completion of
the Asberry at Barry Farm in 2024, and ongoing construction of the Edmonton, the project has
experienced delays that require an amendment to the Barry Farm Redevelopment Act to set new
deadlines for the recordation of the street and alley closing plats and the street and alley
dedication and designation plats.
Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the
Omnibus Barry Farm Redevelopment Clarification Emergency Amendment Act of 2026 be
adopted after a single reading.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
