14 To declare the existence of an emergency, due to congressional review, with respect to the need to
15 amend the Autonomous Vehicle Act of 2012 to prohibit driverless testing of autonomous
16 vehicles (“AV”) on District roadways without an AV testing permit and to require any
17 person or entity testing AVs with a test operator before an AV testing permit becomes
18 available to provide notice to the District Department of Transportation.
19
20 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
21 resolution may be cited as the “Autonomous Vehicle Testing Permit Requirement Congressional
22 Review Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
23 Sec. 2. (a) The Autonomous Vehicle Testing Program Amendment Act of 2020, effective
24 December 23, 2020 (D.C. Law 23-156; D.C. Official Code § 50-2351 et seq.), allowed autonomous
25 vehicle (“AV”) companies to test AVs on District roadways without an AV testing permit until
26 the District Department of Transportation (“DDOT”) makes the permit application available, after
27 which the AV company must apply for an AV testing permit or cease testing.
28 (b) To maintain interim requirements with which AV testing companies must comply as
29 DDOT finalizes its rulemaking on the AV testing, the Council passed the Autonomous Vehicle
30 Testing Permit Requirement Second Emergency Amendment Act of 2024, effective December 24,
31 2024 (D.C. Act 25-674; 72 DCR 14) (“emergency legislation”). The emergency legislation is set
32 to expire on March 24, 2025. The Council also passed the Autonomous Vehicle Testing Permit
33 Requirement Second Temporary Amendment Act of 2024, enacted on January 15, 2025 (D.C. Act
1

34 25-708; 72 DCR 832) (“temporary legislation”). The projected law date for the temporary
35 legislation is March 27, 2025.
36 (c) The Public Life and Activity Zones Amendment (“PLAZA”) Act of 2024, effective
37 January 24, 2025 (D.C. Act 25-715; 72 DCR 1085) (“permanent law”) incorporates and makes
38 permanent provisions from the emergency and temporary legislation. The projected law date for
39 the permanent act is also March 27, 2025.
40 (d) Without the provisions of the emergency legislation, temporary legislation, or
41 permanent law in place, DDOT cannot conduct proper oversight over AV operations in the
42 District, presenting safety challenges for all roadway users.
43 (e) This congressional review emergency legislation is necessary to prevent a gap in the
44 law between the expiration emergency legislation and the effective date of the temporary
45 legislation and permanent law.
46 Sec. 3. The Council finds that the circumstances enumerated in section 2 constitute
47 emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Autonomous Vehicle Testing Permit
48 Requirement Congressional Review Emergency Amendment Act of 2025 be adopted after a single
49 reading.
50 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
