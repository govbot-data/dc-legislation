Talib Shakir
Mr. Talib Shakir currently serves as Acting Director of the Office on
Returning Citizen Affairs. He previously served as Deputy Director of
Community Relations for the Mayor’s Office of Community Relations
and Services, where he oversaw citywide constituent engagement, multi-
agency coordination, and operational support across all eight wards.
Mr. Shakir brings more than a decade of experience in District
Government and the nonprofit sector, with a strong background in field
operations, crisis navigation, and service delivery for residents facing
complex barriers, including justice-involved individuals. His lived
experience navigating the criminal justice system enhances his ability to
build trust, understand reentry challenges, and lead programs grounded
in equity, access, and community impact. Last year, Mr. Shakir was appointed Commercial Safe
Corridor Manager, coordinating engagement and operations at the Chinatown and Anacostia Hubs.
A Ward 1 resident, Mr. Shakir earned a certification in victim advocacy from Adams State
University and studied entrepreneurial and small business operations at Georgetown University.
