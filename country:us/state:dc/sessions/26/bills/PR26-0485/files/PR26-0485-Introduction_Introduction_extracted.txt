Rachna Butani Bhatt
Ms. Rachna Butani Bhatt is the HRGM Corporation Director and a
current principal member of the Water and Sewer Authority Board of
Directors.
In her over twenty-year tenure with HRGM, Ms. Bhatt has supervised a
multi-million-dollar portfolio of large commercial construction projects
for State and Federal government agencies. Ms. Bhatt is the former
Deloitte and Touche, LLP Financial Management Consultant. Ms. Bhatt
has also served on Board of Directors for the Professional Women in
Construction, National Child Research Center, and the DC Chamber of
Commerce Foundation. Ms. Bhatt is certified as a LEED AP and holds a
Class A Contractors License for Virginia.
A Ward 3 resident, Ms. Bhatt received a Bachelor of Science in Business Administration from
Georgetown University and a Master of Business Administration from the University of
Pennsylvania.
