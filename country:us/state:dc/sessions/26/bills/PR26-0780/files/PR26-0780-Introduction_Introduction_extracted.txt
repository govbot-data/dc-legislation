10 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
11
12 ________________
13
14 To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to amend section 47-1332 of
15 the District of Columbia Official Code to cancel the tax sale scheduled for July 15, 2026,
16 prohibit any tax sale from being held until September 20, 2026 at the earliest, and to
17 require notice of the cancellation be mailed to affected property owners.
18
19 RESOLVED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
20 resolution may be cited as the “Real Property Tax Sale Delay Emergency Declaration Resolution
21 of 2026”.
22 Sec. 2. (a) Given the United States Supreme Court’s decision in Pung v. Isabella County,
23 Michigan, 609 U.S. ___, Case No. 25-95 (June 23, 2026), and other recent jurisprudence, the
24 Council believes that it is necessary to work on legislation to modernize the District’s tax sale
25 process and enhance equity protections for District property owners.
26 (b) Currently, a tax sale is scheduled to take place on July 15, 2026. Since there are
27 ongoing questions about the District’s tax sale process in light of Pung and other recent
28 jurisprudence, it is necessary to delay the sale and prohibit any other tax sales from occurring
29 until at least September 20, 2026 to give the Council more time to examine the issues raised by
30 said jurisprudence.
1

31 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
32 enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Real
33 Property Tax Sale Delay Emergency Amendment Act of 2026 be adopted after a single reading.
34 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
