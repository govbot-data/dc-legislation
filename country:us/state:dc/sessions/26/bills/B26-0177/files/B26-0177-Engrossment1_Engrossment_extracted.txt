10 To amend the Rental Housing Act of 1985 to prevent housing providers from evicting tenants on
11 any day when the temperature is predicted to be above 95 degrees.
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
14 act may be cited as the "Extreme Heat Eviction Protection Amendment Act of 2025".
15 Sec 2. Section 501(k) of the Rental Housing Act of 1985, effective July 17, 1985 (D.C.
16 Law 6-10; D.C. Code § 42-3505.01(k)), is amended as follows:
17 (a) Strike the phrase "Celsius; or" and insert the phrase "Celsius;" in its place.
18 (b) Strike the phrase "rental unit." and insert the phrase "rental unit; or" in its place.
19 (c) Add a new paragraph (3) to read follows:
20 "(3) On any day when the National Weather Service predicts at 8:00 a.m. the temperature
21 at the National Airport weather station will rise above 95 degrees Fahrenheit or 35 degrees
22 Celsius.".
23 Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
24 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement in the committee report as the fiscal
25 impact statement required by 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975, approved
26 October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
