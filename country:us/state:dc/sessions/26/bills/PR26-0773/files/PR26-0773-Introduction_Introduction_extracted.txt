19 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this

23 Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to approve Modification Nos. M0I and M02

25 and services received and to be received under the contract.
26 (b) On January 20, 2026, the Office of Contracting and Procurement ("OCP"), on behalf
27 of the Department of Public Works, executed Contract No. CWI 31644 with Shaw Solutions
28 LLC to provide equipment and qualified operators for snow plowing and removal in the not-to
29 exceed amount of $1 million for the period from January 20, 2026, through January 19, 2027.
30 (c) On January 22, 2026, by Emergency Modification No. M0l, OCP increased the not
31 to-exceed amount by $4 million, to a total not-to-exceed amount of $5 million.
1

32 (d) On June 2, 2026, OCP executed Emergency Modification No. M02, an administrative
33 modification to clarify the price schedule, but did not change the total not-to-exceed amount of
34 $5 million.
35 (f) Under section 451(b)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
36 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 803; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.51(b)(1)), Council approval is
37 necessary since the modifications increased the value of the contract by more than $1 million
38 during a 12-month period.
39 (g) Approval is necessary to pay for these vital goods and services. Without this
40 approval, Shaw Solutions LLC cannot be paid for goods and services provided in excess of $1
41 million for the period from January 20, 2026, through January 19, 2027.
42 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2 constitute
43 emergency circumstances making it necessary that Modification Nos. M01 and M02 to Contract
44 No. CW131644 with Shaw Solutions LLC Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Act
45 of 2026 be adopted after a single reading.
46 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
