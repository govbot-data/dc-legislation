10 To order the closing of the former Frederick Douglass Memorial Bridge Right of Way on Lot 817
11 in Square 708 in Southeast Washington, D.C., in Ward 8.
12
13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, that this
14 act may be cited as the “Closing of the former Frederick Douglass Memorial Bridge Right of
15 Way on Lot 817 in Square 708, S.O. 23-05325, Act of 2025”.
16 Sec. 2. Pursuant to section 404 of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
17 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 813; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.04), and consistent with the Street
18 and Alley Closing and Acquisition Procedures Act of 1982, effective March 10, 1983 (D.C. Law
19 4-201; D.C. Official Code § 9-201.01 et seq.), the Council finds that the former Frederick
20 Douglass Memorial Bridge approach Right of Way on Lot 817 in Square 708, as shown on the
21 Surveyor’s plat filed under S.O. 23-05325, is unnecessary for street purposes, and orders it
22 closed, with title to the land to vest as shown on the Surveyor’s plat.
