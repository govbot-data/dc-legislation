8 A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
9
10 _________________________
11
12 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
13
14 _________________________
15
16
17 To recognize honoree Dr. David Johns during the 2025 celebration of DC Black Pride.
18
19 WHEREAS, Dr. David Johns is a nationally recognized education policy expert, equity
20 advocate, and CEO of the National Black Justice Coalition (NBJC);
21
22 WHEREAS, from serving as the first Executive Director of the White House Initiative on
23 Educational Excellence for African Americans under President Obama to shaping policy on
24 Capitol Hill, Dr. David Johns has dedicated his career to uplifting Black LGBTQ+/SGL
25 communities;
26
27 WHEREAS, with degrees from Columbia University and a background as an elementary
28 school teacher, Dr. David Johns’ work blends heart and scholarship;
29
30 WHEREAS, named to Time Magazine’s 2025 “Closers” list, and recognized by BET,
31 Out100, Ebony, and The Root, Dr. David Johns is a bold voice at the forefront of civil rights
32 leadership;
33
34 WHEREAS, DC Black Pride is an annual event to increase awareness of and showcase
35 pride in the diversity of the LGBTQIA+ populations in the African American community, and to
36 support organizations that focus on health disparities, education, youth, and families;
37
38 WHEREAS, as the very first Black Pride festival in the world, DC Black Pride fostered
39 the beginning of the Center for Black Equity (formerly known as the International Federation of
40 Black Prides, Inc. (IFBP)) and the “Black Pride Movement”;
41
42 WHEREAS, since the birth of DC Black Pride, more than 50 other Black Pride
43 celebrations now take place throughout the world and on 4 continents, with many using DC
44 Black Pride as a model;
1

45
46 WHEREAS, May 23, 2025, through May 26, 2025, marks the 34th annual DC Black
47 Pride celebration;
48
49 WHEREAS, the theme for this year’s celebration is “BLACK PRIDE IS FREEDOM”;
50
51 WHEREAS, for more than 30 years, DC Black Pride has been an opportunity for the
52 Black LGBTQIA+ community to gather and celebrate being both African American and
53 members of LGBTQIA+ communities;
54
55 WHEREAS, DC Black Pride is the oldest and one of the largest Black Pride events in the
56 world, drawing thousands of residents and visitors from around the globe; and
57
58 WHEREAS, DC Black Pride brings more than 500,000 people to the nation’s capital
59 from across the United States, Canada, the Caribbean, South Africa, Great Britain, France,
60 Germany, and the Netherlands and will carry that tradition this year and into the future to
61 celebrate their shared community, raise awareness and funding for HIV/AIDS prevention, and
62 stand against homophobia inspired by violence and bigotry.
63
64 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That
65 this resolution be cited as the “Dr. David Johns Recognition Resolution of 2025”.
66
67 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia recognizes and celebrates Dr. David
68 Johns during the 2025 celebration of DC Black Pride.
69
70 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
