ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-63
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
April 1, 2025
To reappoint Lyle M. Blanchard to the District of Columbia Retirement Board.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “District of Columbia Retirement Board Lyle M. Blanchard
Reappointment Resolution of 2025”.
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia reappoints:
Lyle M. Blanchard
32nd Street, NW
Washington, DC 20015
(Ward 4)
as a member of the District of Columbia Retirement Board, established by section 121 of the
District of Columbia Retirement Reform Act, approved November 17, 1979 (93 Stat. 869; D.C.
Official Code § 1-711), for a 4-year term to end January 27, 2029.
Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia shall transmit a copy of this resolution,
upon its adoption, to the appointee, to the chairperson of the District of Columbia Retirement
Board, and to the Mayor.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
