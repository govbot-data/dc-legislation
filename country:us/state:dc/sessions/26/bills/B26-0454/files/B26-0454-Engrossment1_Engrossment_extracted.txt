ENGROSSED ORIGINAL
A BILL
26-454
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
_________________________
1 To authorize, on an temporary basis, the Chief Financial Officer to grant real property tax relief
2 to real property described as Lot 123, Square 2836, located at 3421 14th Street, N.W., in
3 Ward 1.
4
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
6 act may be cited as the “Avanti Real Estate Services, LLC Real Property Tax Relief Temporary
7 Act of 2025”.
8 Sec. 2. (a) In Fiscal Year 2026, the Chief Financial Officer may forgive up to $377,000 in
9 real property taxes, interest, penalties, fees, or other related charges assessed, as of the effective
10 date of the Fiscal Year 2026 Budget Support Emergency Act of 2025, enacted September 3,
11 2025 (D.C. Act 26-146; 72 DCR 9623), against the real property designated for tax purposes as
12 Lot 123, Square 2836, located at 3421 14th Street, N.W., ("Property"), which is owned by Avanti
13 Real Estate Services, LLC; provided, that the Property is occupied by Avanti Real Estate
14 Services, LLC and is used to further the purposes of providing real estate and brokerage services,
15 specializing in creating generational wealth through homeownership, employing District

16 residents, and providing education and training for employment opportunities and careers within
17 the real estate industry.
18 (b) The Council further orders that all tax sales of the Property conducted under Chapter
19 13A of Title 47 of the D.C. Official Code to collect delinquencies forgiven by subsection (a)
20 shall be cancelled pursuant to D.C. Official Code § 47-1366.
21 Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
22 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Budget Director as the fiscal impact
23 statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975, approved
24 October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
25 Sec. 4. Effective date.
26 (a) This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by
27 the Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), a 30-day period of congressional review
28 as provided in section 602(c)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December
29 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 813; D.C. Official Code § 1-206.02(c)(1)), and publication in the District of
30 Columbia Register.
31 (b) This act shall expire after 225 days of its having taken effect.
