16 To amend the District of Columbia Revenue Act of 1937 to authorize the Mayor to design and
17 issue motor vehicle identification tags with a design, identifying words, or emblem that
18 demonstrates support for the return of the Washington Commanders football team to the
19 District, and to direct that the proceeds be used to support the State Athletic Activities,
20 Programs, and Office Fund; and to make a conforming amendment.
21
22 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
23 act may be cited as the “Washington Commanders Motor Vehicle Identification Tags
24 Amendment Act of 2025”.
25 Sec. 2. Title IV of the District of Columbia Revenue Act of 1937, approved August 17,
26 1937 (50 Stat. 679; D.C. Official Code § 50-1501.01 et seq.), is amended as follows:
27 (a) A new section 2o is added to read as follows:
28 “Sec. 2o. Issuance of Washington Commanders motor vehicle identification tags.
29 “(a) The Mayor may design and make available for issue one or more Washington
30 Commanders motor vehicle identification tags to demonstrate support for the return of the
31 Washington Commanders football team to the District.
1

32 “(b)(1) A resident ordering a Washington Commanders tag shall pay a one-time
33 application fee and a display fee each year thereafter. The application fee shall be $25, and the
34 display fee shall be $20, or such other amounts as the Mayor may establish by rule.
35 "(2) The application and annual display fees shall be deposited into the State
36 Athletic Activities, Programs, and Office Fund, established by § 38–2672.
37 (b) Section 3 (D.C. Official Code § 50-1501.03) is amended as follows:
38 (1) Subsection (a)(1) is amended by adding a new subparagraph (S) to read as follows:
39 “(S) Any person ordering a Washington Commanders identification tag shall pay the fees
40 set forth in § 50-1501.02o(b)(1).
41 (2) Subsection (d) is amended as follows:
42 (A) Paragraph (15) is amended by striking the phrase “; and” and inserting a
43 semicolon in its place.
44 (B) Paragraph (16) is amended by striking the period and inserting the phrase “;
45 and” in its place.
46 (C) A new paragraph (17) is added to read as follows:
47 “(17) The fees collected for the Washington Commanders identification tags under
48 section 2o shall be deposited into the State Athletic Activities, Programs, and Office Fund,
49 established by § 38–2672.”.

53 (a) Section 4033 (D.C. Official Code § 38-2672) is amended as follows:

54 (1) Subsection (c) is amended as follows:
55 (A) Paragraph (2)(E) is amended by striking the phrase “; and” and

65 Sec. 4. Applicability
66 (a) This act shall apply upon the date of inclusion of its fiscal effect in an approved
67 budget and financial plan.
68 (b) The Chief Financial Officer shall certify the date of the inclusion of the fiscal effect in
69 an approved budget and financial plan, and provide notice to the Budget Director of the Council
70 for certification.
71 (c)(1) The Budget Director shall cause the notice of the certification to be published in
72 the District of Columbia Register.
73 (2) The date of publication of the notice of the certification shall not affect the
74 applicability of this act.
