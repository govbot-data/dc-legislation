ENROLLED ORIGINAL
AN ACT
_________________________
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
_________________________
To amend section 104 of the Prevention of Child Abuse and Neglect Act of 1977 to require the
Child and Family Services Agency to inform, at the initiation of an investigation,
caretakers of any legal resources available to them, the availability of the Office of the
Ombudsperson for Children as a resource for complaints, and their right to counsel
during critical stages of neglect proceedings.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
act may be cited as the “Empowering Parents in CFSA Investigations Amendment Act of 2026”.
Sec. 2. Section 104(c)(3) of the Prevention of Child Abuse and Neglect Act of 1977,
effective September 23, 1977 (D.C. Law 2-22; D.C. Official Code § 4-1301.04(c)(3)), is
amended as follows:
(a) Subparagraph (B) is amended to read as follows:
“(B) Conducting an interview with the child’s caretaker or caretakers, during
which time the Agency shall inform the caretaker of their right to counsel pursuant to D.C.
Official Code § 16-2304(b)(1), legal resources available to the caretaker, and the availability of
the Ombudsperson for Children as a resource to receive complaints pursuant to section 104 of
the Office of the Ombudsperson for Children Establishment Amendment Act of 2020, effective
April 5, 2021 (D.C. law 23-270; D.C. Official Code § 4–671.04).”.
(b) Subparagraph (F) is amended by striking the phrase “; and” and inserting a semicolon
in its place.
(c) Subparagraph (G) is amended by striking the period and inserting the phrase “; and”
in its place.
(d) A new subparagraph (H) is added to read as follows:
“(H) Written notification of legal resources available to the child’s reported
caretaker or caretakers at the initiation of the investigation process.”.
Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement in the committee report as the fiscal
