14 To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to amend the Omnibus Barry
15 Farm Redevelopment Act of 2022 to authorize the Surveyor to record the street and alley
16 closing and dedication plats in multiple phases.
17
18 RESOLVED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
19 resolution may be cited as the “The Omnibus Barry Farm Redevelopment Clarification
20 Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2026”.
21 Sec. 2. (a) On September 20, 2022, the Council approved the “Omnibus Barry Farm
22 Redevelopment Act” (D.C. Law 24-192; 69 DCR 15293)(“Barry Farm Redevelopment Act”).
23 The Barry Farm Redevelopment Act included provisions in Sections 2 and 3 requiring the
24 Surveyor to record street and alley closing plats and street and alley dedication and designation
25 plats in multiple phases to facilitate the phased nature of the development project and mitigate
26 the adverse impacts of street and alley closures on vehicular and pedestrian traffic flows.
27 (b) While the development project has made significant progress, including completion of
28 the Asberry at Barry Farm in 2024, and ongoing construction of the Edmonton, the project has
29 experienced delays that require an amendment to the Barry Farm Redevelopment Act to set new
30 deadlines for the recordation of the street and alley closing plats and the street and alley
31 dedication and designation plats.
1

32 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
33 enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the
34 Omnibus Barry Farm Redevelopment Clarification Emergency Amendment Act of 2026 be
35 adopted after a single reading.
36 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
