ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-0106
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
May 6, 2025
To confirm the appointment of Dr. Antoinette Mitchell as the State Superintendent of Education.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “State Superintendent of Education Dr. Antoinette Mitchell
Confirmation Resolution of 2025”.
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia confirms the appointment of:
Dr. Antoinette Mitchell
Iowa Ave, NW
Washington, DC 20011
(Ward 4)
as the State Superintendent of Education, established by section 2 of the State Education Office
Establishment Act of 2000, effective October 21, 2000 (D.C. Law 13-176; D.C. Official Code §
38-2601), to serve a 4-year term.
Sec. 3. The Council shall transmit a copy of this resolution, upon its adoption, to the
nominee and to the Mayor.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
