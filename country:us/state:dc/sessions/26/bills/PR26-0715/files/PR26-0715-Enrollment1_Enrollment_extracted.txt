ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-438
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
June 30, 2026
To confirm the appointment of Joshua Blecher-Cohen as a member of the Commission on
Human Rights.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Commission on Human Rights Joshua Blecher-Cohen
Confirmation Resolution of 2026”.
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia confirms the appointment of:
Joshua Blecher-Cohen
K Street, NE
Washington, DC 20002
(Ward 6)
as a member of the Commission on Human Rights, established by section 401 of the Human
Rights Act of 1977, effective December 7, 2004 (D.C. Law 15-216; D.C. Official Code § 2-
1404.01), for a term to end December 31, 2027.
Sec. 3. The Council shall transmit a copy of this resolution, upon its adoption, to the
nominee and to the Mayor.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
