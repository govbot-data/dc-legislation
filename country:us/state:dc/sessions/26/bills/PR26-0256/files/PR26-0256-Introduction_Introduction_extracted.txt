7 A PROPOSED RESOLUTION
8 _________________
9
10 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
11 _________________________
12
13 To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to continue the
14 extension of the Streatery Program endorsements through December 31, 2025 and
15 to clarify endorsement deadlines for certain classes of licensees; and to amend the
16 Legalization of Marijuana for Medical Treatment Initiative of 1999 to make a
17 clarifying amendment.
18
19 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That
20 this resolution may be cited as the “Streatery Program Endorsement Extension and
21 Medical Cannabis Clarification Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
22
23 Sec. 2. (a) On December 3, 2024, the Council enacted the Streatery Program
24 Endorsement Extension Emergency Amendment Act of 2024, (D.C. Act 25-669; 71 DCR
25 16311), and the Streatery Program Endorsement Extension Temporary Amendment Act
26 of 2024, effective March 7, 2025 (D.C. Law 25-294; 72 DCR 3105) (“temporary
27 legislation”), to allow licensed establishments that register with the Alcoholic Beverage
28 and Cannabis Board to offer beer, wine, or spirits for sale on expanded outdoor space
29 until July 31, 2025 (“Streatery Program”), and to clarify the geographic requirements for
30 medical cannabis license holders and applicants.
31 (b) On April 30, 2025, the Committee on Business and Economic Development
32 held a hearing on B26-157, the “Streatery Program and Protest Process Amendment Act
33 of 2025”. On May 2, 2025, the District Department of Transportation (“DDOT”) sent the
34 Committee a letter in response to its hearing and released proposed rulemaking for the
1

35 permanent Streatery program. The permanent legislation must complete the legislative
36 process.
37 (d) It is necessary that the provisions of the temporary legislation continue
38 without interruption, until the permanent legislation is in effect.
39 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2
40 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Streatery Program
41 Endorsement Extension and Medical Cannabis Clarification Emergency Amendment Act
42 of 2025 be adopted after a single reading.
43 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
