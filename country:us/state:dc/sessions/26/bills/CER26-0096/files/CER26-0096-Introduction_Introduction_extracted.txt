16 To recognize and honor Dr. Dock Winston on the occasion of the conclusion of his term as
17 President of the Medical Society of the District of Columbia.
18
19 WHEREAS, Dr. Dock Winston completed his medical training at Rutgers University-New
20 Jersey Medical School, earned a Master of Public Health degree with a concentration in
21 community health from Harvard University, and holds a Master of Business Administration from
22 the Wharton School of the University of Pennsylvania;
23
24 WHEREAS, Dr. Winston is a urologist with the Mid-Atlantic Medical Group (MAPMG),
25 where he has practiced general urology with a special interest in reconstruction surgery, men’s
26 sexual health, and urological cancer survivorship since 2015;
27
28 WHEREAS, Dr. Winston introduced several cutting-edge procedures to the practice of
29 urology, including Rezum and UroLift, expanding treatment options for patients while reducing
30 recovery times and improving qualify of life;
31
32 WHEREAS, Dr. Winston developed and moderated the first Schwartz Rounds for
33 MAPMG, fostering open dialogue and a culture of compassion, resilience, and shared learning
34 among clinicians;
35
36 WHEREAS, Dr. Winston created and implemented the Live and Learn program, an
37 evolution of the traditional Morbidity and Mortality conference, broadening the scope to examine
38 the full spectrum of the physician experience, including professional growth, resilience, and future
39 of medical practice;
40
41 WHEREAS, Dr. Winston conceived and the led to the development of an online surgical
42 scheduling platform, introducing an asynchronous, patient-centered method for securing surgical
43 slots that streamlined workflows and enhanced patient convenience;
44
1

45 WHEREAS, Dr. Winston played a pivotal role in the implementation and growth of the
46 ambulatory robotic surgery program, advancing minimally invasive surgical care in the outpatient
47 setting and setting a new standard for efficiency and patient outcomes;
48
49 WHEREAS, in addition to his clinical responsibilities, Dr. Winston serves as the Assistant
50 Physician-in-Chief for Perioperative Services and Regional Chief of Vascular Surgery with
51 MAPMG;
52
53 WHEREAS, Dr. Winston is a Ward 6 resident and has been a member of the Medical
54 Society of the District of Columbia (MSDC) since 2016, in addition to his memberships in the
55 American Urological Association and the World Professional Association for Transgender Health;
56
57 WHEREAS, Dr. Winston has been regularly featured as a trusted medical expert in national
58 and local media outlets, including NBC and AARP, on topics ranging from kidney stones to sexual
59 wellness, broadening public understanding of urological health;
60
61 WHEREAS, Dr. Winston has been honored multiple times as a Top Doctor by
62 Washingtonian magazine, underscoring peer recognition and sustained excellence in patient care;
63
64 WHEREAS, Dr. Winston has volunteered as a physician speaker on men’s health as part
65 of the MedStar Family Choice CommUNITY events;
66
67 WHEREAS, in 2019, Dr. Winston became a member of the MSDC Board of Directors,
68 and was inaugurated as MSDC’s 157th President on November 1, 2024;
69
70 WHEREAS, in 2025, Dr. Winston guided MSDC to work with the Council and the District
71 government to navigate complex changes being proposed to the healthcare system;
72
73 WHEREAS, Dr. Winston participated with the DC Health Benefits Exchange on its Basic
74 Health Plan Advisory Council, providing invaluable expertise and guidance; and
75
76 WHEREAS, Dr. Winston concludes his term as President of MSDC on November 3, 2025,
77 a term defined by exemplary engagement, leadership, and guidance to the District’s medical
78 community in addition to the Council and the District government.
79
80 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, that this
81 resolution may be cited as the “Dr. Dock Winston Recognition Resolution of 2025”.
82
83 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia recognizes and honors Dr. Dock Winston
84 on the occasion of the conclusion of his term as President of the Medical Society of the District of
85 Columbia.
86
87 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
