11 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA

13 ____________________
14
15 To honor and recognize Taylor Townsend for her historic victory in women’s doubles at the
16 2025 Mubadala Citi DC Open and for her extraordinary achievement of becoming the first
17 mother in professional tennis history to reach world No. 1 in any discipline.
18 WHEREAS, Taylor Townsend, born in Chicago, Illinois, turned professional in 2012 and
19 has since emerged as one of the most accomplished and inspiring athletes in American tennis,
20 recognized for her powerful left-handed play, aggressive net game, and resilience in overcoming
21 adversity;
22 WHEREAS, Taylor Townsend, alongside her partner Katerina Siniaková, captured the
23 women’s doubles title at the 2025 Mubadala Citi DC Open, adding to her growing list of
24 championships and further establishing her as one of the top doubles players in the world;
25 WHEREAS, this triumph in Washington, D.C. marked not only a tournament victory but
26 also propelled Taylor Townsend to the WTA world No. 1 doubles ranking, a milestone that made
27 her the first mother in professional tennis history to achieve a No. 1 ranking in any discipline;
28 WHEREAS, Taylor Townsend’s victory at the Mubadala Citi DC Open holds special
29 significance for the District of Columbia, a city where she has trained and competed, and where
30 her success stands as an inspiration to residents, particularly young athletes who aspire to reach
31 the highest levels of competition;
32 WHEREAS, Taylor Townsend’s accomplishments are not limited to athletic
33 performance, as she has become a symbol of resilience and representation in professional sports,
34 overcoming barriers of body-shaming, institutional bias, and balancing the responsibilities of
35 motherhood while excelling on the world stage;
36 WHEREAS, through her victories at Wimbledon (2024), the Australian Open (2025), and
37 now the Mubadala Citi DC Open (2025), Taylor Townsend has cemented her place among the

38 most decorated doubles players in recent years and continues to inspire with her perseverance
39 and passion;
40 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, that this
41 resolution may be cited as the “Taylor Townsend Mubadala Citi DC Open Recognition
42 Resolution of 2025.”
43 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia recognizes and honors Taylor Townsend for her
44 extraordinary athletic achievements and congratulates her on her championship victory at the
45 Mubadala Citi DC Open. The Council commends her for making history as the first mother to
46 reach the world No. 1 doubles ranking, applauds her continued leadership as a role model for
47 athletes everywhere, and celebrates her contributions to the spirit of perseverance, excellence,
48 and representation in sports.
49
50 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately upon the first date of publication in the
51 District of Columbia Register.
