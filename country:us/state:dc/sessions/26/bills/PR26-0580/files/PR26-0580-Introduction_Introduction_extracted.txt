Dear Chairman Mendelson:
Enclosed for consideration and enactment by the Council of the District of Columbia is an
emergency bill, the “Contract No. CW127336 with Compost Crew, Inc. Approval and Payment
Authorization Emergency Act of 2026,” and the accompanying emergency declaration resolution
in the amount of not-to-exceed $4,120,588.45.
The legislation will approve definitive Contract No. CW127336 with Compost Crew, Inc. to
provide organic waste collection, hauling, and processing services for single family residential
households in the District, including the provision of waste bins to participating households.
Legislation is required to correct an error in the Compost Crew, Inc. contract proposal.
My administration is available to discuss any questions you may have regarding the proposed
contract. In order to facilitate a response to any questions you may have, please have your staff
contact Marc Scott, Chief Operating Officer, Office of Contracting and Procurement, at (202) 724-
8759.
I urge the Council to take prompt and favorable action on the enclosed legislation.
Sincerely,
Muriel Bowser

21 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
22 resolution may be cited as the "Contract No. CW127336 with Compost Crew, Inc. Approval and
23 Payment Authorization Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2026".
24 Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to approve Contract No. CW127336 with
25 Compost Crew, Inc. and to authorize payment for the goods and services received and to be
26 received under the contract.
27 (b) On January 15, 2025, the Office of Contracting and Procurement ("OCP"), on behalf
28 of the Department of Public Works, executed a letter contract (Contract No. CW127336) with
29 Compost Crew, Inc. in the not-to-exceed amount of $1,000,000 to provide organic waste
30 collection, hauling, and processing services from single-family residential households in the
31 District, including the provision of waste bins to participating households.
32 (c) According to the terms of the letter contract, OCP must definitize Contract No.
33 CW127336 by April 15, 2025, for the period January 15, 2026, through January 14, 2027. The
1

34 not-to-exceed amount of Contract No. 127336 is $4,120,588.45 and the minimum amount is
35 $117.35.
36 (d) It has come to the attention of OCP that Compost Crew, Inc.’s contract proposal for
37 Contract No. 127336 should have been rejected as non-responsive because it failed clearly to
38 confirm in its subcontracting plan that 35% of the base year contract price would be
39 subcontracted to small business enterprises and certified business enterprises as required by
40 sections 2346(d)(1) and (3) and (e) of the Small and Certified Business Enterprise Development
41 and Assistance Act of 2005, effective October 20, 2005 (D.C. Law 16-33; D.C. Official Code §
42 2-218.46(d)(1) and (3) and (e)). The Office of the Attorney General has requested the subject
43 legislation to correct this error.
44 (e) Council approval is now necessary to allow the continuation of these vital services.
45 Without this approval, Compost Crew, Inc. cannot be paid for goods and services provided in
46 excess of $1 million for the period from January 15, 2026, through January 14, 2027.
47 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2 constitute
48 emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Contract No. CW127336 with Compost
49 Crew, Inc. Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2026 be adopted after a
50 single reading.
51 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
