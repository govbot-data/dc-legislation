Ms. Brianna Jones is a QAPI-certified, licensed nursing home
administrator with extensive experience in long-term care leadership. In
her current role as Administrator of Inspire Rehabilitation and Health
Center, she leads interdisciplinary teams including nursing, therapy,
social services, dietary, EVS, and maintenance to deliver high-quality

Ms. Jones is passionate about intersectional approaches to minority and
middle market aging services, with a particular emphasis on improving
access, equity, and quality outcomes for diverse older adult populations.
She has been recognized for her leadership and service, including the
Howard University Health Management Leadership and Community Service Award (2016), the
Provost’s Distinguished Service Award (2021), and the LCS Senior Living Management
Scholarship (2021).
A Ward 5 resident, Ms. Brianna Jones earned her Bachelor of Science in Health Science from
