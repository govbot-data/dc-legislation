Helaina Roisman
Ms. Helaina Roisman is the Injury Prevention and Outreach Coordinator
at George Washington University Hospital. She works to help prevent
injuries, including alcohol- and drug-related prevention and violence
related-prevention. She works with trauma teams, intensive care unit
staff, and patients to gain a better understanding of common ways to
decrease injuries.
In her role at George Washington University Hospital, Ms. Roisman also
runs the hospital’s Trauma Survivors Network, which entails meeting
with patients, offers resources, and provides opportunities for trauma
survivors to meet.
A Ward 1 resident, Ms. Roisman earned her Bachelor of Arts from New York University and
her Master of Social Work from the Catholic University of America.
