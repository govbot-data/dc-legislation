Company (National IPA) through the City of Fort Worth, TX. The District exercised option years
one and two under the contract. This action represents a shortened period of performance for option
year three.

logistics, additives and strategic solutions across North America. The contractor has been delivering
approximately three billion gallons of fuel and complementary products annually from their 900 fuel

(L) The reason why the proposed change was sent to Council for approval after it is intended to
take effect:
This is a cooperative procurement. The District could not exercise the option year three of its task
order until the City of Fort Worth extended its contract term. The extension did not occur until
November 10, 2024, which did not leave sufficient time for the action to be submitted to the
Council for approval prior to the start of the option period.
(M) The reason for the proposed change:
The Department of Public Works is responsible for providing fuel to all of the District’s fleet. This
request seeks to exercise the final partial option year option of the contract. There was not
sufficient time for the option period to receive Council approval prior to the start date.
