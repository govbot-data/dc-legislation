11 To amend, on a temporary basis, the Advisory Neighborhood Commissions Act of 1975 to
12 modify the notice requirement for certain business licenses; and to amend Chapter 28 of
13 Title 47 of the District of Columbia Official Code to authorize 4-year basic business
14 licenses, to allow licensees to renew their expired licenses after 9 months, and to
15 authorize the Mayor to issue refunds for erroneously paid business license fees.
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
18 act may be cited as the “Revised Business Licensing Reform Temporary Amendment Act of
19 2025”.
20 Sec. 2. Section 13(q) of the Advisory Neighborhood Commissions Act of 1975, effective
21 March 26, 1976 (D.C. Law 1-58; D.C. Official Code § 1-309.10(q)), is amended to read as
22 follows:
23 “(q)(1) Before granting or renewing a license for a theater, cinema, skating rink, dance
24 hall, exhibition, lecture, or other entertainment, the Department of Licensing and Consumer
25 Protection shall give 30-days notice by first-class mail or electronic mail to the affected
26 Commission. The notice shall contain the name of the applicant and a description, by street
27 number, or other plain designation, of the particular location for which the license is requested.
