15 To amend, on a temporary basis, section 1507 of the Criminal Justice Coordinating Council for
16 the District of Columbia Establishment Act of 2001 to change the date by which the
17 Prearrest Diversion Task Force shall issue initial recommendations for prearrest diversion
18 of certain misdemeanor offenses and categories of persons to July 2026; and to amend
19 Title 20 of the District of Columbia Official Code to allow for notice of request for
20 formal probate, notice from a foreign personal representative of a decedent who owned
21 any property located in the District of Columbia, and notice of appointment to interested
22 persons, creditors and unknown heirs in a legal periodical or newspaper of general
23 circulation in the District.
24
25 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
26 act may be cited as the “Prearrest Diversion Task Force Recommendations Temporary
27 Amendment Act of 2026”.
28 Sec. 2. Section 1507 of the Criminal Justice Coordinating Council for the District of
29 Columbia Establishment Act of 2001, effective June 8, 2024 (D.C. Law 25-175; D.C. Official
30 Code § 22-4237), is amended as follows:
31 (a) Subsection (g) is amended by striking the phrase “Within 3 months after
32 the applicability date of section 29 of the Secure DC Omnibus Amendment Act of 2024, passed
33 on 2nd reading on March 5, 2024 (Enrolled version of Bill 25-345),” and inserting the phrase
34 “No later than June 2025,” in its place.

35 (b) Subsection (h) is amended by striking the phrase “Within one year after
36 the applicability date of section 29 of the Secure DC Omnibus Amendment Act of 2024, passed
37 on 2nd reading on March 5, 2024 (Enrolled version of Bill 25-345),” and inserting the phrase
38 “No later than July 31, 2026,” in its place.
39 Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
40 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Budget Director as the fiscal impact
41 statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975, approved
42 October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
43 Sec. 4. Effective date.
44 (a) This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by
45 the Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), a 60-day period of congressional review
46 as provided in section 602(c)(2) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December
47 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 813; D.C. Official Code § 1-206.02(c)(2)).
48 (b) This act shall expire after 225 days of its having taken effect.
