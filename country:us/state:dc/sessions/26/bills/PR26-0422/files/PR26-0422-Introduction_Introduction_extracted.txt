Dear Chairman Mendelson:
Enclosed for consideration and enactment by the Council of the District of Columbia is an
emergency bill, the “Modification Nos. M0013 and M0014 to Human Care Agreement No.
CW100338 with Catholic Charities of the Archdiocese of Washington, Inc. Approval and Payment
Authorization Emergency Act of 2025,” and the accompanying emergency declaration resolution
in the amount not to exceed $1,185,376.98.
The legislation will approve Modification Nos. M0013 and M0014 to Human Care Agreement
CW100338 with Catholic Charities of the Archdiocese of Washington, Inc. to provide Permanent
Supportive Housing III program case management services, and to fully exercise option year three
of the contract. In addition, the legislation will approve payment for the goods and services
received and to be received under the modifications.
My administration is available to discuss any questions you may have regarding the proposed
contract modification. In order to facilitate a response to any questions you may have, please have
your staff contact Marc Scott, Chief Operating Officer, Office of Contracting and Procurement, at
(202) 724-8759.
I urge the Council to take prompt and favorable action on the enclosed legislation.
Sincerely,
Muriel Bowser

20 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
21 resolution may be cited as the "Modification Nos. M0013 and M0014 to Contract No.
22 CW100338 with Catholic Charities of the Archdiocese of Washington, Inc. Approval and
23 Payment Authorization Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025".
24 Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to approve Modification Nos. M0013 and
25 M0014 to Contract No. CW100338 with Catholic Charities of the Archdiocese of Washington,
26 Inc., and to authorize payment for the goods and services received and to be received under the
27 modifications.
28 (b) On June 25, 2025, by Modification No. M0013, the Office of Contracting and
29 Procurement, on behalf of the Department of Human Services, exercised a partial option of
30 option year three of Contract No. CW100338, for the period of July 1, 2025 through December
31 31, 2025, with Catholic Charities of the Archdiocese of Washington, Inc. to provide Permanent
32 Supportive Housing III program case management services in the not-to-exceed amount of
33 $561,364.26.
1

34 (c) Proposed Modification No. M0014 will exercise the remainder of option year three
35 and increase the not-to-exceed amount by $624,012.72, to a total not-to-exceed amount of
36 $1,185,376.98.
37 (d) Under section 451(b)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
38 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 803; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.51(b)(1)), Council approval is
39 necessary since the modifications will increase the value of the contract to more than $1 million
40 during a 12-month period.
41 (e) Approval is necessary to allow the continuation of these vital services. Without this
42 approval, Catholic Charities of the Archdiocese of Washington, Inc. cannot be paid for goods
43 and services provided in excess of $1 million for the period from July 1, 2025, through June 30,
44 2026.
45 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2 constitute
46 emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Modification Nos. M0013 and M0014 to
47 Contract No. CW100338 with Catholic Charities of the Archdiocese of Washington, Inc.
48 Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2025 be adopted after a single reading.
49 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
