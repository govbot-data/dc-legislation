ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-480
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
July 14, 2026
To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to approve Modification Nos.
M0026 and M0027 to Contract No. CW90941 with Aramark Correctional Services, LLC,
and to authorize payment for the goods and services received and to be received under
the modifications.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Modification Nos. M0026 and M0027 to Contract No. CW90941
with Aramark Correctional Services, LLC Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency
Declaration Resolution of 2026”.
Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to approve Modification Nos. M0026 and
M0027 to Contract No. CW90941 with Aramark Correctional Services, LLC to provide inmate
food management services, and to authorize payment for the goods and services received and to
be received under the modifications.
(b) On July 11, 2025, by Modification No. M0022, the Office of Contracting and
Procurement (“OCP”), on behalf of the Department of Corrections, exercised a full option of
Option Year 4 in the not-to-exceed amount of $6,107,415 for the period from September 15,
2025, through September 14, 2026. The modification was deemed approved (CA26-303).
(c) By Modification No. M0026, OCP increased the not-to-exceed amount for Option
Year 4 by $955,000, to a total not-to-exceed amount of $7,062,415.
(d) Proposed Modification No. M0027 would increase the not-to-exceed amount for
Option Year 4 by $750,000, to a total not-to-exceed amount of $7,812,415.
(e) Council approval is necessary because the modifications will increase the value of the
contract to more than $1 million during a 12-month period.
(f) Council approval is necessary to allow the continuation of these vital services.
Without Council approval, Aramark Correctional Services, LLC cannot be paid for goods and
services provided in excess of $1 million for the period from September 15, 2025, through
September 14, 2026.
Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the
Modification Nos. M0026 and M0027 to Contract No. CW90941 with Aramark Correctional
