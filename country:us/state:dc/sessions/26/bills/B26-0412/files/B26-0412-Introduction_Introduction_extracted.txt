15 To amend the Prevention of Child Abuse and Neglect Act of 1977 to define “CAC” as an
16 accredited Children’s Advocacy Center, to mandate the use of an accredited Children’s
17 Advocacy Center during Multidisciplinary Team investigations, to establish an annual
18 reassessment and designation of the District’s Children’s Advocacy Center, and to
19 require the Children’s Advocacy Center supply to Council proof of its accreditation status
20 upon annual designation.
21
22 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this act may
23 be cited as the “Child Abuse Investigation Multidisciplinary Team Certification
24 Amendment Act of 2025”.
25 Sec. 2. The Prevention of Child Abuse and Neglect Act of 1977, effective September 23,
26 1977 (D.C. Law 2-22; D.C. Official Code § 4-1301.51 et seq) is amended as follows:
27 (a) Section 102(2B) (D.C. Official Code § 4-1301.02), is amended as follows:
28 “(2B) “CAC” means an accredited Children’ s Advocacy Center under an
29 agreement for services with the District of Columbia.”
30 (b) A new subsection (c) is added to read as follows:
31 “(c) The MDT shall make reasonable efforts to enter into an agreement for services with
32 an accredited Children’s Advocacy Center for the purposes of coordinating the MDT
33 investigation. The absence of a CAC shall not constitute grounds for the District’s investigating
1

34 authority to delay or fail to carry out an MDT investigation.
35 (c) A new subsection (d) is added to read as follows:
36 “(d) The CAC serving as a member of the MDT shall, on an annual basis for the life of its
37 service agreement, provide the D.C. Council with documentation reflecting its accreditation
38 status in good standing by a national accrediting body with demonstrated expertise in the CAC
39 model.”
40 Sec. 4. Fiscal impact statement.
41 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement in the committee report as the fiscal
42 impact statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975,
43 approved October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
44 Sec. 5. Effective date.
45 This act shall take effect after approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by the
46 Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), a 30-day period of congressional review as
47 provided in section 602(c)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December
48 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 813; D.C. Official Code § 1-206.02(c)(1)), and publication in the District of
49 Columbia Register.
