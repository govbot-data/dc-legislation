14 To amend, on a temporary basis, the District of Columbia Housing Authority Act of 1999 to
15 revise the structure of the board of the Authority, provide flexibility for board and
16 executive director training, and update the public housing resident bill of rights; to amend
17 the District of Columbia Government Comprehensive Merit Personnel Act of 1978 to
18 adjust Housing Authority board stipends; to amend the Confirmation Act of 1978 to
19 conform terminology regarding the Housing Authority board; and to repeal superseded
20 emergency and temporary legislation.
21
22 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
23 act may be cited as the “Housing Authority Resident Empowerment Temporary Amendment Act
24 of 2025”.
