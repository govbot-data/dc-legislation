12 To amend, on a temporary basis, the Volunteers Services Act of 1977 to clarify that the
13 Attorney General for the District of Columbia has the authority to promulgate
14 regulations governing the Attorney General’s use of volunteers.
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA,
17 That this act may be cited as the “Volunteer Services Clarification Temporary Amendment
18 Act of 2025”.
19 Sec. 2. Section 3 of the Volunteers Services Act of 1977, effective June 28, 1977
20 (D.C. Law 2-12; D.C. Official Code § 1-319.02), is amended by striking the phrase
21 “District of Columbia Board of Education” and inserting the phrase “District of Columbia
22 Board of Education, Attorney General for the District of Columbia,” in its place.
23 Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
24 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Budget Director as the fiscal
25 impact statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975,
26 approved October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
