Dear Chairman Mendelson:
Enclosed for consideration and enactment by the Council of the District of Columbia is
an emergency bill, the “Contract No. CW133373 with JJ Prime Services LLC Approval and
Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2026,” and the accompanying emergency declaration
resolution in the not-to-exceed $1,511,658.90.
The legislation will approve emergency Contract No. CW133373 with JJ Prime Services LLC to
provide emergency snow removal equipment and operators for period from January 29,
2026, through February 13, 2026. The legislation will approve payment for the goods and
services received under the contract.
My administration is available to discuss any questions you may have regarding the proposed
contract modification. In order to facilitate a response to any questions you may have, please
have your staff contact Marc Scott, Chief Operating Officer, Office of Contracting and
Procurement, at (202) 724-8759.
I urge the Council to take prompt and favorable action on the enclosed legislation.
Sincerely,
Muriel Bowser

27 emergency snow removal equipment and operators in the not-to-exceed amount of $1,511,658.90
28 for the period January 29, 2026, through February 13, 2026.
29 (c) Under section 451(b)(l) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
30 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 803; D.C. Official Code§ 1-204.Sl(b)(l)), Council approval is
31 necessary since the contract is more than $1 million during a 12-month period.
