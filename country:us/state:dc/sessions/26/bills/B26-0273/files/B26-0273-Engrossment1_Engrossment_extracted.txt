12 To amend, on a temporary basis, the Rental Housing Act of 1985 to extend the deadline from 90
13 days to 180 days for housing providers to file a new registration statement after the
14 completion of the publicly accessible rent control housing database, and to require
15 owners of rental accommodations that are exempt from rent stabilization to report current
16 and prior rent amounts for the previous two calendar years at the time of registration.
17
18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
19 act may be cited as the “Rental Housing Registration Extension Temporary Amendment Act of
20 2025”.
