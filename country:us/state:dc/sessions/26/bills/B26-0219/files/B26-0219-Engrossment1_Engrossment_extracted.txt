10 To amend the Malcolm X Surplus Declaration and Disposition Approval Act of 2022 to
11 extend the time limit for the disposition of certain District-owned real property
12 located at 1351 Alabama Avenue, S.E., and to approve an amendment to the term
13 sheet for the disposition of the property.
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA,
16 That this act may be cited as the “Malcolm X Extension of Disposition Authority and
17 Term Sheet Amendment Act of 2025”.
18 Sec. 2. The Malcolm X Surplus Declaration and Disposition Approval Act of
19 2022, effective March 10, 2023 (D.C. Law 24-316; 70 DCR 3547) is amended as follows:
20 (a) Section 2(6) is amended to read as follows:
21 “Project” means a mixed-use project that will include approximately 150
22 residential units, 7,500 square feet of daycare space, and 2,000 square feet of retail space,
23 as further described in the amended term sheet and draft land disposition and
24 development agreement transmitted by the Mayor to the Council with the Malcolm X
25 Extension of Disposition Authority and Term Sheet Amendment Act of 2025.”
26 (b) Section 3 is amended as follows:
1

27 (1) Subsection (i) is amended by striking the phrase “, as further described
28 in the executed term sheet and draft land disposition and development agreement
29 documents transmitted by the Mayor to the Council with this act” and inserting the phrase
30 “as further described in the amended term sheet and draft land disposition and
31 development agreement transmitted by the Mayor to the Council with the Malcolm X
32 Extension of Disposition Authority and Term Sheet Amendment Act of 2025” in its
33 place.
34 (2) Subsection (j) is amended by striking the phrase “transmitted by the
35 Mayor with this act” and inserting the phrase “transmitted by the Mayor with the
36 Malcolm X Extension of Disposition Authority and Term Sheet Amendment Act of
37 2025” in its place.

39 date” and inserting the phrase “4 years after the effective date” in its place.
40 Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
41 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement in the committee report as the
42 fiscal impact statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act
43 of 1975, approved October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).

45 This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto
46 by the Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), a 30-day period of

48 Rule Act, approved December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 813; D.C. Official Code § 1-
49 206.02(c)(1)), and publication in the District of Columbia Register.
