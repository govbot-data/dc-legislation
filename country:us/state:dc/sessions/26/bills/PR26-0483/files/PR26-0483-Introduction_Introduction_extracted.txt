Anthony Washington
Mr. Anthony Washington joined Merchants Capital in 2020 and served as
Vice President, Originations. He spent over twenty-five years of his
career in commercial lending, the bulk of it in the agency lending space
with companies including Oak Grove Capital (now Jones Lang LaSalle),
American Property Financing (now Wells Fargo), and AMI Capital (now
Wells Fargo).
Prior to that, Mr. Washington was a founding partner of the EagleBank
FHA Lending platform. He financed over $1 billion of multifamily
transactions across Fannie Mae, Freddie Mac, and FHA platforms over
his career.
A Ward 4 resident, Mr. Washington earned his Bachelor of Science in Business Administration
from Saint Francis University and his Master of Business Administration in Finance from the
University of Maryland College Park.
