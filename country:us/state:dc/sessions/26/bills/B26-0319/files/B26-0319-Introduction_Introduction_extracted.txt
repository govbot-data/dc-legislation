16 To amend, on an emergency basis, the Urban Forest Preservation Act of 2002 to require that the
17 Mayor issue a permit for the removal of a Heritage Tree abutting or within the site of the
18 Long Bridge Project under certain conditions.
19
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
21 act may be cited as the “Long Bridge Project Emergency Amendment Act of 2025”.
22 Sec. 2. Section 104a(b) of the Urban Forest Preservation Act of 2002, effective July 1, 2016
23 (D.C. Law 21-133; D.C. Official Code § 8–651.04a(b)), is amended by adding a new paragraph
24 (3) to read as follows:
25 “(3) The Mayor shall issue a Heritage Tree removal permit under this section for a
26 Heritage Tree that abuts or is within the site of the Long Bridge Project managed by the Virginia
27 Passenger Rail Authority if:
28 “(A) There is no reasonable design alternative that can avoid the location of
29 the Heritage Tree;
30 “(B) The Urban Forestry Division has determined that the tree cannot
31 reasonably be protected in place;
32 “(C) The applicant has demonstrated that:
33 “(i) The cost of relocation exceeds $1 million; or
1

34 “(ii) Relocation would cause an undue burden to the public; and
35 “(D) The applicant has agreed to pay $1,200 per each inch of the
36 circumference of the Heritage Tree in question.”.
37 Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
38 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Budget Director as the fiscal impact
39 statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975, approved
40 October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
41 Sec. 4. Effective date.
42 This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by the
43 Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto) and shall remain in effect for no longer than 90
44 days, as provided for emergency acts of the Council of the District of Columbia in section 412(a)
45 of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 788; D.C.
46 Official Code § 1-204.12(a)).
