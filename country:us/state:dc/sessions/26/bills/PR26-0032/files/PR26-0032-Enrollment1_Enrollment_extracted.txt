ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-26
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
February 4, 2025
To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to approve Modification Nos.
1, 2, 3, 4, 5, and 6 to Contract No. DCAM-22-CS-RFP-0026 with Chiaramonte
Construction Company, to provide construction services for the Public Safety
Communications Center building renovations and to authorize payment for the goods and
services received and to be received under the contract.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as “Modification Nos. 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, and 6 to Contract No. DCAM-22-CS-
RFP-0026 with Chiaramonte Construction Company Approval and Payment Authorization
Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
Sec. 2 (a) There exists an immediate need to approve Modification Nos. 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, and
6 to Contract No. DCAM-22-CS-RFP-0026 (“Contract”) with Chiaramonte Construction
Company to provide construction services for the Public Safety Communications Center building
renovations, and to authorize payment for the goods and services received and to be received
under the contract.
(b) From April 17, 2024, to September 05, 2024, by Modification Nos. 1, 2, 3, 4, and 5,
the Department of General Services incorporated the added costs for additional construction
services into the Contract, increasing the total contract value amount by $998,611.36 to
$12,470,486.36.
(c) Proposed Modification No. 6 would increase the total contract value amount by
$1,223,392.70 to $13,693,879.06. Council approval is necessary because the aggregate value of
Modification Nos. 1, 2, 3, 4, and 5 and proposed Modification No. 6 is $2,222,004.06, an amount
over $1 million.
(d) Council approval is necessary to allow the continuation of the building renovation
services provided pursuant to the contract. Without Council approval, Chiarmonte Construction
Company cannot be paid for goods and services provided in excess of $1 million.
Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the
Modification Nos. 1, 2, 3, 4, 5, and 6 to Contract No. DCAM-22-CS-RFP-0026 with
