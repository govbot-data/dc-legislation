16 To amend, on an emergency basis, the Strengthening Traffic Enforcement, Education, and
17 Responsibility (“STEER”) Amendment Act of 2024 to modify its applicability clause to
18 correctly reflect its funding status with respect to the District’s Intelligent Speed Assistance
19 Program.
20
21 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this act
22 may be cited as the “STEER Clarification Emergency Amendment Act of 2026”.
23 Sec. 2. Section 8(d) of Strengthening Traffic Enforcement, Education, and Responsibility
24 (“STEER”) Amendment Act of 2024, effective April 20, 2024 (D.C. Law 25-161; 71 DCR 2248), is
25 amended to read as follows:
26 “(d)(1) Section 3 shall apply:
27 “(A) With respect to the Ignition Interlock Device established by section 10a
28 of the District of Columbia Traffic Act, 1925, approved March 3, 1925 (43 Stat. 1124; D.C. Official
29 Code § 50–2201.05a), as of January 1, 2027; and
30 “(B) For all other purposes, including the Intelligent Speed Assistance
31 Program established by section 10a-1 of the District of Columbia Traffic Act, 1925, approved March
32 3, 1925 (43 Stat. 1124; D.C. Official Code § 50–2201.05a–1) as of the effective date of the STEER

33 Clarification Emergency Amendment Act of 2026 (passed on emergency basis on February 3, 2026)
34 (Enrolled version of B26-XXXX).
35 “(2) Sections 4(d) and (f), 5, and 6 shall apply as of January 1, 2027.”.
36 Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
37 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Budget Director as the fiscal impact
38 statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975, approved
39 October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
40 Sec. 4. Effective Date.
41 This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by the
42 Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), and shall remain in effect for no longer than
43 90 days, as provided for emergency acts of the Council of the District of Columbia in section
44 412(a) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 788;
45 D.C. Official Code § 1-204.12(a)).
