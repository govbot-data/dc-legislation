Dear Secretary Howard,
Today, I, along with Councilmember Nadeau, am introducing the “Malachi Jackson Way
Designation Act of 2025.” Please find enclosed a signed copy of the legislation.
This legislation would symbolically designate Gales Street NE, between 15th Street NE and 21st
Street NE, in Ward 7 as “Malachi Jackson Way.” This symbolic designation is in honor and
memorial of Malachi Jackson, who was tragically shot and killed at just fifteen years old.
On April 11, 2022, Malachi Jackson was killed by a senseless act of gun violence on the 3000
block of 13th Street NW. During his childhood, Malachi loved going to the Rosedale Recreation
Center on Gales Street NE and participating in their boxing program. He truly found a safe space
at this recreation center, where he could grow, learn, and thrive.
With the support of the Rosedale community and Advisory Neighborhood Commission (ANC)
7D, the designation of Gales Street NE as “Malachi Jackson Way”, would honor the memory of
Malachi Jackson and raise awareness around the horrendous effects of gun violence.
Please feel free to reach out to me or my Legislative Director, LJ Chavis, with any questions. He
can be reached at achavis@dccouncil.gov or by phone at 202-741-0912.
Sincerely,
Wendell Felder
Ward 7 Councilmember
