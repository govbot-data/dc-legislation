10 To amend, on a temporary basis, section 1507 of the Criminal Justice Coordinating Council for
11 the District of Columbia Establishment Act of 2001 to change the date by which the
12 Prearrest Diversion Task Force shall issue initial recommendations for prearrest diversion
13 of certain misdemeanor offenses and categories of persons to July 2026; and to amend
14 Title 20 of the District of Columbia Official Code to allow for notice of request for
15 formal probate, notice from a foreign personal representative of a decedent who owned
16 any property located in the District of Columbia, and notice of appointment to interested
17 persons, creditors and unknown heirs in a legal periodical or newspaper of general
18 circulation in the District.
19
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
21 act may be cited as the “Prearrest Diversion Task Force Recommendations Temporary
22 Amendment Act of 2025”.
