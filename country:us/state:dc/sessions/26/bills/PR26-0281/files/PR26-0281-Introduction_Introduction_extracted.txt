45th Street, NW
Washington, DC 20016
(Ward 3)
for appointment as a professional engineer licensed in the District member of the Board of Professional
Engineering, replacing Barry Lucas, for a remainder of an unexpired term to end January 19, 2028.

do not hesitate to contact me, or Steven Walker, Director, Mayor’s Office of Talent and
Appointments, should the Council require additional information.

Career Details:
Daniel E. Hanlon, P.E., F.NSPE is a seasoned technical and business professional,
with over 40 years of experience in both the private and public sectors. His work has
included program management and administration, management of engineering
design, construction and operations for complex systems, facilities, and infrastructure
projects with particular emphasis on strategic and budgetary planning and
organizational structure and operations. Mr. Hanlon has extensive experience in
capital and operational budget development and management, construction
management, energy management, transportation, and environmental systems,
building renovation, historic preservation, and infrastructure design, operations, and
modernization with a particular focus on facility design and construction for campus
environments, transportation systems, telecommunications, heating, ventilating and
air conditioning, central plant operations, security, fire protection and life safety. He
has also served as a technical and professional witness and panel member for many
institutional and governmental entities including universities, the U.S. Congress, state
legislatures and local units of government.
