16 To amend, on a temporary basis, the Volunteers Services Act of 1977 to clarify that the
17 Attorney General for the District of Columbia has the authority to promulgate
18 regulations governing the Attorney General’s use of volunteers.
19
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA,
21 That this act may be cited as the “Volunteer Services Clarification Temporary Amendment
22 Act of 2025”.
23 Sec. 2. Section 3 of the Volunteers Services Act of 1977, effective June 28, 1977
24 (D.C. Law 2-12; D.C. Official Code § 1-319.02), is amended by striking the phrase
25 “District of Columbia Board of Education” and inserting the phrase “District of Columbia
26 Board of Education, Attorney General for the District of Columbia,” in its place.
27 Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
28 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Budget Director as the fiscal
29 impact statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975,
30 approved October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
31 Sec. 4. Effective date.

32 (a) This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of
33 veto by the Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), a 30-day period of
34 congressional review as provided in section 602(c)(1) of the District of Columbia Home
35 Rule Act, approved December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 813; D.C. Official Code § 1-
36 206.02(c)(1)).
37 (b) This act shall expire after 225 days of its having taken effect.
