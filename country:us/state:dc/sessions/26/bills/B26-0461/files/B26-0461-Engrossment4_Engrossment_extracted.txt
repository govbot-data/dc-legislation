A BILL
26-461
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
___________________
1 To amend the Juvenile Curfew Act of 1995 to set curfew hours to begin at 11:00 p.m. every
2 night, to extend the applicability of the curfew to 17-year-olds, to remove the word
3 juvenile, to authorize the Mayor and Chief of Police to designate extended youth curfew
4 zones, to establish specific notice and signage requirements for extended youth curfew
5 zones, to allow Advisory Neighborhood Commissions to petition the Chief of Police for
6 an extended youth curfew zone, to provide guardrails on the enforcement of curfew
7 violations in extended youth curfew zones, and to require the provision of youth
8 programming when extended youth curfew zones are established.
9
10 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
11 act may be cited as the "Youth Curfew Amendment Act of 2025".
