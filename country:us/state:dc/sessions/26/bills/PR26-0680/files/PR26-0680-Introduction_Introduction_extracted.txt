KARLA STYLES
Visual Artist | Muralist | Teaching Artist | Creative Director | Graphic Designer
PROFESSIONAL SUMMARY
Visual artist, muralist, and teaching artist working at the intersection of art, healing, and cultural
engagement. With over a decade of experience in public art, creative direction, and brand
collaboration, Karla develops work that explores identity, symbolism, and human connection.
She has led workshops, executed large-scale projects, and contributed to the creative
landscape of Washington, D.C..
CORE SKILLS
Public Art & Murals • Creative Direction • Workshop Facilitation • Community Engagement •
Visual Design • Brand Collaboration • Project Management • Client Relations • Adobe Creative
Suite • Procreate

Karla Styles
Ms. Karla Styles is a District-based visual artist, muralist, and teaching
artist whose work explores the intersection of art, healing, and spirituality.
Her vibrant, symbolism-driven pieces reflect themes of self-discovery,
empowerment, and transformation.
Through murals, commissions, and community-centered workshops,
Styles uses art as a tool for storytelling, connection, and emotional
healing. As the founder of Bumswag Millionaire, she continues to expand
her creative practice into a purpose-driven brand that inspires others to
align with their highest selves and live abundantly.
A Ward 7 resident, Ms. Styles is a DC native.
