12 To order the closing of a portion of a public alley in Square 756 abutting Lot 47, and the
13 dedication of a portion of Lot 806 for public alley purposes in Ward 6.
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
16 act may be cited as the “Closing of a Portion of a Public Alley and Dedication of Land for Alley
17 Purposes in Square 756, S.O. 24-00310, Act of 2025”.
18 Sec. 2. (a) Pursuant to section 404 of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
19 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 787; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.04) and consistent with Street and
20 Alley Closing and Acquisition Procedures Act of 1982, effective March 10, 1983 (D.C. Law 4-
21 201; D.C. Official Code § 9-202.01 et seq.)(“Act”), the Council finds that the portion of the
22 public alley in Square 756, as shown on the Surveyor’s plat filed under S.O. 24-00310, is
23 unnecessary for alley purposes and orders it closed, with title to the land to vest as shown on the
24 Surveyor’s plat.
25 (b) Pursuant to section 302 of the Act (D.C. Official Code § 9-203.02), the Council
26 accepts the dedication of a portion of Lot 806 in Square 756 for public alley purposes, as shown
27 on the Surveyor’s plat filed under S.O. 24-00310.
