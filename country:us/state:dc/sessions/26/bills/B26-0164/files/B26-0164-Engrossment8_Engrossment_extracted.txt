15 summons period, to provide courts that hear eviction cases with discretion not to dismiss
16 cases for certain deficiencies, and to codify and update processes for the deposit of rental
17 payments into the court registry during eviction cases for nonpayment; to amend the
18 Rental Housing Act of 1985 to grant courts discretion to determine when equities require

22 proceedings stemming from dangerous crime or crime of violence; to amend the Rental
