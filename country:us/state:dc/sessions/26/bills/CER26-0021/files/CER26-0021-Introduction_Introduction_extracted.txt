12 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA

14 _________________________
15
16
17 To recognize and honor the Friends of Mitchell Park on the occasion of their 20th anniversary
18 and their outstanding contributions to preserving and enhancing a historic green space in
19 the District of Columbia.
20
21 WHEREAS, Mitchell Park, located in the Sheridan-Kalorama neighborhood, has a rich
22 history dating back to 1663 when the original 600 acres were deeded to George Calvert, the first
23 Lord Baltimore;
24 WHEREAS, in 1918, Elizabeth Mitchell generously donated the property to the District
25 of Columbia, establishing what would become a cherished community gathering space;
26 WHEREAS, in 1992, two dedicated community members, Holly Sukenik and Betsy
27 Santarlasci, began crucial conversations about the park's deterioration, leading to the formation
28 of the Friends of Mitchell Park;
29 WHEREAS, the Friends of Mitchell Park was officially established as a 501(c)(3) non-
30 profit organization dedicated to preserving and enhancing this historic green space for the
31 enjoyment of all District residents;
32 WHEREAS, through successful partnership with the D.C. Department of Parks and
33 Recreation, the Friends of Mitchell Park led a comprehensive restoration effort, culminating in
34 the park's grand reopening on October 31, 2004;
1

35 WHEREAS, the Friends of Mitchell Park has overseen the planting of nearly 25,000
36 trees, shrubs, and flowers, transforming the space into a verdant oasis in the heart of the District;
37 WHEREAS, the organization hosts beloved community events, including the annual Fall
38 Fun Day which regularly attracts over 300 neighbors, and the summer films series held every
39 third Thursday from June to September;
40 WHEREAS, the Friends of Mitchell Park maintains the historical significance of the site,
41 which is listed on the National Register of Historic Places and contains the foundation of the
42 historic Holmead-Kall Home dating to the 18th century;
43 WHEREAS, for two decades, this all-volunteer organization has demonstrated
44 exceptional dedication to providing a safe, beautiful, and well-maintained gathering place for all
45 Sheridan-Kalorama residents; and
46 WHEREAS, the Friends of Mitchell Park continues to serve as an exemplary model of
47 community-led park stewardship in the District of Columbia.
48 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
49 resolution may be cited as the "Friends of Mitchell Park 20th Anniversary Recognition
50 Resolution of 2025".
51 Sec. 2. Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia recognizes and celebrates the
52 Friends of Mitchell Park for 20 years of outstanding service to the community and their tireless
53 dedication to preserving and enhancing one of the District's most cherished historic green spaces.
54 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately upon the first date of publication in
55 the District of Columbia Register.
