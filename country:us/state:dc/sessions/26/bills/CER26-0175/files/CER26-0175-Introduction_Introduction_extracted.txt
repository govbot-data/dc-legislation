17 To recognize honoree Justin Stewart during the 2026 celebration of DC Black Pride.
18
19 WHEREAS, Justin Stewart is a multimedia journalist, producer, and social media
20 influencer recognized for his vibrant storytelling, commanding media presence, and commitment
21 to amplifying diverse voices in journalism;
22
23 WHEREAS, a Washington, DC, native, Justin Stewart launched his media career after
24 earning a Bachelor of Arts degree from the University of Maryland Eastern Shore in 2010;
25
26 WHEREAS, Justin Stewart has built an impressive career across broadcast, digital, and
27 entertainment media, contributing to respected outlets including NBC4 Washington, NBC
28 Network, WJLA-ABC 7, WTTG-FOX 5, and Metro Weekly;
29
30 WHEREAS, Justin Stewart currently produces and edits engaging content for NBC
31 Washington’s morning show, “News4 Today,” which includes coordination of high-profile
32 interviews and development of compelling news coverage;
33
34 WHEREAS, Justin Stewart’s work and commentary have been featured by national
35 media outlets including USA Today, The Associated Press, LGBTQ Nation, Rolling Out, and
36 The Wendy Williams Show;
37
38 WHEREAS, beyond the newsroom, Justin Stewart is widely recognized for using his
39 platform to bridge the gap between traditional journalism and LGBTQIA+ advocacy, with a
40 strong focus on visibility for the Black queer community throughout the DMV region;
41
42 WHEREAS, openly embracing his identity as a “Black queer fem man,” Justin Stewart is
43 passionate about creating space for underserved communities and ensuring authentic
44 representation across media platforms;
1

45
46 WHEREAS, through his digital presence and behind-the-scenes storytelling, Justin
47 Stewart works to make the media industry feel more accessible and inclusive for aspiring
48 journalists and creatives of color;
49
50 WHEREAS, in addition to journalism, Justin Stewart is an experienced live host and
51 moderator who has led numerous high-profile events celebrating the arts, culture, nonprofits, and
52 community organizations;
53
54 WHEREAS, Justin Stewart’s hosting credits include the DC Mayor’s Arts Awards, Miss
55 Earth USA, and the Literary Arts Awards;
56
57 WHEREAS, recognized for both his professionalism and cultural impact, Justin Stewart
58 was nominated for Truth Prism Journalist of the Year at the 2026 Prism3D Awards;
59
60 WHEREAS, whether behind the scenes producing news or center stage hosting major
61 events, Justin Stewart continues to bring energy, authenticity, and purpose-driven storytelling to
62 every platform he touches;
63
64 WHEREAS, DC Black Pride is an annual event to increase awareness of and showcase
65 pride in the diversity of the LGBTQIA+ populations in the African American community, and to
66 support organizations that focus on health disparities, education, youth, and families;
67
68 WHEREAS, as the very first Black Pride festival in the world, DC Black Pride fostered
69 the beginning of the Center for Black Equity and the “Black Pride Movement”;
70
71 WHEREAS, since the birth of DC Black Pride, more than 50 other Black Pride
72 celebrations now take place throughout the world and on 4 continents, with many using DC
73 Black Pride as a model;
74
75 WHEREAS, May 22, 2026, through May 25, 2026, marks the 35th annual DC Black
76 Pride celebration;
77
78 WHEREAS, the theme for this year’s celebration is “New Black Renaissance,” which
79 centers Black LGBTQIA+ “artists, leaders, healers, and organizers while confronting what still
80 makes Black Pride necessary, including HIV, inequity, homophobia, and transphobia;”
81
82 WHEREAS, for more than 30 years, DC Black Pride has been an opportunity for the
83 Black LGBTQIA+ community to gather and celebrate being both African American and
84 members of LGBTQIA+ communities;
85
86 WHEREAS, DC Black Pride is the oldest and one of the largest Black Pride events in the
87 world, drawing thousands of residents and visitors from around the globe; and
88
2

89 WHEREAS, DC Black Pride brings more than 500,000 people to the nation’s capital
90 from across the United States, Canada, the Caribbean, South Africa, Great Britain, France,
91 Germany, and the Netherlands and will carry that tradition this year and into the future to
92 celebrate their shared community, raise awareness and funding for HIV/AIDS prevention, and
93 stand against homophobia inspired by violence and bigotry.
94
95 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That
96 this resolution be cited as the “Justin Stewart Recognition Resolution of 2026”.
97
98 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia recognizes and celebrates Justin Stewart
99 during the 2026 celebration of DC Black Pride.
100
101 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
