13 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
14 act may be cited as the “Alfred Dudley Sr. Way Designation Act of 2025”.
15 Sec. 2. Pursuant to sections 401, 403a, and 423 of the Street and Alley Closing and
16 Acquisition Procedures Act of 1982, effective March 10, 1983 (D.C. Law 4-201; D.C. Official
17 Code §§ 9-204.01, 9-204.03a, and 9-204.23), the Council symbolically designates Mills Avenue,
18 N.E., between Hamlin Place, N.E., and Rhode Island Avenue, N.E., as “Alfred Dudley Sr. Way”.
19 Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
20 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement in the committee report as the fiscal
21 impact statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975,
22 approved October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
23 Sec. 4. Effective date.
24 This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by the
25 Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto) and a 30-day period of congressional review
