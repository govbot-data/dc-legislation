17 To declare the existence of an emergency, due to congressional review, with respect to the need
18 to amend the Volunteers Services Act of 1977 to clarify that the Attorney General for the
19 District of Columbia has the authority to promulgate regulations governing the Attorney
20 General’s use of volunteers.
21
22 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
23 resolution may be cited as the “Volunteer Services Clarification Congressional Review
24 Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2026”.
25 Sec. 2. (a) On May 5, 2026, the Council adopted the Volunteer Services Clarification
26 Emergency Amendment Act of 2026, effective May 29, 2026 (D.C. Act 26-336; 73 DCR 8200)
27 (“emergency act”), which expires on August 27, 2026.
28 (b) The Council later passed the Volunteer Services Clarification Temporary
29 Amendment Act of 2026, enacted on June 25, 2026 (D.C. Act 26-350; 73 DCR 9211)
30 (“temporary act”), which is projected to become law on September 19, 2026.
1

31 (c) This congressional review emergency legislation is necessary to prevent a gap in the
32 law between the expiration of the emergency act and the effective date of the temporary act.
33 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
34 enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the
35 Volunteer Services Clarification Congressional Review Emergency Amendment Act of 2026 be
36 adopted after a single reading.
37 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
