21 A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
22 __________
23
24 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
25 __________
26
27
28 To recognize the 70th anniversary of Continental Societies, Inc. and honor the
29 organization for its extraordinary legacy of service and unwavering commitment to excellence.
30
31 WHEREAS, Continental Societies, Inc., founded in 1956, has devoted seventy years of
32 exemplary service to enriching the lives of children and youth across the nation, including here
33 in the District of Columbia;
34
35 WHEREAS, this organization has strengthened communities through impactful service,
36 civic engagement, and an enduring commitment to the well-being of young people;
37
38 WHEREAS, through the dedicated efforts of its members, Continental Societies, Inc. has
39 generously contributed its time, talent and resources to advance programs in health, education,
40 employment, recreation, and the arts and humanities;
41
42 WHEREAS, through its signature programs and hands-on initiatives, this organization
43 has addressed critical needs facing children and youth, fostering academic enrichment, cultural
44 enrichment, and workforce readiness;
45

46 WHEREAS, Continental Societies, Inc. has cultivated generations of women leaders
47 committed to volunteerism, philanthropy, and advocacy, setting a standard of excellence and
48 service that extends far beyond its membership;
49
50 WHEREAS, through their strong emphasis on mentorship and leadership development
51 Continental Societies, Inc. has inspired young people to realize their full potential and become
52 the next generation of leaders and change-makers;
53
54 WHEREAS the legacy of Continental Societies, Inc. is defined not only by its longevity,
55 but by the lives transformed, the opportunities created, and the enduring spirit of service carried
56 forward by its members; and
57
58 WHEREAS the celebration of this 70th anniversary marks a remarkable milestone,
59 honoring a proud history of service while reaffirming a steadfast commitment to shaping brighter
60 futures in the years ahead.
61
62 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, that the
63 Council of the District of Columbia recognizes Continental Societies, Inc. for their extraordinary
64 commitment to service and looks forward to the continued impact that the organization will have
65 in their next 70 years.
66
67 Sec. 2. The resolution may be cited as the “Continental Societies, Inc. Recognition
68 Resolution of 2026.”
69
70 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately upon the first date of publication in
71 the District of Columbia Register.
