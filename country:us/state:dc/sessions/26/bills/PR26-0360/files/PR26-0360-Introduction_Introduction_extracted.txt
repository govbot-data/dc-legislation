Edward Hubbard
Mr. Edward Hubbard currently serves as Board Secretary of the DC
Green Bank Board of Directors and the General Counsel for the
Renewable Fuels Association (RFA) where he provides counsel and
advice on a wide array of federal renewable fuel and clean energy issues
and policy. He advocates for the ethanol industry on key energy,
agriculture, transportation, and clean air policy and regulatory matters
before Congress and federal agencies.
Mr. Hubbard serves on the Agricultural Technical Advisory Committee
for Trade (ATAC) in Grains, Feed, Oilseeds and Planting Seeds, jointly
managed by the U.S. Department of Agriculture and U.S. Trade
Representative. He also serves on the Board of Directors of Green Seal,
Inc.
Prior to joining RFA, Mr. Hubbard served as Legislative Director and Counsel in the office of U.S.
Representative Albert Wynn, overseeing the Congressman’s Maryland delegation leadership
responsibilities and chair activities for the Congressional Black Caucus Small Business Task
Force, Hydrogen Caucus, and Wireless Caucus.
A Ward 4 resident, Mr. Hubbard earned his Bachelor of Arts in Political Science from the
University of California and his Juris Doctor from Howard University.
