11 To amend, on an emergency basis, the Volunteers Services Act of 1977 to clarify that the
12 Attorney General for the District of Columbia has the authority to promulgate regulations
13 governing the Attorney General’s use of volunteers.
14
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
16 act may be cited as the “Volunteer Services Clarification Emergency Amendment Act of 2026”.
17 Sec. 2. Section 3 of the Volunteers Services Act of 1977, effective June 28, 1977 (D.C.
18 Law 2-12; D.C. Official Code § 1-319.02), is amended by striking the phrase “District of
19 Columbia Board of Education” and inserting the phrase “District of Columbia Board of
20 Education, Attorney General for the District of Columbia,” in its place.
21 Sec. 3. Applicability.
22 This act shall apply as of April 15, 2026.
23 Sec. 4. Fiscal impact statement.
24 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Budget Director as the fiscal impact
25 statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975, approved
26 October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
27 Sec. 5. Effective date.

28 This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by the
29 Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), and shall remain in effect for no longer than
30 90 days, as provided for emergency acts of the Council of the District of Columbia in section
31 412(a) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 788;
32 D.C. Official Code § 1-204.12(a)).
