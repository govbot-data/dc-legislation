Denise Blackson
Mr. Denise Blackson has served on the DC Housing Authority Board of
Commissioners since 2022, when she was elected to a resident
commissioner seat.
She previously served as resident council president of Sibley Plaza
Townhomes and as a Community Outreach Specialist for the Office of
the People’s Counsel. A mother of eight, she prioritizes caring for her
community, passing out food, cleaning products and backpacks for
school-age children.
A Ward 6 resident, Ms. Blackson earned both her Associate of Applied
Science and her Bachelor of Arts in Administration of Justice from
University of the District of Columbia.
