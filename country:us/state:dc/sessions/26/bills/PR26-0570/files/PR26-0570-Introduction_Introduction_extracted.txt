Janice Kim
Ms, Janice Kim has a long history ofsupporting non-profit endeavors,
and in particular, non-profits focused onbroadeningthe reachoftheArts.
She is currently a member of the Board of The Washington Ballet,
committee chair of Community Engagement overseeing new programs,
Dance for All and Dance Program Partnership for DC Public and Private
Schools, anda memberofthe board ofTransformer Gallery, a non-profit
gallery based in Washington, DC that works to promote local and
international emerging contemporary artists
Previously, she was a National Council Member of the Smithsonian
American Art Museum and a former co-chair of Artrageous, the annual
fund-raising gala for the same organization, Ms. Kim has been a long-time supporter of
Washington Performing Arts, EMerge DC Art Fair, and the National Gallery of Art. Ms. Kim
initially worked in commercial lending with First Union Bank then became CFO of a private
commercial and residential development firm, eventually retiring as COO in 2004.
A Ward 3 resident, Ms. Kim has lived in District since 1985.
