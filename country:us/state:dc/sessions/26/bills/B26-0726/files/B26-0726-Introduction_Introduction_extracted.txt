Dear Secretary Howard,
Today, I am introducing the “Willie J. Hardy Way Designation Act of 2026.” Please find
enclosed a signed copy of the legislation.
This legislation would designate the 5000 block of Benning Road SE, between H Street SE and
Southern Avenue SE, in Ward 7 as “Willie J. Hardy Way.” This designation is in honor of Willie
J. Hardy, a longtime Ward 7 resident, community advocate, and public servant.
Willie J. Hardy dedicated her career to advancing opportunity and dignity for District residents,
including service as Executive Director of the Metropolitan Community Aid Council, where she
worked to provide emergency assistance, employment support, and anti-poverty services to
families in need. She also served as the first ever Councilmember from Ward 7 on the Council of
the District of Columbia for six years, including two years as the Chair pro tempore.
With the support of Advisory Neighborhood Commission (ANC) 7E, the designation of Benning
Road SE as “Willie J. Hardy Way” would honor the memory of Willie J. Hardy, and her
dedication and legacy in the Ward 7 community.
Please feel free to reach out to me or my Legislative Director, Ben Raab, with any questions. He
can be reached at braab@dccouncil.gov or by phone at 202-741-0912.
Sincerely,
Wendell Felder
Ward 7 Councilmember
