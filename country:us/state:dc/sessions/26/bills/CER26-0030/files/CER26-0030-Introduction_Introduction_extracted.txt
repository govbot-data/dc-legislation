10 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA

14
15 To recognize and honor Eloise Paige, on the occasion of her 94th birthday, as an outstanding
16 member of her community and for her contributions to the District.
17
18 WHEREAS, Eloise Paige was born in Madison County, Virginia on March 3, 1931;
19 WHEREAS, Eloise Paige later moved to the District in 1952 and was a homemaker for
20 many years, becoming known as a homemaker to not only her own children but to every child in
21 the neighborhood, making sure that every child she came across had something to eat;
22 WHEREAS, Eloise Paige lived on Benning Road, East Capital Street, C Street, SE, Ridge
23 Road, SE, and Capital Heights, Maryland, where she currently resides;
24 WHEREAS, Eloise Paige was and continues to be an outstanding deaconess at Northeast
25 Holy Trinity Church in Ward 6, having been a member of the church community since 1955;
26 WHEREAS, Eloise Paige was also an usher at Northeast Holy Trinity Church, assisting
27 parishioners during church services with whatever needs they had;
28 WHEREAS, Eloise Paige was heavily involved in and present at the church’s regular
29 clothing giveaways;
30 WHEREAS, Eloise Paige, alongside her fellow deaconesses in the church, would go to
31 community members’ homes to help clean and cook for those who were unable to do it for
32 themselves;
1

33 WHEREAS, Eloise Paige was known as a helper to those in the community, a woman of
34 faith, and is beloved by her church community and family; and
35 WHEREAS, Eloise Paige celebrated her 94th birthday on March 3, 2025.
36 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, that this
37 resolution may be cited as the "Eloise Paige Recognition Resolution of 2025".
38 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia recognizes and honors Eloise Paige, on the
39 occasion of her 94th birthday, as an outstanding member of her community and for her
40 contributions to the District.
41 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
