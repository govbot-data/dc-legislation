Angela Hardiman
Ms. Angela Hardiman is a social worker licensed in the District. She
presently works as a social work case manager with Medstar Health as
well as an adjunct professor of social work at Howard University. She
recently served in District government as a permanent supportive housing
monitor for the DC Department of Human Services’ Homeless Services
division.
Ms. Hardiman’s previous work in the District has centered around
juvenile and youth services with the Department of Youth and
Rehabilitative Services, DC Superior Court, and the Public Defender
Service of the District of Columbia. She also formerly worked with
economically disenfranchised and Indigenous communities in Arizona.
A Ward 7 resident, Ms. Hardiman earned her Bachelor of Science in Criminal Justice and
Correctional Services from Jackson State University and her Master of Science from Howard
University.
