Dear Chairman Mendelson:
Enclosed for consideration and approval by the Council of the District of Columbia is the “Salary
Schedule Changes for Non-Union, Uniformed Police Officials Emergency Declaration Resolution
of 2025” and the “Salary Schedule Changes for Non-Union, Uniformed Police Officials
Emergency Approval Resolution of 2025.”
These resolutions approve salary schedules for non-union, uniformed police officials at the
Metropolitan Police Department (“MPD”) that are on a par with the salary schedules for unionized,
uniformed police officials of MPD. The resolutions also approve retroactive pay for affected
employees effective October 8, 2023, for Fiscal Year 2024 and October 6, 2024, for Fiscal Year
2025.
I appreciate your attention to this important agreement that will benefit our police officials by
ensuring pay parity between union and non-union police officials. Please contact me or City
Administrator Kevin Donahue should you have questions concerning this transmittal.
Sincerely,
Muriel Bowser
Mayor

15 To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to approve proposed changes
16 to the salary schedule for non-union, uniformed police officials of the Metropolitan
17 Police Department.
18
19 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
20 resolution may be cited as the "Salary Schedule Changes for Non-Union, Uniformed Police
21 Officials of the Metropolitan Police Department Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025".
22 Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to adjust the salary schedules for non-union,
23 uniformed police officials of the Metropolitan Police Department to provide parity between the
24 salary schedules of union and non-union, uniformed police officials of the Metropolitan Police
25 Department.
26 (b) The Mayor has proposed that the pay-parity salary schedules for current non-union,
27 uniformed police officials of the Metropolitan Police Department be effective retroactively to
28 October 8, 2023, for Fiscal Year 2024; effective retroactively to October 6, 2024, for Fiscal Year
29 2025; and effective October 5, 2025, for Fiscal Year 2026.
30 (c) Under the Mayor's proposal, current non-union, uniformed police officials of the
31 Metropolitan Police Department will receive the following retroactive pay in accordance with the
32 pay-parity salary schedules:

33 (1) For Fiscal Year 2024, current non-union, uniformed police officials will
34 receive retroactive pay for compensation received from October 8, 2023, to October 5, 2024.
35 (2) For Fiscal Year 2025, current non-union, uniformed police officials will
36 receive retroactive pay for compensation received from October 6, 2024, to October 4, 2025.
37 (d) The changes will not apply to former non-union, uniformed police officials of the
38 Metropolitan Police Department, except as provided by 1106(d)(2) of the District of Columbia
39 Government Comprehensive Merit Personnel Act of 1978, effective March 3, 1979 (D.C. Law 2-
40 139; D.C. Official Code § 1-611.06(d)(2)).
41 (e) In order to implement these pay-parity salary schedules, the Department of Human
42 Resources and the Office of the Chief Financial Officer must carry out a number of lengthy
43 administrative and technological actions.
44 (f) It is important that the updated pay schedules and retroactive payments be approved as
45 soon as possible so that the Department of Human Resources and the Office of the Chief
46 Financial Officer can promptly begin to carry out and complete these actions and non-union,
47 uniformed police officials of the Metropolitan Police Department can benefit in a timely manner
48 from the additional income they will receive.
49 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
50 enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Salary
51 Schedule Changes for Non-Union, Uniformed Police Officials of the Metropolitan Police
52 Department Emergency Approval Resolution of 2025 be adopted on an emergency basis.
53 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
