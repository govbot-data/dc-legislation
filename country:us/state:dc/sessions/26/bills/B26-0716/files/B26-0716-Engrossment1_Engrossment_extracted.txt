11 To amend, on a temporary basis, An Act Making appropriations to provide for the expenses of the
12 government of the District of Columbia for the fiscal year ending June thirtieth, nineteen
13 hundred and fourteen and for other purposes, to extend the holdover period for
14 commissioners of the Public Service Commission.
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
17 act may be cited as the “Public Service Commission Holdover Period Extension Temporary
18 Amendment Act of 2026”.
19 Sec. 2. Paragraph 97(a) of section 8 of An Act Making appropriations to provide for the
20 expenses of the government of the District of Columbia for the fiscal year ending June thirtieth,
21 nineteen hundred and fourteen and for other purposes, approved March 4, 1913 (37 Stat. 995; D.C.
22 Official Code § 34-801), is amended by adding a new subsection (c) to read as follows:
23 “(c) Notwithstanding subsection (a) of this paragraph, and section 2(c) of the Confirmation
24 Act of 1978, effective March 3, 1979 (D.C. Law 2-142: D.C. Official Code § 1-523.01(c)), a
25 commissioner may serve in a holdover capacity for no longer than 270 days after expiration of the
26 term to which he or she was appointed.”.
