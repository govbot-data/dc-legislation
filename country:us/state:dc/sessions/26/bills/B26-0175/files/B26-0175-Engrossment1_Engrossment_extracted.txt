11 To amend section 215 of the Uniform College Athlete, Name, or Likeness Act of 2022 to permit
12 an institution, conference, or athletic association to assist a college athlete in selecting,
13 arranging for, or providing payment to a name, image or likeness agent and in selecting,
14 arranging for, or collecting payment from a third party engaged in specific name, image,
15 or likeness agreements with college athletes, and to remove the prohibition against
16 institutions or conferences providing compensation to a college athlete for the use of the
17 athlete’s name, image, or likeness
18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
19 act may be cited as the “Uniform College Athlete Name, Image, or Likeness Amendment Act of
20 2025.”
21 Sec. 2. Section 215 of the Uniform College Athlete Name, Image, or Likeness Act of
22 2022, effective February 23, 2023 (D.C. Law 24-245, D.C. Official Code § 38-1631.05) is
23 amended as follows:
24 (a) Subsection (a) is amended as follows:
25 (1) Paragraph (2) is amended by striking the phrase “; and” and inserting a
26 semicolon in its place.
27 (2) Paragraph (3) is amended by striking the phrase “; and” and inserting a
28 semicolon in its place.
