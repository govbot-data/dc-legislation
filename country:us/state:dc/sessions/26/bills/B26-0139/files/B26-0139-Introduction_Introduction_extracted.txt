Nyasha Howard, Secretary
Council of the District of Columbia
1350 Pennsylvania Avenue, NW
Washington, D.C. 20004
Dear Secretary Howard:
Today, I am introducing the “Gallery Court Designation Act of 2025”. Please find enclosed a
signed copy of the legislation.
This bill would officially designate the public alley system bounded by 3rd Street, N.E., K Street,
N.E., 4th Street, N.E., and I Street, N.E., in Ward 6 as “Gallery Court”. There is a project currently
underway to construct 4 townhomes that face the public alley system and are across the way
from an art gallery. The developers have done extensive engagement with neighbors and the
community and plan to bring townhomes that align with the neighborhood’s characteristics.
Advisory Neighborhood Commission 6C voted on February 12, 2025 to support the naming of the
alley as “Gallery Court”, noting that the name was selected by a consensus of nearby residents.
Please feel free to reach out to me or my Legislative Director, Antonio Nunes, with any questions
or for additional information.
Sincerely,
Councilmember Charles Allen, Ward 6
Chairperson, Committee on Transportation & the Environment
