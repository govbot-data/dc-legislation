ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-350
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
March 3, 2026
To confirm the appointment of Sasha-Gaye Angus to the Housing Production Trust Fund Board.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Housing Production Trust Fund Board Sasha-Gaye Angus
Confirmation Resolution of 2026”.
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia confirms the appointment of:
Sasha-Gaye Angus
Delafield Place, NE
Washington, DC 20017
(Ward 5)
as a representative of an organization that advocates for the production, preservation, and
rehabilitation of affordable housing for lower-income households member of the Housing
Production Trust Fund Board, established by section 3a of the Housing Production Trust Fund
Act of 1988, effective June 8, 1990 (D.C. Law 8-133; D.C. Official Code § 42-2802.01), for a
term to end January 14, 2027.
Sec. 3. The Council shall transmit a copy of this resolution,
upon its adoption, to the nominee and to the Mayor.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
