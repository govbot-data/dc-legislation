5 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
6
7
8 ____________________
9
10 To recognize the work of childcare providers, to celebrate their continuous and important impact
11 on individuals and communities, and to declare Friday, May 8, 2026, as Provider
12 Appreciation Day in the District of Columbia.
13
14 WHEREAS, Child Care Aware® of America and organizations nationwide are
15 recognizing Child Care Providers on this day;

16 WHEREAS, the childcare system supports children’s growth, development, and
17 educational advancement while creating positive economic impacts for families and
18 communities;
19 WHEREAS, childcare providers are essential to the health of our communities by
20 fostering safe and welcoming spaces, supporting working families, and creating enriching
21 environments where children develop lifelong skills;
22 WHEREAS, the District of Columbia recognizes that childcare has been and continues to
23 be a lifeline for families, communities, and the economy;
24
25 WHEREAS, support for affordable, accessible, high-quality childcare represents a
26 worthy commitment to help families work, support children’s healthy development and learning,
27 and keep our economy growing;
28 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this resolution
29 may be cited as the “Provider Appreciation Day Ceremonial Resolution of 2026”.
30
31 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia declares May 8, 2026, as Provider
32 Appreciation Day in the District of Columbia.
33
34 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately upon the first date of publication in
35 the District of Columbia Register.
