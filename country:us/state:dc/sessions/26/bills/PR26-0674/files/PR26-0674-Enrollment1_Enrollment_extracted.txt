ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-414
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
June 2, 2026
To confirm the appointment of James Turner to the Alcoholic Beverage and Cannabis Board.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Alcoholic Beverage and Cannabis Board James Turner
Confirmation Resolution of 2026”.
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia confirms the appointment of:
James Turner
Girard Street, NW
Washington, DC 20009 (Ward 1)
as a member of the Alcoholic Beverage and Cannabis Board, established by D.C. Official Code
§ 25-201, for a term to end May 7, 2027.
Sec. 3. The Council shall transmit a copy of this resolution, upon its adoption, to the
nominee and to the Mayor.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
