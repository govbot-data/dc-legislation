9 To amend, on a temporary basis, the Uniform College Athlete Name, Image, or Likeness
10 Temporary Amendment Act of 2025 to further clarify the applicability date for expanded
11 financial reporting requirements for certain boards and commissions under Section 5710
12 of Title 3 of the District of Columbia Municipal Regulations.
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
15 act may be cited as the “District of Columbia Boards and Commissions Financial Reporting
16 Temporary Amendment Act of 2025”.
17 Sec. 2. Section 3 of the Uniform College Athlete Name, Image, or Likeness Temporary
18 Amendment Act of 2025, effective August 21, 2025 (D.C. Law 26-26; 72 DCR 9565), is
19 amended to read as follows:
20 “5710.3 This section shall not apply before April 3, 2026.”.
21 Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
22 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement in the committee report as the fiscal
23 impact statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975,
24 approved October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
25 Sec. 4. Effective date.
26 (a) This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by
