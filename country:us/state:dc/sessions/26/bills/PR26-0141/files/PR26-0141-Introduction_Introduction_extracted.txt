Appointments, should the Council require additional information.

Roberta Bell
Ms. Roberta Bell, who is currently retired, has held both professional
and academic leadership roles within Howard University Hospital. Most
recently, she served as a Clinical Nurse for the Department of Maternal
Child Health Nursing .
Ms. Bell plays an active role in her community as a member of numerous
local and national agencies, including the National Association of Bench
and Bar Spouses, Inc. Ms. Bell has also been highly regarded by her
community, winning several awards from the Department of Maternal
Health Nursing and the District of Columbia Nurses Association.
A Ward 4 resident, Ms. Bell earned her Bachelor of Science in Biology
from Paine College, her Bachelor of Science in Nursing Administration from the Medical College
of Georgia, and her Master of Nursing focusing on Nursing Administration from Howard
University.
