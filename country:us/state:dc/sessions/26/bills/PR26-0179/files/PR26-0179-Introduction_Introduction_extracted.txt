Enclosed for consideration and enactment by the Council of the District of Columbia is an
emergency bill, the “Contract No. CW108863 with La Clinica del Pueblo, Inc. Approval and
Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2025,” and the accompanying emergency declaration
resolution.
The legislation will approve Contract No. CW108863 with La Clinica del Pueblo, Inc., for
substance use disorder outpatient treatment services, and Modification Nos. M0001, M0002, and
M0003 thereto. In addition, the legislation will approve payment for the goods and services
received and to be received under the contract and the modifications.

45 approval, La Clínica del Pueblo, Inc. cannot be paid for goods and services provided in excess of
