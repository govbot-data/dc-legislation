Stacie Birenbach
Ms. Stacie Birenbach is an affordable housing and community
development leader with more than 20 years of progressive experience.
She currently serves as a Vice President of Real Estate Development with
Enterprise Community Development, an affiliate of Enterprise
Community Partners. She leads staff in managing development functions
from initial feasibility through stabilization throughout Maryland and the
District of Columbia. She has led the preservation and production of more
than 1,200 homes in the District.
Ms. Birenbach brought extensive local and national affordable housing
finance experience to this role having previously served at the District of
Columbia’s Department of Housing and Community Development and at
the Local Initiatives Support Corporation. She began her community development career as a
Peace Corps Volunteer in the former Soviet Union. She is a Certified Public Manager and Lean
Six Sigma Green Belt. She currently serves on the Board of the Coalition of Nonprofit Housing
and Economic Development and has been a Ward 5 resident since 2006.
A Ward 5 resident, Ms. Birenbach earned her Bachelor of Arts in Spanish and International Studies
from Emory University and her Master of Public Administration from Columbia University.
