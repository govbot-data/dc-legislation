16 To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to officially designating the public alley
17 system within square 1084 as Elmore-Friendship Court.
18
19 RESOLVED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
20 resolution may be cited as the “Elmore-Friendship Court Designation Emergency Declaration
21 Resolution of 2025”.
22 Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to officially designate the public alley system
23 within square 1084 as Elmore-Friendship Court.
24 (b) Bill 26-220, the “Elmore-Friendship Court Designation” was adopted on final
25 reading by the Council on July 28, 2025.
26 (c) The underlying legislation will allow the applicant who owns a lot in fronting the
27 alley to obtain District agency approvals for re-development of an existing carriage house into a
28 residential unit, including permits and utility location, before the permanent legislation
29 completes the 30-day congressional review period.
30 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia finds that the circumstances enumerated
31 in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the “Elmore-Friendship
32 Court Designation Emergency Act of 2025” be adopted after a single reading.
33 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
