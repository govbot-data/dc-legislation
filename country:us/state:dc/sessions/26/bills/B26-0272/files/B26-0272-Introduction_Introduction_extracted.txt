14 To amend, on an emergency basis, the Rental Housing Act of 1985 to extend the deadline from
15 90 days to 180 days for housing providers to file a new registration statement after the
16 completion of the publicly accessible rent control housing database, and to require
17 owners of rental accommodations that are exempt from rent stabilization to report current
18 and prior rent amounts for the previous two calendar years at the time of registration.
19
20 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
21 act may be cited as the “Rental Housing Registration Extension Emergency Amendment Act of
22 2025”.
23
24 Sec.2. Section 205(f) of the Rental Housing Act of 1985, effective October 22, 2015
25 (D.C. Law 21-36; D.C. Official Code § 42-3502.05(f)), is amended as follows:
26 (a)Paragraph (1) is amended by striking the number “90” and inserting the number “180”
27 in its place.
28 (b)Paragraph (2) is amended by striking the number “90” and inserting the number “180”
29 in its place.
30 (c)Paragraph (4) is amended as follows:
31 (1)Subparagraph (A) is amended by striking the number “90” and inserting the
32 number “180” in its place.
33 (2)Subparagraph (B)(i) is amended by striking the number “91” and inserting the
34 number “181” in its place.
35 (d)A new paragraph (5) is added to read as follows:
1

36 “(5)(A) The owner of a rental accommodation that is exempt from rent
37 stabilization pursuant to subsection (a) of this section shall, at the time of registration, provide
38 the following information for each rental unit:
39 “(i) The current monthly rent amount; and
40 “(ii) The monthly rent amount charged for the same unit during
41 each of the two prior calendar years.
42 “(B) The information required under subparagraph (A) of this paragraph
43 shall be submitted only at the time of registration and shall not be subject to any ongoing or
44 annual reporting requirements.”
