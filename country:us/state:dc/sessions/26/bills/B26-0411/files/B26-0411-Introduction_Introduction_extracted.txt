15 To amend the Child Fatality Review Committee Establishment Act of 2001 to revise the
16 composition of the committee to include the relevant persons within the Council of the
17 District of Columbia with oversight over the Child and Family Services Agency.
18
19 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
20 act may be cited as the “Child Fatality Review Committee Amendment Act of 2025”.
21 Sec. 2. Section 4604(a-1) of the Child Fatality Review Committee Establishment Act of
22 24 2001, effective October 3, 2001 (D.C. Law 14-28, D.C. Official Code § 4-1371.04), is
23 amended by striking the phrase “human services” and inserting the phrase “child and family
24 services” in its place.
25 Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
26 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Budget Director as the fiscal impact
27 statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975, approved
28 October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
29 Sec. 4. Effective date.
30 This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by the
31 Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), and shall remain in effect for no longer than
32 90 days, as provided for emergency acts of the Council of the District of Columbia in section

33 412(a) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 788;
34 36 D.C. Official Code § 1-204.12(a)).
