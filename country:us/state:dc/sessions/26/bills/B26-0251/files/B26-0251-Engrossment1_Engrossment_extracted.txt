12 To amend, on a temporary basis, the District of Columbia Health Occupations Revision Act of
13 1985 to allow an exemption from licensure for health professionals licensed in foreign
14 countries who accompany foreign sports teams for athletic competitions in the District of
15 Columbia.
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
18 act may be cited as the “Health Occupations Revision Exemption Temporary Amendment Act of
19 2025”.
20 Sec. 2. Section 502(a) of the District of Columbia Health Occupations Revision Act of
21 1985, effective March 25, 1986 (D.C. Law 6-99; D.C. Official Code § 3-1205.02(a)), is amended
22 by adding a new paragraph (3B) to read as follows:
23 “(3B) To an individual licensed, registered or certified in good standing to
24 practice a health occupation in another country who is in the District to attend to the healthcare
25 needs of an athletic team from another country that is competing in a an organized sporting
26 event, as defined by section 3(3A) of the District of Columbia Smoking Restriction Act of 1979,
27 effective September 28, 1979 (D.C. Law 3-22; D.C. Code § 7-1702(3A)); provided that,:
1

28 “(A) The time period during which the provisions of this act prohibiting
29 the practice of a health occupation without a District of Columbia license, registration, or
30 certification shall not apply to the individual (the “exemption”) shall not exceed 30 consecutive
31 days;
32 “(B) The exemption shall be limited to healthcare provided to the athletes,
33 support personnel, and coaching staff of the athletic team at the team basecamp, lodging, and
34 training sites and at the site of any matches within the District while the team is competing in
35 those matches; and
36 “(C) The exemption shall not provide the authority to the individual to
37 practice at a healthcare clinic or facility in the District.”.
38 Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
39 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Chief Financial Officer as the fiscal
40 impact statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975,
41 approved October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
42 Sec. 4. Effective date.
43 (a) This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by
44 the Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), a 30-day period of congressional review
45 as provided in section 602(c)(1) of the District of Columbia in section 412(a) of the District of
46 Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 813; D.C. Official Code § 1-
47 1206.02(c)(1)), and publication in the District of Columbia Register.
