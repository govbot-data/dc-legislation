11 To amend the Prevention of Child Abuse and Neglect Act of 1977 to require the Child and
12 Family Services Agency to inform, at the initiation of an investigation, caretakers of any
13 legal resources available to the caretaker, the availability of the Office of the
14 Ombudsperson for Children as a resource for complaints, and their right to counsel
15 during critical stages of neglect proceedings.
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this act may
18 be cited as the “Empowering Parents in CFSA Investigations Amendment Act of 2025”.
19 Sec. 2. Title 1 of the Prevention of Child Abuse and Neglect Act of 1977, effective
20 September 23, 1977 (D.C. Law 2-22; D.C. Official Code § 4-1301.04 et seq.), is amended as
21 follows:
22 (a) A new subsection (c)(3)(H) is added to read as follows:
23 “(H) Written notification of legal resources available to the child’s reported
24 caretaker or caretakers at the initiation of the investigation process.
25 (b) Subsection (c)(3)(B) is amended to read as follows:
26 “(B) Conducting an interview with the child’s caretaker or caretakers during
27 which the agency shall inform the caretaker of their right to counsel pursuant to §16-2304(b)(1),
