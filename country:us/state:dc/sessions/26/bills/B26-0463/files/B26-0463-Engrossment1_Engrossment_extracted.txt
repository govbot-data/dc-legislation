17 of 2026”.

45 “(2) Within 6 months of the applicable date of this title, DHCF shall submit for
46 approval from the Centers for Medicare and Medicaid Services an amendment to the Medicaid

154 “Sec. 414. Premiums.
155 “(a) To determine countable net income, DHCF shall subtract from the enrollee’s total
156 gross income:
157 “(1) The general income exclusions and disregards and the specific income
158 exclusions and disregards for aged, blind, or disabled coverage groups as specified under section
159 405; and
160 “(2) Any documented cost-sharing for current employer-sponsored insurance
161 coverage for the applicant.
162 “(3) DHCF shall include in the enrollee’s total gross income any annuity, pension,
163 retirement, or disability benefits to which the enrollee is entitled, including:
164 “(A) Veterans’ compensation and pensions;

165 “(B) Social Security Disability Insurance and other Social Security
166 benefits;
167 “(C) Railroad retirement benefits; and
168 “(D) Unemployment compensation.
169 “(b) An enrollee’s assets or income may not be considered in determining an enrollee’s
170 eligibility for coverage under the program.
171 “(c) An enrollee shall pay a monthly premium of:
172 “(1) For incomes at or below 138% of the federal poverty level, $0 per month;
173 “(2) For incomes above 139% to 200% of the federal poverty level, $25 per
174 month;
175 “(3) For incomes above 200% to 250% of the federal poverty level, $40 per
176 month;
177 “(4) For incomes above 250% to 300% of the federal poverty level, $55 per
178 month;
179 “(5) For incomes above 300% to 450% of the federal poverty level, 4% of net
180 countable monthly income;
181 “(6) For incomes above 450% to 600% of the federal poverty level, 5% of net
182 countable monthly income; and
183 “(7) For incomes above 600% of the federal poverty level, 7.5% of net countable
184 monthly income.

185 “(d) DHCF may, through rulemaking, adjust annually the monthly premium amounts to
186 account for changes to the Consumer Price Index.
187 “(e)(1)(A) An enrollee shall timely submit the applicable monthly premium unless they

257 “Sec. 417. Scope of coverage.
258 “(a) An enrollee shall be eligible for medically necessary services if the services are:
259 “(1) Furnished by a provider who is approved by DHCF to participate in the
260 District’s Medicaid program;
261 “(2) Covered under the District’s Medicaid state plan;

263 health insurance available to the enrollee; and
264 “(4) Not subject to third party liability.
265 “(b) The District shall not cover copayments, deductibles, or coinsurance applicable to

285 “Sec. 420. District Workers with Disabilities Premium Fund.
286 “(a) There is established as a special fund the District Workers with Disabilities Premium
287 Fund (“Fund”), which shall be administered by DHCF in accordance with subsections (c) and (d)
288 of this section.
289 “(b) All premium revenue funds collected pursuant to section 414 shall be deposited in the
290 Fund.
291 “(c) Money in the Fund shall be used for the following purposes:
292 “(1) To offset the District’s share of Medicaid expenditures incurred for the
293 program; and
294 “(2) To support administrative costs directly attributable to the program.
295 “(d)(1) The money deposited into the Fund but not expended in a fiscal year shall not
296 revert to the unassigned fund balance of the General Fund of the District of Columbia at the end
297 of a fiscal year, or at any other time.

299 appropriated in the Fund shall be continually available without regard to fiscal year limitation.”.
