Tracy Hamilton
Ms. Tracy Hamilton is a social worker licensed in the District. She most
recently worked as the Family Services Director for Collaborative
Solution for Communities in Washington, DC where she was promoted
from her role as a Clinical Social Work Contractor.
Previously, Ms. Hamilton served as the Chairperson of the Citizen
Review Panel for Child Abuse and Neglect from 2019 to 2021 and has
served in a variety of family and youth positions including with the DC
Child and Family Services Agency. Her fields of expertise include social
services, child welfare, and mental health, in addition to training in
cognitive behavioral therapy, solution focused therapy, crisis
intervention, and QSR.
A Ward 7 resident, Ms. Hamilton earned her Bachelor of Social Work from the University of the
District of Columbia and her Master of Social Work for Catholic University of America.
