11 To amend, on a temporary basis, the Rental Housing Act of 1985, to require owners of rental
12 accommodations that are exempt from rent stabilization to report current and prior rent
13 amounts for the previous two calendar years and the utilities, facilities and services, and
14 appliances at the time of registration.
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
17 act may be cited as the “Rental Housing Registration Data Integrity Temporary Amendment Act
18 of 2026”.
19 Sec.2. Section 205(f) of the Rental Housing Act of 1985, effective October 22, 2015
20 (D.C. Law 21-36; D.C. Official Code § 42-3502.05(f)), is amended as follows:
21 (1) A new paragraph (5) is added to read as follows:
22 “(5)(A) The owner of a rental accommodation that is exempt from rent
23 stabilization pursuant to subsection (a) of this section shall, at the time of registration, provide
24 the following information for each rental unit:
25 (i) The current monthly rent amount;
26 (ii) The monthly rent amount charged for the same unit during each of the
27 two prior calendar years; and
1

28 (iii) The utilities, facilities and services, and appliances.
29 (B) The information required under subparagraph (A) of this paragraph shall be
30 submitted only at the time of registration and shall not be subject to any ongoing or annual
31 reporting requirements.”
32 Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
33 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Budget Director as the fiscal impact
34 statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975, approved
35 October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
36 Sec. 4. Effective date.
37 (a) This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by
38 the Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), a 30-day period of congressional review
39 as provided in section 602(c)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December
40 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 813; D.C. Official Code § 1-206.02(c)(1)), and publication in the District of
41 Columbia Register.
42 (b) This act shall expire after 225 days of its having taken effect.
