Ms. Jennifer Anderson is a Private Client Advisor for Berry Bros, Rudd.
She isa freelance political andcorporateeventproducer, a sommelier, and
a current member of the Commission on Asian and Pacific Islander

Prior to her current roles, Ms. Anderson served on the founding executive
leadership team of Project N95, which tackled personal protective
equipment shortages at the onset of the COVID-19 pandemic. She also
managed on-the-ground logistics for campaign events, as a trip and
advance lead for Senator Elizabeth Warren’s presidential campaign.
A Ward 5 resident, Ms. Anderson earned her Bachelor of Arts in
International Studies from American University.
