15 To amend, on an temporary basis, Chapter 46 of Title 47 of the District of Columba
16 Official Code to designate the property as affordable housing for which Fair Market Rents, as
17 calculated by the US Department of Housing and Urban Development, apply.
18
19 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
20 Act may be cited as the “Reservoir District Tax Exemption Temporary Amendment Act
21 of 2026”.
22 Sec. 2. § 47–4683 is amended as follows:
23 (a) Section 47-4683(b)(2) is amended to read as follows:
24 “(2) Set aside 1/3 of the operating rental housing units in the Property as affordable as
25 defined by 26 U.S. Code § 42(g)(2)(A). The units designated as affordable will be available to
26 households qualifying for the 80 Percent Income Limit Category of the Multifamily Tax Subsidy
27 Project Income Limits for the Washington-Arlington-Alexandria, DC-VA-MD HUD Metro Fair
28 Market Rent Area as reported annually by the US Department of Housing and Urban
29 Development and made in a manner consistent with I.R.C. § 142(d)(2)(B).
