ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
26-182
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
June 30, 2026
To recognize and honor A. Litteri Inc. on its 100th anniversary of providing quality food, drink
and flavors of Italy to District residents.
WHEREAS, A. Litteri Inc. was opened in 1926 by Antonio Litteri, a World War One
veteran, who immigrated to the United States from Sicily in the early 1900s and was joined in
1932 by his brother in-law Mariano DeFrancisci, a native of Valguarnera, Sicily, and;
WHEREAS, A. Litteri Inc. was originally opened in what is now Ward 2 at 6th and G
Streets NW and in 1931 moved to 5th and K Street NE before settling at the current location in
Ward 5, 517 Morse Street, NE in 1932;
WHEREAS, A. Litteri Inc. is the oldest remaining business of Union Market’s
wholesaler district which has provided authentic, high-quality, Italian products to residents
across the city since it first opened in 19311;
WHEREAS, the business has been repeatedly recognized as a local favorite by outlets
like the Washingtonian and has received awards including Winner of the 2017 Leccio d'Oro
Award for Best Wine Shop from the Consorzio del vino Brunello di Montalcino; and
WHEREAS, Michael DeFrancisci, grandson of Mariano DeFrancisci, passed the business
to its current owner Max Evans in 2020, continuing the 100 year legacy of providing exceptional
products and service as a neighborhood staple beloved by residents and visitors alike.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “A. Litteri Inc. 100th Anniversary Recognition Resolution of
2026”.
