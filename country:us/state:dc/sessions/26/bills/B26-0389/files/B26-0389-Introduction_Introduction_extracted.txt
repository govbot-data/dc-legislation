16 To amend, on a temporary basis, the Office of District Waterways Management Establishment Act
17 of 2022 to make the process for Mayoral appointment of members of the District
18 Waterways Advisory Commission consistent with that of similar commissions, to add the
19 Department of Parks and Recreation as an ex-officio non-voting member of the District
20 Waterways Advisory Commission, to adjust the District Waterways Advisory Commission
21 meeting schedule to allow sufficient time for engagement with stakeholders and the District
22 Waterways Advisory Commission in development of the Advisory Plan, and to provide
23 the District Waterways Advisory Commission with authority to determine whether the
24 Advisory Plan needs to be updated and extend the update period to at least every 3 years;
25 and to amend the Confirmation Act of 1978 to make a conforming amendment.
26
27 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
28 act may be cited as the “Office of District Waterways Management Establishment Temporary
29 Amendment Act of 2025”.
