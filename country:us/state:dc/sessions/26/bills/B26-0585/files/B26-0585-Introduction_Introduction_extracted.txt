16 To amend, on an emergency basis, the Green Building Act of 2006 to exempt certain residential
17 and nonresidential projects from maintaining net zero energy compliance and to modify net
18 zero energy compliance requirements for nonresidential and residential projects; to amend
19 section 2 of the Clean Energy DC Building Code Amendment Act of 2022 to modify the
20 definition of net-zero-energy standards and the types of projects for which the net-zero-
21 energy standards apply; and to repeal section 6 of the Housing Production Trust Fund Act of
22 1988 pertaining to additional net zero energy requirements for subsidized projects.
23
24 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this act
25 may be cited as the “Net Zero Modification and Preservation Emergency Amendment Act of 2026”.
