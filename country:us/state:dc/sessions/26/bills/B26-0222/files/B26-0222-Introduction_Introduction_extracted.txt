Dear Secretary Smith,
I am introducing the “Alfred Dudley, Sr. Way Designation Act of 2025”. Please see enclosed
signed copy of the legislation.
This legislation will symbolically designate the 2900 block of Mills Avenue, N.E., in Ward 5, as
Alfred Dudley, Sr. Way. I previously introduced the Alfred DeGreat Dudley, Sr. Posthumous
Recognition Resolution of 2023, and I am proud to introduce this legislation with the support of
his family that will create a permanent memorial to his legacy.
Over the course of his illustrious career, Mr. Alfred DeGreat Dudley, Sr. purchased and became
President of Dudley Beauty College located at 2031 Rhode Island Avenue, N.E., in Ward 5.
Dudley Beauty College sits adjacent to the 2900 block of Mills Avenue, N.E. Mr. Dudley, Sr. was
instrumental in helping many sales representatives, cosmetologists, barbers, and nail
technologists become successful entrepreneurs, and mentored many as they strove to reach their
full potential. In 2006, Mr. Dudley, Sr. received the Outstanding Individuals and Organizations
Award for Demonstrated Leadership in Business given by the Washington, D.C. Chapter of the
National Association for the Advancement of Colored People. Alfred DeGreat Dudley, Sr. passed
on May 21, 2023, and leaves a legacy that will live on in the beauty industry and for generations
to come.
Please feel free to reach out to me or my Legislative Director, Neferteria Brown, with any
questions or for additional information. She can be reached at nbrown@dccouncil.gov or by
phone at (771) 333-9734.

Sincerely,
Zachary Parker
Ward 5 Councilmember
Chair, Committee on Youth Affairs
