emergency bill, the “Modification Nos. M0009, M0010, M0011, M0012, M0013, and M0014 to
Contract No. CW101626 with Collaborative Solutions for Communities Approval and Payment
Authorization Emergency Act of 2025,” and the accompanying emergency declaration resolution.
