Clarence Miles
Mr. Clarence Miles is a life-long resident of Washington, DC and serves
as the Executive Director of Uniting Our Youth, LLC (UOY), a nonprofit
organization whose mission is to combat juvenile crime by providing life-
readiness and social aptness skills to the youth of DC. In his role, Mr.
Miles oversees programs partnering with the DC Housing Authority in
some of the city’s hardest hit underserved communities.
Prior to the inception of UOY in 2008, Mr. Miles’s professional career
expanded an array of community advocate organizations, including
serving as Youth Director/Outreach for the Alliance for Concerned Men
of Washington, DC. He served as Youth Counselor at Sasha Bruce
Youthwork’s Bruce House a 24-hour youth emergency safe house and as
a mental health counselor at Community Connection and Life Stride.
A Ward 6 resident, Mr. Miles earned his high school diploma from Dunbar High School.
