ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-246
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
November 4, 2025
To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to grant the Chief Financial
Officer the authority to utilize funds allocated in the Fiscal Year 2026 budget for the
forgiveness of up to $377,000 in real property taxes, interest, penalties, fees, and other
related charges assessed against the real property designated for tax purposes as Lot 123,
Square 2836, located at 3421 14th Street, NW (“Property”), which is owned by Avanti
Real Estate Services, LLC.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Avanti Real Estate Services, LLC Real Property Tax Relief
Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
Sec. 2. (a) The Avanti Real Estate, Inc. Real Property Tax Exemption Amendment Act of
2024, as introduced on July 5, 2024 (Bill 25-906), was introduced by Councilmember Nadeau. A
hearing was held on the bill by the Committee on Business and Economic Development on
September 16, 2024. This legislation was reintroduced in Council Period 26 as the Avanti Real
Estate, Inc. Real Property Tax Exemption Amendment Act of 2025, as introduced on January 3,
2025 (Bill 26-0019).
(b) Nadeau Amendment No. 1, passed at second reading of the Fiscal Year 2026 Budget
Support Act of 2025, enacted on September 12, 2025 (D.C. Act 26-148; 72 DCR 9825),
allocated $377,000 in Fiscal Year 2026 for the forgiveness of real property taxes, interest,
penalties, fees, or other related charges assessed against the real property designated for tax
purposes as Lot 123, Square 2836, located at 3421 14th Street, NW, (“Property”), which is
owned by Avanti Real Estate Services, LLC, to further the purposes of providing real estate and
brokerage services, specializing in creating generational wealth through homeownership,
employing District residents, and providing education and training for employment opportunities
and careers within the real estate industry.
(b) Emergency legislation is necessary to grant the Chief Financial Officer the authority
to issue the funds allocated in the Fiscal Year 2026 budget.
Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
enumerated in in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances, making it necessary that the

Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
