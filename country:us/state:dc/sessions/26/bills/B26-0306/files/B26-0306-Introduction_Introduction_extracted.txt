21 To amend, on an emergency basis due to congressional review, section 215 of the Uniform
22 College Athlete, Name, Image, or Likeness Act of 2022 to permit an institution,
23 conference, or athletic association to assist a college athlete in selecting, arranging for, or
24 providing payment to a name, image, or likeness agent and in selecting, arranging for, or
25 collecting payment from a third party engaged in specific name, image, or likeness
26 agreements with college athletes, and to remove the prohibition prohibiting institutions or
27 conferences from providing compensation to a college athlete for the athlete's name,
28 image, or likeness, and to amend section 5710 of Title 3 of the District of Columbia
29 Municipal Regulations to delay applicability of expanded financial disclosure
30 requirements for certain boards and commissions.
31
32 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
33 act may be cited as the "Uniform College Athlete Name, Image, or Likeness Congressional
34 Emergency Review Amendment Act of 2025."
35 Sec. 2. Section 215 of the Uniform College Athlete Name, Image, or Likeness Act of
36 2022 (D.C. Law 24-245; D.C. Official Code § 38-1631.05), is amended as follows:
37 (a) Subsection (a) is amended as follows:
38 (1) Paragraph (2) is amended by striking the phrase “; and’ and inserting a
39 semicolon in its place.
1

40 (2) Paragraph (3) is amended by striking the phrase “; and” and inserting a
41 semicolon in its place.
42 (3) New paragraphs (4) and (5) are added to read as follows:
43 “(4) With selecting, arranging for, or providing payment to a name, image, or
44 likeness agent; and
45 “(5) With selecting, arranging for, or collecting payment from a third party
46 engaged in specific name, image, or likeness agreements with a college athlete or athletes.”.
47 (b) Subsection (e) is repealed.
48 Sec. 3. Section 5710 of Title 3 of the District of Columbia Municipal Regulations (3
49 DCMR § 5710) is amended by adding a new subsection 5710.03 to read as follows:
50 “5710.3 This section shall not apply before January 1, 2026.”
51 Sec. 4. Fiscal impact statement.
52 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Budget Director as the fiscal impact
53 statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975, approved
54 October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
55 Sec. 5. Effective date.
56 This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by the
57 Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), and shall remain in effect for no longer than
58 90 days, as provided for emergency acts of the Council of the District of Columbia in section
59 412(a) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 788;
60 D.C. Official Code § 1-204.12(a)).
