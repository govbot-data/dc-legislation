Asha Shagle
Ms. Asha Shagle has over twenty years of experience in managing all
phases of a project, on a multitude of project scales, ranging from
masterplan, civic, urban, and private landscape projects. She believes her
global experience, with exposure and thorough understanding of laws and
ordinances at various municipalities, all focused on health, safety, and
welfare of each community are at the foundation of her work.
Ms. Shagle grew up in a vibrant culture that is deeply rooted in the arts
with reverence for all things big and small. She embraces this creative
culture, especially all aspects that are relevant to nature and community.
Ms. Shagle’s academic training both as an architect and a landscape
architect has ingrained in her to think and illustrate in fluid third
dimensions. With these foundations, she strives for holistic design through creative problem
solving.
A Ward 6 resident, Ms. Shagle earned her Bachelor of Architecture Degree from the BMS College
of Engineering in Bangalore, India. Ms. Shagle additionally earned her Post Graduate Diploma in
Landscape Architecture from CEPT, School of Architecture in Ahmedabad India, her Certification
in GIS Analysis from the University of Washington, and is a Registered Landscape Architect in
the District of Columbia.
