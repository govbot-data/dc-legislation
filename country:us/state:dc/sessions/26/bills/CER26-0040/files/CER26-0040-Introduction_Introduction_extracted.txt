16 A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
17
18 __________
19
20 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
21
22 ______________
23
24 To recognize and honor Father Michael J. Kelley on the 50th anniversary of his ordination
25 as a priest.
26
27 WHEREAS, Father Michael J. Kelley was born in Spokane, Washington and
28 ordained as a priest of the Archdiocese of Washington on May 17, 1975;
29
30 WHEREAS, in 1992, Father Michael J. Kelley was named pastor of St. Martin of
31 Tours parish in Washington, DC, where he continues to serve as pastor today;
32
33 WHEREAS, St. Martin of Tours Catholic Church is an open and diverse parish that
34 promotes justice, peace, and service under the direction and leadership of Father Michael
35 J. Kelley;
36
37 WHEREAS, Father Michael J. Kelley partnered with Catholic Charities, local
38 community groups, and the District of Columbia government to build and manage The
39 Summit at St. Martin’s Apartments, an affordable workforce housing apartment complex;
40
41 WHEREAS, Father Michael J. Kelley opens St. Martin’s Community Center any
42 day of the week to give children in the neighborhood a safe place to play basketball;
43
44 WHEREAS, Father Michael J. Kelley provides space for community organizations at the
45 St. Martin’s Community Center, including numerous Alcoholics Anonymous and Narcotics
46 Anonymous groups that meet seven days a week;
47

48 WHEREAS, Father Michael J. Kelley is a founding member of the North Capitol Street
49 and Rhode Island Avenue Ecumenical Council comprised of seven churches that serve the
50 spiritual, educational, and social needs of their respective communities;
51
52 WHEREAS, Father Michael J. Kelley serves as an example of humility, selflessness, and
53 generosity and has guided people, families, and St. Martin’s parish through difficult times; and
54
55 WHEREAS, Father Michael J. Kelley led the St. Martin’s parish through the worst
56 months of the COVID-19 pandemic by transitioning mass to a virtual platform in order to
57 continue ministering in a time of great need.
58
59 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, that this
60 resolution be cited as the “Father Michael J. Kelley Recognition Resolution of 2025”.
61
62 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia recognizes and honors Father Michael J.
63 Kelley for his many years of service to St. Martin of Tours parish, to the Bloomingdale
64 community, and to the District of Columbia.
65
66 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately upon the first date of publication in
67 the District of Columbia Register
