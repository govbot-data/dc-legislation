ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-185
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
July 28, 2025
To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to adopt the local portion of
the budget of the District of Columbia for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2026.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Fiscal Year 2026 Local Budget Emergency Declaration
Resolution of 2025”.
Sec. 2. (a) The Mayor transmitted the Fiscal Year 2026 Local Budget Act of 2025, passed
on 2nd reading on July 28, 2025 (Enrolled version of Bill 26-260) (“Fiscal Year 2026 Local
Budget Act of 2025”), to the Council on May 27, 2025; that measure is scheduled for its second
and final reading on July 28, 2025.
(b) In order for the District government to operate fully and provide necessary services to
District residents, visitors, businesses, and others in Fiscal Year 2026, the Fiscal Year 2026
Budget and Financial Plan must be effective prior to October 1, 2025, which is the start of Fiscal
Year 2026.
(c) Due to the timing of the submission and adoption of the Fiscal Year 2026 budget and
the congressional recess schedule, the Fiscal Year 2026 Local Budget Act of 2025 is not
expected to complete its congressional review period before October 1, 2025.
(d) Accordingly, the Mayor transmitted to the Council the Fiscal Year 2026 Local Budget
Emergency Act of 2025, which is not subject to a congressional review period but is limited in
the duration of its effectiveness, so that an approved budget and financial plan for Fiscal Year
2026 may be effective before the start of the fiscal year while the Fiscal Year 2026 Local Budget
Act of 2025 completes congressional review.
Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Fiscal
Year 2026 Local Budget Emergency Act of 2025 be adopted after a single reading.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
