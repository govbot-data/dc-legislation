13 To honor and recognize the 30th Anniversary of the Million Man March, a historic demonstration
14 for unity, atonement, and responsibility that took place on the National Mall in the District of
15 Columbia on October 16, 1995.
16 WHEREAS, on October 16, 1995, thousands of Black men from across the United States
17 gathered peacefully on the National Mall for the Million Man March, one of the largest
18 demonstrations in American history;
19 WHEREAS, the Million Man March was organized chiefly by Minister Louis Farrakhan
20 with the aid of other leaders such as Benjamin Chavis Jr., the National African American
21 Leadership Summit, and other Grassroot organizers and Black Faith Leaders;
22 WHEREAS, the purpose of the Million Man March was to emphasize spiritual renewal,
23 family responsibility, community service, and social justice, inspiriting millions of individuals
24 nationwide to commit to personal and collective uplift;

25 WHEREAS, the District of Columbia served as the host city for this historic gathering,
26 which showcased the power of peaceful assembly and the enduring importance of the nation’s
27 capital as the center of civic expression;
28 WHEREAS, the March generated long-lasting impacts, including increased civic
29 participation, voter registration efforts, and community development initiatives that continue to
30 influence movements for racial and social justice today;
31 WHEREAS, 2025 marks the 30th anniversary of the Million Man March, providing an
32 opportunity for the District of Columbia to commemorate this defining moment in American
33 history, to reflect on its legacy and to honor the ongoing struggle for equality and justice; and
34 WHEREAS, the Council of the District of Columbia recognizes the Million Man March
35 as a milestone in the continuing pursuit of justice, unity, and empowerment for Black
36 communities in the District and nationwide:
37 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, that this
38 resolution may be cited as the “Million Man March 30th Anniversary Recognition Resolution of
39 2025.”
40 Sec.2. The Council of the District of Columbia honors and recognizes the 30th
41 Anniversary of the Million Man March and commends the legacy of unity, responsibility, and
42 commitment to social justice that it represents.
43 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
