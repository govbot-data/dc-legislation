4 A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
5 ______________
6
7 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
8 _________________
9
10
11 To recognize, honor, and express gratitude to James Spaulding for his years of service to the
12 District of Columbia.
13
14 WHEREAS, James Spaulding received a bachelor’s degree from Haverford College,
15 Pennsylvania, and a Master in Economics and Doctorate in Economics from the University of
16 Wisconsin, Madison;
17
18 WHEREAS, James Spaulding joined the Office of the Chief Financial Officer’s (OCFO)
19 Office of Budget and Planning in 2000 as a Branch Chief and worked on the District’s financial
20 plan and labor costs. In 2002, he served as Director, and was responsible for grants’ budgeting. He
21 then spent several years as Director of the Capital Improvements Program. He was appointed
22 Associate Deputy Chief Financial Officer for the Office of Budget and Planning in April 2008. In
23 this role, he worked on all aspects of the District of Columbia’s operating and capital budgets, as
24 well as the Office of Budget and Planning’s reporting functions;
25
26 WHEREAS, Prior to joining the District of Columbia government, Mr. Spaulding spent 11
27 years at the U.S. General Accountability Office, where he was a senior evaluator, primarily focused
28 on labor and higher education issues;
29
30 WHEREAS, James Spaulding represented the OCFO’s ideal of a public servant who strives
31 every day to provide the highest standard of service to residents, businesses, visitors, and the
32 government of the District of Columbia by being Service Driven with a Strong Work Ethic;
33 Motivated and Mission Focused; Accountable; Respectful of his Customers and Co-Workers;
34 Trustworthy and Team-Focused; Empowered to Achieve Excellence; and Results Oriented, or
35 SMARTER; and
36
37 WHEREAS, James Spaulding will be remembered professionally with gratitude, by
38 countless colleagues who were better able to perform their jobs because of his commitment to
39 share his extensive knowledge in service to the District of Columbia; and
40
41 WHEREAS, James Spaulding gained the respect and admiration of members in the
42 legislative and executive branches of the District government as a professional who delivered high-
43 quality service to the residents of the District of Columbia for 23 years prior to his retirement in
44 2023.
45

46 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
47 resolution may be cited as the “James Spaulding Ceremonial Recognition Resolution of 2025”.
48 Sec. 2. The Council recognizes and commends James Spaulding for his work in the OCFO
49 and for his distinguished service and valuable contributions he has made to the District of
50 Columbia.
51 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
