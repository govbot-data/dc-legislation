17 To declare the existence of an emergency, due to congressional review, with respect to the need
18 to enact and amend provisions of law necessary to support the Fiscal Year 2026 budget.
19
20 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
21 resolution may be cited as the “Fiscal Year 2026 Budget Support Congressional Review
22 Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
23 Sec. 2. (a) On July 28, 2025, the Council passed the Fiscal Year 2026 Budget Support
24 Emergency Act of 2025, enacted September 3, 2025 (D.C. Act 26-146; 72 DCR 9623)
25 (“Emergency Act”), which enacted and amended provisions of law necessary to support the Fiscal
26 Year 2026 budget and will expire on December 2, 2025.
27 (b) On July 28, 2025, the Council also passed the Fiscal Year 2026 Budget Support Act of
28 2025, enacted on September 4, 2025 (D.C. Act 26-148; 72 DCR 9825) (“Permanent Act”), which is
29 substantially similar to the Emergency Act. The Permanent Act was transmitted to Congress on
30 September 10, 2025.
31 (c) Due to the congressional calendar, the Permanent Act is not expected to be law until
32 after the Emergency Act expires, thereby creating a gap in authority between expiration of the
33 Emergency Act and the effective date of the Permanent Act. This congressional review emergency

34 act is necessary to avoid that gap in the implementation of the Fiscal Year 2026 Budget and
35 Financial Plan.
36 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
37 enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Fiscal
38 Year 2026 Budget Support Congressional Review Emergency Act of 2025 be adopted after a
39 single reading.
40 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
