______________________________
Councilmember Trayon White, Sr.
A BILL
_______________
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
_______________
1 To amend, on an emergency basis, the Green Building Act of 2006 to exempt District-built
2 recreation centers in Ward 8 with indoor pools and indoor gyms from being required to
3 maintain net zero energy compliance standards with respect to the pools and gyms, and to
4 require that Ward 8 recreation center indoor pools be built to at least LEED Silver
5 standards.
6
7 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
8 act may be cited as the “Ward 8 Recreation Facility Emergency Amendment Act of 2025”.
9 Sec. 2. Section 3(a)(2)(D) of the Green Building Act of 2006, effective March 8, 2007
10 (D.C. Law 16-234; D.C. Official Code § 6-1451.02(a)(2)(D)), is amended to read as follows:
11 “(D) Maintain net zero energy compliance unless:
12 “(i) The project is for the installation of temporary structures;
13 “(ii) The project is for an indoor pool at a recreation center in Ward 8 and such
14 indoor pool shall be built to at least LEED Silver standards as determined by the Department of
15 Buildings; or
16 “(iii) The project is for a recreation center in Ward 8 with an indoor gym.”.
17 Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
1

18 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement in the committee report as the fiscal
19 impact statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975,
20 approved October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code §1-301.47a).
21 Sec. 4 Effective date.
22 This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by the
23 Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), and shall remain in effect for no longer than
24 90 days, as provided for emergency acts of the Council of the District of Columbia in section
25 412(a) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act approved December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 788;
26 D.C. Official Code §1-204.12(a)).
