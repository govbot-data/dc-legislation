Dear Chairman Mendelson:
Enclosed for consideration and enactment by the Council of the District of Columbia is
an emergency bill, the “Contract No. CW122630 with Patriot Harley Davidson Approval
and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2025,” and the accompanying emergency
declaration resolution in the not-to-exceed amount of $1,686,086.89 from October 1, 2024
through September 30, 2025.

minimum of 30 and maximum of 35 police motorcycles with side cars for use by the Metropolitan
Police Department. In addition, the legislation will approve payment for the goods and services
received and to be received under the contract.
My administration is available to discuss any questions you may have regarding the proposed
contract. In order to facilitate a response to any questions you may have, please have your staff
contact Marc Scott, Chief Operating Officer, Office of Contracting and Procurement, at (202) 724-
8759.
I urge the Council to take prompt and favorable action on the enclosed legislation.
Sincerely,
Muriel Bowser

to D.C. Official Code §§ 4719.1(b), 4719.2, 4719.3(b), 4719.4, 4719.5(a)(b)(c)(d), and 4719.6, it is
determined in the District’s best interest to procure a minimum of 30 ($1,443,479.34) and a
maximum of 35($1,686,086.89) HD FLHTP police motorcycles for MPD SOD via
an emergency procurement.
