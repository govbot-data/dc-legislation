ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
26-115
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
December 16, 2025
To congratulate and celebrate the 2025 Coolidge Senior High School Colts Varsity Football Team
on a successful season and Turkey Bowl victory.
WHEREAS, the Coolidge Senior High School Colts Varsity Football Team completed an
outstanding 2025 season, demonstrating discipline, teamwork, and excellence on and off the field;
WHEREAS, the 2025 Turkey Bowl Championship game saw the Colts overcome a 14–0
deficit and mount a dramatic comeback, securing a 15–14 victory with a decisive fourth-quarter
two-point conversion;
WHEREAS, this championship marks the Colts’ first Turkey Bowl victory since 1986,
representing a historic achievement nearly four decades in the making;
WHEREAS, the team’s performance throughout the season and in the championship game
demonstrated resilience, grit, and unity, qualities that reflect the strength and pride of the Coolidge
community;
WHEREAS, the team’s success reflects the dedication of its student-athletes, including:
