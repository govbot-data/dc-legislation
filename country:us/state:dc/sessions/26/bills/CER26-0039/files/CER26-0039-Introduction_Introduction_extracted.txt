2 Chairman Phil Mendelson

4 A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
5 ______________
6
7 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
8 _________________
9
10
11 To recognize and celebrate the musical career and accomplishments of DJ Kool.
12
13 WHEREAS, John W. Bowman, Jr, better known as DJ Kool, was born in Washington, DC
14 on March 20, 1958;
15
16 WHEREAS, DJ Kool attended both public and private schools, and graduated from Eastern
17 Senior High School in 1976;
18
19 WHEREAS, DJ Kool began his career as a mobile DJ and soon thereafter began gaining
20 attention and traction in clubs and performance venues around the DC region. During this time, he
21 was heavily influenced by the Godfather of Go-Go, Chuck Brown.
22
23 WHEREAS, in the 1990s, DJ Kool released four albums, The Music Ain’t Loud Enough,
24 20 Minute Workout, Let Me Clear My Throat, and Gimme That Beat, establishing himself as a
25 presence in the musical genres of hip-hop and go-go;
26
27 WHEREAS, the title track of the Let Me Clear My Throat album became DJ Kool’s most
28 recognized and notable hit. In 1996, he released the single (of the same name) on American
29 Recordings, which charted around the world including the top 40 on the Billboard Hot 100 and top
30 10 in the United Kingdom and the Netherlands in March 1997;
31
32 WHEREAS, in 2023, nearly three decades after its initial release, “Let Me Clear My
33 Throat” was certified platinum by the Recording Industry Association of America (RIAA);
34
35 WHEREAS, DJ Kool was a featured performer at the District’s annual Emancipation Day
36 Parade and Concert in 2025; and
37
38 WHEREAS, DJ Kool continues to vocally and visibly express love of and pride in his
39 native Washington, DC in his travels, both domestically and internationally.
40
41 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
42 resolution may be cited as the “DJ Kool Ceremonial Recognition Resolution of 2025”.
43 Sec. 2. The Council recognizes DJ Kool for his artistry and achievements over the course
44 of his career and commends him for his valuable contributions to the musical history and culture
45 of the District of Columbia.

46 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
