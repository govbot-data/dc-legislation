Dear Chairman Mendelson:
Enclosed for consideration and enactment by the Council of the District of Columbia is an emergency
bill, the “Modification Nos. 15, 16, 17, 17a, 17b, 17c, and 18 to Contract No. CW98887 with Great
American Corporation Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2025,” and the
accompanying emergency declaration resolution in the not-to-exceed amount of $4,841,171.40, from
November 16, 2024 through May 15, 2025.
The legislation will approve Modification Nos. 15, 16, 17, 17a, 17b, 17c, and 18 to Contract No.
CW98887 with Great American Corporation to provide hot/cold ready-to-eat, frozen boxed meals and
supplements to Department of Aging and Community Living meal delivery program participants. In
addition, the legislation will approve payment for the goods and services received and to be received
under the modifications.
My administration is available to discuss any questions you may have regarding the proposed contract
modifications. In order to facilitate a response to any questions you may have, please have your staff
contact Marc Scott, Chief Operating Officer, Office of Contracting and Procurement, at (202) 724-8759.
I urge the Council to take prompt and favorable action on the enclosed legislation.
Sincerely,

21 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this

25 Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to approve Modifications Nos. 15, 16, 17,
26 17a, 17b, 17c, and 18 to Contract No. 98887 with Great American Corporation, and to authorize
27 payment for the goods and services received and to be received under the modifications.
28 (b) On November 13, 2024, via Modification No. 15, the Office of Contracting and
29 Procurement ("OCP"), on behalf of the Department of Aging and Community Living, extended
30 the period of performance of option year two through January 16, 2025 and increased the not-to-
31 exceed ("NTE") amount of option year two by $490,000, from $2,082,639.53 to $2,572,639.53.
32 (c) On November 25, 2024, via Modification No. 16, OCP deleted the price schedules of
33 option years two, three, and four of the contract and replaced them with revised price schedules.

34 (d) On December 5, 2024, via Modification No. 17, OCP extended the period of
35 performance of option year two through February 7, 2025.
36 (e) On January 16, 2025, via Modification No. 17a, OCP extended the period of
37 performance of option year two through March 7, 2025 and increased the NTE amount of option
38 year two by $490,000, from $2,572,639.53 to $3,062,639.53.
39 (f) On February 19, 2025, via Modification No. 17b, OCP extended the period of
40 performance of option year two through April 4, 2025 and revised the price schedules of option
41 years two, three, and four of the contract.
42 (g) On February 21, 2025, via Modification No. 17c, OCP increased the NTE amount of
43 option year two by $20,000, from $3,062,639.53 to $3,082,639.53.
44 (h) Modification No. 18 will fully exercise option year two, extend the period of
45 performance of option year two through May 15, 2025, and increase the NTE amount of option
46 year two by $1,758,531.87, from $3,082,639.53 to $4,841,171.40.
47 (i) Under section 451(b)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
48 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 803; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.51(b)(1)), Council approval is
49 necessary since the modifications will increase the value of the contract to more than $1 million
50 during a 12-month period.
51 (j) Approval is necessary to allow the continuation of these vital services. Without this
52 approval, Great American Corporation cannot be paid for goods and services provided in excess
53 of $1 million for the period from May 16, 2024, through May 15, 2025.
54 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2 constitute
55 emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Modification Nos. 15, 16, 17, 17a, 17b,

56 17c, and 18 to Contract No. CW98887 with Great American Corporation Approval and Payment
57 Authorization Emergency Act of 2025 be adopted after a single reading.
58 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
