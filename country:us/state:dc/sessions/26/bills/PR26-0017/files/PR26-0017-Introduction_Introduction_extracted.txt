16 To declare the existence of an emergency, due to congressional review, with respect to the need
17 to amend the Day Care Policy Act of 1979 update the minimum salaries child
18 development facilities must pay assistant and lead teachers beginning in January 2025 to
19 participate in the Early Childhood Pay Equity Program.
20
21 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
22 resolution may be cited as the “Early Childhood Educator Pay Scales Congressional Review
23 Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
24 Sec. 2. (a) On October 15, 2024, the Council adopted the Early Childhood Educator Pay
25 Scales Emergency Amendment Act of 2024, enacted October 31, 2024 (D.C. Act 25-619; 71
26 DCR 13549) which expires on January 29, 2025.
27 (b) On November 22, 2024, the Early Childhood Educator Pay Scales Temporary
28 Amendment Act of 2024 (D.C. Act 25-637, 71 DCR 14472) was enacted, which is pending
29 congressional review.
30 (c) This identical emergency legislation is necessary to prevent a gap in the law between
31 the expiration of the emergency act and the effective date of the temporary measure.
32 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
33 enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Early

34 Childhood Educator Pay Scales Congressional Review Emergency Amendment Act of 2025 be
35 adopted after a single reading.
36 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
