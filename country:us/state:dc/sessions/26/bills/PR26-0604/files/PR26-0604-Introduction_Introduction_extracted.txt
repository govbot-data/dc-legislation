Vernon Martin
Mr. Vernon Martin is the Owner of Vernon Martin Salon and the Creative
Director and Founder of Vema Hair.
Mr. Martin is a hair artisan and entrepreneur who has spent over 20 years
in the hair styling industry. Mr. Martin offers advanced styling classes on
haircutting approaches, hair and scalp treatments, and weaving
techniques. Mr. Martin’s client list boasts of numerous high-profile public
figures and celebrities. His creations have been featured in the pages of
prominent magazines such as Glamour, People, Essence, Ebony, U.S.
Weekly, and Sister 2 Sister and on red carpet media events including the
BET Awards, MTV Fashion Awards, and several others. In addition, Mr.
Martin has styled guests on various television shows including Oprah,
Good Morning America, and Jimmy Kimmel Live.
A business owner, Mr. Martin also established the Studio One salon and day spa in 1998 to serve
long-term clients and mentor a new generation of talent artists in his hometown of Washington,
DC.
A Ward 1 resident, Mr. Martin received advanced training from Vidal Academy and Graham Webb
International programs and graduated from the Scanner Beauty Academy.
