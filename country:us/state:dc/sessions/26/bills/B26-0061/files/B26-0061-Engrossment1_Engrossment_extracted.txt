12 To amend the District of Columbia Workers’ Compensation Act of 1979 to provide that the
13 payment or award of compensation under the workers’ compensation law of any other
14 state shall not bar a claim for compensation under the District’s workers’ compensation
15 law for the same injury or death; provided, that any such award under the District’s
16 workers’ compensation law shall be reduced by the amount of compensation received or
17 awarded under the workers’ compensation law of any other state.
18
19 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
20 act may be cited as the “Parity in Workers’ Compensation Recovery Amendment Act of 2025”.
21 Sec. 2. Section 4(a-1) of the District of Columbia Workers’ Compensation Act of 1979,
22 effective July 1, 1980 (D.C. Law 3-77; D.C. Official Code § 32-1503(a-1)), is amended to read
23 as follows:
24 “(a-1)(1) Notwithstanding any other law, the payment or award of compensation under
25 the workers’ compensation law of any other state to an employee or his dependent shall not bar a
26 claim for compensation under this act for the same injury or death; provided, that any such award
27 of compensation under this act shall be reduced by the amount of compensation received or
28 awarded under the workers’ compensation law of any other state.
