11 To amend, on a temporary basis, the Personal Delivery Device Act of 2018 to increase the weight
12 limit for personal delivery devices.
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
15 act may be cited as the “Personal Delivery Device Weight Limit Temporary Amendment Act of
16 2026”.
17 Sec. 2. The Personal Delivery Device Act of 2018, effective July 17, 2018 (D.C. Law 22-
18 137; D.C. Official Code § 50-110.01 et seq.), is amended as follows:
19 (a) Section 2(8)(B) (D.C. Official Code § 50-110.01(8)(B)) is repealed.
20 (b) Section 4(b) (D.C. Official Code § 50-110.03(b)) is amended as follows:
21 (1) Paragraph (4) is amended by striking the phrase “; and” and inserting a
22 semicolon in its place.
23 (2) Paragraph (5) is amended by striking the period and inserting the phrase “; and”
24 in its place.
25 (3) A new paragraph (6) is added to read as follows:
26 “(6) Not exceed 250 pounds in weight, excluding any property being carried by the
27 PDD, or a higher weight limit established by the Department through rulemaking.”.
