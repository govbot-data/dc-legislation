ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-481
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
July 14, 2026
To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to retroactively approve the
Letter Contract, dated September 8, 2025, and Modification Nos. M001, M002, M003,
M004, and M005 to Contract No. CW120680 with Vector Fleet Management, LLC, and
to authorize payment for the goods and services received and to be received under the
contract.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Letter Contract and Modification Nos. M001, M002, M003,
M004 and M005 to Contract No. CW120680 with Vector Fleet Management, LLC Approval and
Payment Authorization Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2026”.
Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to approve the Letter Contract, dated
September 8, 2025, executing Option Period One, and subsequent Modification Nos. M001,
M002, M003, M004, and M005 to Contract No. CW120680 with Vector Fleet Management,
LLC to provide onsite fleet management and maintenance for the Metropolitan Police
Department’s (“MPD”) vehicles and equipment, and to authorize payment for the goods and
services received and to be received under the contract.
(b) By Letter Contract, dated September 8, 2025, the Office of Contracting and
Procurement (“OCP”), on behalf of MPD, exercised Option Period One in the not-to-exceed
amount of $1 million for the period from October 1, 2025, through September 31, 2026.
(c) Because a letter contract is the incorrect vehicle to exercise an option period, OCP
executed Modification No. M001 to replace the Letter Contract and exercise a partial option of
Option Year One in the not-to-exceed amount of $1 million for the period from October 1, 2025,
through December 1, 2025.
(d) Modification No. M002 exercised another partial option of Option Year One for the
period from December 2, 2025, through January 30, 2026, and increased the not-to-exceed
amount by $1,738,260.16, to a total not-to-exceed amount of $2,738,260.16.
(e) Modification No. M003 extended the period of partial Option Year One from January
31, 2026, through March 31, 2026, and increased the not-to-exceed amount by $1,369,130.08, to
a total not-to-exceed amount of $4,107,390.24.

(f) Modification No. M004 extended the period of partial Option Year One from April
01, 2026, through May 31, 2026, and increased the not-to-exceed amount by $1,369,130.08, to a
total not-to-exceed amount of $5,476,520.32.
(g) Modification No. M005 extended the term of the task order by exercising the
remainder of Option Year One from June 1, 2026, through September 30, 2026, and increased
the not-to-exceed amount by $3,023,479.68, to a total not-to-exceed amount of $8.5 million.
(h) Council approval is necessary because the modifications will increase the value of the
contract to more than $1 million during a 12-month period.
(i) Council approval is necessary to allow the continuation of these vital services.
Without Council approval, Vector Fleet Management, LLC cannot be paid for goods and
services provided in excess of $1 million for the period from October 1, 2025, through
September 30, 2026.
Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Letter
Contract, dated September 8, 2025, and Modification Nos. M001, M002, M003, M004 and
M005 to Contract No. CW120680 with Vector Fleet Management, LLC Approval and Payment
Authorization Emergency Act of 2026 be adopted after a single reading.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
