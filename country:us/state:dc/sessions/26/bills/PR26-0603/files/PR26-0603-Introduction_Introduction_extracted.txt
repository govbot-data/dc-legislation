Kimberely Grimes
Dr. Kimberley S. Grimes, DPC, is a distinguished professional in the field
of cosmetology and education. Currently, she serves as a freshman
professor at the award-winning Bennett Career Institute (BCI), where she
inspires and educates the next generation of beauty professionals.
Dr. Grimes is a proud member of Theta Nu Sigma, having pledged in
Spring 2023. She is also a published co-author, contributing her voice to
important conversations around women's experiences.
A Ward 8 resident, Dr. Grimes earned her Bachelor of Business
Management from the University of the District of Columbia. Dr. Grimes
also earned in Bachelor’s, Master’s and Doctorate of Professional
Cosmetology from the National Beauty Culturist League Institute of Cosmetology.
