Dear Chairman Mendelson:
Enclosed for consideration and enactment by the Council of the District of Columbia is an
emergency bill, the “Modification Nos. M04, M05, and M06 to Contract DCAM-23-NC-RFP-
0007 with AAA Complete Building Services, Inc., Approval and Payment Authorization
Emergency Act of 2026” and the accompanying emergency declaration resolution.
The legislation will approve Modification Nos. M04, M05 and M06 to Contract No. DCAM-23-
NC-RFP-0007 with AAA Complete Building Services, Inc., to provide property management
services, including the management, supervision, labor, materials, supplies, and equipment to
ensure effective performance of operations, maintenance, and repair services, at the John A.
Wilson Building under its agreement with the Department of General Services (“DGS”). In
addition, the legislation will approve payment to AAA Complete Building Services, Inc., for
services received and to be received under option year two of the contract.
My administration is available to discuss any questions you may have regarding this legislation.
In order to facilitate a response to any questions you may have, please contact Delano Hunter,
Director, Department of General Services, or have your staff contact Xavier Beltran, Interim Chief
Procurement Officer, DGS, at (202) 727-2800.
I look forward to the Council’s favorable consideration of this contract modification.
Sincerely,
Muriel Bowser
