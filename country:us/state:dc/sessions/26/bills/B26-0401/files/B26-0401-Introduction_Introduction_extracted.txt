13 To amend Chapter 23 of Title 16 of the D.C. Official Code to clarify juvenile case and social
14 records access provided to appropriate Council entities and the Office of the
15 Ombudsperson for Children for the purposes of enhancing oversight of the child welfare
16 and juvenile justice systems.
17
18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
19 Act may be cited as the "Youth Records Transparency and Oversight Amendment Act of 2025”.
20 Sec. 2. Chapter 23 of Title 16 of the D.C. Official Code as follows:
21 (a) Section 2331(c)(4) is amended by adding new subsubsections (H-J) to read as
22 follows:
23 “(H) In delinquency or need of supervision cases, the Chair of the Council Committee
24 with oversight over the Department of Youth Rehabilitation Services and their designee(s) when
25 necessary for the discharge of their official duties;
26 “(I) In neglect cases, the Chair of the Committee with oversight over the Child and
27 Family Services Agency and their designee(s) when necessary for the discharge of their official
28 duties; and
29 “(J) The Office of the Ombudsperson for Children when necessary for the discharge of its
30 official duties.”.
1

31 (b) Section 2332(c)(4) is amended by adding new subsubsections (F-H) to read as
32 follows:
33 “(F) In delinquency or need of supervision cases, the Chair of the Council Committee
34 with oversight over the Department of Youth Rehabilitation Services and their designee(s) when
35 necessary for the discharge of their official duties;
36 “(G) In neglect cases, the Chair of the Committee with oversight over the Child and
37 Family Services Agency and their designee(s) when necessary for the discharge of their official
38 duties; and
39 “(H) The Office of the Ombudsperson for Children when necessary for the discharge of
40 its official duties.”.
41 Sec. 4. Fiscal impact statement.
42 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement in the committee report as the fiscal
43 impact statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975,
44 approved October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
45 Sec. 5. Effective date.
46 This act shall take effect after approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by the
47 Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), a 30-day period of congressional review as
48 provided in section 602(c)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December
49 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 813; D.C. Official Code § 1-206.02(c)(1)), and publication in the District of
50 Columbia Register.
