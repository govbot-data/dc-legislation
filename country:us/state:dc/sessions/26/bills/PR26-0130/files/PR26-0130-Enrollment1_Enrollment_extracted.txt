ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-119
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
June 3, 2025
To confirm the reappointment of Patrick Williams to the Historic Preservation Review Board.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Historic Preservation Review Board Patrick Williams
Confirmation Resolution of 2025”.
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia confirms the reappointment of:
Patrick Williams
Daniel Lane, NW
Washington, DC 20015
(Ward 4)
as an architect member of the Historic Preservation Review Board, established by Mayor’s Order
83-119, dated May 6, 1983 (30 DCR 3031), in accordance with section 4 of the Historic
Landmark and Historic District Protection Act of 1978, effective March 3, 1979 (D.C. Law 2-
144; D.C. Official Code § 6-1103), for a term to end July 21, 2028.
Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia shall transmit a copy of this resolution,
upon its adoption, to the nominee and to the Mayor.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
