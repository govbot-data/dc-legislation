ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-203
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
________________
To confirm the appointment of Dr. LaQuandra Nesbitt to the District of Columbia Health Benefit
Exchange Authority Executive Board.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Health Benefit Exchange Authority Executive Board Dr.
LaQuandra Nesbitt Confirmation Resolution of 2025”.
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia confirms the appointment of:
Dr. LaQuandra Nesbitt
Rittenhouse Street, NW
Washington, DC 20011
(Ward 4)
as a voting member of the District of Columbia Health Benefit Exchange Authority Executive
Board, established by section 6 of the Health Benefit Exchange Authority Establishment Act of
2011, effective March 2, 2012 (D.C. Law 19-94; D.C. Official Code § 31-3171.05), for a term to
end July 6, 2028.
Sec. 3. The Council shall transmit a copy of this resolution, upon its adoption, to the
nominee and to the Mayor.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
