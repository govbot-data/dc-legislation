________________________
Councilmember Brooke Pinto
A BILL
_________________________
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
_________________________
1 To amend, on an emergency basis, the Second Chance Amendment Act of 2022 to clarify the
2 timeline for the automatic sealing of nonconvictions.
3
4 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
5 act may be cited as the “Second Chance Clarification Emergency Amendment Act of 2025”.
6 Sec. 2. Amended section 16-805(c)(1) within section 101(b) of the Second Chance
7 Amendment Act of 2022, effective March 10, 2023 (D.C. Law 24-284; 70 DCR 913), is
8 amended as follows:
9 (a) Subparagraph (A) is amended by striking the phrase “prior to the effective date of the
10 Second Chance Amendment Act of 2022, passed on 2nd reading on December 20, 2022
11 (Enrolled version of Bill 24-63)” and inserting the phrase “on or before September 30, 2027” in
12 its place.
13 (b) Subparagraph (B) is amended by striking the phrase “the effective date of the Second
14 Chance Amendment Act of 2022, on 2nd reading on December 20, 2022 (Enrolled version of
15 Bill 24-63), shall be sealed” and inserting the phrase “October 1, 2027, shall be sealed by
16 October 1, 2027, or” in its place.
17 Sec. 3. Applicability.
18 This act shall apply as of March 1, 2025.
19 Sec. 4. Fiscal impact statement.

20 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Budget Director as the fiscal impact
21 statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975, approved
22 October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
23 Sec. 5. Effective date.
24 This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by the
25 Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), and shall remain in effect for no longer than
26 90 days, as provided for emergency acts of the Council of the District of Columbia in section
27 412(a) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 788;
28 D.C. Official Code § 1-204.12(a)).
