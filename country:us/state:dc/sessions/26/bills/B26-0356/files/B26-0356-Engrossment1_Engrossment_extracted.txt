11 To amend the Telehealth Reimbursement Act of 2013 to cover and reimburse remote patient
12 monitoring of specific health care services and expenses for pregnant patients, including
13 blood pressure and blood glucose levels during pregnancy and up to 12 months
14 postpartum.
15
16 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
17 act may be cited as the “Prenatal and Postpartum Remote Patient Monitoring Clarification
18 Amendment Act of 2026”.
19 Sec. 2. Section 3a(2) of the Telehealth Reimbursement Act of 2013, effective October 20,
20 2020 (D.C. Law 23-132; DC Official Code § 31-3862.01(2)), is amended to read as follows:
21 “(2) Digital health interventions intended to remotely monitor a patient’s blood
22 pressure and blood glucose levels during a patient’s pregnancy and up to 12 months
23 postpartum.”.
24 Sec. 3. Applicability.
25 (a) Section 2 shall apply upon the date of inclusion of its fiscal effect in an approved
26 budget and financial plan.
