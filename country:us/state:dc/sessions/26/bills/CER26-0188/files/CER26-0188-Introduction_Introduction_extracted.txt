11 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA

15
16 To recognize and honor Maureen L. Dwyer and the Sitar Arts Center for their contributions to
17 the District of Columbia.
18
19 WHEREAS, Maureen L. Dwyer served the community at Sitar Arts Center for 27 years,
20 including 15 years as Executive Director, working to ensure Sitar Arts Center serves as a strong
21 community hub for Ward 1 and the District;
22 WHEREAS, Ms. Dwyer helped grow enrollment, expand artistic and workforce
23 partnerships, build coalitions and strengthen community partnerships, and deepen and diversify
24 programming;
25 WHEREAS, under Ms. Dwyer’s leadership Sitar Arts Center has been recognized with
26 several honors and awards, including the 2015 Washington Post Award for Excellence in
27 Nonprofit Management, the 2017 Mayor’s Arts Award, and the National Arts and Humanities
28 Youth Program Award, among others;
29 WHEREAS, Sitar Arts Center advances equitable access to high-quality arts education
30 and workforce development in the visual, performing, and digital arts;
31 WHEREAS, Sitar Arts Center serves up to 900 families each year from all eight wards,
32 removing financial and cultural barriers to arts education and career training; and
1

33 WHEREAS, Sitar Arts Center provides quality, accessible arts education and supports
34 youth creativity through classes in visual arts, music, drama, dance, digital arts, and creative
35 writing.
36 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, that this
37 resolution may be cited as the “Maureen L. Dwyer Recognition Resolution of 2026”.
38 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia honors and celebrates the Sitar Arts Center
39 and its longtime Executive Director Maureen L. Dwyer for their contributions to the community
40 and arts education in the District of Columbia.
41 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately upon the first date of publication in
42 the District of Columbia Register.
