________________________
Councilmember Brooke Pinto
A BILL
_________________________
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
_________________________
1 To amend, on an emergency basis, due to congressional review, section 1507 of the Criminal
2 Justice Coordinating Council for the District of Columbia Establishment Act of 2001 to
3 change the date by which the Prearrest Diversion Task Force shall issue initial
4 recommendations for prearrest diversion of certain misdemeanor offenses and categories
5 of persons to July 2026; and to amend Title 20 of the District of Columbia Official Code
6 to allow for notice of request for formal probate, notice from a foreign personal
7 representative of a decedent who owned any property located in the District of Columbia,
8 and notice of appointment to interested persons, creditors and unknown heirs in a legal
9 periodical or newspaper of general circulation in the District.
10
11 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
12 act may be cited as the “Prearrest Diversion Task Force Recommendations Congressional
13 Review Emergency Amendment Act of 2026”.
14 Sec. 2. Section 1507 of the Criminal Justice Coordinating Council for the District of
15 Columbia Establishment Act of 2001, effective June 8, 2024 (D.C. Law 25-175; D.C. Official
16 Code § 22-4237), is amended as follows:
17 (a) Subsection (g) is amended by striking the phrase “Within 3 months after
18 the applicability date of section 29 of the Secure DC Omnibus Amendment Act of 2024, passed
19 on 2nd reading on March 5, 2024 (Enrolled version of Bill 25-345),” and inserting the phrase
20 “No later than June 2025,” in its place.
21 (b) Subsection (h) is amended by striking the phrase “Within one year after
22 the applicability date of section 29 of the Secure DC Omnibus Amendment Act of 2024, passed

23 on 2nd reading on March 5, 2024 (Enrolled version of Bill 25-345),” and inserting the phrase
24 “No later than July 31, 2026,” in its place.
25 Sec. 3. Title 20 of the District of Columbia Official Code is amended as follows:
26 (a) Section 20-323(a) is amended by striking the phrase “legal periodical of general
27 circulation in the District” and inserting the phrase “legal periodical or newspaper of general
28 circulation in the District” in its place.
29 (b) Section 20-343(a) is amended by striking the phrase “legal periodical of general
30 circulation in the District” and inserting the phrase “legal periodical or newspaper of general
31 circulation in the District” in its place.
32 (c) Section 20-704(a) is amended by striking the phrase “legal periodical of general
33 circulation in the District” and inserting the phrase “legal periodical or newspaper of general
34 circulation in the District” in its place.
35 Sec. 4. Fiscal impact statement.
36 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Budget Director as the fiscal impact
37 statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975, approved
38 October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
39 Sec. 5. Effective date.
40 This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by the
41 Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), and shall remain in effect for no longer than
42 90 days, as provided for emergency acts of the Council of the District of Columbia in section
43 412(a) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 788;
44 D.C. Official Code § 1-204.12(a)).
