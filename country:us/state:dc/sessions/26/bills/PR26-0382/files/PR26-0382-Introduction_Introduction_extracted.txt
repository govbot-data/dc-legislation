14 To establish the date by which the Mayor shall submit to the Council the proposed budget for the
15 government of the District of Columbia for the fiscal year ending September 30, 2027, to
16 identify information and documentation to be submitted to the Council with the proposed
17 budget for the government of the District of Columbia for the fiscal year ending

22 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this

24 2025”.
