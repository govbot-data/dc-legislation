14 To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to continue foreclosure
15 protections for homeowners who applied for funding from the DC Homeowner
16 Assistance Fund (“DC HAF") program before September 30, 2022 and whose
17 applications remain under review, pending approval, pending payment, or under appeal,
18 and to require that notices continue to be sent to homeowners informing them of the DC
19 HAF program before a foreclosure action.
20
21 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
22 resolution may be cited as the “Foreclosure Moratorium and Homeowner Assistance Fund
23 Coordination Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
24 Sec. 2. (a) The Council has passed a series of emergency and temporary measures that,
25 among other things, have protected from foreclosure certain homeowners who applied for DC
26 HAF no later than 2022 and whose applications were still unresolved.
27 (b)The most current such legislation, the Foreclosure Moratorium and Homeowner
28 Assistance Fund Coordination Temporary Amendment Act of 2024, enacted July 26, 2024 (D.C.
29 Law 25-212), will expire on May 1, 2025.
30 (c)As of March 2025, District legal services providers report that payment administration
31 and communication issues have prevented final resolution of a small number of cases for
32 homeowners who continue to benefit from foreclosure protection.
33 (d)Emergency legislation is necessary to prevent a lapse in the law prior to the
1

34 fulfillment of its purpose.
35 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2 constitute
36 emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Foreclosure Moratorium and Homeowner
37 Assistance Fund Coordination Emergency Amendment Act of 2025 be adopted after a single
38 reading.
39 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
