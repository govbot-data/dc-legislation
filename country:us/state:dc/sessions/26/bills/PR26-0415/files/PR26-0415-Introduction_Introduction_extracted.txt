Dear Chairman Mendelson:
Enclosed for consideration and enactment by the Council of the District of Columbia is an
emergency bill, the “Modification Nos. M0014 and M0015 to Contract No. CW100401 with
Wheeler Creek Estates Community Development Corporation Approval and Payment
Authorization Emergency Act of 2025,” and the accompanying emergency declaration resolution
in the amount not to exceed $1,066,327.04.
The legislation will approve Modification No. M0015 to Human Care Agreement No. CW100401
with Wheeler Creek Estates Community Development Corporation, for the Permanent Supportive
Housing III case management and financial assistance program to fully exercise option year three
of the modification. In addition, the legislation will approve payment for the goods and services
received and to be received under the modification.
My administration is available to discuss any questions you may have regarding the proposed
modification. In order to facilitate a response to any questions you may have, please have your
staff contact Marc Scott, Chief Operating Officer of the Office of Contracting and Procurement,
at (202) 724-8759.
I urge the Council to take prompt and favorable action on the enclosed legislation.
Sincerely,
Muriel Bowser

20 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this

23 Payment Authorization Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025".
24 Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to approve Modification Nos. M0014
25 and M0015 to Contract No. CW100401 with Wheeler Creek Estates Community Development
26 Corporation, and to authorize payment for the goods and services received and to be received
27 under the modifications.
28 (b) On July 1, 2025, by Modification No. M0014, the Office of Contracting and
29 Procurement ("OCP"), on behalf of the Department of Human Services, exercised a partial
30 option of option year three of Contract No. CWl 00401, for the period of July 1, 2025 through
31 December 31, 2025, with Wheeler Creek Estates Community Development Corporation to
32 provide Permanent Supportive Housing III program case management services in the not-to-
33 exceed amount of $530,066.04.

34 (c) Proposed Modification No. M0015 will exercise the remainder of option year three
35 and increase the not-to-exceed amount by $536,261, to a total not-to-exceed amount of
36 $1,066,327.04.
37 (d) Under section 451(b)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
38 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 803; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.51(b)(1)), Council approval is
39 necessary since the modifications will increase the value of the contract to more than $1 million
40 during a 12-month period.
41 (e) Approval is necessary to allow the continuation of these vital services. Without this
42 approval, Wheeler Creek Estates Community Development Corporation cannot be paid for goods
43 and services provided in excess of $1 million for the period from July 1, 2025, through June 30,
44 2026.
45 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2 constitute
46 emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Modification Nos. M0014 and M0015 to
47 Contract No. CW100401 with Wheeler Creek Estates Community Development Corporation
48 Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2025 be adopted after a single reading.
49 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
