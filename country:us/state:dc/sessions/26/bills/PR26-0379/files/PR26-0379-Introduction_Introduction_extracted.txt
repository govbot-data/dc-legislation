15 To disapprove, on an emergency basis, reprogramming request No. 26-91 of $5,500,000 Local

17
18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
19 resolution may be cited as the “Reprogramming Request No. 26-91 Disapproval Emergency
20 Resolution of 2025”.
21 Sec. 2. Pursuant to D.C. Official Code § 47–363, on October 24, 2025, the Mayor
22 transmitted a reprogramming request to the Council to reprogram $5,500,000 of Local Funds
23 from the Office of Unified Communications and the Department of Corrections to the
24 Metropolitan Police Department to “address year-end spending pressures.” This reprogramming
25 request is hereby disapproved.
26 Sec. 3. The Secretary of the Council shall transmit a copy of this resolution, upon its
27 adoption, to the Office of the Mayor.
28 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
