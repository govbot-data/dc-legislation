DearChairman Mendelson:
Enclosed for consideration and enactment by the Council of the District of Columbia is an
emergency bill, the “Modification Nos. 4, 5, and 6 to ContractNo. CW88823 with Mansfield Oil
Company ofGainesville, Inc. Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Actof2025,”and
theaccompanying emergency declaration resolution.
The legislation will approve Modification Nos. 4, 5, and 6 to Contract No. CW88823 with
Mansfield Oil Company of Gainesville, Inc., for various types of fuel in several storage sites
throughout the District, to fully exercise option year three of the contract. In addition, the
legislation will approve payment for the goods and services receivedand to be received underthe
modifications.
My administration is available to discuss any questions you may have regarding the proposed
contract modifications. In orderto facilitate aresponse toany questionsyou may have, please have
yourstaffcontact Mare Scott, Chief Operating Officer, OfficeofContracting and Procurement, at
(202) 724-8759.
I urge the Council to take prompt and favorable action on the enclosed legislation.
Sincerely,
Muriel Boyser

21 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
22 resolution may be cited as the "Modification Nos. 4, 5, and 6 to Contract No. CW88823 with
23 Mansfield Oil Company of Gainesville, Inc. Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency
24 Declaration Resolution of 2025".
25 Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to approve Modifications Nos. 4, 5, and 6 to
26 Contract No. CW88823 with Mansfield Oil Company of Gainesville, Inc., and to authorize
27 payment for the goods and services received and to be received under the modifications.
28 (b) On November 22, 2024, the Office of Contracting and Procurement ("OCP"), on
29 behalf of the Department of Public Works, executed Modification No. 4 to exercise a partial
30 option of option period three of Contract No. CW88823 with Mansfield Oil Company of
31 Gainesville, Inc. to provide mid-grade, diesel, bio-diesel and ethanol fuel to the District fueling
32 sites, in the not-to-exceed amount of $1 million for the period from November 19, 2024 through
33 January 18, 2025.

34 (c) Modification No. 5 extended the partial option period through March 19, 2025 in the
35 not-to-exceed amount of $1 million.
36 (d) Modification No. 6 exercised the remainder of option period three and increased the
37 total not-to-exceed amount of the contract by $4.3 million.
38 (e) Under section 451(b)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
39 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 803; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.51(b)(1)), Council approval is
40 necessary since the modifications will increase the value of the contract to more than $1 million
41 during a 12-month period.
42 (f) Approval is necessary to allow the continuation of these vital services. Without this
43 approval, Mansfield Oil Company of Gainesville, Inc. cannot be paid for goods and services
44 provided in excess of $1 million for the period from November 19, 2024 through June 30, 2025.
45 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2 constitute
46 emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Modification Nos. 4, 5, and 6 to Contract
47 No. CW88823 with Mansfield Oil Company of Gainesville, Inc. Approval and Payment
48 Authorization Emergency Act of 2025 be adopted after a single reading.
49 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
