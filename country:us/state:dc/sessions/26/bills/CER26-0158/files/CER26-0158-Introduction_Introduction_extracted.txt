6 A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
7
8 ____________
9
10 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
11
12 ____________
13
14 To acknowledge and express gratitude to David C. Tseng for his years of service to the District
15 of Columbia on the occasion of his retirement from public service.
16
17 WHEREAS, David C. Tseng received a bachelor’s degree, with honors, from Columbia
18 University in English Literature and a Juris Doctor from the University of California at Los
19 Angeles;
20
21 WHEREAS, Mr. Tseng was appointed General Counsel for the Office of the Chief
22 Financial Officer (OCFO) in July of 2007 and served under four Chief Financial Officers (CFO).
23 Mr. Tseng served as the principal legal advisor to the CFO, directing the legal resources of the
24 OCFO with respect to federal and District appropriations, public finance, contracting and
25 procurement, tax accounting, and economic development. Mr. Tseng also represented the CFO in
26 other legal matters in which the CFO was a party or had a legal interest, supervised and managed
27 outside counsel in all legal matters, and ensured the legal sufficiency of the OCFO's programs and
28 policies;
29 WHEREAS, Prior to his current position, Mr. Tseng served as the OCFO’s Associate
30 General Counsel for Legislative Affairs from 2006 to 2007, and Deputy Associate General
31 Counsel for the Office of Tax and Revenue from 2004 to 2006. Before joining the District of
32 Columbia government, Mr. Tseng was a White House Senior Policy Advisor and Staff Director
33 for the National Economic Council under President Bill Clinton from 2000 to 2001. He also served
34 as Chief Assistant Treasurer for the City and County of San Francisco from 1998 to 2000. Mr.
35 Tseng worked as a Special Assistant to the Assistant Secretary of Labor for Pension and Welfare
36 Benefits at the U.S. Department of Labor from 1993 to 1998;
37
38 WHEREAS, Mr. Tseng represents the OCFO’s ideal of a public servant who strives every
39 day to provide the highest standard of service to residents, businesses, visitors, and the government
40 of the District of Columbia by being Service Driven with a Strong Work Ethic; Motivated and
41 Mission Focused; Accountable; Respectful of his Customers and Co-Workers; Trustworthy and
42 Team-Focused; Empowered to Achieve Excellence; and Results Oriented, or SMARTER; and
43
44 WHEREAS, Mr. Tseng will be remembered, with gratitude, by countless colleagues who
45 were better able to perform their jobs because of his willingness to share his extensive knowledge
46 in service to the District of Columbia. Mr. Tseng is a lawyer who exemplifies the highest ideals of

47 the legal profession, and one who does, and has done, good work worth doing. Mr. Tseng also
48 gained the respect and admiration of members of the Legislative and Executive branches of the
49 District government as a professional who delivered high-quality service to the residents of the
50 District of Columbia for 22 years.
51
52 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
53 resolution may be cited as the “David C. Tseng Ceremonial Recognition Resolution of 2026”.
54
55 Sec. 2. The Council honors Mr. Tseng on the occasion of his retirement from the OCFO
56 for his distinguished service and the valuable contributions he has made to the District of
57 Columbia.
58
59 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
