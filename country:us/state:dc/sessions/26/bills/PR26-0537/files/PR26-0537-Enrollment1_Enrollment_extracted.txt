ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-385
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
May 5, 2026
To confirm the reappointment of Christopher Murphy to the District of Columbia Housing
Authority Board of Commissioners.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “District of Columbia Housing Authority Board of Commissioners
Christopher Murphy Confirmation Resolution of 2026”.
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia confirms the reappointment of:
Christopher Murphy
Mckinley Street, NW
Washington, DC 20015
(Ward 4)
as a commissioner who has professional experience in federal housing laws and regulations of
the District of Columbia Housing Authority Board of Commissioners, established by section 4a
of the District of Columbia Housing Authority Act of 1999, effective December 31, 2025 (D.C.
Law 26-80; D.C. Official Code § 6-203.01), for a 3-year term.
Sec 3. The Council shall transmit a copy of this resolution, upon its adoption, to the
nominee and to the Mayor.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
