20 A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
21
22 _______
23
24 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
25
26 _______
27
28 To celebrate the ceremony of the District of Columbia’s famous groundhog weatherman and
29 political prognostician, Potomac Phil, as he delivers his highly anticipated annual forecast
30 on February 2, 2025, and to declare that date “D.C. Groundhog Day.”
31
32 WHEREAS, On February 2, 2025, residents and visitors of the District of Columbia will
33 emerge to be educated on the American folklore tradition of Groundhog Day by Dupont Festival;
34 WHEREAS, on that morning, District residents will be reminded that if Potomac Phil
35 emerges from his burrow and sees his shadow due to clear weather, he will retreat to his den and
36 winter will persist for 6 more weeks and if he does not see his shadow because of cloudiness,
37 spring will arrive early;
38 WHEREAS, District residents and visitors will be enlightened with the knowledge of
39 Potomac Phil’s uncanny power of weather and political predictions; and

40 WHEREAS, Potomac Phil will elucidate the seasonal forecast for hundreds of individuals
41 in and around Dupont Circle during the morning of February 2, 2025.
42 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
43 resolution may be cited as the “D.C. Groundhog Day Recognition Resolution of 2025”.
44 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia hereby declares February 2, 2025, as
45 D.C. Groundhog Day in the Nation’s Capital.
46 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately upon the first date of publication in
47 the District of Columbia Register.
