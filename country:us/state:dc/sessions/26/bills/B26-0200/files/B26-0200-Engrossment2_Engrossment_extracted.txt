12 To amend, on a temporary basis, the Open Meetings Act to clarify the definition of “meeting”; to
13 provide for a public body’s ability to be briefed about potential terrorist or public health
14 threats so long as no official action is taken; to exempt from the act meetings between the
15 Council and the Mayor provided that no official action is taken at such meetings; and to
16 provide that a meeting shall be deemed open to the public if the public body takes steps
17 reasonably calculated to allow the public to view or hear the meeting while the meeting is
18 taking place, or, if doing so is not technologically feasible, as soon thereafter as
19 reasonably practicable.
20
21 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
22 act may be cited as the “Open Meetings Clarification Temporary Amendment Act of 2025”.
23 Sec. 2. The Open Meetings Act, effective March 31, 2011 (D.C. Law 18-350; D.C.
24 Official Code § 2-571 et seq.), is amended as follows:
25 (a)(1) Section 404(1) (D.C. Official Code § 2-574(1)) is amended to read as follows:
26 “(1)(A) “Meeting” means any gathering of a quorum of the members of a public
27 body, including hearings and roundtables, whether formal or informal, regular, special, or
28 emergency, at which the members consider, conduct, or advise on public business, including
29 gathering information, taking testimony, discussing, deliberating, recommending, and voting,
