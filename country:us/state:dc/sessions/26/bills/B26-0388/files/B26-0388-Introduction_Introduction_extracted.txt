15 To amend, on an emergency basis, the Office of District Waterways Management Establishment
16 Act of 2022 to make the process for Mayoral appointment of members of the District
17 Waterways Advisory Commission consistent with that of similar commissions, to add the
18 Department of Parks and Recreation as an ex-officio non-voting member of the District
19 Waterways Advisory Commission, to adjust the District Waterways Advisory
20 Commission meeting schedule, to allow sufficient time for engagement with stakeholders
21 and the District Waterways Advisory Commission in development of the Advisory Plan,
22 and to provide the District Waterways Advisory Commission with authority to determine
23 whether the Advisory Plan needs to be updated and extend the update period to at least
24 every 3 years; and to amend the Confirmation Act of 1978 to make a conforming
25 amendment.
26
27 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
28 act may be cited as the “Office of District Waterways Management Establishment Emergency
29 Amendment Act of 2025”.
30 Sec. 2. The Office of District Waterways Management Establishment Act of 2022,
31 effective March 22, 2023 (D.C. Law 24-336; D.C. Official Code § 8-191.01 et seq.), is amended
32 as follows:
33 (a) Section 3 (D.C. Official Code § 8-191.02) is amended as follows:
34 (1) Subsection (b)(1)(A) is amended by striking the phrase “section 2(e) of the
35 Confirmation Act of 1978, effective March 3, 1979 (D.C. Law 2-141; D.C. Official Code § 1-

36 523.01(e)),” and inserting the phrase “section 2(f) of the Confirmation Act of 1978, effective
37 March 3, 1979 (D.C. Law 2-141; D.C. Official Code § 1-523.01(f)),” in its place.
38 (2) Subsection (c) is amended as follows:
39 (A) Paragraph (9) is amended by striking the phrase “; and” inserting a semicolon
40 in its place.
41 (B) Paragraph (10) is amended by striking the period and inserting the phrase “;
42 and” in its place.
43 (C) A new paragraph (11) is added to read as follows:
44 “(11) The Department of Parks and Recreation.”.
45 (3) Subsection (f) is amended by striking the phrase “once every month” and inserting the
46 phrase “once every 2 months” in its place.
47 (b) Section 4 (D.C. Official Code § 8-191.03) is amended as follows:
48 (1) The lead-in language of subsection (a) is amended as follows:
49 (A) Strike the phrase “one year” and insert the phrase “2 years” in its
50 place.
51 (B) Strike the phrase “develop and adopt” and insert the word “develop”
52 in its place.
53 (2) Subsection (b)(2) is amended by striking the phrase “At least 60 days prior to
54 adoption of an Advisory Plan, the” and inserting the word “The” in its place.
55 (3) Subsections (c) and (d) are amended to read as follows:
56 “(c) After conclusion of the public comment period, and within 60 days after completing
57 revisions, if any, the voting members of the Commission shall adopt the Advisory Plan.
2

58 Thereupon, the Advisory Plan shall be submitted to the Office of Waterways Management for
59 publication.
60 “(d) If considered necessary by the Commission, the Advisory Plan shall be
61 updated at least once every 3 years, following the same process required for the initial Advisory
62 Plan pursuant to subsections (b) and (c) of this section.”.
