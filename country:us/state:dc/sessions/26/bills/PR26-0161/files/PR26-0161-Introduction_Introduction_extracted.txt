18 To declare the existence of an emergency, due to congressional review, with respect to the need
19 to amend the Secure DC Omnibus Amendment Act of 2024 to remove the sunset date of
20 certain pretrial detention provisions; and to amend the Criminal Justice Coordinating
21 Council for the District of Columbia Establishment Act of 2001 to clarify certain
22 reporting requirements.
23
24 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
25 resolution may be cited as the “Secure DC Pretrial Detention Extension and Reporting
26 Congressional Review Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
27 Sec. 2. (a) On February, 4, 2025, the Council passed the Secure DC Pretrial Detention
28 Extension and Reporting Emergency Amendment Act of 2025, effective February 13, 2025
29 (D.C. Act 26-7; 72 DCR 1645) (“emergency act”), which expires on May 14, 2025.
30 (d) On February 18, 2025, the Secure DC Pretrial Detention Extension and Reporting
31 Temporary Amendment Act of 2025, passed on 2nd reading on February 18, 2025 (Enrolled
32 version of Bill 26-0082) (“temporary act”), which has a projected law date of July 12, 2025.
33 (e) This emergency congressional review legislation is necessary to prevent a gap in the
34 law between the expiration of the emergency act and the effective date of the temporary act.
1

35 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances in
36 section 2 constitute emergency circumstances, making it necessary that the Secure DC Pretrial
37 Detention Extension and Reporting Congressional Review Emergency Act of 2025 be adopted
38 after a single reading.
39 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
