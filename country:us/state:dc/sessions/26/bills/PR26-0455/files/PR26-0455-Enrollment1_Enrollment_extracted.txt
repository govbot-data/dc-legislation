ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-326
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
February 3, 2026
To confirm the appointment of Kayla Smith to the Board of Dietetics and Nutrition.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Board of Dietetics and Nutrition Kayla Smith Confirmation
Resolution of 2026”.
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia confirms the appointment of:
Kayla Smith
Southern Avenue, SE
Washington, DC 20020
(Ward 7)
as a licensed dietitian member of the Board of Dietetics and Nutrition, established by section 202
of the District of Columbia Health Occupations Revision Act of 1985, effective March 25, 1986
(D.C. Law 6-99; D.C. Official Code § 3-1202.02), for a term to end March 12, 2028.
Sec. 3. The Council shall transmit a copy of this resolution, upon its adoption, to the
nominee and to the Mayor.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
