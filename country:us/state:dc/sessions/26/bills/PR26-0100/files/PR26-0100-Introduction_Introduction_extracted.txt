for the Hospital Medicine Division at Children’s National Hospital, an
Associate Professor at the George Washington University School of
Medicine, and a current member of the Board of Medicine.

quality and safety, including Director for Medical Quality, a Medical Unit
Director, and Order Set Oversight Committee Chair. She has been
involved with numerous unit-based and house wide improvement processes, specifically focused
on discharge planning, reducing readmissions, and family centered rounds and care for patients
with limited English proficiency. At Children’s National, Dr. Pavuluri directs the Fellows’ College
Quality Improvement Curriculum. Her research interests include readmissions and any
opportunity to improve workflow efficiency and enjoyment.
