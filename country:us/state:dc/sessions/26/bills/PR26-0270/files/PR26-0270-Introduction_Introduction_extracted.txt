Jasminelee Scott
Ms. Jasminelee Scott currently serves as a Community Support Worker
at the Healthy Place. Ms. Scott brings a wealth of experience and passion
to the table, with a diverse background spanning education, emergency
services, community engagement, and nonprofit initiatives. With a strong
commitment to serving others, Ms. Scott has dedicated their career to
making positive impact in the Washington DC area.
Ms. Scott began her professional journey in 2014 at the D.C Fire and EMS
Department, where she served as a Firefighter and EMT. Over the years,
she has honed her skills and expertise, eventually transitioning to the
Bellevue Family Success Center, where she provided direct support to
individuals and families in the community.
Beyond her professional endeavors, Ms. Scott is deeply committed to giving back to the
community. She has been actively involved in community outreach activities and volunteering
with various nonprofit organizations and small businesses in DC, working tirelessly to uplift and
empower underserved individuals and families. Moreover, Ms. Scott is currently spearheading
efforts to establish a nonprofit organization dedicated to supporting youth and families in the
District, driven by their unwavering belief in the power of community driven solutions.
A Ward 8 resident, Ms. Scott is currently studying Health Services Management at Trinity
Washington University.
