IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA

1 To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to amend the Fraudulent
2 Vehicle Tag and Parking Enforcement Modernization Amendment Act of 2024 to
3 address technical errors in the subject to appropriations provision.
4
5 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
6 resolution may be cited as the “Parking Enforcement Modernization Technical Correction
7 Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2026”.
8 Sec. 2. (a) On December 17, 2024, the Council approved at final reading Bill 25-0435,
9 the Fraudulent Vehicle Tag and Parking Enforcement Modernization Amendment Act of 2024
10 (D.C. Law 25-0325; D.C. Official Code § 50-2431 et seq.). Along with updating statutes that
11 govern parking enforcement procedures, the law enhances the District’s ability to enforce against
12 vehicles that use fraudulent or obscured vehicle tags, and prioritizes the immobilization and
13 impoundment of abandoned, unidentifiable, and dangerous vehicles.
14 (b) Through a combination of funds secured by the Committee on Public Works &
15 Operations, the Committee on Transportation and the Environment, and the Committee of the
16 Whole, nearly the entirety of the fiscal impact of Law 25-0325 was fully funded in the Council's
1

17 approved FY 2026 budget at approximately $10 million across the financial plan, primarily in
18 the Department of Public Works and the Department of Motor Vehicles.
19 (c) However, certain sections of the law that are now funded remain subject to
20 appropriations, including provisions that require the Mayor to establish rules by a date certain.
21 (d) Emergency legislation is necessary to ensure that all funded provisions of the
22 Fraudulent Vehicle Tag and Parking Enforcement Modernization Amendment Act of 2024 are in
23 effect within the fiscal year they have been funded, to enable timely implementation of the law.
24 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances in
25 section 2 constitute emergency circumstances, making it necessary that the Parking Enforcement
26 Modernization Technical Correction Emergency Act of 2026 be adopted after a single reading.
27 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
