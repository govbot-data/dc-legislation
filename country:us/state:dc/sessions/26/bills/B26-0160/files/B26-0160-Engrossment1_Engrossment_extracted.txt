9 To approve the closing of a cul-de-sac in Square 4350, abutting Lot 0006, and bounded by
10 Douglas Street, N.E., in Ward 5.
11
12 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
13 act may be cited as the “Closing of a Cul-de-Sac in Square 4350, S.O. 22-05506, Act of 2025”.
14 Sec. 2. Pursuant to section 404 of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
15 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 787; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.04), and consistent with the Street
16 and Alley Closing and Acquisition Procedures Act of 1982, effective March 10, 1983 (D.C. Law
17 4-201; D.C. Official Code § 9-201.01 et seq.) (“Act”), the Council finds the cul-de-sac in Square
18 4350, as shown on the Surveyor’s plat filed under S.O. 22-05506, is unnecessary for alley
19 purposes and orders it closed, with title to the land to vest as shown on the Surveyor’s plat.
20 Sec. 3. The closing of the cul-de-sac described in section 2 is contingent upon the
21 satisfaction of all conditions set forth in the official file for S.O. 22-05506 prior to the
22 recordation of the plat by the Surveyor.
