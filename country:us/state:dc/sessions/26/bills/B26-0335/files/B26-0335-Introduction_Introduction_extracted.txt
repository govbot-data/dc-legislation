17 To amend, on an emergency basis, the Volunteers Services Act of 1977 to clarify that the
18 Attorney General for the District of Columbia has the authority to promulgate
19 regulations governing the Attorney General’s use of volunteers.
20
21 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA,
22 That this act may be cited as the “Volunteer Services Clarification Emergency Amendment
23 Act of 2025”.
24 Sec. 2. Section 3 of the Volunteers Services Act of 1977, effective June 28, 1977
25 (D.C. Law 2-12; D.C. Official Code § 1-319.02), is amended by striking the phrase
26 “District of Columbia Board of Education” and inserting the phrase “District of Columbia
27 Board of Education, Attorney General for the District of Columbia,” in its place.
28 Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
29 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Budget Director as the fiscal
30 impact statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975,
31 approved October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
32 Sec. 4. Effective date.

33 This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto
34 by the Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), and shall remain in effect for no
35 longer than 90 days, as provided for emergency acts of the Council of the District of
36 Columbia in section 412(a) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
37 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 788; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.12(a)).
