12 To amend Chapter 39 of Title 28 of the District of Columbia Official Code to clarify that the
13 District government is not a merchant under the District’s consumer protection law,
14 except with respect to the application of that chapter’s landlord-tenant relations
15 provisions to the District of Columbia Housing Authority.
16
17 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
18 act may be cited as the “Consumer Protection Clarification Amendment Act of 2025”.
19 Sec. 2. Section 28-3901 of the District of Columbia Official Code is amended as follows:
20 (a) Subsection (a)(3) is amended to read as follows:
21 “(3)(A) “merchant” means a person, whether organized or operating for
22 profit or for a nonprofit purpose, who in the ordinary course of business does or would sell, lease
23 (to), or transfer, either directly or indirectly, consumer goods or services, or a person who in the
24 ordinary course of business does or would supply the goods or services which are or would be
25 the subject matter of a trade practice.
26 “(B) Except as provided in subsection (e) of this section,
27 “merchant” shall not include the District of Columbia, its agencies or instrumentalities, or any
28 employee thereof acting within the scope of the employee’s official duties;”.

29 (b) Subsection (e) is amended to read as follows:
30 “(e) Notwithstanding any other provision of this chapter, this chapter’s application to
31 landlord-tenant relations shall include the District of Columbia Housing Authority’s activities as
32 a landlord; provided, that this chapter shall not be construed to otherwise apply to the District of
33 Columbia, its agencies or instrumentalities, or any employee thereof acting within the scope of
34 the employee’s official duties.”.
35 Sec. 3. Applicability.
36 (a) Section 2(a) shall apply as of June 12, 2007.
37 (b) Section 2(b) shall apply as of December 19, 2016.
