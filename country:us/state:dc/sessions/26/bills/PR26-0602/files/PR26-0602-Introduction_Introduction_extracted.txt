David Cavalcante
Mr. David Cavalcante is a tattoo artist with decades of experience in the
industry. His career began in 1999 with a split apprenticeship at High
Roller Tattoo in Hicksville, New York, followed by a stint at lnksmith
and Rogers Studio in Jacksonville, Florida.
Mr. Cavalcante began working full time as a tattoo artist in August 2001,
and has worked at Tattoo Paradise in Washington, D.C. since June 2010.
At Tattoo Paradise, he enjoys being able to focus on his tattooing, as
well as assisting with business management.
Mr. Cavalcante is a Ward 4 resident and received his Bachelor of Arts
in Public Relations from Long Island University.
