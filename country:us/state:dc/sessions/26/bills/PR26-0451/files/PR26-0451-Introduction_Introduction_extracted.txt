Nate Handy
Mr. Nate Handy is the Director of Marine Operations at City Experiences
in Washington, DC. With over 27 years of experience in the passenger
vessel industry, Mr. Handy has developed a deep expertise in managing
marine operations, ensuring compliance with safety regulations, and
enhancing guest experiences.
Throughout his career, Mr. Handy has held various leadership roles,
including Associate Director of Marine Operations at Entertainment
Cruises and Senior Captain at Spirit Cruises. His commitment to
operational excellence has been instrumental in improving safety
protocols and crew training programs, resulting in consistently high guest
satisfaction.
A Ward 7 resident, Mr. Handy earned his Bachelor of Arts in Political Science from St. Mary’s
College of Maryland.
