8 A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
9
10 _________________________
11
12 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
13
14 _________________________
15
16
17 To recognize, honor, and celebrate the life of Dr. David W. Schwartzman for his decades-long
18 commitment to climate, economic, and social justice in the District of Columbia.
19
20 WHEREAS, Dr. David W. Schwartzman is a native of Brooklyn, New York who moved
21 to the District of Columbia in 1976, and;
22 WHEREAS, Dr. Schwartzman was a long-time resident of Ward 4 and an active and
23 engaged member of the Ward 4 community; and
24 WHEREAS, Dr. Schwartzman served as Professor Emeritus and formerly led the
25 Department of Biology at Howard University, where he mentored generations of students and
26 contributed to the advancement of scientific knowledge; and
27 WHEREAS, Dr. Schwartzman was a world-class climate and energy scientist whose
28 research and scholarship informed policy and activism aimed at creating a more sustainable and
29 equitable future; and
30 WHEREAS, Dr. Schwartzman was a tireless advocate for D.C. Statehood and devoted
31 many years to advancing democratic representation for District residents; and
1

32 WHEREAS, through his organizing and advocacy, Dr. Schwartzman significantly
33 advanced economic and social justice for District residents, particularly in the areas of affordable
34 housing, climate justice, access to childcare, and the expansion of the DC Earned Income Tax
35 Credit, and;
36 WHEREAS, Dr. Schwartzman was the D.C. Statehood Green Party’s nominee for
37 Councilmember At-Large Councilmember in both 2018 and 2022, running on a platform center
38 on environment protection, economic equity, and full statehood for the District of Columbia,
39 and;
40 WHEREAS, Dr. Schwartzman was a dedicated eco-socialist and lifelong activist for
41 human rights, peace, and justice, whose work left a lasting impact on the District of Columbia
42 and its people.
43 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, that this
44 resolution may be cited as the “David W. Schwartzman Memorial Recognition Resolution of
45 2025”.
46 Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia recognizes, honors, and remembers and
47 the life of Dr. David W. Schwartzman.
48 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately upon the first date of publication in
49 the District of Columbia Register.
