13 To declare the existence of an emergency, due to congressional review, with respect to the need
14 to approve the development and financing agreement for the redevelopment of the
15 downtown arena; approve an agreement for the disposition by lease of certain real
16 property owned by the District government for the use and operation of the downtown
17 arena; approve a purchase and sale agreement for the downtown arena; authorize the
18 granting of certain easements related to the downtown arena property; authorize airspace
19 leases associated with the downtown arena; continue and provide for property tax
20 abatements and deed transfer and recordation tax exemptions for the downtown arena;
21 and authorize the issuance of rules to provide for additional signage on the downtown
22 arena.
23
24 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
25 resolution may be cited as the “Downtown Arena Revitalization Congressional Review
26 Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
27 Sec. 2. (a) On December 3, 2024, the Council unanimously approved the “Downtown
28 Arena Revitalization Emergency Act of 2024.” The Emergency Act became law on December
29 17, 2024, and is scheduled to expire on March 17, 2025.
30 (b) On December 17, 2024, the Council unanimously approved the “Downtown Arena
31 Revitalization Act of 2024.” The permanent version of this legislation was transmitted to
32 Congress on February 13, 2025, and has a projected law date of April 3, 2025.
33 (c) In order to address the imminent gap in the law between the expiration of the
34 Emergency Act and the effective date of the permanent legislation, it is necessary to approve
35 congressional review emergency legislation
1

36 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2 constitute
37 emergency circumstances making it necessary that the that the Downtown Arena Revitalization
38 Congressional Review Emergency Act of 2025 be adopted after a single reading.
39 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
