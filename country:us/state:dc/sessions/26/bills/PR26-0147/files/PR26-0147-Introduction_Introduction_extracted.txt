Stephen Lanning.
Mr. Stephen Lanning is currently a Labor Consultant with Lanning
Consulting, LLC. In this role, he provides guidance to unions on
creating strategic organizing campaign plans and developing training
programs to execute these strategies.
Mr. Lanning has been in the construction industry for over twenty years
and is a strong advocate for construction job training programs for
District residents. Mr. Lanning works to improve wages, benefits, and
working conditions of the area’s construction workers.
A Ward 1 resident, Mr. Lanning received a Bachelor of Arts in Political
Science and Philosophy from Miami University and a Master of Arts in
Political Science from the University of Toronto.
