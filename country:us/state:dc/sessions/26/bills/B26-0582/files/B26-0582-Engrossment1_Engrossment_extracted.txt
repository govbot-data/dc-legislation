10 To amend, on a temporary basis, the Deputy Mayor for Planning and Economic Development
11 Limited Grant-Making Authority Act of 2012 to reduce the long-term lease period to no
12 fewer than 5 years.
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
15 act may be cited as the “Rhode Island Avenue Corridor Long-Term Lease Adjustment
16 Temporary Amendment Act of 2026”.
17 Sec. 2. Section 2032 of the Deputy Mayor for Planning and Economic Development
18 Limited Grant-Making Authority Act of 2012, effective September 20, 2012 (D.C. Law 19-168;
19 D.C. Official Code § 1-328.04), is amended as follows:
20 (a) Paragraph (6)(C) is amended by striking the phrase “10 years” and inserting the
21 phrase “5 years, with a minimum of 3 years remaining on an existing lease as of the closing date
22 of the application period,”.
