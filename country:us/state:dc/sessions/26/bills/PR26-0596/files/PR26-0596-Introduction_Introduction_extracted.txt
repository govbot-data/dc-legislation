Damayra Moses Perez
Ms. Damayra Moses Perez is a licensed real estate salesperson in the
District with over 15 years of experience in the real estate industry.
Recently taking leadership of BBL Processors LLC, supporting real estate
transactions in the District. She is currently serving on the DC Association
of Realtors (DCAR) Public Policy Committee, as well as the Greater
Capitol Association of Realtors (GCAAR) Property Management
Advisory Committees.
A Ward 5 resident, Ms. Moses Perez earned her Bachelor of Arts from
Syracuse University and her Master of Public Administration from New
York University.
