Omololu Majekodunmi
Mr. Omololu Majekodunmi hasbeen an integralpart ofthe Washington,
DC healthcare community for 10 years. A retired cheerleader with the
Baltimore Ravens, he is currently the CEO ofTriumph Therapeutics
Throughout his career he has frequently provided mentorship and
professional development to others, including time serving as a clinical
instructor to occupational therapy students pursuing theirdegree.
With a strongbeliefinprofessionalaccountability, hehas been an active
memberofthe American Occupational Therapy Association since 2012
and currently serves as the vice president of the DC Occupational
Therapy Association.
‘A Ward5 resident, Mr. Majekodunmi earned his Bachelor ofScience in Government and Politics
from UniversityofMaryland College Park, and his Master of Science in Occupational Therapy
from Howard University.
