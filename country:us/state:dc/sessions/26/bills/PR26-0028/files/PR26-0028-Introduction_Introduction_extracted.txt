Ian Callender
Mr. Ian Callender, owner of the Culture House, was born and raised in
Washington, DC. After graduating from Drexel University, he moved
back to the District and worked for the Departmen t of Defense. He started
a business exporting and importing sneakers and eventually began
working directly with retailers such as Adidas and Reebok.
Always looking for the next chance to share subcultures with the
mainstream, Mr. Callender and various artists and community members
came across the opportunity to transform a century-old church in
Southwest, DC, creating the Culture House.
A Ward 6 resident, Mr. Callender earned his Bachelor of Science in Engineering and Information
and Technology from Drexel University.
