Dr. Sean Morgan
Dr. Sean G. Morgan currently serves as the Acting Medical Director at
the Fire and Emergency Medical Services Department (FEMS). He
previously served as the FEMS Assistant Medical Director. Dr. Morgan
is a double-board certified Emergency Medicine and EMS Medicine
physician with a robust career dedicated to advancing emergency
medical care.
Before joining FEMS, Dr. Morgan resided in Northeastern, PA, where
he served as the Medical Director for both the Lackawanna County
Communication Center and Pennsylvania Ambulance. In that role, he
supervised a team of over 200 dedicated professionals, ensuring the
highest standards of care and operational excellence. His leadership
extended to the EMS Training Institute, where he shaped the future of emergency medical services
by training EMTs and AEMTs. Dr. Morgan has been recognized for his exemplary contributions
to the medical field, earning a spot on the Top 40 Under 40 Physicians in Pennsylvania.
In addition to his clinical leadership, Dr. Morgan is a committed educator and national contributor
to EMS standards. He serves as a board examiner for the American Board of Emergency Medicine,
an Advanced Trauma Life Support (ATLS) Instructor for the American College of Surgeons, a
CPR guideline author for the American Heart Association, and a national site visitor for paramedic
programs.
A Ward 7 resident, Dr. Morgan earned his Bachelor of Science from Johns Hopkins University
and his Doctor of Medicine from Georgetown University.
