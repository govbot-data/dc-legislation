Dr. Kami Cooper
Dr. Kami Cooper is a licensed nurse practitioner and Behavioral Health
and Wellness Officer with the NOAA National Weather Service, as well
as an Emotional Wellness Co-Lead with the US Public Health Service.
In her former position as branch chief at USPHS, Dr. Cooper guided
future deployments through policy development, multi-agency relations,
and officer engagement. This experience allows Dr. Cooper to continue
to improve the health of the nation, as well as built opportunities and
training to ensure officers were prepared for deployments with the
appropriate clinical skills andcompetencies. In total, Dr. Cooperhasbeen
deployed for more than fifteen disasters or community health and service
missions since her commissioning, inboth direct patientcareclinical roles
and leadership positions. In addition, as a Doctorof Nursing, she is a bedside clinician forpatients
across the age spectrum in primary care, intensive care, and mental health,
Asan adjunctprofessoartWashington AdventistUniversity and American University, Dr. Cooper
teaches both the undergrad and master level in nursingandpublic health programs. Shealso serves
as a mentor to college youth, nursing students, public health students, and junior USPHS officers.
‘A Ward4 resident, Dr. Cooper earned her Bachelor of Science in Nursing from University of
Cincinnati, her Master of Science in Nursing from University of Maryland, and her Doctor of
Nursing Practice in Community Health Education and Promotion from Duquesne University.
