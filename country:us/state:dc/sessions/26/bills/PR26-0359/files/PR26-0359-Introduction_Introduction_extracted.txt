Peter Winkler
Mr. Peter Winkler is a current member of the Public Employee
Relations Board (PERB). He is a highly experienced professional and
proven public administrator who spent twenty years at the National
Labor Relations Board (NLRB) as an attorney until 2017. At the NLRB,
he helped decide representation cases including questionsof unit scope
and unit placement, unfair labor practice cases, unlawful discharges,
coercion of employees by employers and unions, the duty to bargain,
and negotiability disputes.
Mr. Winkler began his service at the National Labor Relations Board’s
Appellate Court Branch where he briefed and argued cases before the
United States Court of Appeals. Inthis capacity, Mr. Winkler appeared
many times in U.S. District Courts, trying cases and engaging in motions practice. During Mr.
Winkler’s time at the United States Court of Appeals, he drafted the Board’s briefs in several
cases beforethe United States Supreme Court.
Currently, Mr. Winkler remains involved with his community as a volunteer at Washington
Studio and Northwest Neighbors Village. Mr. Winkler is a past board member and officer at
Temple Sinai DC.
