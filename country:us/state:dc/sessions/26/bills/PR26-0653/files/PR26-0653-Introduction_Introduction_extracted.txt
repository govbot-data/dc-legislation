Zena Wubneh
Ms. Zena Wubneh is a finance and operations professional, entrepreneur,
and founder of Savvy Numbers LLC, where she partners with nonprofits,
small businesses, and service-based organizations to build strong
financial systems and drive sustainable growth. With more than a decade
of experience across community development, arts and culture, and
mission-driven enterprises, Ms. Wubneh brings a strategic, solutions-
oriented approach to advancing economic opportunity.
A trusted advisor to growing organizations, Ms. Wubneh specializes in
financial management, budgeting, and grant reporting – helping leaders
translate vision into actionable, measurable results.
Deeply rooted in the African communities of the District of Columbia, Ms. Wubneh is passionate
about inclusive growth, economic mobility, and community-centered leadership.
A Ward 5 resident, Ms. Wubneh earned her Bachelor of Arts in Exercise and Sport Science from
the University of North Carolina at Chapel Hill.
