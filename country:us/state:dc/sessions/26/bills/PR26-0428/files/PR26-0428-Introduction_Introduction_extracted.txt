_______________________________
Councilmember Trayon White, Sr.
A PROPOSED RESOLUTION
_____
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
_____
1 To disapprove, on an emergency basis, reprogramming request No. 26-0097 of $7,000,000 of FY
2 2026 Capital Funds budget authority and allotment within the Department of Parks and
3 Recreation from the Anacostia pool replacement and Congress Heights modernization to
4 complete the construction of the Southeast Tennis Learning Center project.
5
6 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
7 resolution may be cited as the “Department of Parks and Recreation Funding Reprogramming
8 Request No. 26-0097 Disapproval Emergency Resolution of 2025”.
9 Sec. 2. (a) Pursuant to D.C. Official Code § 47-363, the Mayor transmitted to the Council
10 reprogramming request No. 26-0097 in the amount of $7,000,000 of FY 2026 Capital Funds
11 budget authority and allotment within the Department of Parks and Recreation (DPR) from the
12 Anacostia pool replacement and Congress Heights Recreation Center modernization to complete
13 the construction of the Southeast Tennis Learning Center.
14 (b) The Council disapproves the $7,000,000 reprogramming request.
15 Sec. 3. The Secretary of the Council of the District of Columbia shall transmit a copy of
16 this resolution, upon its adoption, to the Mayor.

17 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
2
