Patricia Howard-Chittams
ia Howard-Chittams is an Adult Geriatric Care Nurse
Practitioner for the Washington Area Nurse Practitioners Group and is a
sixth generation Washingtonian.
Ms. Howard-Chittams began her nursing career with TriFax Nursing
providing home visits to infants who were considered at risk by their
physicians and eventually began providing nursing coverage in DC
Public Schools. Over the next several years, she worked with a variety of
nursingagenciesandthrough them providednursingcaretoarea hospitals
such as Washington Hospital Center, Howard University Hospital,
Hadley Hospital, and Columbia Hospital for Women among others. In
that capacity, she worked in a varietyofspecialties such as post-partum
care, gynecology, and psychiatriccare.
Afterworking as an agency nurse forChildren’s School Services for several years, sheapplied for
and was hired as a Community Health Nurse in 2011. In the fall of the same year, she was
promoted to Nurse Manager. In that capacity, she was honored as the 2016 Nurse Administrator
ofthe Year forthe District ofColumbia by the National School Nurses Association. In2018, she
became certified as a Faith Community Health Nurse at Children’s National Medical Center.
‘A Ward 7 resident, Ms. Howard-Chittams eared her Bachelor of Science in Nursing from the
University of the District of Columbia,her Master of Science in Nursingfrom Benedictine
University, and her Doctorof Nursing Practice from Eastern Kentucky University.
