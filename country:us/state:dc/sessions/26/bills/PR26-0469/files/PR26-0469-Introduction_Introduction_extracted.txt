Danielle Schmutz
Ms. Danielle Schmutz has spent the past 15+ years building teams and
strengthening programs, interventions, fund development, and operations
at startups and nonprofit organizations in health and education. She
currently serves as the Executive Director of Beacon House, an
organization that provides after-school education and youth development
services to nearly 400 children, ages 5-18, annually.
Before becoming the Executive Director of Beacon House, Ms. Schmutz
was the Director of Programs and Operations and played a key role in
program development and transformation, operational effectiveness,
fundraising, donor relations, and financial management.
Most recently, Ms. Schmutz held multiple leadership roles on the founding teams of Surgo
Ventures, a global public health organization focused on leveraging data to solve major health
challenges, and Surgo Health, an affiliated data and data analytics health tech startup.
A Ward 5 resident, Ms. Schmutz received her Master of Social Work from the University of
Michigan and her Bachelor of Science in Sociology and Child Development from Central
Michigan University.
