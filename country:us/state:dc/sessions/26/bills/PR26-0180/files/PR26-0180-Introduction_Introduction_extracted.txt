DearChairman Mendelson:
Enclosed for consideration and enactment by the Council of the District of Columbia is an
emergency bill, the “Modification Nos. 8 and 9 to Contract No. DCAM-20-CS-RFQ-0002F with
American Combustion Industries, Inc. Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of
2025,” and the accompanying emergency declaration resolution,
The legislation will approve a modification to a proposed contract for HVAC systems
maintenance, replacement, and installation services for schools, parks, recreation facilities,
municipal buildings, fire and police stations, short-term family housing units, and other public
locations, and will authorize payment to American Combustion Industries, Inc. for services
received and to be received under the contract.
My administration is available to discuss any questions you may have regarding the proposed
contract modification. In order to facilitate a response to any questions you may have, please
contact Delano Hunter, Director, Department of General Services (“DGS”), or have your staff
contact Eric Njonjo, Acting ChiefProcurement Officer, DGS, at(202) 727-7138.
Lurge the Council to take prompt and favorable action on the enclosed legislation.
Sincerely,
Mufiel Bawser

21 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
22 resolution may be cited as the "Modification Nos. 8 and 9 to Contract No. DCAM-20-CS-RFQ-
23 0002F with American Combustion Industries, Inc. Approval and Payment Authorization
24 Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025".
25 Sec. 2 (a) There exists an immediate need to approve Modification No. 8 and proposed
26 Modification No. 9 to Contract No. DCAM-20-CS-RFQ-0002F, and to authorize payment for the
27 goods and services received and to be received under the modification.
28 (b) On June 6, 2024, via Modification No. 8, the Department of General Services
29 partially exercised Option Year 004 of the Contract for the period June 8, 2024, through June 7,
30 2025, in the not-to-exceed amount of $990,000.
31 ( d) Proposed Modification No. 9 would fully exercise Option Year 004 of the Contract,
32 increasing the not-to-exceed amount by $9,010,000 to $10,000,000.

33 (e) Council approval is necessary because the modifications will increase the value of the
34 contract to more than $1 million during a 12-month period.
35 (f) Council approval is necessary to allow the continuation of these vital services.
36 Without Council approval, American Combustion Industries, Inc., cannot be paid for goods and
37 services in excess of $1 million for the period June 8, 2024, through June 7, 2025.
38 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
39 enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the
40 Modification Nos. 8 and 9 to Contract No. DCAM-20-CS-RFQ-0002F with American
41 Combustion Industries, Inc. Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2025 be
42 adopted after a single reading.
43 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
