ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-440
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
June 30, 2026
To confirm the appointment of Jennifer Wade as a member of the Green Finance Authority
Board.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
resolution may be cited as the “Green Finance Authority Board Jennifer Wade Confirmation
Resolution of 2026”.
Sec. 2. The Council of the District of Columbia confirms the appointment of:
Jennifer Wade
Naylor Road, SE
Washington, DC 20020
(Ward 8)
as a member with financial, project development, or legal expertise in clean energy, clean
infrastructure, clean transportation, stormwater management, or green infrastructure of the Green
Finance Authority Board, established by section 201 of the Green Finance Authority
Establishment Act of 2018, effective August 22, 2018 (D.C. Law 22-155; D.C. Official Code §
8-173.21), for a term to end July 9, 2028.
Sec. 3. The Council shall transmit a copy of this resolution, upon its adoption, to the
nominee and to the Mayor.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
