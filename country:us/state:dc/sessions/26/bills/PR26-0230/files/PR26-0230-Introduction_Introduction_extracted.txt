48th Street, NW

Frederick P. Moosally
Mr. Frederick P. Moosally has served as the Director of the Alcoholic
Beverage and Cannabis Administration (ABCA) since 2009. Prior to
leading ABCA, he helmed the agency’s adjudication division as the
GeneralCounsel forseven years. He has more than 20 yearsofexperience
in local and federal government.
During his tenure, he has focused his efforts on delivering outstanding
customer service, soliciting and incorporating community input,
increasing transparency, boosting access to DC-made products, and
protectingthe healthand safety ofresidents and visitors. His efforts have
earned ABCA national accolades including theNational Liquor Law
Enforcement Association’s Agency of the Year (2018) and Innovative
Alcohol Law Enforcement Programofthe Year (2017 and 2020).
A Ward 3 resident, Mr. Moosally earned his BachelorofScience in Political Science and History
from James Madison University, and his Juris Doctorfrom Catholic University.
