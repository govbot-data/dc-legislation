MURIEL BOWSER
MAYOR
January 8, 2025
The Honorable Phil Mendelson
Chairman, Council of the District of Columbia
1350 Pennsylvania Avenue NW, Suite 504
Washington, DC 20004
Dear Chairman Mendelson:
Enclosed for consideration and enactment by the Council of the District of Columbia is
an emergency bill, the “Contract No. CW122630 with Patriot Harley Davidson Approval
and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2025,” and the accompanying emergency
declaration resolution in the not-to-exceed amount of $1,686,086.89 from October 1, 2024
through September 30, 2025.
The legislation will approve Contract No. CW122630 with Patriot Harley Davidson to purchase a
minimum of 30 and maximum of 35 police motorcycles with side cars for use by the Metropolitan
Police Department. In addition, the legislation will approve payment for the goods and services
received and to be received under the contract.
My administration is available to discuss any questions you may have regarding the proposed
contract. In order to facilitate a response to any questions you may have, please have your staff
contact Marc Scott, Chief Operating Officer, Office of Contracting and Procurement, at (202) 724-
8759.
I urge the Council to take prompt and favorable action on the enclosed legislation.
Sincerely,
Muriel Bowser

19
20 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
21 resolution may be cited as the "Contract No. CW122630 with Patriot Harley Davidson Approval
22 and Payment Authorization Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025".
23 Sec. 2. (a) There exists an immediate need to approve Contract No. CW122630 with
24 Patriot Harley Davidson and to authorize payment for the goods and services received and to be
25 received under the contract.
26 (b) On December 11, 2024, the Office of Contracting and Procurement ("OCP"), on
27 behalf of the Metropolitan Police Department, executed emergency Contract No. CW122630
28 with Patriot Harley Davidson to purchase a minimum of 30 and a maximum of 35 police
29 motorcycles with side cars in the not-to-exceed amount of $1,686,086.89.
30 (c) Under section 451(b)(l) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved
31 December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 803; D.C. Official Code§ 1-204.51(b)(l)), Council approval is
32 necessary because the value of the contract is more than $1 million during a 12-month period.

33 (d) Emergency legislation is necessary to allow for the provision of these vital goods and
34 services. Without Council approval, Patriot Harley Davidson cannot be paid for goods and
35 services provided in excess of $1 million for the period from December 11, 2024, through
36 December 10, 2025.
37 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2 constitute
38 emergency circumstances making it necessary that Contract No. CW122630 with Patriot Harley
39 Davidson Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2025 be adopted after a single
40 reading.
41 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
