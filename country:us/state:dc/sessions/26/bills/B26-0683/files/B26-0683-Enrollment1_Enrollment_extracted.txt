ENROLLED ORIGINAL
AN ACT
________
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
________________
To approve, on an emergency basis, Modification No. 4 to multiyear Contract No. CW91301
with Thales DIS USA, Inc., and to authorize payment for the goods and services received
and to be received under the contract.
BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
act may be cited as the “Modification No. 4 to Multiyear Contract No. CW91301 with Thales
DIS USA, Inc. Approval and Payment Authorization Emergency Act of 2026”.
Sec. 2. Pursuant to section 451(c)(3) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act,
approved December 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 803; D.C. Official Code § 1-204.51(c)(3)), the Council
approves Modification No. 4 to multiyear Contract No. CW91301 with Thales DIS USA, Inc., in
the not-to-exceed amount of $13,707,400. The period of performance is from July 1, 2023,
through June 30, 2030.
Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Chief Financial Officer as the fiscal
impact statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975,
approved October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
Sec. 4. Effective date.
This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by the
Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), and shall remain in effect for no longer than
