Bernard White II
Mr. Bernard White II, has served in several event-based positions at
Howard University. He currently serves as the Senior Event Planner in
the Department of Events and Protocol at Howard University.
In his current role, he is responsible executing Howard’s most visible
annual events to include commencement, homecoming, and college
anniversaries. These events provide the backdrop for Howard
University’s fundraising initiatives.
Beyond his role at Howard University, Mr. White leads a student
internship program that provides Howard students the opportunity to
volunteer at major events and garner real world experience. Mr. White
has also created local partnerships with businesses and non-profit organizations in the DC
community.
A Ward 1 resident, Mr. White earned his Bachelor of Science in Health Management and Business
Management from Howard University.
