12 To amend, on a temporary basis, the Department of For-Hire Vehicles Establishment Act of 1985
13 to authorize the Department of For-Hire Vehicles to enforce traffic laws against carrier-for-
14 hire vehicles.
15 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
16 act may be cited as the “Department of For-Hire Vehicles Delivery Vehicle Traffic Enforcement
17 Expansion Temporary Amendment Act of 2025”.
18 Sec. 2. The Department of For-Hire Vehicles Establishment Act of 1985, effective March
19 25, 1986 (D.C. Law 6-97; D.C. Official Code § 50-301.01 et seq.), is amended as follows:
20 (a) Section 4 (D.C. Official Code § 50-301.03) is amended as follows:
21 (1) A new paragraph (3A) is added to read as follows:
22 “(3A) “Carrier-for-hire” means a class of transportation service by which a
23 network of private operators, couriers, or carriers provide delivery of parcels, food, or beverages
24 in the District for compensation.”.
25 (2) Paragraph (30B) is amended as follows:
1

26 (A) Strike the phrase “regulations governing public and private vehicle-
27 for-hire service” and insert the phrase “regulations governing public and private vehicle-for-hire
28 and carrier-for-hire service” in its place.
29 (B) Strike the phrase “traffic stops of public and private vehicles-for-hire,
30 pursuant to protocol prescribed” and insert the phrase “traffic stops of public and private
31 vehicles-for-hire and carrier-for-hire vehicles, pursuant to protocol prescribed in its place.
32 (b) Section 7(a) (D.C. Official Code § 50-301.06) is amended as follows:
33 (1) Paragraph (4)(F) is amended by striking the phrase “Performing vehicle-for-
34 hire inspections” and inserting the phrase “Performing public and private vehicle-for-hire and
35 carrier-for-hire inspections” in its place.
36 (2) Paragraph (5) is amended by striking the phrase “and dispatch services,” and
37 inserting the phrase “dispatch services, and carriers-for-hire,” in its place.
38 (c) Section 8 (D.C. Official Code § 50-301.07) is amended to read as follows:
39 “(b-1) The DFHV shall employ vehicle inspection officers to enforce the laws, rules, and
40 regulations pertaining to vehicles-for-hire and carriers-for-hire. A primary function of vehicle
41 inspection officers shall be to ensure the proper provision of service and to support safety
42 through street enforcement efforts, including traffic stops of public and private vehicles-for-hire
43 and carriers-for-hire, pursuant to protocol prescribed by the DFHV.”.
44 (2) Subsection (c) is amended as follows:
45 (A) Paragraph (19) is amended by striking the phrase "; and" and inserting
46 a semicolon in its place.
47 (B) A new paragraph (19A) is added to read as follows:
2

48 “(19A) Establish rules relating to the enforcement of traffic regulations
49 against carrier-for-hire vehicles; and”.
50 (d) Section 20k(a) (D.C. Official Code § 50-301.30(a)) is amended as follows:
51 (1) Strike the phrase “shall undergo training on the rules and regulations
52 governing private and public vehicles-for-hire and undergo” and insert the phrase “shall undergo
53 training on the rules and regulations governing private and public vehicles-for-hire and carrier-
54 for-hire vehicles and undergo” in its place.
55 (2) Strike the phrase “inspection officers shall be prohibited from making traffic
56 stops of on-duty private or public vehicles-for-hire in the act of transporting a fare, unless there
57 is reasonable suspicion of a violation” and insert the phrase “inspection officers shall be
58 prohibited from making traffic stops of on-duty private or public vehicles-for-hire in the act of
59 transporting a fare, or a carrier-for-hire vehicle in the act of transporting a parcel, food, or
60 beverage, unless there is reasonable suspicion of a violation” in its place.
61 Sec. 3. Section 303(b) of the Migrant Services and Supports Extension Temporary
62 Amendment Act of 2024, effective September 18, 2024 (D.C. Law 25-215; 71 DCR 11539), is
63 amended by striking the number “225” and inserting the number “450” in its place.
64 Sec. 4. Fiscal impact statement.
65 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Budget Director as the fiscal impact
66 statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975, approved
67 October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
