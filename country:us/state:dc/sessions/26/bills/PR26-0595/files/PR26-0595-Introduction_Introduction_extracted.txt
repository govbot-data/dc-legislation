Anthony Graham
Mr. Anthony Graham is a licensed real estate broker in Washington, D.C.,
Maryland, and Virginia. His credentials include the NAIOP Advanced
Study in Commercial Real Estate Development, the Novogradac
Community Development Certificate, and the Graduate REALTOR®
Institute (GRI) designation.
Mr. Graham completed the Open Access Fellowship as the Tony
Goldman Fellow with the National Trust Community Investment
Corporation (NTCIC), where he gained expertise in compliance,
New Markets Tax Credits, and financial oversight. Essential
competencies for regulatory governance, all of which reflect his deep
commitment to professional growth and technical mastery.
These certifications have equipped me to address the multifaceted regulatory and financial aspects
of the real estate sector.
During Mr. Graham’s tenure as Public Policy Chair for the DC Association of REALTORS®, to
protect property rights and housing affordability, his team secured over $165,000 in grant funding
to curate a study on DC’s 40-year-old rent control policies with Howard University. This initiative
exemplified his ability to navigate legislative frameworks, engage stakeholders, and produce data-
driven recommendations that shape policy decisions.
A Ward 5 resident, Mr. Graham earned his Bachelor of Arts in Legal Communications from
Howard University.
