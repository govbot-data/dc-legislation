ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
26-187
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
June 30, 2026
To honor and recognize Brett Greene on the occasion of the 25th anniversary of the American
Management Corporation.
WHEREAS, Brett Greene is a respected leader, entrepreneur, and advocate whose vision
and dedication led to the founding of American Management Corporation (AMC) in 2001;
WHEREAS, for 25 years AMC has been noted as a premier government relations and
consulting firm serving corporate, nonprofit, and government clients across the metropolitan
Washington, DC region;
WHEREAS, under the leadership of Brett Greene, AMC has provided strategic advice,
government relations expertise, and community engagement services that have strengthened
partnerships between the public and private sectors;
WHEREAS, Brett Greene and AMC have consistently demonstrated a deep commitment
to assisting local organizations effectively navigate complex public policy, regulatory and
community issues that surface in the District of Columbia;
WHEREAS, the success and longevity of AMC reflect not only sound business
leadership, but also a strong commitment to the civic and economic vitality of the District;
WHEREAS, as a proud and enduring DC-based small business, AMC represents among
the finest examples of the depth of DC’s entrepreneurial community;
WHEREAS, the 25th anniversary of American Management Corporation on June 11,
2026, marked a significant milestone in the history of this distinguished local company; a
company that for a quarter of a century has demonstrated a steadfast commitment of excellence,
integrity, innovation, and dedicated service to Washington, DC; and
WHEREAS, we gratefully acknowledge Mr. Greene’s investment and thank him for the
contribution he has made to our community.
1

ENROLLED ORIGINAL
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That the
Council of the District of Columbia recognizes Brett Green for his leadership and congratulates
him on the 25th anniversary of the American Management Corporation, with appreciation for his
contributions to the economic life of the District.
Sec. 2. The resolution may be cited as the “Brett Green and American Management
Corporation Recognition Resolution of 2026.”
Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
