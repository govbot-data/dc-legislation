10 To amend, on a temporary basis, section 28-5404 of the District of Columbia Official Code to
11 allow retail establishments with a valid on-premises Class C or Class D retailer license to
12 refuse cash as a form of payment in certain circumstances.
13
14 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
15 act may be cited as the “Entertainment Establishment Employee Safety Extension Temporary
16 Amendment Act of 2026”.
17 Sec. 2. Section 28-5404 of the District of Columbia Official Code is amended as follows:
18 (a) Paragraph (3)(C) is amended by striking the phrase “; or” and inserting a semicolon in
19 its place.
20 (b) Paragraph (4) is amended by striking the period and inserting the phrase “; or” in its
21 place.
22 (c) A new paragraph (5) is added to read as follows:
23 “(5) Provided that written, conspicuous notice to the public is displayed on the door of
24 the establishment or menu that cash payments are not accepted, to:
25 “(A) A retail establishment with a valid on-premises Class C or Class D retailer
26 license; or
27 “(B) A restaurant location, as that term is defined in § 25-101(43), that operates:

29 “(ii) At 3:00 am at least 2 days a week.”.
