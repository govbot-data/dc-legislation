Marquisha Powell
Ms. Marquisha Powell is a Senior Associate at StudioMB where she plays
a key role in shaping impactful designs across Washington, D.C.
With nearly two decades of experience, Ms. Powell specializes in
transforming existing structures to maximize their potential while
preserving green sites and reducing embodied energy. Ms. Powell’s
expertise lie in balancing historical preservation with modern innovation,
creating spaces that are both functional and environmentally responsible.
Prior to joining StudioMB, Ms. Powell spent over 10 years at Page
Southerland Page, Inc., where she refined her skills in project architecture
and comprehensive design processes.
A Ward 4 resident, Ms. Powell earned her Bachelor of Architecture from University of Houston.
