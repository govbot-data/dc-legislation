ENGROSSED ORIGINAL
A BILL
26-358
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
________________
1 To amend the Policemen and Firemen's Retirement and Disability Act to remove the
2 Metropolitan Police Department and Fire and Emergency Medical Services mandatory
3 retirement age.
4
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
6 act may be cited as the “First Responder Retention Efforts Amendment Act of 2026”.
7 Sec. 2. Section 12(h)(2) of the Policemen and Firemen's Retirement and Disability Act,
8 approved September 1, 1916 (39 Stat 718; D.C Official Code § 5-712(b)), is amended to read as
9 follows:
10 “(b) Any member of the United States Secret Service Uniformed Division or of the
11 United States Park Police force or of the United States Secret Service Division, having reached
12 the age of 60 years, to whom this section apply shall, in the discretion of the head of the
13 member’s department, be retired from the service and shall be entitled to receive an annuity as
14 computed under paragraph (1) of this subsection.”.
