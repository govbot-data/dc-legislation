14 To amend, on a temporary basis, the Omnibus Barry Farm Redevelopment Act of 2022 to
15 authorize the Surveyor to record the street and alley closing and dedication plats in
16 multiple phases.
17
18 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
19 act may be cited as the “The Omnibus Barry Farm Redevelopment Clarification Temporary
20 Amendment Act of 2026”.
21 Sec. 2. The Omnibus Barry Farm Redevelopment Act of 2022, effective December 13,
22 2022 (D.C. Law 24-192; 69 DCR 015293), is amended as follows:
23 (a) Section 2(b) is amended to read as follows:
24 “(b) The ordering of the street and alley closures and removal of building restriction lines
25 set forth in subsection (a) of this section is contingent upon satisfaction of all the conditions set
26 forth by the District Department of Transportation in the official file for S.O. 17-26688 for each
27 phase or subphase prior to the recordation of a plat by the Surveyor for such phase or
28 subphase. The Surveyor shall record the street and alley closing plats in multiple phases or
29 subphases as the work is completed and approved by the District Department of Transportation,
30 with the plat or plats for each phase or subphase for Phase 1A recorded no later than December
31 2028; the plat or plats for each phase or subphase for Phase 1B recorded no later than December

32 2030; and the plat or plats for each phase or subphase for Phases 2A and 2B recorded no later
33 than December 2034.”.
34 (b) Section 3(b)(2) is amended to read as follows:
35 “(b)(2) The Surveyor shall record the street and alley dedication and designation plats in
36 multiple phases, as indicated in S.O. 17-26689, with the plat or plats for each phase or subphase
37 for Phase 1A recorded no later than December 31, 2028, the plat or plats for each phase or
38 subphase for Phase 1B recorded no later than December 31, 2026, and the plat or plats for each
39 phase or subphase for Phases 2A and 2B recorded no later than December 31, 2034.”.
40 Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
41 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement of the Budget Director as the fiscal impact
42 statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975, approved
43 October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
44 Sec. 4. Effective date.
45 (a) This act shall take effect following approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by
46 the Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), a 30-day period of congressional review
47 as provided in section 602(c)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December
48 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 813; D.C. Official Code § 1-206.02(c)(1)), and publication in the D.C.
49 Register.
50 (b) This act shall expire after 225 days of its having taken effect.
