18 To declare the existence of an emergency, due to congressional review, with respect to the need
19 to amend section 205 of the Clemency Board Establishment Act of2018 to authorize the
20 Clemency Board of the District of Columbia to, for applicants seeking a pardon, grant a
21 waiver of the five-year waiting period.
22
23 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
24 resolution may be cited as the “Clemency Board Waiver Authority Congressional Review
25 Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
26 Sec. 2. (a) On March 4, 2025, the Council approved the Clemency Board Waiver
27 Authority Emergency Amendment Act of 2025, effective March 20, 2025 (D.C. Act 26-27; 72
28 DCR 3320) (“emergency measure”). The emergency measure is set to expire on June 18, 2025.
29 (b) The Clemency Board Waiver Authority Temporary Amendment Act of 2025 (B26-
30 37) was approved by the Council on final reading at the April 1, 2025, legislative meeting.
31 Because the temporary measure must undergo congressional review, it will not be in effect
32 before the emergency measure expires.
33 (c) This congressional review emergency is necessary to prevent a gap in the law between
34 the expiration of the emergency measure and effective date of the temporary measure.

35 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances in
36 section 2 constitute emergency circumstances, making it necessary that the Clemency Board
37 Waiver Authority Congressional Review Emergency Amendment Act of 2025 be adopted after a
38 single reading.
39 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
