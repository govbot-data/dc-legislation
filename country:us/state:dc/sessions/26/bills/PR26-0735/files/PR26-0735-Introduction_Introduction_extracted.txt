15 To declare the existence of an emergency, due to congressional review, with respect to the need
16 to amend the Robert F. Kennedy Memorial Stadium and the District of
17 Columbia National Guard Armory Public Safety Act of 1977 to include the Downtown
18 Arena and the Southeast Arena in the prohibition on unauthorized entry
19 onto a playing surface.
20
21 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
22 resolution may be cited as the “Arena Safety Conformity Congressional Review Emergency
23 Declaration Resolution of 2026”.
24 Sec. 2. (a) On March 31, 2026, the Council passed the Arena Safety Conformity
25 Emergency Amendment Act of 2026, effective April 8, 2026 (D.C. Act 26-291; 73 DCR 6059)
26 (“emergency act”), which expires on July 7, 2026.
27 (b) On May 5, 2026, the Council passed the Arena Safety Conformity Temporary
28 Amendment Act of 2026, passed on 2nd reading on May 5, 2026 (Enrolled version of Bill 26-635)
29 (“temporary act”), which does not yet have a projected law date.
30 (c) This congressional review emergency legislation is necessary to prevent a gap in the
31 law between the expiration of the emergency act and the effective date of the temporary act.
1

32 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
33 enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the Arena
34 Safety Conformity Congressional Review Emergency Amendment Act of 2026 be adopted after a
35 single reading.
36 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
