4 A CEREMONIAL RESOLUTION
5 ______________
6
7 IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
8 _________________
9
10
11 To recognize and acknowledge Charles C. “Sandy” Wilkes for his work as the founding
12 Chairman of the Board of the D.C. Policy Center, and his many other notable
13 professional achievements.
14
15 WHEREAS, Charles C. “Sandy” Wilkes has devoted a lifetime of leadership and service
16 to the Washington, D.C. community through his pioneering work in law, business, civic
17 engagement, and urban development;
18
19 WHEREAS, As Chairman of The Wilkes Company, Mr. Wilkes has demonstrated a
20 commitment to shaping a better future for the District through innovative vision;
21
22 WHEREAS, Mr. Wilkes’ leadership extends beyond the D.C. Policy Center as evidenced
23 by his many distinguished roles—including Chairman of the Washington Children’s Foundation,
24 Chairman of Board of Directors of the National Theatre Foundation, Trustee and Leadership
25 Council Member of the Federal City Council, founding member of The Developer Roundtable,
26 and founding Director of both the Mount Vernon Triangle Community Improvement District and
27 the NoMa Business Improvement District—and further enriched by his service as Chairman of the
28 NoMa Parks Foundation;
29
30 WHEREAS, Mr. Wilkes’ commitment to community development, education, and the arts
31 is further exemplified by his appointment to the Board of Trustees of St. Mary’s College of
32 Maryland by Governor Parris Glendening and his membership in the James Madison Council at
33 The Library of Congress;
34
35 WHEREAS, Mr. Wilkes’ many contributions have not only fostered significant progress
36 in urban planning and policy but also set a benchmark for civic responsibility and visionary
37 leadership within the broader D.C. community, enriching both individual lives and shared
38 communities;
39
40 WHEREAS, under Mr. Wilkes leadership, the D.C. Policy Center expanded its research
41 areas to include the city’s economy, housing, workforce, and public education, contributing
42 reliable and timely research that informed many policies adopted by the Council and the Mayor.
43 Deliverables include in-depth analyses of the city’s budget and financial position, elements that
44 make the District more competitive, initiatives that make housing more plentiful, and programs
45 designed to improve equitable access to education and the success of the District’s public school
46 students;

47
48 WHEREAS, the D.C. Policy Center’s expanded research efforts have resulted in the
49 implementation of important legislation, including proposals that: bolstered housing preservation
50 programs, created the Equitable Access option in the District’s Common Lottery and the Education
51 to Employment data system, formed the Research Practice Partnership in education, and helped
52 reduce costs for thousands of small businesses;
53
54 WHEREAS, Mr. Wilkes stepped down as Chairman of the Board of the D.C. Policy Center
55 on October 15, 2024, but continues to provide leadership and guidance to the organization as its
56 Chairman Emeritus; and
57
58 WHEREAS, Charles C. “Sandy” Wilkes has created an enduring legacy of public service
59 and dedication to the improvement of residential life and business in District of Columbia.
60
61 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
62 resolution may be cited as the “Charles C. “Sandy” Wilkes Ceremonial Recognition Resolution of
63 2025”.
64 Sec. 2. The Council recognizes and commends Charles C. “Sandy” Wilkes for his steadfast
65 service, guidance, and contributions as the founding Chairman of the Board of the D.C. Policy
66 Center and celebrates the significant benefits that his rich professional legacy has provided to the
67 District of Columbia.
68 Sec. 3. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
