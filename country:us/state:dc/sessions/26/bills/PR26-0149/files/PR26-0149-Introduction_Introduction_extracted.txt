Frank Chiaramonte
Mr. Frank Chiaramonte serves as the President of Chiaramonte
Construction Company (“CCC”). Founded in 2004, CCC is a certified
business enterprise in the District of Columbia and specializes in self-
performance of construction trade work such as drywall, masonry, and
concrete, contracting over $100,000,000 of construction work, and
employing over 75 fulltime employees annually.
Mr. Chiaramonte has consistently increased revenue for his company
every year since inception and it continues to grow. As an organization
within the District, CCC gives back to the community and understands the importance of growing
the community along with the company.
A Ward 8 resident, Mr. Chiaramonte earned his Associate of Science from Mongomery College
and his Bachelor of Science from the University of Maryland.
