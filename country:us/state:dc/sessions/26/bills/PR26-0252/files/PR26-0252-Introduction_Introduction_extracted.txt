22 RESOLVED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, that this
23 resolution may be cited as the "USBC Economic Development Corporation Revenue Bonds Project
24 Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2025".
25 Sec. 2. (a) USBC Economic Development Corporation, is a nonprofit corporation
26 organized and existing under the laws of the District of Columbia (the "Borrower"), which seeks
27 to have District of Columbia revenue bonds issued and receive a loan of the proceeds from the sale
28 thereof for the financing, refinancing or reimbursing of all or a portion of the Borrower's costs of:
29 (1) Acquisition of the fee simple interest of 1301 W Street, N.E., Washington, D.C.,
30 and the acquisition of a leasehold interest of 1900 W Place, N.E., 1235 W Street, N.E., and Parcel
31 C, a 1.73 acre parking lot (Square 142, Lots 133, 134, 125, and 123) to be partially renovated by
32 the Applicant and be used by the Applicant's as its headquarters facility (collectively, the
33 "Facility");

34 (2) Purchase and installation of certain equipment and furnishings for the Facility,
35 together with other property, real and personal, functionally related and subordinate thereto;
36 (3) Funding any credit enhancement costs, liquidity costs, capitalized interest or
37 debt service reserve fund relating to the Bonds; and
38 (4) Paying Issuance Costs and other related costs to the extent permissible. (the
39 “Project”).
40 (b) The planned financing will make available funds critically needed to finance, refinance,
41 or reimburse the Borrower for costs of the Project.
42 (c) Due to the current economic uncertainty in the financial markets, it is important for the
43 Council to expedite the process for the issuance of the Bonds and avoid any delay that may
44 adversely affect the ability of the Borrower to market the Bonds to investors and/or to obtain an
45 interest rate within the range contemplated by the Project budget.
46 (d) Council approval of the bond resolution authorizing the issuance of up to $37.5 million
47 of District of Columbia revenue bonds would permit the revenue bonds to be issued promptly to
48 provide maximum savings for the Borrower and enable the Project described in Section 2(a) to be
49 completed.
50 Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
51 enumerated in section 2 hereof constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the
52 “USBC Revenue Bonds Project Emergency Approval Resolution of 2025” be adopted after a single
53 reading.
54 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
