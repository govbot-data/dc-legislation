15 To congratulate and celebrate the 2025 Coolidge Senior High School Colts Varsity Football Team
16 on a successful season and Turkey Bowl victory.
17
18 WHEREAS, the Coolidge Senior High School Colts Varsity Football Team completed an
19 outstanding 2025 season, demonstrating discipline, teamwork, and excellence on and off the field;
20 WHEREAS, the 2025 Turkey Bowl Championship game saw the Colts overcome a 14–0
21 deficit and mount a dramatic comeback, securing a 15–14 victory with a decisive fourth-quarter
22 two-point conversion;
23 WHEREAS, this championship marks the Colts’ first Turkey Bowl victory since 1986,
24 representing a historic achievement nearly four decades in the making;
25 WHEREAS, the team’s performance throughout the season and in the championship game
26 demonstrated resilience, grit, and unity, qualities that reflect the strength and pride of the Coolidge
27 community;
28 WHEREAS, the team’s success reflects the dedication of its student-athletes, including:
