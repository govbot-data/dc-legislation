ENROLLED ORIGINAL
A RESOLUTION
26-18
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
February 4, 2025
To declare the existence of an emergency, due to congressional review, with respect to the need
to amend the Rental Housing Act of 1985 to maintain a moratorium on applications for,
and the Mayor’s issuance of, certificates of assurance associated with District rent
stabilization laws.
RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this resolution
may be cited as the “Certificate of Assurance Moratorium Congressional Review Emergency
Declaration Resolution of 2025”.
Sec. 2. (a) On November 12, 2024, the Council passed the Certificate of Assurance
Moratorium Emergency Amendment Act of 2024, effective November 22, 2024 (D.C. Act 25-
633; 71 DCR 14464), which will expire on February 20, 2025.
(b) On November 26, 2024, the Council passed the Certificate of Assurance Moratorium
Temporary Amendment Act of 2024, enacted on December 19, 2024 (D.C. Act 25-663; 71 DCR
16298), which was transmitted to Congress on January 23, 2025, and is projected to become law
on March 6, 2025.
(c) This emergency legislation is necessary to prevent a gap in the law between the
expiration of the emergency act and the effective date of the temporary act.
Sec. 3. The Council of the District of Columbia determines that the circumstances
enumerated in section 2 constitute emergency circumstances making it necessary that the
Certificate of Assurance Moratorium Congressional Review Emergency Amendment Act of
2025 be adopted after a single reading.
Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
