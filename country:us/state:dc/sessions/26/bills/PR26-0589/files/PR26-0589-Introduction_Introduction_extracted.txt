16 To declare the existence of an emergency with respect to the need to confirm the appointment of
17 Vince Micone to the District of Columbia Water and Sewer Authority Board of Directors.
18
19 RESOLVED, BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
20 resolution may be cited as the “District of Columbia Water and Sewer Authority Board of Directors
21 Vince Micone Confirmation Emergency Declaration Resolution of 2026”.
22 Sec. 2. (a) The District of Columbia Water and Sewer Authority (“DC Water”) is governed
23 by a Board of Directors (“Board”), consisting of 11 principal and 11 alternate members. Six board
24 members must be “District residents, appointed by the Mayor with the advice and consent of the
25 Council.”
26 (b) On December 15, 2025, the District of Columbia Water and Sewer Authority Board of
27 Directors Vince Micone Confirmation Resolution of 2026, as introduced on December 15, 2025
28 (P.R. 26-486), was introduced by Chairman Mendelson at the request of the Mayor. The legislation
29 would confirm Vince Micone as an alternate District resident member of the Board.
1

30 (c) PR26-486 was referred to the Committee on Transportation and the Environment on
31 January 6, 2026, and the Committee held a public roundtable on the measure on March 20, 2026.
32 The Committee did not consider and vote on PR26-486 at its March 24, 2026, Additional Meeting.
33 (d) Without Council action, PR26-0486 will be deemed disapproved on April 7, 2026. If
34 the Council does not act to confirm Mr. Micone’s nomination at the March 31, 2026, Legislative
35 Meeting and PR26-0586 is disapproved, there will be a vacant seat on the Board reserved for
36 District residents.
37 Sec. 3. The Council determines that the circumstances enumerated in section 2 constitute
38 emergency circumstances making it necessary that the District of Columbia Water and Sewer
39 Authority Board of Directors Vince Micone Confirmation Emergency Approval Resolution of
40 2026 be adopted on an emergency basis.
41 Sec. 4. This resolution shall take effect immediately.
