Councilmember Brianne K. Nadeau
A BILL
_________________________
IN THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA
_________________________
1 To amend the Government Employer-Assisted Housing Amendment Act of 1999 to designate
2 public transit employees as eligible recipients for the Government Employer-Assisted
3 Housing Program.
4
5 BE IT ENACTED BY THE COUNCIL OF THE DISTRICT OF COLUMBIA, That this
6 act may be cited as the “Home Purchase Assistance for Transit Workers Amendment Act of
7 2025”.
8 Sec. 2. The Government Employer-Assisted Housing Amendment Act of 1999, effective
9 May 9, 2000 (D.C. Law 13-96; D.C. Official Code § 42-2501 et seq.), is amended as follows:
10 (a) Section 2 (D.C. Official Code § 42-2501) is amended by adding a new paragraph (11)
11 to read as follows:
12 “(11) “Public transit employee” means a person employed and licensed to operate
13 or maintain a publicly-owned mass transit system within the District of Columbia, including
14 Metrobus, Metrorail, MetroAccess, and DC Streetcar.
15 (b) Section 4 (D.C. Official Code § 42-2503) is amended as follows:
16 (1) Subsection (a)(1) is amended to read as follows:
17 “(1) A District of Columbia government employee, a first-responder, an educator,
1

18 or a public transit employee; and”.
19 (2) Subsection (c) is amended by striking the phrase “with the District of
20 Columbia or as an educator” and inserting the phrase “with the District of Columbia, as an
21 educator, or as a public transit employee” in its place.
22 Sec. 3. Fiscal impact statement.
23 The Council adopts the fiscal impact statement in the committee report as the fiscal
24 impact statement required by section 4a of the General Legislative Procedures Act of 1975,
25 approved October 16, 2006 (120 Stat. 2038; D.C. Official Code § 1-301.47a).
26 Sec. 4. Effective date.
27 This act shall take effect after approval by the Mayor (or in the event of veto by the
28 Mayor, action by the Council to override the veto), a 30-day period of congressional review as
29 provided in section 602(c)(1) of the District of Columbia Home Rule Act, approved December
30 24, 1973 (87 Stat. 813; D.C. Official Code § 1-206.02(c)(1)), and publication in the District of
31 Columbia Register.
